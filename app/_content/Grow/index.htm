Grow

Seeking God in everyday life

 

Jesus is the most concrete and complete representation we have of God and his nature. So our growth with God depends on how willing we are to make choices to know him better and apply his teaching in our lives.

 

Essentials of Growth

We have to make choices to seek and depend on God in order to really experience his power and intentions for our lives. "Spiritual disciplines" are ways we can create spaces for God to work in our lives. Ready to grow? Subscribe to The Daily—a tool that enables you to spend time every day engaging with God.

 

Community

We grow closer to God as we grow closer to others in community. If you’re not in a smaller community of some kind, you’re missing out on good times and huge growth opportunities. Check out how to get in community.

 

Baptism

Being baptized means you’ve decided to accept and follow Jesus, and are ready to declare that decision publicly. Baptism is kind of like a wedding ring: it’s an outward symbol of an inward commitment to something bigger than oneself. It shows we identify with Christ’s death and resurrection into new life.

 

Lead Your Family

 If you’re a parent or a spouse, there are resources to help you lead your family’s spiritual growth, for marriage, general parenting and specific tools for parents of kids and teens.
