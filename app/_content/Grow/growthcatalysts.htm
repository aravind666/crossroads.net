Growth Catalysts

Ways to turbo-charge your spiritual growth or mark milestones on your journey:

Baptism

Baptism is an outward symbol of your commitment to follow Jesus, and a way to identify with his death and resurrection into new life.

Read Welcome to the Revolution

Email us for your free copy of this book that serves as a field guide for a new follower of Jesus.

Attend the Welcome to the Revolution Community Group

Learn about how to live your faith and discuss your spiritual journey at this Community Group specifically for new followers of Jesus.

Attend Last Wednesdays

The Bible encourages believers to meet together regularly. Join us at Crossroads Oakley for prayer, worship and communion the last Wednesday of the month at 7pm.

Participate in the Journey

Every year, our community goes on a spiritual journey together. We zero in on one topic for six weeks and everyone (kids, teens and adults) grows in similar ways through weekend teaching, personal exploration and small group gatherings. You can learn about past journeys here.

Sign Up for a Trip

Join in with a larger group for a short blitz of cross-cultural serving. Everything about these event-based serving opportunities—from the planning to the fundraising to connecting with community in a different environment—will grow your faith. Sign up to for a GO trip so you can be the church in our world.
