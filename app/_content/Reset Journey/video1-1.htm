Week One: Jesus Reset Assumptions

This week the Reset journey begins by taking a look at the evidence for Jesus' existence and challenging us to reset any assumptions we have about him. Each week during the journey we'll post links to relevant resources, discussions and more that will be helpful in our attempt to reset our Jesus assumptions. (Learn more about Reset.)

In addition, each week we'll provide some extra, uncut footage from our Reset DVD interviews.

Miss the weekend message? Watch the video here.

Week One:
Dr. Greg Boyd shares his story of moving from atheism to belief in God. Dr. Greg Boyd shares why biblical accounts are trustworthy. Dr. Keith Crutcher discusses the beliefs of early Jesus-followers. Dr. Edwin Yamauchi discusses the writing of the New Testament.
