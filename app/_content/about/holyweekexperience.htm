Holy Week Experience

Engage in the story of God

 

In anticipation of Easter, Christ-followers often remember the week leading up to Jesus’ death and resurrection as Holy Week. Come and join us this week at Crossroads Oakley for the Holy Week Experience, an interactive journey through the last week of Jesus’ life, encompassing worship, prayer, stories and tactile experiences. It’s an immersive way to engage more deeply in the story of God we’ve been exploring during our current weekend message series.

 

Dates and Times:

  • Wednesday, April 20: 5-9pm
  • Thursday, April 21: 7-9am, 11:30am-1:30pm* & 5-9pm*
  • Friday, April 22: 7-9am, 11:30am-1:30pm* & 5-9pm

*Childcare available for kids up through fourth grade at selected times only; child check-in closes an hour before experience ends. 


Please arrive as early as possible to avoid a long line! Also, the experience lasts about 45 minutes, so plan accordingly (don’t show up 15 minutes before the experience closes). Finally, a request: if you have kids and you and your spouse can trade-off and attend separately, please do so to free up childcare space for parents without another option.
