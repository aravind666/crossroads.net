Last Wednesday, a time of worship through music, prayer and communion, meets on the last Wednesday of each month at 7pm in Florence.

Kids’ Club
Fun-learning environment designed especially for kids (birth through fifth grade). It’s free, totally secure and offered during all services. Visit here to get new KC info, videos, free music and podcasts, parent resources, volunteer info, FAQ and contacts/directions. 

We’re Open.

In addition to weekend services, Crossroads’ facility in Florence is open three days a week (Tues and Thurs, 6am-9pm, Sat 7am-7pm). Free coffee, free Wi-Fi and plenty of couches. Come for a meeting, just to hang out with friends, or even for a quiet place to read or pray. 

Directions

We’re located at 828 Heights Blvd, just south of the Florence Mall.
