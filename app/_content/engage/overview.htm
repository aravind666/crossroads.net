Get Involved

Want to engage?

God calls us to serve to bless others’ lives, to find a sense of community and to increase our own capacity to love. Whether you want to serve inside Crossroads, on our city streets, or in another country, we have exciting ways for you to get involved. 

It takes thousands of volunteers to make Crossroads happen every weekend. We’re always looking for more folks to do everything from hang out with kids to make coffee. Just give it a try. It doesn’t have to be ’til-death-do-we-part. You can meet new friends, grow spiritually and maybe learn the secret handshake. Choose an area of interest below.
