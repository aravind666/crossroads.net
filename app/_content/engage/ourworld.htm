ReachOut: Our World

Change is real, and it's now.

Whether it’s South Africa, New Orleans or India, we leave our own neighborhoods to be in others. We don’t go as fixers; we go as servant-learners. And we believe in forming lasting, reciprocal relationships with the people we serve. That’s how homes get built, victims comforted, children rescued and lives literally changed.

See where we're going next, and how we're getting ready at home. Even if you never thought of yourself as a "missionary," you might be surprised how it takes all sorts of people to change this world.
