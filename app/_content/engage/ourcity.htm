ReachOut: Our City

We believe in changing our city. Join us?

God commands his followers to serve the poor and oppressed. We strive to take that command seriously, and we seek to learn from those who have less. Sometimes it’s a one-day citywide blitz, sometimes it’s investing time with the same child or prison inmate from week to week.

We believe there’s something for everyone, whether you can give one day, one week or 52. Try out some of these serving opportunities, until you find one that really fits. You might find a new talent or hobby, and you’ll certainly grow in community and impact lives.

Thanksgiving Food Drive: meal collection and giveaway
Thanksgiving Day Fall Feast: holiday meal serving with City Gospel Mission
The Diner: meal-serving at City Gospel Mission
The Outlet: game days at City Gospel Mission
Habitat for Humanity: home building
Whiz Kids: tutoring and mentoring
Wheels: auto ministry
StepUp: prison mentoring
TeamWorks: job mentoring
CityLink: initiatives for the poor
Angel Tree: gift-giving for kids with parents in prison
