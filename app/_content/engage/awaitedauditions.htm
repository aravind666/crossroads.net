Thanks for your interest in auditioning for Awaited. 

Awaited: A Christmas Show is an original, contemporary retelling of the Christmas story and it’s returning for an 8th season. The show is a Crossroads production of full-scale theatrical performances utilizing all local talent for everything from musical arrangements to dance to costume and set design.

The performance includes a ten-piece band, adult choir, children’s choir, original dance performances and layered video projections. Audience members are immersed in the experience—from stars on the ceiling to snow falling to choir members and dancers moving throughout the auditorium. To make room for our friends, this year we’ve expanded seating capacity with 29 free, ticketed shows which will take place at Crossroads Oakley starting on December 5th.

Each year, we try to find hidden gems in our community who have the kind of talent and experience that can enrich Awaited. In the past, our cast has included touring musicians, recording artists, broadway dancers and card-carrying members of SAG. 

Awaited show producers will be holding auditions for cast roles on June 17, June 24, July 15 and July 22 for actors, dancers, drummers and singers. 

(For more info on stage crew or kids’ roles, please email awaitedvolunteers@crossroads.net.)
