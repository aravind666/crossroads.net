Serve: Student Ministry

It

Student Ministry at Crossroads is a place for students in Middle School and High School to make friends, have fun and explore a relationship with Jesus. You don’t have to be an expert in the Bible to get involved. There are plenty of opportunities to help with high school students—whether it’s leading a small group or helping set up a connect event, we have a spot for you.

Download the Student Ministry Volunteer application to get started.

For more info, go to crossroads.net/studentministry.
