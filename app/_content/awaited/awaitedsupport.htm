Awaited Ticket Return or Support

If you can’t use your tickets, please return them so others can attend the show. You don’t have to return all of your tickets, just the ones you don’t need. To return your tickets, please call the support number at 513.731.7400 ext 1589 or email the following to awaitedsupport@crossroads.net. If you are leaving a voicemail, please include a phone number in the event that we need to call you back. Thank you. 

 
1. First & Last Name on the reservation
2. The event (date & time) the ticket is for
3. The barcode (# on the right of your ticket) OR email address used to reserve the tickets
4. Section, row & seat numbers you are returning
 
Note–if you are reserving handicap tickets, please call the number above or email awaitedsupport@crossroads.net. Please include information regarding your preferred show date & time and how many handicap seats are required.
