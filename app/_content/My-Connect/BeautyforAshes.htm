Beauty for Ashes

Mondays: April 14-July 7. 7-8:30pm.

A safe place for women who have experienced sexual abuse. Discuss the possibility of recovery and joy.

You’re invited to join us for a 12 week course using the book Beauty for Ashes by Joyce Meyer. Meyer was a victim of physical, mental and sexual abuse. Today, she has a nationwide ministry of emotional healing for abuse victims.

The group will focus on:
  • How to deal with the emotional pain of abuse
  • How to understand your responsibility in overcoming abuse
  • Why victims of abuse often suffer from other addictive behaviors
  • How to receive God’s unconditional, renewing love
  • The importance of God’s timing in working through painful memories
Strict anonymity and confidentiality will be maintained to protect participants and family members. Group limited to 15 women. The deadline to register is April 6.

Twelve Mondays: April 14, 21, 28; May 5, 12, 19; June 2, 9, 16, 23, 30; July 7. 7-8:30pm.

Childcare
If you make a reservation in advance, childcare is available for nursery through fifth grade in Kids’ Club. Donations of $5/child or $10/family will help cover the cost. A 10-child minimum is required per session/date. If this minimum isn’t met, childcare won’t be provided for that session/date and you will be notified three days prior to the session.

Pre-work
Purchase the expanded edition of Beauty for Ashes by Joyce Meyer. (Family Christian Bookstore in Hyde Park offers a 20% discount; just mention you’re from Crossroads.)
