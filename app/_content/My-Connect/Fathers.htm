Fathers

Friday mornings: Beginning October 3 for 31 weeks, ending May 15, 2009. 6:03-7:30am.

For married men with children who want to answer the questions: How do I become the man that my wife genuinely enjoys and my kids seek out? What choices do I make today that will bring the best results in the long term? How do I meet my family's needs in the midst of my career? What will be my legacy?

 

Are other dads struggling with the same things as I am?

How to be a dad was never modeled for many of us. Some of us are workaholics. Some of us seem to be distant from our kids. Some of us are just confused about marriage and parenting. Some of us do not realize we are off track, and aren't sure we should ask.

Join in learning practical tips from fathers who have been there, enjoy mutual accountability, free coffee and food, and encouragement for your own goals in a community of like-minded men. Get to know other dads.
