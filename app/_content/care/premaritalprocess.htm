Premarital Process 

You’re officially in the premarital process! Congrats. Now it’s time to get started with the real work of investing in your relationship. The requirements in the premarital process aren’t hoops to jump through, each step is intentionally designed to create conversation between you and your fiance. We suggest planning ahead to allow these requirements to become an important part of your engagement–go on dates and talk about what you’re learning. We believe if you give each step your full attention, you will feel the influence in your relationship for years to come.

In the first 30 days of our premarital process, we ask that you get 4 key things done. Click below for more information:

- Turn in Letters of Recommendation

- Write, discuss and turn in your Spiritual Journey Statements

- Schedule your Dollars & Sense consultation

- Schedule Building Blocks for Marriage or Alternative Options for Premarital Counseling

If you’re using the Crossroads Chapel, please submit a check for $400 by mailing it to 3500 Madison Road or dropping it off at the front office during regular business hours (M-F 9AM-5PM). Keep in mind that right now your date is tentatively reserved. After we receive all of your information, your wedding date will be confirmed. If you don’t complete all of the steps of the process by your due date, we’ll be obligated to release your date.
