ReachOut: CityLink


We believe we need a revolutionary approach to defeating the cycle of poverty in our city.

Because there is no current infrastructure equipped to handle the thousands of volunteer hours per year that Crossroads could generate, we’ve joined a consortium of local churches and non-profit organizations to build CityLink, a centralized campus where people can connect with each other and with the services they need to find holistic life change and break the cycles of poverty.

The process of making CityLink a reality has been challenging, but we’re making a lot of progress.  In the meantime, we’re forging ahead with several CityLink Now initiatives alongside several partner organizations:

  • TeamWorks Job Mentoring
  • StepUp Prison Mentoring
  • The Outlet at City Gospel Mission
