Habitat for Humanity

Pick up a hammer and help us build a home for a family in need.

Our city has one of the lowest home ownership rates in the country. Only 42% of residents in our city own their homes, compared to 68% nationally. Statistically speaking, home ownership leads to significant increases in family stability, financial security and a sense of belonging to the community. And all of those things increase the likelihood that children can escape a cycle of poverty.

We believe that decent, affordable housing for all people is something that God cares about deeply.

So join us on Saturdays as we give volunteer labor to help build homes in Cincinnati. Come every week, one Saturday or anywhere in between. No construction experience is required.

If you'd like to help out, click here for more specific information about our current construction project and to fill out an application. (If you're not already signed in, you'll be asked to do so, or to register for crossroads.net.)
