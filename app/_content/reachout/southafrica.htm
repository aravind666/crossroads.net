Be a part of something bigger than yourself!

Join hundreds of Americans and South Africans as we provide hope on the other side of the world. Crossroads is sending XXX volunteers to work alongside our partner in South Africa, Grace Bible Church. Grace Bible is one of the largest churches in South Africa and is a light for those battling the HIV/AIDS epidemic and living in extreme poverty.

Anyone over the age of 13 is invited to GO South Africa—it doesn’t matter whether or not you attend Crossroads. So, invite your friends, family, and/or people you want to be your friends or family.
