ReachOut: Habitat

Serving Habitat for Humanity

We believe simple, decent, affordable housing for all people is something God cares about deeply.

Our city has one of the lowest home ownership rates in the country. Only 42% of residents in our city own their homes, compared to 68% nationally. Statistically speaking, home ownership leads to significant increases in family stability, financial security and a sense of belonging to the community. And all of those things increase the likelihood that children can escape a cycle of poverty.

We’re finishing a new home for the Thomas family and we’d love your help. It’s good to know:

- No construction experience required, but if you’ve got it, we’ll take it.

- Habitat provides all the tools and equipment but if you’re a pro and have tools in your truck, make sure your name is on ‘em and bring ‘em.

- You must be 18 to serve. Sorry, no exceptions.

- We arrive on site every Saturday at 7:45am sharp to pray and receive instructions for the day. We work from 8am-4pm.

- Lunch is provided and is served at 11:30am.

- You must sign up below to serve.

- Please put the dates on your calendar as you sign up, so a reminder email from us is not needed.
