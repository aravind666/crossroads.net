Background

In 2005, Hurricane Katrina devastated a community that was already struggling. At that time, Crossroads sent $150,000 and a truckload of donated supplies to the Gulf Region. Over the last 4 summers, Crossroads followed up by sending hundreds of people to build homes with Habitat for Humanity, to love on kids at Vacation Bible School, and to provide family portraits to families that lost their pictures in the flood. Even now, almost seven years later, much work remains for us to do. So we’re going back. God calls us to help those in need, and we will do everything we can to be a blessing.
