Thanksgiving Food Drive

Reach out this holiday by providing meals to families in Cincinnati, in Mamelodi, South Africa, or both.

For the last 12 years, Crossroads has collected food during the Thanksgiving season and distributed it to local Cincinnati churches so that they can be a blessing to their communities. We also ship food to our partner church in Mamelodi, South Africa, so that they can do the same. We'll feed thousands of people here and across the world. And you can help.
