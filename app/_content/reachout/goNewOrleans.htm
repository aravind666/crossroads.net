Background

In 2005, Hurricane Katrina devastated a community that was already struggling. At that time, Crossroads sent $150,000 and a truckload of donated supplies to the Gulf Region. Over the last 3 summers, Crossroads followed up by sending hundreds of people to build homes with Habitat for Humanity, to love on kids at Vacation Bible School, and to provide family portraits to families that lost their pictures in the flood. You can learn more about what we did from last year’s trip blog. Even now, almost six years later, much work remains for us to do. So we’re going back. God calls us to help those in need, and we will do everything we can to be a blessing. This summer, we’ll send 400 people to New Orleans (That’s two trips of 200 each). Think you might want to GO? Attend an info session, and read the FAQs.
