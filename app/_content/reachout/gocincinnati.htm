We love Cincinnati. (All of it.)

For the last nine years an army of volunteers has impacted Cincinnati through service projects in a one-day, citywide blitz of compassion. We show love to hundreds of schools, churches, neighborhoods, social agencies and non-profits to put our faith in action and to say thank you for the work they do in our community.

We call it GO Cincinnati. We take on projects all over the region, from Middletown to Burlington and all places in between. And let’s face it, GO Flomasonoakwesteastside doesn’t really flow off the tongue. We recently tackled over 450 projects with the help of 8000 volunteers. Check out the recap video of GO Cincinnati 2014 and stay tuned on how you can get involved for next year.

Still have questions? Email us at gocincinnati@crossroads.net.
