Kids' Club Videos

Channel 34

Episode One

 

When most people think of sharing their faith, they picture preachers on the street corners or people going door-to-door trying to talk total strangers into believing what they do. Episode one shows kids that sharing your faith can be a natural part of your life, not something you force. In fact, the best people to share you faith with are you friends.

 

Episode Two

Everyone is tempted by something. Episode two introduces a Superman action figure as Jesus, and challenges kids to resist temptation when it comes knocking. Temptation itself is not a sin, but if you don't make a plan before you get tempted, resisting temptation is very difficult.

 

 

Episode Three

There are so many distractions in the world of a kid today that it can be difficult to focus on anything. Episode three shows some of the different things that can distract a kid from focusing on God, and gives some ideas on how we can keep our focus.

 

 

Episode Four

In episode four, a gnome plays poker with Oreos while baking bread for communion. Communion is the way that we remember what Jesus did for us. And communion doesn't have to be special bread or wine, you can even use pickle juice.

 

 

Episode Five

Getting baptized is like holding up a huge sign to the world that says, " I've decided to follow Jesus." It's a celebration of the new life you have when you choose to be Jesus' forever friend. In episode five we see some of the technical aspects of baptism, when our friend Noel reports from inside the water, and answers some frequently asked questions about baptisms.

 

 

 

Episode Six

Putting others first feels really strange. And putting God first can seem even stranger. After all, we're used to looking out for ourselves. Episode six shows some practical ways to put others first.

 

 

 

Episode Seven

On a scale of one to ten, episode seven is the color blue. Yes, it's that good. We make lots of choices every day, but the most important choice we'll make is either to build our lives on God's word, or on something else. By having a conversation about making wise choices and then practicing regularly, kids feel more confident when they find themselves making a choice.


 

 

Episode Eight

When was the last time someone did something that offended you? What was your response? Episode eight encourages kids to forgive others. Hear the story of a father who forgives his son, and what to do if you feel angry long after someone hurts you. Everyone has someone they could forgive.
