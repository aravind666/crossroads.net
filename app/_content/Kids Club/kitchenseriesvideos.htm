The Kitchen Series

 

Episode One

Shrimpola (a fun-loving, relatively large, talking blue shrimp) comes home from school to find that he has been gifted a personal chef/ professional Mexican wrestler (Chef-O Libre, in his first major acting role since MIME! International ‘03). With the help of Music Mae, Rachel, and many others, Shrimpola learns more about the life God wants for us and how to become a friend with Jesus.




Episode Two

Every day is an "interesting" day with Shrimpola, and this one is no exception. We learn more about Shrimpola (he is radioactive to a certain degree and has an unusual gym class) and Chef-O (his cooking utensils were given to him by a loved one), while also finding out how we can talk with God (pray) anytime we want.




Episode Three

Shrimpola finds out that there are many ways to give besides money (time, talents, jokes, and even bad jokes count) when he tries to help a friend. Professor Clauu (a Scottish lobster that lives in the roof of a barn), kangaroo boots, Chef-O’s alma mater and a bag of potatoes all guest star.



Episode Four

Shrimpola thinks about having a music career and wonders what his stage name could be. He also has to deal with the worst back itch ever and learns about obedience along the way.



Episode Five

Chef-O gets an assist from Farmer Karen and Professor Clauu when he is in need of some ingredients. Shrimpola finds out we can ask Jesus for help with anything...and that getting a merry-go-round to achieve light speed is no small feat!




Episode Six

With Narky’s birthday dinner fast approaching, Shrimpola and Chef-O both have their own menu they want to cook and need the other person to help. What to do! With the help of Rachel and Music Mae, Shrimpola finds out about how cooperation can save the day. Noel Gnomon guest stars and continues to follow in Jackie Chan’s footsteps by performing his own stunts.



Episode Seven

Shrimpola comes home to find Chef-O making all kinds of foods and drinks in preparation for talking about communion. However, Shrimpola misunderstands and believes Chef-O wants to talk about Cah-Mun-Yuns (a distant cousin to the American White Onion). Eventually everyone gets on the same page and Shrimpola learns about how communion is one of the many ways we can worship Jesus. Noel Gnomon guest stars in a flashback that explains why Shrimpola has a fear of toast.




Episode Eight

For the series finale, Chef-O has to try to win the Mexican Wrestling Championship match! Shrimpola spends some time with Rachel and learns that we can all be helpful in many different ways because of the gifts and talents God has given us.
