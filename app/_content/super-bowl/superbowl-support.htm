Super Bowl Support

If you can

1. First & Last Name on the reservation

2. The event (location, date & time) the ticket is for

3. The barcode (# on the right of your ticket) OR email address used to reserve the tickets

4. Section, row & seat numbers you are returning

Note–if you need wheelchair or handicap tickets for Oakley, please call the number above or email ticketingsupport@crossroads.net. Please include information regarding your preferred show time and whether you need a wheelchair and wheelchair escort seat, or a handicap seat. Wheelchair and handicap seats for Florence and Mason do not need special reservation.
