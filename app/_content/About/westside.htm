Crossroads West Side

 

8575 Bridgetown Road

If you’d like to be added to our email interest list, please sign up here. If you are new to Crossroads, explore our website here.

 

Interested in serving at Crossroads West Side? We have a place for you. 

 

Serve: Kids’ Club & Middle School

Kids’ Club is a fun learning environment designed just for kids. It’s free, totally secure and offered during all weekend services. Kids’ Club happens because of awesome volunteers. If you’re interested, connect with us and attend an info session. We will give you more information on the variety of ways to get involved including leading a small group of kids, welcoming new families, or creating videos. We look forward to helping you find the best role for you. To start your adventure, click here.

 

 

Serve: First Impressions

Create loving environments that help people experience God. From directing cars to handing out pens and programs, the First Impression (FI) Team does it. So if you like being host—or at least can open five pounds of front door—join FI. And you don’t have to be a lifer. Try it out for a few weeks and see how it suits you. You can choose between weekday and weekend opportunities.

 

If you’ve made the decision to serve in First Impressions, the first step in your serving experience is to attend FI 101, where you will learn the ins and outs of First Impressions. From there, you’ll take the next step to begin serving on the team of your choosing. To sign up to serve at the West Side, click here.
