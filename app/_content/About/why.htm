Why Register

Why Register?

Because community is about saying "hi" and sticking around.

Also, registering gets you:

  • Personalized content. Tell us who you are, what you care about and what you want to hear. We'll give you the info you want, when you need it.
  • Full access to all our media. Totally free (as always). Hundreds of messages in MP3 format, original music and videos.
  • Corkboard privileges. Here you'll have the ability to give and receive free stuff, peruse events and even search (or post) job opportunities.
  • Plugged into the community. Sign up for groups and serving opps within and outside Crossroads.
  • Connected to events. You'll get a personalized calendar with information pertinent to you.
