We’re Open. 

In addition to weekend services, Crossroads’ facility in Oakley is open six days a week (Mon-Fri 6am-9pm, Sat 7am-8pm). Free coffee, free Wi-Fi and plenty of couches. Come for a meeting, just to hang out with friends, or even for a quiet place to read or pray. 

 

FAQ

Wasn’t the building at 3500 Madison a hardware store?

Yes. Crossroads’ building used to be a big-box store called Home Quarters until it went out of business in 2000. Bad for HQ, good for Crossroads.

 

What is the Annex?

The building across the street (Vandercar Way) from Crossroads at 3500 Madison. It used to be a car dealership, but now it’s the Annex, used for parking, meetings, events and sometimes petting zoos.
