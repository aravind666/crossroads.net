Spiritual growth isn't one-size-fits-all, but here are some recommended steps for being intentional about your personal growth. We call these steps the "Three Staples of Growth":

  1. Attend the Weekend Experience

    Connect with others to explore questions about God and the Bible, and be challenged to apply what you learn to your life. Here are service times and directions.

  2. Subscribe to “The Daily”

    Spend time with God on a daily basis. We make it easy for you with The Daily, a tool that enables you to engage with the Bible and prayer regularly. Able to be completed in just 15 minutes, each day follows a simple format of reading, reflection and response. You can subscribe to receive The Daily via e-mail, or read it online. (You'll be prompted to sign in or register for crossroads.net.)

  3. Join a Small(er) Group

    We weren’t meant to do life alone. Join a small group or a Community Group to grow closer to God as you grow closer to others in community.


In addition to the three recommended staples of growth, there are also many growth catalysts that can mark milestones on your journey to help turbo-charge your spiritual growth.
