Moms

Being a Mom is a privilege that is both amazing and hard. It challenges us, grows us and sometimes even kicks our butt. The beauty is God can use this gift, to teach us about his character and how he feels about us. And He wants us to be a strong influencer in our family and train kids to experience his goodness too.

 

Even on good days this responsibility can seem overwhelming. Thank goodness God doesn’t want us to do it alone. Community is a necessary element in this journey of motherhood. Join other moms who want to know God more while learning practical skills for parenting.

 

This group consists of teaching and small group discussion. We celebrate success and we celebrate failures. We move closer to God and encourage one another along the way. Get a glimpse of how this group is changing lives in our community by viewing a short clip here. 

 

Series include:

1. Mom Power: We’ll talk about how we seek God, love our kids and maintain some order in our family by relying on God’s power.

2. My Role: God gives each of us gifts to serve our family and others with. We’ll talk about what some of those gifts are and how to let God use them to help our family.

3. Family Team: God intends for his kingdom to keep growing and he loves to use families to accomplish this. In this series we’ll talk about living as a family team.

4. Stewarding God’s Investment: God has given all of us capital in the form of time, money, physical health and many other things. When we steward them in an inspired way, we can see the Kingdom move.

5. Sharing Your Story: We all have a unique story God gives us and He loves when we tell it to others. We’ll talk about why it’s important and how your story is connected to our partnership with God.
