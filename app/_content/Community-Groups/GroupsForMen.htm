Dudes only. No girls allowed.

Groups for Men focus on topics or discussions relevant to men, or offer the chance to connect with other men about a general topic.

SEASONAL GROUPS
Gatherings of people who want to explore a specific interest, study a book, get in-depth teaching and share personal growth. Groups meet for a specific period of time-usually a few weeks or months. Sign-ups happen twice a year (spring and fall). Choose a group for more info, and to sign up:

  • Fathers

ON-GOING GROUPS
On-Going Community Groups are like visiting on the front porch—casual, and come-when-you-can. They run all year, and are open to anyone, anytime. It’s a place where you can find community with others, ask questions, pursue answers and grow deep with God. Select a group below to learn more.

  • Men’s Healthy Sexuality
  • Men’s Healthy Choices
