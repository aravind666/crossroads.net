Good Sex Follow Up Groups

 

Good Sex for Married Couples (All sites)

- Oakley: Tuesdays, July 9 & 30, 7–8:30pm, Meeting Room B, childcare available with RSVP

- Florence: Tuesdays, June 18 & 25, 7–8pm, Meeting Room, no childcare

- Mason: Sundays, June 23 & 30, 5:30–6:30pm, Garage at the White House, no childcare

 

Improve your sexual intimacy as a couple since God desires married couples to delight in good sex. We’ll give you a discussion guide and we’ll discuss the difficulties and delights that will help you talk about sex with your spouse over several date nights taking place at all three sites. See registration information below.

Women’s Healthy Sexuality

Meets July 10 at 7-8:30PM in Meeting Room B

 

In our society there is a stereotype that only men struggle with compulsive sexual behaviors but research has found that 40-50% of those who struggle with compulsive sexual behaviors are women. If you are a woman that faces this struggle, you are not alone

 

This follow-up group is for women who have been affected by or who have struggled with compulsive sex behaviors, from masturbation, pornography and/or sexual activities. Women can RSVP to communitygroups@crossroads.net.
