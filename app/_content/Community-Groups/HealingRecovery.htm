Healing is possible. Don’t go it alone.

Healing and Recovery groups are for individuals looking to overcome personal crises and/or addictions.

SEASONAL GROUPS
Gatherings of people who want to explore a specific interest, study a book, get in-depth teaching and share personal growth. Groups meet for a specific period of time—usually a few weeks or months. Sign-ups happen twice a year (March/April and September). Registration for some groups is still available. Choose a group for more info, and to sign up:

  • Divorce Rebuilding
  • Grief Support

ONGOING GROUPS
Ongoing Community Groups are like visiting on the front porch—casual, and come-when-you-can. They run all year, and are open to anyone, anytime. It’s a place where you can find community with others, ask questions, pursue answers and grow deep with God. Select a group below to learn more.

  • Healing Betrayed Hearts
  • Men’s Healthy Sexuality
  • Men’s Healthy Choices
