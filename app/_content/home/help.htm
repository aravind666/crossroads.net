I forgot my password

Click here to reset your password.

 

 

I forgot my username

Click here to request your username.

 

I want to change my password.

1. Log into crossroads.net
2. Hover Over My Crossroads
3. Click on Profile
4. Click on the Account Tab
5. Enter a new password, then enter again to confirm
6. Click Save
7. You will be prompted to enter your old password
8. Click Submit and your Password will be changed

 

I can’t find what I’m looking for.

Go to this Google search page that will search only our website. After clicking the link, you’ll notice that there’s already something in the Google search box that says "site:crossroads.net". Leave that there and type whatever you’re looking for immediately after it. For example your search might look like this site:crossroads.net grace

 

 

I can’t find my group to sign up to serve

Ugh, sorry about this, it’s likely that you have two accounts in our system. This might have happened for a couple different reasons:

1. You created two accounts for yourself (oops!)
2. You signed up to serve, gave a check, or registered as a new family. When that happens, sometimes we forget to check if you already have an account.

 

We can fix it though. Just contact your ministry leader letting them know what’s wrong. They’ll help you get squared away.

 

 

I signed up for The Daily but I got an error.

Sorry that this happened. Drop us a note, letting us know what email address you’d like to have subscribed to The Daily. We’ll get you added within a week. In the meantime, login to read it online (after logging in, click the tab called "The Daily").

 

 

I’m signed up for The Daily already (I think), but I’m not receiving it.

First, add yourself, then check your email tomorrow morning. If you still didn’t receive it, check your spam and also create a contact with thedaily@crossroads.net as the email address. Add the contact will help your email provider know that email from thedaily@crossroads.net is legitimate. If none of that works, it’s likely an issue with your internet service provider, so we recommend getting an account with GMail. 

 

 

I can’t find my giving statement 

Make sure you’re logged into crossroads.net, then go here. For future, you can go to the "My Crossroads" menu item and click on "Giving History".

 

 

Someone gave to my trip, but I don’t see it in my donations

It’s likely that we’re still working on it. It takes about a week for checks to show up in our system and about 3 days for credit card donations to show up. So, give it a week and if it’s still not there, contact the Finance Team.

 

 

I need help with GO (Cincinnati, South Africa, India, etc)

You’ll find every contact you need in the ReachOut section of the staff contact list.

 

 

I’ve gone through everything on this page and nothing has solved my problem

Sorry that you’re still having trouble. Please contact us and we’ll do our best to get everything straightened out. Please be very detailed in your description of the problem. For many problems, it’s helpful if we know if you’re on a Mac or a Windows computer and also which web browser you’re using (including version). We’ll also need to know the web page that’s giving you trouble (please copy / paste a link).
