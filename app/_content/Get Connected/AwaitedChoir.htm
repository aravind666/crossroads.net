So, you want to sing? 

Choir auditions are being held for anyone interested in being in Awaited (new folks, as well as returning choir members) on Tuesday, October 16 and Wednesday, October 17 from 6-9pm. Please read over all the requirements and prayerfully consider your involvement before signing up to audition. 

 

All choir members must attend EVERY rehearsal and perform in EVERY show. This year there are 15 shows, but we are taking off a day/night in the middle of the run, and we have less double show days. We realize this is a big commitment, but we’re excited to have consistency and clarity around roles and responsibilities.
