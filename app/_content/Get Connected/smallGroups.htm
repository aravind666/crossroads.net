Small Groups

The weekend service can be inspiring, but it can't entirely fill the need we have for community.

That’s why we pare down into small groups of six, ten or so: gatherings where you can get comfortable, share, invest, change. We believe that big impact results from the life change that happens in small community. Because we're not made to do life alone. So God uses intimate connections with other people as catalysts for change—in us, in our relationships with him and others, in our families and in the world. Big stuff. Ready for something big? Start by getting small.
