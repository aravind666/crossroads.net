Healing and Recovery groups are for individuals looking to overcome personal crises and/or addictions. Healing is possible. Don't go it alone.

Thursdays: 7-9pm at Crossroads Oakley
Mondays, 7:30-9pm at Vineyard Community Church, Springdale
A program for men who seek healthy life choices around their sexual behaviors. MHS groups are Christ-centered, anonymous communities of healing and hope. More Details

MEN'S HEALTHY CHOICES
Thursdays: 7-9pm at Crossroads Oakley
