Welcome to Crossroads!

We’re so glad that you’ve recently checked out a service at Crossroads in Mason. If you’re interested in finding out more about this place and the things going on around here, or if you’d like to get involved, here’s an easy way to get connected:

If you haven’t already done so, I encourage you to visit www.crossroads.net and register on our website. You can find out more information about who we are and why we do the things we do, check out events that are happening, download past messages or sign-up for a serving opportunity. And by registering, you’ll be able to personalize your Crossroads experience, so everything you need is right at your fingertips.

Registering is easy. To create your personal account, go to www.crossroads.net and choose a username and password. This way, you can own your information, such as address, phone number and favorite coffee flavor (kidding), and update it all on your time. And all of your personal information is kept confidential and is stored securely.

I hope that you’ll find our website to be a useful tool as you explore Crossroads. But if you have additional questions, please feel free to email us.
