Marriage Resources

Want to grow your marriage? Here are some resources to get you started.

Local Program

The Third Option
(14-week class at Montgomery Community Church)
This ongoing marriage enrichment/ marriage crisis group program uses a drop-in format, skill-building and sharing by couples who have overcome difficulties.
Thursdays 7-9pm
513-489-0892 x234
www.thethirdoption.com

Conferences & Retreats

Retrouvaille (pronounced “retro–vie”)
A program for couples with serious problems who are distant, cold, disillusioned, separated and/ or who have experienced affairs, alcoholism, gambling, violence, etc. Trained volunteer couples who have “been to the brink” teach how to heal marriages.
January 8—Kings Island Resort—Mason, OH
www.retrouvaille.org

Marriage Encounter
Designed to give married couples the opportunity to learn a technique of loving communication that they can use for the rest of their lives.
February 12-14—Comfort Inn—Erlanger, KY
www.wwme.org

Weekend to Remember (sponsored by Family Life)
You will learn timeless blueprints for marriage, commitment, communication, romance and resolving conflict.
March 5-7—Hyatt Regency—Cincinnati
www.familylife.com
