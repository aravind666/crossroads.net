Groups for Men focus on topics or discussions relevant to men, or offer the chance to connect with other men about a general topic. Men only. No girls allowed.

FATHERS
Fridays: April 16-May 14 from 6:03-7:30am (Three locations)
Crossroads Oakley
Anderson Center Community Room
Panera Bread at Union Center
For married fathers who want to become a husband their wife enjoys, the father their kids seek out, a provider who meets his family’s needs in the midst of his career, and a man who leaves a legacy. More Details

MEN'S HEALTHY CHOICES
Thursdays: 7-9pm at Crossroads Oakley
If you’re struggling with addictive behaviors and you’re ready to do something about it, come and learn how to overcome addictions in a safe environment. We’ll explore topics focused on recognizing, acknowledging and owning the addictions in our lives on a path toward recovery. More Details

HALFTIME: MOVING FROM SUCCESS TO SIGNIFICANCE
Tuesdays: April 20-May 5 from 6:45-7:45am at Panera Bread on Mason-Montgomery Road and at Anderson Center
For men who want to make their middle years not just successful, but also significant. Join other men on this journey through Bob Buford’s book “Halftime.” More Details

Mondays: 7:30-9pm at Vineyard Community Church, Springdale
