What is a Journey Host?

Being a host means that you welcome a small group of 6-12 people into your home—or another suitable spot—for six weeks. You don’t need to have all the answers, and we don’t expect you to be a Bible expert or therapist. Just be friendly, inclusive, a quiet leader and a good role model.

We

 

-Help set some ground rules. We

-Be a timekeeper. Just be sure your group is starting and ending at the agreed upon time. 

-Bring everyone into the conversation. If you notice someone being quiet, ask if they’d like to give some input.

-Make it safe—respect any and all questions. 

-Feel free to pray for your group during group time. 

-Did we mention have fun? Have some.
