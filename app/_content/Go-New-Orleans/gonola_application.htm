GO New Orleans Application


Welcome to the Go New Orleans On-line Application and congrats on taking the first step toward an incredible experience!

 

On this application please provide us with as much truthful information as possible as it will help us to better plan the trip. Also, please know that no person will be turned away from the trip based on their skills, spirituality or overall demeanor. We want everyone to go if we can make it work. So don’t fret on your answers to some of the application questions. They are here simply so the leaders get a sense of who we are dealing with and how our team can best be a blessing to the world.

Application Process

You can sign up as a(n):

  • individual

  • married couple

  • family

The person signing up will respond for his or her spouse and children.

All applications must be received by 2:23pm on February 23. There are 600 spots available between the two weeks, 300 for each one-week trip. If more than 600 applications are received, we will conduct a random lottery and will notify you of your trip status by March 2.

Remember, you will need to submit a deposit for $200. You can make your deposit via credit/debit card on the application. Or, drop off a check before 2:23pm on February 23.

Traveling with a Group? If you would like to work and travel with people outside of your immediate family (ie. friends, small group, etc.), then nominate one person to register as the Group Connector. Everyone in your group must indicate this one person in the application process. We’ll do our best to accommodate your request.

Ready to Get Started? Grab your credit/debit card if you wish to pay your deposit on-line, and address book and have them near by for a few questions.
