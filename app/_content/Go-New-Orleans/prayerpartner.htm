What is as Prayer Partner?

A prayer partner is someone that will support and encourage you through prayer, both before and during the trip. If there is anything that we've learned through the past GO New Orleans trips, it is the amazing power of prayer, so make sure to take advantage of this opportunity to have someone pray for you. Your prayer partner could be a spouse, family member or a close friend. If you do not have a prayer partner, we will help find one for you, but it is always better to have someone you know as your prayer partner.
