First Impressions Open House

 

First impressions go a long way. From the moment a Crossroads-curious person pulls into the parking lot, fills up a coffee cup or takes a seat in auditorium—odds are the First Impressions Team had something to do with it. So whether you’ve always wanted try your hand as a barista or think you may have missed your calling to direct traffic—join FI. (And you don’t have to be a lifer. Try it out for a few weeks and see how it suits you.).
