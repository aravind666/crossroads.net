Daily Recipe 3.28

15 Bean soup



  • 1 pound 15 bean mix (sometimes called 16 bean mix)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • seasoning pack (comes with beans)
  • 3 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper

This one is easy! Saute the onion, and add beans and water to 3 inches over beans. Bring to a boil, and add other ingredients. Lower heat to a simmer. The seasoning pack that comes with the beans can be ham or cajun flavored, and adds flavor. Simmer for 4 hours or 8 hours in crock pot on high.
