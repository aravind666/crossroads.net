Serve: I’m a Teen

You can change things.

 

We don't want a place run by grownups. Crossroads is all about YOU participating. We use your ideas, your music, your words, your talent, and your ability to love others in a way that only teens can. So there's a lot you can do. Come to CSM Jr. and Sr. and get to know us. Join in on a mission experience. Or start your own thing.

Also, we’d love you to serve in Kids’ Club.

One thing younger kids love is teens who hang out with them. We know little brothers and sisters can be a drag, but what if they're not yours? What if you could build a 4-year old the coolest tent he's ever been in? What if you could push the Fussy Baby Bus and make them laugh instead of cry? You can do this stuff.  Jr. Highers can work with 1 year olds – 2nd grade; Sr. Highers can work with 1 year olds – Area 56.
