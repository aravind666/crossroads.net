Open House: Make it Happen

 

From the simmering coffee in the atrium to the Play-Doh squeezed in Kids’ Club—odds are a volunteer had something to do with it. And let’s face it, volunteers run this place. So whether you’ve always wanted to direct traffic in the parking lot or be part of a fun learning environment for kids—we’d love for you to be part of our team. (And you don’t have to be a lifer. Try it out for a few weeks and see if it fits.)

 

Learn more about serving and specific roles by attending our upcoming Open House.

 

Event Details
