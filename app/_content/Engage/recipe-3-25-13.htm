Daily Recipe 3.25

Italian Wedding Soup (vegetarian)



  • 1 pound navy beans (dried)
  • 1 can greens (spinach, collard, or mixed greens)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning (or 1t. oregano and 1t. basil)
  • 3 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder

Saute onion in olive oil in bottom of large pot. Add beans and water to 3-4 inches above beans. Bring to a boil and lower heat to a simmer, cook for one hour. Add greens, and seasonings, cook for 2 more hours or until beans are tender. You can also cook all day in a crock pot.
