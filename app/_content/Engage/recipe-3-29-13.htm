Daily Recipe 3.29

Cuban Black Bean Soup



  • 1 pound dried black beans
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and freshly-ground black pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons white or cider vinegar

Saute onion, pepper and garlic in olive oil, in a large pot. Add beans and cover with water to 2 inches above beans. Bring to a boil and lower temperature to keep it simmering. Add  2 teaspoons of salt and 2 teaspoons of pepper (or to taste). Add vinegar and cook for 2 more hours, until beans are breaking down and creating a creamy soup.
