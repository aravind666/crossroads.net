Start a Small Group

Post a listing for your journey small group

 

If you’re interested in creating or adding members to a small group, create a small group posting. It will make it easier for other people with similar interests to find you.

Remember, you don’t need to be a bible expert or professional facilitator to host a group. You just need to be willing to put yourself out there and let others know about your group.
