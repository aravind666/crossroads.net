Introduction



How can we help?

Pastoral Care & Crisis

Crises happen. We’ll do all we can to get you into a safe place with good counsel. If you have an urgent need to speak with a Care Pastor call 513.731.7400 during the workday, or 513.731.7400 x8101 if it’s after hours.

Funerals

We’re ready to help you. Our chapel is available for funerals and memorial services at no cost. We can seat around 250, provide a pastor, and talk to you about what you need and when.

Counselors

Here’s a list of local Christian counselors—a sample of professional help that’s been recommended by people who attend Crossroads, friends and staff.

Weddings

Everything we do for your life of "I do."

Prayer Requests

We believe in the power of prayer. Your prayer requests can remain confidential, and we are ready to hear you. If you have an urgent need, please call 513.731.7400 (during work hours) 513.731.7400 x 8101 (after hours) so we can find someone to talk to you right away.

Corkboard

Here’s the cork and pushpin catch-it-all: just post what you need, what you’d like to give away, or what you want people in your community to know about.

Care Pages

An external site for hospitalized patients and/or family members, to help you stay in touch before, during and after the hospital stay. Share news, ask for prayer, send love. (Please note that Crossroads doesn’t maintain this external site, nor divulge or provide any patient information for this service.)
