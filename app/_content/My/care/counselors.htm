Pastoral Care Overview Counselors

Counselors

The following is a list of local counselors who have been referred to us by people who attend Crossroads, friends and staff. This is not a comprehensive listing of Christian counselors in the great Cincinnati Area; we know that there are many more well qualified counselors who integrate their Christian faith with their counseling work. The list is updated periodically and the information is subject to change.

 

Alternately, you might find a counselor who is helpful to you that is not affiliated with a particular faith. Regardless of whether you choose a counselor that incorporates your faith into the sessions or not, we hope this information will be helpful in your search.

 

Most of these counselors do individual, marriage and family counseling, and work with clients on a variety of concerns, including: depression, anxiety, stress management, relationship issues, grief, anger management, mental and emotional disorders, chemical dependency, sex addiction, compulsive behaviors, recovery from abuse, men’s and women’s issues, pre-marital counseling and more.
