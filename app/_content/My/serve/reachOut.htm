ReachOut

ReachOut
Engaging in our world

God says, "Serve the poor." Love them, learn from them, fight for their freedom.
So we mobilize hundreds of people—sometimes thousands—to build relationships and meet needs. And God shows up.

ReachOut: Our City

Sometimes it’s a one-day citywide blitz, sometimes it’s investing time with the same child or prison inmate from week to week. We take God’s command to serve the poor seriously, and we seek to learn from those who have less.

ReachOut: Our World

Whether it’s South Africa or Asia, we leave our own neighborhoods to be in others. We don’t go as fixers; we go as servant-learners. And that’s how homes get built, victims comforted, children rescued and lives literally changed.
