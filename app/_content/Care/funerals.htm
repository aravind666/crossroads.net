Funerals

We are ready to help you.

 

The death of a family member is a significant loss. We would love to help prepare a service of remembrance for the loved ones of regular Crossroads attenders and their immediate families.

 

If your plans include scheduling services with a funeral home, please contact us BEFORE any plans are finalized with them so that we can guarantee a pastor for you. Crossroads’ chapel is available for funerals and memorial services at no cost. We can seat around 250 comfortably.

 

Contact our Pastoral Care Coordinator at 513.731.7400 x1727 for more information and to schedule a pastor to meet with you to discuss your needs and make service arrangements.
