Pastoral Care

We want you to heal, grow and find purpose.

Getting into community, such as a Small Group, Community Group or Healing Group, gives you a gathering of people to grow with, and lean into. Because often, it’s the person right next to you who can give you a strong arm and an open ear. But we also know there are crises, and we want to do all we can to get you quickly into a safe place with good counsel.

Speak with a Care Pastor

If you would like to meet with someone who will listen to whatever you’re going through, pray for you and even visit you or a loved one in the hospital, contact Pastoral Care.
