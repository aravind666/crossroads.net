Counselors

The following list of local counselors is not comprehensive, but just a sample of Christian counselors in the greater Cincinnati Area. Many have been referred to us by people who attend Crossroads, friends and staff. The list is updated periodically and the information is subject to change.

Please note that many insurance companies list counselors by special interest area. Be sure to ask your insurance provider for their list of Christian counselors. Alternately, you might find a counselor who is helpful to you that is not affiliated with a particular faith. Regardless of whether you choose a counselor that incorporates your faith into the sessions or not, we hope this information will be helpful in your search.
