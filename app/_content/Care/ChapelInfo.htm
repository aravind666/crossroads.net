Chapel Information

Crossroads’ chapel, completed in 2006, is located at the East Entrance of the building. The chapel features a center aisle and neutral décor. Pews inside the chapel can seat up to 250 people for your wedding celebration. The chapel is available Monday through Saturday, but please contact us for specific dates and times.

Directions to the chapel:


From Hyde Park
Go North on Marburg Ave. (turns into Ridge). Turn left on Madison Road. Then turn right into Crossroads. The Chapel is located at the East Entrance.

From I-71 North or Norwood Lateral (562)
Take the Ridge South exit. Turn right at stoplight onto Ridge Ave. Turn right on Madison Road. Then turn right into Crossroads.  The Chapel is located at the East Entrance.

From I-71 South
Take Ridge Ave exit. Turn left off exit onto Highland Avenue. Turn left at traffic light onto Ridge Avenue. Continue on Ridge up the hill. Turn right on Madison Rd. Then turn right into Crossroads. The Chapel is located at the East Entrance.
