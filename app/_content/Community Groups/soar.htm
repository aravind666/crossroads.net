Survivors of Abuse Recovery (S.O.A.R)

 

Family Nurturing Center offers a 12 week program serving adults who have experienced childhood sexual abuse. Groups will help participants recognize feelings and impact, create healthy coping strategies and develop effective communication skills for healthy relationships.
