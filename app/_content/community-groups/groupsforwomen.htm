Sometimes it’s better without boys around.

Groups for Women focus on topics or discussions relevant to women, or offer the chance to connect with other women about a general topic.

SEASONAL GROUPS
Gatherings of people who want to explore a specific interest, study a book, get in-depth teaching and share personal growth. Groups meet for a specific period of time-usually a few weeks or months. Sign-ups happen twice a year (spring and fall). Choose a group for more info, and to sign up:

  • Women’s Bible Study: Study of Romans
  • Moms

ONGOING GROUPS
Ongoing Community Groups are like visiting on the front porch—casual, and come-when-you-can. They run all year, and are open to anyone, anytime. It’s a place where you can find community with others, ask questions, pursue answers and grow deep with God. Select a group below to learn more.

  • Women’s Ongoing Group
  • Healing Betrayed Hearts
