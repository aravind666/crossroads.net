Healing Groups

Heal and recover.

Healing Groups are safe, non-judgmental environments for those who need help overcoming personal crises or addictions. These groups provide opportunities to connect with God and others facing similar challenges in an encouraging and discrete setting. Healing is possible; don’t go through it alone.
