High School Ministry

HS Experience is designed specifically for high school students to explore who God is in a language they understand and have fun doing it. If you like taking selfies with farm animals, you’ll love HS.  

 

Weekly Meetings

Our weekly meetings allow teens in our community to hang out, connect with other students, learn about God and have some fun. Nobody expects you to be a Bible expert—whether you are a seeker or Christ-follower, this is a place for you.

Serve in HS

There are plenty of opportunities to help with high school students and you don’t have to be an expert in the Bible to get involved. Whether it’s leading a small group or helping set up a Connect Event, we’d love to have you serve with us.
