CSM Tuesday Night Community Groups

Starting Jan. 31st we will be running our 4th installment of CSM Community Groups.  Groups will run for 8 weeks beginning at 6:30 and ending at 8:30. Groups include:

 

How to Share your Faith- Learn how to know your story and share your faith.

Teaching- Become a stronger teacher and live a life that strengthens those around you.

JR. High Bible Study- Dig deeper into the Bible in community.

Mason CG- Mason lead bible study on various topics.
