East Side Grill & Go

Eat, drink and make some friends.

Join us for the East Side “Grill & Go” Connect Event—a great opportunity for folks in our community to build relationships and make this place feel a bit smaller.

Here’s how this event works:

- Everyone will split up into groups of 10-15 

- Each group will be assigned to a dedicated host who will serve up a tasty meal in their home. Upon signing up, you’ll receive additional details prior to the event.

- Guests will arrive at their host’s house between 6–6:30pm.

- After dinner, you’ll head back to the Anderson Center for drinks and dessert in the atrium.

East Side RSVP

Saturday, October 4, 6-10pm

Adults Only - it’s not that we don’t like kids, we just want the adults to have a chance to connect.

Hosts will provide the meat and participants will be asked to bring a side dish.
