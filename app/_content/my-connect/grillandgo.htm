Eat, drink and make some friends.

Last year, Crossroads Florence hosted the first ever "Grill & Go" and this year, the West Side wanted in on the action. Join us for the “Grill & Go” Connect Event—a great opportunity for folks in our community to build relationships and make this place feel a bit smaller. So grab your flip flops, shades and join the fun.

Here’s how this event works:

- Everyone will split up into groups of 8-10 in Florence; 4-5 in West Side.

- Each group will be assigned to a dedicated host who will serve up a tasty meal in their home. Upon signing up, you’ll receive additional details prior to the event.

- Guests will arrive at their host’s house between 6–6:30pm.

- After dinner, you’ll head back to your Crossroads site for drinks and dessert in the atrium.
