Forgiven and Set Free

Wednesdays: October 1-December 10.

If you're a woman suffering remorse and guilt after an abortion, join us to find God's healing and grace. Based on the book Forgiven & Set Free by Linda Cochrane, women who may be suffering and hurting are guided to bring their emotional scars from abortion "out of the dark past and into his holy light" where true and lasting healing can take place.

In addition to the book, Bible verses addressing issues such as relief, denial, anger, forgiveness, depression, letting go and acceptance will be discussed through teaching and small group interaction.

Sessions
10 Wednesdays: Oct. 1, 8, 15, 22, 29; November 5, 12, 19; Dec 3, 10; 7-9pm
