New Beginnings

Help restore hope to families making a fresh start. 

 

Imagine taking a shower and not having a towel, or trying to prepare a meal without pots, pans, plates or utensils. New Beginnings works with families in our community who need these household essentials. We don’t just give them stuff; we connect with the families, build relationships with them and get to know their unique circumstances. Then we help fill their home with hope, trust and love, as well as needed household items.

 

If you’d like to show God’s love to a family making a new beginning, there are lots of ways you can help:

 

Serve

Flexible serving opportunities are available. Opportunities include moving, decorating, organizing supplies, cleaning and making household repairs.

 

Donate

We’re looking for new or gently used furniture and household items. We’re talking about nice stuff here, people—things you wouldn’t be embarrassed to display or use in your own house. Commonly needed items are:

-Furniture

-Kitchenware: pots, pans, utensils, appliances, etc.

-Fans (ceiling or box)

-Portable heaters and air conditioners

-Bed linens (any size)

-Bathroom essentials

-Home decorations

 

Drop off gently-used items at the Crossroads Oakley Annex on the 1st & 3rd Saturday of the month from 10-1pm and the 1st & 3rd Tuesday from 5-7:30pm.

 

Sign up at the Info Center or email us to get connected to the New Beginnings team.
