Give Stuff

Bless others with what you can share (or buy).

 

Whether you can shop for gifts, donate supplies, or even have a car to give, there are lots of ways to bless others with your stuff.

 

Thanksgiving Food Drive

Buy a meal, and we’ll give it to one of over 50 local churches and organizations who distribute the meals to bless their communities.

 

Angel Tree

At Christmas, bless a child in need: buy a special gift from the child’s wish list. We deliver the gifts to kids and caregivers all over Cincinnati.

 

Wheels

Do you have a used car in decent condition that you’re thinking of trading or selling? Consider giving it to the Wheels ministry instead. You can get a tax deduction for the fair market value, and bless someone with the gift of transportation.

 

New Beginnings

Help restore hope to families making a fresh start in a new home by donating gently used furniture and household items. You can also help move, decorate, organize or clean.

 

Corkboard

Bless those in need in our community by posting FREE items you’re willing to give away. You can also post your own needs, upcoming events or job opportunities.
