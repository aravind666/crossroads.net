GO Somewhere

Whether it’s South Africa, New Orleans or India, we leave our own neighborhoods to be in others.

 

We don’t go as ﬁxers; we go as servant-learners. And we believe in forming lasting, reciprocal relationships with the people we serve. That’s how homes get built, victims comforted, patients cared for, children rescued and lives literally changed.

 

Trying to decide which trip is right for you? Click here to learn how we think about GO trips. You can also get a preview here. 

 

Below are the trips that are coming up soon. Signups for each trip open up about five months before the trip begins.

 

Big Trip

This trip is open to anyone, and offers a variety of ways for people to serve. 

 

Go South Africa          
March 21-31, 2013   

Crossroads is sending 130 volunteers to work alongside our partner in South Africa, Grace Bible Church. Grace Bible is one of the largest churches in South Africa and is a light for those battling the HIV/AIDS epidemic and living in extreme poverty. Signups begin Saturday, August 17 and will close by

9.30 at 5pm. Learn more at crossroads.net/gosouthafrica.

 

Go NOLA 2014

July 19-27 and July 26-Aug 3. 

Crossroads is sending 400 volunteers to work alongside our partner church in New Orleans. From helping build homes to showing love to kids through Vacation Bible School, our work is much more than just hurricane relief. Sign-ups begin Saturday, January 25 and will close Monday, March 3 at 5pm. Learn more at www.crossroads.net/goneworleans.

 

Go India

October 19-28, 2014

Crossroads is sending 25 volunteers to work alongside our partner in Mumbai for and outdoor adventure camp with survivors of trafficking. Signups begin May 5 and end June 6. Learn more at crossroads.net/goindia.

Specialized Trips 

These smaller trips have a more narrow focus, and require both specialists and regular folks to help out.

 

We will support our aftercare efforts by building infrastructure and relationships with local churches. Who we need: People with a heart for justice, relationship builders, GO India vets, people who can interact with families and kids.
