ReachOut: The Diner

Prepare, serve and share a meal with the guests of City Gospel Mission.

 

Friday Evening Dinner

-Second, third, fourth and fifth Fridays of every month.

-Dinner served at 7pm.

-Be a cook: plan a menu, purchase ingredients and prepare the meal. Arrive at 4:30pm.

-Be a server: set up the dining area, serve dinner and share the meal with residents of City Gospel Mission. Arrive at 6:30pm. 

-Stay after dinner if you’re able to hang out, play cards and engage in community. 

 

Saturday Morning Breakfast

-The fifth Saturday of every month.

-Breakfast is served at 6:45am.

-Be a cook: plan a menu, purchase ingredients, and prepare the meal. Arrive at 4:30am.

-Be a server: set up the dining area, serve breakfast and share the meal with residents and guests of City Gospel Mission. Arrive at 6:30am.

 

Kids ages 6-17 are welcome to serve when accompanied by a parent.

Cooks must be 18 or older.

Ready to serve on a Friday night or Saturday morning? Sign up at Crossroads Oakley at the Info Center or email us and let us know what date you’d like to serve and how many people you’ll bring.
