Angel Tree

God tells us to care for children in need so that they feel loved. So we do.

 

Every Christmas season, we get to give gifts to children in need—some of whom have a parent in prison, some we know through Whiz Kids and other relationships in the area. 

 

Pick up a tag or two in the Atriums during the weekend services November 30-December 1 and December 7-8. Return gifts at Florence and Mason during the weekend services December 7-8 and December 14-15.

 

Please return your gifts ASAP since all gifts must be returned no later than Sunday, December 15.
