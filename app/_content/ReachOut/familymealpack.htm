Family Meal Pack

Serving up 300,000 meals for our partners in South Africa

 

Join us on Saturday, July 12 for the 1st Annual Family Meal Pack event at all four sites. Families will help assemble 300,000 meals (yeah, that’s five zeros) that will ultimately get shipped to our friends and partners at the Topsy Foundation in South Africa. 

 
Each pack is a meal for six people, and includes rice, vegetables, soy protein and vitamin powder. In order to accomplish our first year goal, there are a number of serving opportunities that are required  and we encourage you to invite your friends. Whether you’d like to serve in one our supporting roles (e.g. clean-up crew, table leader, greeters, etc.) or help assemble meals, sign up below at the site that suits you best.
