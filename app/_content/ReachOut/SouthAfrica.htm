Be a part of something bigger than yourself!

 

Join hundreds of Americans and South Africans as we provide hope on the other side of the world.

From March 21–31, Crossroads is sending 130 volunteers to work alongside our partner in South Africa, Grace Bible Church. Grace Bible is one of the largest churches in South Africa and is a light for those battling the HIV/AIDS epidemic and living in extreme poverty.

 

Anyone over the age of 13 is invited to GO South Africa—it doesn’t matter whether or not you attend Crossroads. So, invite your friends, family, and/or people you want to be your friends or family.

 

The deadline to sign-up for the trip and secure your $300 deposit is September 30 at 5pm.

 

> Sign up here 

 

(*Note:  If more than 130 people sign-up, a random lottery will determine who goes. If you are selected, your deposit is non-refundable, but if you aren’t selected, we’ll return your deposit.)
