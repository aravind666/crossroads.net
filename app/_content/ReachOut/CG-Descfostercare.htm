Foster Care

God’s heart breaks for the orphan and the Bible has a lot to say about it. Learn more about God’s heart towards foster care and how you can become involved. 

 

OHIO RESIDENTS

All residents of the state must become licensed in the state of Ohio. We recommend anyone who is contemplating foster care/adoption to attend Spiritual Boot Camp and training in an effort to set expectations and prepare you spiritually.
