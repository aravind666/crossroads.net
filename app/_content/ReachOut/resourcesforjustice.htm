Justice Resources

Books

  • Sold by Patricia McCormick
  • A Crime So Monstrous by Benjamin Skinner
  • Disposable People by Kevin Bales
  • The Slave Next Door by Kevin Bales

Websites

ijm.org: International Justice Mission is a human rights agency that secures justice for victims of slavery, sexual exploitation and other forms of violent oppression.

callandresponse.com: The first documentary to use contemporary music to expose the secret of 27 million slaves around the world. Contains many opportunities to engage directly in the work of ending human trafficking.

chainstorereaction.com: An offshoot of Call and Response, this site allows you the consumer to influence the brands you buy everyday towards more humane ways of creating the goods you consume regularly.

freetheslaves.net: Free the Slaves liberates slaves around the world, helps them rebuild their lives and researches real world solutions to eradicate slavery forever.
