ReachOut

Be the church.

 

God tells us to serve those in need. To love them, learn from them, fight for their freedom. So we mobilize hundreds of people—sometimes thousands—and we respond to God’s call.

 

Sometimes it’s a one-day citywide blitz, sometimes it’s investing time with the same child or job-seeker from week to week. It might mean feeding a hungry family, or even traveling to the other side of the world to care for someone in need. No matter how you get involved, you’re sure to impact lives in the process—including your own.

 

Choose how you’d like to engage:

  • Give time: whether you can give a day or 52 weeks, we’ve got ways for you to get involved.
  • Give stuff: shop for gifts, donate supplies, help a family making a fresh start.
  • GO somewhere: whether it’s South Africa, New Orleans or India, we leave our neighborhoods to be in others.
