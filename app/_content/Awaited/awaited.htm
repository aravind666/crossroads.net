Atrium Experience

Arrive up to an hour before the performance and enjoy a fun, free old-fashioned Christmas. Get in the spirit by experiencing a bustling streets-cape that serves up free coffee, hot cocoa, cookies, kids' activities and DIY photo opps. Allow plenty of time to park and you'll be asked to take your seat 5 minutes before showtime.
