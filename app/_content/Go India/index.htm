Bestow love, uncover beauty, bring hope.


In 2009, people from Crossroads first traveled to India. They returned home forever changed by the reality they faced and the love they received. They showed love to girls rescued from forced prostitution, and joined a movement that’s bringing hope in the darkness.

 

Since then, our partners in India have continued working tirelessly in an effort to bring justice and rescue to girls trapped in modern day slavery.

 

In 2014, we’re going back again, fueled by a vision for Mumbai and Kolkata. We want to provide healing and restoration for these girls, and experience our own growth through what God has to offer us. Working alongside local Indian churches, we’ll help develop long-term solutions (learn more about the background and current vision of our work). This is an uphill battle, but we’re in it for the long haul. We take God’s call to "seek justice and encourage the oppressed" (from Isaiah 1:17) seriously, and we believe he’s positioned us uniquely to make a real difference, one girl at a time.

 

The movement is growing, and you’re invited to get involved. Read on to explore the possibility of going on a GO India trip, or sign up to receive updates with our India Interest Group.
