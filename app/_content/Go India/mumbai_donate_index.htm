Thank you for helping to send love to Mumbai.


Our partners in India have been working relentlessly to bring justice and rescue to girls who are trapped in one of the most egregious forms of modern day slavery. From afar, we have provided financial support, prayed for rescue, sent care packages and hosted the heroes who have been working on the front lines to rescue girls from forced prostitution. Now, we are going to Mumbai to bestow love on these girls, remind them of their beauty and bring them hope.
