Introducing the Crossroads’ mobile giving app

 

Giving started a long, long time ago with goats and wheat. Then came pieces of metal. Then checkbooks. Then credit cards. Now all you need is your phone. Welcome to the future, everybody.

 

How to use the Give web app:

 

1. Go to crossroads.net/give from your mobile device.

2. Give the amount you want.

 

 

3. Log in or create an account.

 

 

4. Get thanked.

 

 

5. Receive email summary of your transaction. Your mobile gift will also show up on your giving statement that we mail you each quarter.

 

iOS User? Get “give” on your home screen.

Visit crossroads.net/give from your mobile device. Tap the icon and select “Add To Home Screen.”

 

 

FAQs

 

Is giving this way secure?

Absolutely. We are serious about security and focused a lot of energy on making sure your data is under lockdown. If you feel safe using amazon.com, you can feel safe using our give app.

 

Does using this app replace my E-gift?

Not unless you use it to set up or modify your E-gift. The give app default is to give a one-time gift, in addition to any existing E-gift you may have.

 

Can I give during the weekend service?

Permission granted. We know you probably don’t carry cash and wanted to make it simple for you to engage in the work God’s doing at Crossroads. Feel free to give through this app as they’re passing the bag on the weekend. Or when Brian’s going off on a tangent.

 

How do I download the app?

First of all, skip the app store—this starts with your phone’s web browser. If you’re an iOS user, you can go to crossroads.net/give from your mobile device. You’ll get prompted to add the app to your home screen. If you’re an Android user, bookmark crossroads.net/give and add it to your home screen. All others, just go to crossroads.net/give in your phone’s web browser.

 

Why can’t I get this app from the app store?

Going through the traditional app store would skim off 30% from your gift. Gross. We’d rather have 100% go straight to God’s work around here.

 

I have a BlackBerry, can I use this app?

Yes, this app works on all mobile devices with a browser (aka smartphones).

 

I give regularly, but the prompt still asked me for my bank account info. Why?

Sorry. Once you enter your bank account via the give app, that’ll be the last time.

 

Will I get a receipt statement for giving with this app?

Yes. Your gift will show up on the quarterly statement we mail to your house. You can use this for tax deduction purposes. If you’ve never received a quarterly statement, you’ll start getting one via email (because you gave us your contact info).

 

Can I set up a recurring gift (E-gift) with this app?

You sure can! Just hit the "Setup/Modify E-gift" button on the main Mobile Give screen.

 

I already have a crossroads.net account. Can I login with that?

Yes, please do. If you can’t remember your password, use the “forgot password” link.

 

Can I change my bank account info?
You sure can. Simply click the person icon in the upper right corner and update your bank info in your profile. After you save the new information, we’ll use that account for any future one-time gifts.

 

Where do I go if I have more questions about giving to Crossroads?

You can read our giving FAQs or contact finance@crossroads.net.
