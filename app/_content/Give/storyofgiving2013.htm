Giving Freedom
2013 was unheard-of

 

Last year was like climbing the biggest mountain we’ve ever faced. It was exhausting, energizing, uncertain and wonderful, and now we can look back at the amazing numbers and life change that took place. 

 

2014. This year means more. More freedom, more healing, more people connected with the God who loves and pursues them. This looks like a West Side location in the spring, a brand new building in Mason this fall, and experiments all over town in places like UC and the East Side. It’s gonna be sweet.
