Who We Are

We wanted a place to bring our friends, minus the church lingo, bad coffee and kum-ba-yah.

So the eleven friends who started Crossroads in 1995 turned into thousands of people seeking truth and authentic community. And you’re invited to join in. We don’t expect you to agree with everything we do or say. We just ask that you’re open to considering the possibility that there is a God who loves and pursues you.
