Community Group Overview

Community Groups

Meet others like you. Listen, learn, laugh, heal, grow.

Community Groups connect people who are running after the same thing—whether that’s spiritual growth, recovery and healing, or just finding people in a similar life phase and asking the same questions. Community Groups are a time and place to be taught, swap stories and grow.

Registration deadline: Sept 15 (unless noted as an Ongoing Group).

 

Ongoing Groups

Jump in any time during the year, and come when you can.  

- Fathers: hang out, learn and grow alongside other fathers

- Men’s Ongoing: pray, study and connect with men

- Going Deeper: new spiritual growth topics every 6 weeks

- Small Group Connect (formerly All Year Journey): find a group of 6-12 people in a fun, simple way; new topic every 6 weeks

- CRUX: for 18-35 year olds; introduction to a lifestyle of discipleship

- Adoption and Foster Care: For Parents: support and teaching

Growth

Six to twelve-week groups. Spiritual growth for the skeptic to the all-in believer.

- The God Experiment: for skeptics and God newbies

- Understanding the Bible: learn the Bible basics

- Story Formed Life: train in faith by comparing the American Dream and God’s story

- Grow: learn and live out time-tested spiritual practices/disciplines

- Stories of the Bible: discover the big Bible stories, in context

- All For Love: how to let God’s love transform you

- Captivating: For Women: discover adventure, gifts and purpose from God

Life Stage

Six to twelve-week groups, unless noted as “ongoing.”

- Moms: share the journey and learn alongside other moms

- Fathers (ongoing): hang out, learn and grow alongside other fathers

- Adoption and Foster Care: For Parents (ongoing): support and teaching

- Adoption and Foster Care: For Moms: support and teaching

- After the Honeymoon: for couples married less than 3 years

- CRUX (ongoing): for 18-35 year olds; introduction to a lifestyle of discipleship

- Halftime: for men in their middle years looking to have a significant life

- Blueprint for Single Women: the single lifestyle and how Jesus approached it

- Dave Ramsey’s Financial Peace University: a new way to manage money

- Real Marriage: for spouses; authentic conversation and teaching

- Love and Respect: for spouses: how to make a marriage thrive

- Young Adult Group: for 18-35 year olds; introduction to growing in faith and community.

Healing Groups

These are safe, non-judgmental environments for those of us who need help overcoming personal crises or addictions. Learn more about Healing Groups.
