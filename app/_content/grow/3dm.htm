3DM Learning Community

Crossroads is partnering with an organization called 3DM to host and participate in a two-year “Learning Community,” the primary vehicle through which 3DM teaches everything they’ve learned over the course of 25 years of training leaders in Discipleship and Mission. Please watch the video below for more background, and check out an (optional) Info Session or the FAQ document below for more information.

Info Sessions

If you’ve been invited to take part in the initial 3DM Learning Community group, please feel free to attend any of the following Info Sessions for more details:

  • Wednesday, Feb. 1 from 7-8pm at the Mason White House
  • Thursday, Feb. 2 from 10-11am in Oakley Meeting Room C
  • Monday, Feb. 13 from 7-8pm in Oakley Meeting Room A

Regardless of which Crossroads location you typically attend, you’re free to attend any of these meetings. If you choose to attend an (optional) Info Session, please RSVP to Molly Lindner (and let us know the names and ages of any kids you’ll bring that will require childcare).
