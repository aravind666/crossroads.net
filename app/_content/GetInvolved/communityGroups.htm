Community Groups



Join in with people who are where you are, or need a hand through the same kind of thing—like divorce, fathering, making ends meet.

Community Groups are gatherings of people who want to explore a specific interest, study a book, get in-depth teaching and share personal growth. Most groups meet for a specific period of time—usually a few weeks or months. But we also offer ongoing groups, that meet throughout the year. Registration is now open for spring groups. The deadline to register is April 11.

Get started by selecting an area of interest below.

If you’re not already signed-in, you’ll be prompted to do so or to register to get the full details and sign up for a group.
