Community Activities

Community Activities

Concerts, outside events, art openings and more.

Check out what’s on tap, and what people in this community are up to. Whether it's live music, art shows or special gatherings, we want you to know you're invited. And for the record, you can always come over (it’s called 3500 Open).

 

Pilates Fusion

Every Wednesday, noon-1pm in CSM
Every Saturday, 10-11am under the tree
Work your core. Balls provided, but please bring a mat.

 

Tae Bo® Fitness

Saturdays from 11am-noon
Free class with a certified instructor. Mixes cardio and strength training. Wear gym shoes and bring water.

 

Family Sing-Along/Story Time at 3500

Tuesdays from 10:30-11:30am
For kids and parents: come for a story under the tree, and stay for a few runs down the slide.

 

Knitting Classes

Second and fourth Saturdays from 10am-noon.
Beginning and experienced knitters are invited to come and learn new skills. Materials provided for beginning knitters. Classes will take place in the Atrium near the Auditorium.

 

Maximizing Your Job Search: Three Week Workshop

Tuesdays, April 6, 13 and 31 from 7-8:30pm
In Oakley and at the Mason Heights Annex
Improve your online networking, resume and interview skills through this three-part workshop with experts in the HR and online media fields.

 

 

Reel to Real

First Friday of the month from 7-10pm
Watch a movie in the Auditorium and engage in lively discussion on its spiritual and philosophical messages.  18 and up.
