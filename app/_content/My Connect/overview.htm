Get Connected

We weren’t created to do life alone.

 

We believe that a building is just a building, and that the true Church can be as small as a few people in a living room or as big as all-people-across-the-world who have a relationship with Jesus.

 

We want people to grow by doing life together, moving beyond the arm’s-length distance of the auditorium to real, authentic relationships. Authentic community can help you grow in your relationships with God and with others.

 

Small Groups, Community Groups and High School are great places to get started.
