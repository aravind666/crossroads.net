Divorce Rebuilding


Saturday, April 12 from 8:30am-4:30pm
Thursdays: April 17-May 22. 7-9pm

Discover practical steps for piecing your life back together, and biblical ways to make a fresh start.

For those who want help recovering and rebuilding during the process of divorce, or after its impact. Come connect with other people experiencing similar challenges. Explore practical steps to putting life’s pieces back together, apply biblical wisdom, and regain hope. Brief presentations and small-group breakouts. We’ll discuss:

• Processing emotions
• Moving forward in the grief process
• Understanding the importance of forgiveness
• Becoming part of a community
• Moving on and thriving in life
• Getting finances back on track

Small groups are encouraged to continue meeting after the series ends.
