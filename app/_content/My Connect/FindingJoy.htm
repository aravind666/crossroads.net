Finding Joy

Thursdays from 7-8:30pm at Crossroads

With all of life’s stresses, deadlines and commitments, it’s hard to find joy when life feels like a pressure cooker. But biblical joy is something very different from just happiness, and it relates to being in relationship with God. Connect with others while learning practical tips from the biblical letter Paul wrote to the Philippians.
