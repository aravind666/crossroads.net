People are all around. People who want to meet you, connect with you, journey through life with you.

Seriously improve your life by learning how to depend on others, and letting others be blessed by you. (If you're nervous about trusting other people right now, come in slowly: read people's stories, listen to some music or messages, or serve someone.)

Forum

Talk about stuff: books, ideas, philosophies, what you heard over the weekend. All of it.

Corkboard

Give, receive, exchange, try. (But sell thee NOT, or you'll be really really sorry.)

Small Groups

We're not made to do life alone. God uses intimate connections as catalysts for change—in us, in our relationships with him and others.

Community Groups

Connect and grow in relationship with God and others in similar life stages or common situations.
