“Grill & Go” Connect Event

 

Crossroads Florence will be hosting their 1st Annual “Grill & Go” Connect Event on August 9. This adult-only event will be a great opportunity for folks in our community to build relationships and make this place feel a bit smaller. It’s not that we don’t like kids, we just want the adults to have a chance to actually connect.

 

Now that your taste buds are beginning to swell up, this is how the evening will go down.

 

- Depending on the number of RSVPs, everyone will split up into groups of 8-10.

- Each group will be assigned to a dedicated host who will be serving up a tasty meal at their house. Upon signing up, details for the event will go out the week of July 29.

- Guests will arrive at their host’s house between 6:00–6:30pm. Hosts will provide the meat and all other participants will be asked to bring a side dish. After dinner, Crossroads Florence will be serving up dessert in the atrium at 8PM for all Grill & Go participants. The event should conclude around 10pm.

 

So grab your flip flops and shades and join in the fun. It will be an evening where new friendships will be formed and our community will bond together.

 

Note – the deadline to signup was Tuesday, July 30.

 

Families, couples and singles are encouraged. If you’d like to be a host, all you need is a grill and the willingness to invite some new friends into your home.
