HS All Nighter, Friday, Aug. 8, 9pm - 7am (ends Aug. 9)
Sleep is overrated. Free Chipotle for a year... that’s not.
 
On August 8, all 9th-12th graders are welcome to join us for the HS All Nighter, being held at Crossroads Florence (828 Heights Blvd, Florence, KY 41042), from 9pm-7am. This will be an all night experience with plenty of games, inflatables, a 9 square tournament, and huge giveaways, including free Chipotle for a year. Our very own Chuck Mingo (Oakley campus pastor) will kick off the festivities with an encouraging message.
 
We’ll also be hosting our 2nd annual Pizza Olympics and crowning the X-box "Madden" and "Just Dance" champions for the top moves in town.
 
If you’re in high school, you don’t want to miss out -- RSVP HERE.
You’ll also be required to read, sign, and bring the required release form prior to entering the event. No form = no all-nighter. You can download that form HERE.
 
Parents, don’t worry; we’ll have plenty of adult leaders around all night making sure each student is safe, secure, and cared for. Any questions, contact Neville "Paco" Pancham.
