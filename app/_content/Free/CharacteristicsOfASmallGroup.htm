The 5 Elements (of a Small Group)

Thriving Small Groups seem to possess the same five elements—being on the same page, mutual trust, compassion, ownership and a challenging environment.

The Five Elements is a tool designed to educate Small Groups on the five characteristics of thriving groups. It's a great study for existing Small Groups or for those considering starting a group. Download materials here or pick them up at the Crossroads' Info Center.
