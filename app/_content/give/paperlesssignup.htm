Paperless Statements

Go green (and save us some green)

Crossroads.net now allows you to receive your giving statements online rather than in a printed, paper format. If you opt into paperless statements, you will not receive statements in the mail. Instead, you will receive an email message each quarter notifying you that your statement is available for viewing/printing online.
