’Tis the season to be generous. As you’re making your list and thinking about your spending and giving decisions for the end of the year, consider this: your tax-deductible gift to Crossroads will change lives locally and around the world—and a gift right now helps us do more, sooner. (Not to mention it’ll never go out of style.)

You can give a one-time gift right now or sign up for E-gift, an easy way to give regularly from your bank account or credit card. If you’re not already signed in, you’ll be prompted to do so, or to register for crossroads.net so we can keep everything super-secure.
