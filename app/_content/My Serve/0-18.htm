0-18

It’s never a dull moment. Never an un-impacting day.

We think kids are just to die for, and we want them to have a super fun, super adventure when they’re around this place. And we need people who can inspire a teenager, walk a toddler, and help a 5th grader figure out their faith and how to relate to their peers. There’s a ton of roles—even things that are entirely behind the scenes—so think about what you could do (or let us think with you). The reward is great.

Kids’ Club

From the size of the toilets to the bright colors and nutty dances, everything in Kids’ Club is created just for kids. We want kids to know God loves them and he offers a fun, adventurous life. While experiences vary by age, volunteers and staff in Kids’ Club are committed to showing kids a great time with lots of love while they explore God. Want to be a part of it? Sing, storytell, clean toys, paint murals, greet at the doors, run videos.

I’m a Kid

We love kids around here. We need you, just as much as we need the oldies. If you’re at least 10, you can work with two-year-olds through pre-kindergarten. Help them build cool things, hug them when they’re crying, smile at their parents. Just make sure you’re also going to your Kids’ Club class or the main auditorium services.

High School

If you’re an adult who thinks teens are hilarious and cool, you could be a serious influence on our youth. We have a ton of roles, so even if you don’t want to mentor or teach, you can set up the game room, help create video or plan bus rides for an inner city service project. We promise it’ll be a little wild, a little challenging and a lot of wonderful.
