Like you heard in the video, we’re looking for folks to declare themselves as hosts for the fall journey. We especially need hosts who will open their groups to new people. Whether you can take in two new people or ten, every post allows those who aren’t currently connected to find a group. Check your calendar and let us know if we can count on you.
