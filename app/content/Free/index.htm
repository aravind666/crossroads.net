Six weeks. 50,000 people. Are you up for the STRONG Challenge?

 

It’s not a race, but it is a commitment—for 20 minutes a day, five days a week for six weeks, we’ll experiment with challenges and rhythms designed to open us up to the God who spiritually strengthens us. Take the challenge, have some fun and discover what it means to be strong.

 

The Strong Challenge begins the weekend of October 8-9. Sign up to be a host at StrongChallenge.com.
