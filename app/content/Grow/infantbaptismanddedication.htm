Infant Baptism and Dedication

 

We’re so glad you’re interested in dedicating or baptizing your infant. This is a significant "mark the moment" time in the life of your family. So that we can keep the service to less than an hour, only a limited number of families can participate in each ceremony. Click here for a packet containing all the information you need about Infant Baptism and Dedication.

 

Spots are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis. If you would like to participate, it is best to submit your information ASAP. To sign up, please complete the form below.
