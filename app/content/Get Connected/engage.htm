Want to get engaged? Here are some places to start.

It takes thousands of volunteers to make Crossroads happen every weekend. And as our community grows, we’ll need even more folks to do everything from hanging out with kids to making coffee. So, pick a team and join the ranks of everyday revolutionaries that make this place run. Just give it a try, two weeks a month for six months. You can meet new friends, grow spiritually and maybe learn the secret handshake.

The areas where we need the most help right now are listed below:

(If you’re not already signed-in, you’ll be prompted to do so, or to register for crossroads.net so we know who you are.)

First Impressions

Greet people, hold doors, make coffee, clean restrooms, spread the love. First Impressions is all about welcoming people and creating loving environments that help people experience God. There are weekend and weekday serving opportunities at both the Oakely and Mason locations.

Kids’ Club

Lead activities, share stories, greet families, buy supplies, bless kids. Kids’ Club is a fun, learning environment created just for kids, newborn to sixth grade. There are weekend and weekday serving opportunities at both the Oakely and Mason locations.

CSM (Crossroads Student Movement)

Mentor students, fight injustice, inspire teens, change the world. CSM is a place where junior and senior high students hang out together, explore the Bible and change the world through God’s love. There are weekend and weekday serving opportunities at both the Oakely and Mason locations.

Mason Set Up and Tear Down

Unload storage, set up rooms, arrange stage, tear down, and reload. Create Crossroads environment in Mason. The Set Up and Tear Down teams are part of the overall First Impressions team, with specific roles and some different start and end times.

 

Awaited Atrium Experience

Help serve hot cocoa, park cars, hold doors, make coffee and more during the Atrium experience of Awaited. Doors open to the Atrium one hour before the show, so you will need to arrive one and a half to two hours before the show. If you want to sign up multiple people, first have everyone in your group sign up for the same show individually, then please email us with everyone’s names and the dates and hours of the shows you would like to serve. We will do our best to accommodate you, so that you can all serve together. To sign up, click here and choose the performance at which you’d like to serve. Serving roles will be assigned at a later date.
