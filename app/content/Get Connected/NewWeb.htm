Welcome to Crossroads!

It appears that you're newer around here, and we're really glad you're checking things out! If you're interested in finding out more about this place and the things going on around here, or if you'd like to get involved, here are some easy ways to get connected:

Now that you've registered for our website, take some time to look around. At crossroads.net, you can find out more information about who we are and why we do the things we do, check out events that are happening, download past messages or sign-up for a serving opportunity. And now that you're registered, you'll be able to personalize your Crossroads experience, so everything you need is right at your fingertips.

You're also invited to Context Live, an in-person event where you can discover more about the vision behind Crossroads, and how you can get involved (if you want). Childcare and lunch are provided. Visit crossroads.net/contextlive for details on the next event, and to RSVP.

I hope that you'll find our website to be a useful tool as you explore Crossroads. But if you have additional questions, please feel free to contact us.

Brian Tome
Senior Pastor
