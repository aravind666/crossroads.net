Community Groups

Here’s a place to start: join in with people who are where you are, or need a hand through the same kind of thing…like divorce, fathering, making ends meet. Don’t know what you need? There’s a group for that, too. They last a few months—or sometimes for the long haul—and there’s plenty to go around.

Small Groups

The weekend service can be inspiring, but it can't entirely fill the need we have for community. That’s why we pare down into small groups of six, ten or so: gatherings where you can get comfortable, share, invest, change. And it all happens outside Crossroads' walls.

Community Activities

Concerts, one-nighters, openings. Check out what’s on tap, and what people in this community are up to. And for the record, you can always come over (it’s called 3500 Open).

Serve Within

Want to know what makes Crossroads happen? Volunteers. Thousands of them, doing everything from holding babies and building sets, to leading big ReachOut events. It’s all important, and it’s all done by average Crossroads people. And yes, we need you.
