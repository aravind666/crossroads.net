Going Deeper groups are for people ready to go to the next place in their spiritual journey. They often require a higher degree of commitment and involvement, and maybe even some homework.

GROW
Thursdays: April 22-May 27 from 6:30-8:30pm at Crossroads Oakley.
God is always accessible to us, and the Bible lays out certain practices we can engage in to help us connect more deeply with him. Come learn spiritual disciplines that can increase your intimacy with God. More Details

WOMEN'S BIBLE STUDY: STUDY OF RUTH
Thursdays: April 15-May 13 from 9:30-11am at Crossroads Oakley
An in-depth Bible study of the book of Ruth. Learn personal study skills and how to apply relevant wisdom to your life. More Details

GOING DEEPER (ONGOING GROUP)
Wednesdays: 7-8:30pm at Crossroads Oakley
Examine how your thinking impacts your perception of God and how you relate to him. Learn how your beliefs affect your behavior, and how you can allow God to transform your thoughts to show you his true nature. More Details

Thursdays: April 22-July 8 from 6:30-8:30pm at Crossroads Oakley

OVERVIEW OF THE BIBLE
Wednesdays April 21-June 9 from 6:30-8:30pm at Crossroads Oakley
