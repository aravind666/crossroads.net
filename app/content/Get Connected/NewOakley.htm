Welcome to Crossroads!

We’re so glad you’re interested in getting connected to Crossroads. Whether you checked us out for the first time, visited Kids’ Club, signed up to serve or gave to Crossroads, here are some ways to stay connected:

If you haven’t already done so, I encourage you to visit crossroads.net and update your personal information. You can sign in using your email address and personal access code (see below). Once logged in, go to "My Crossroads" and select "Profile." By telling us more about you---like your address, phone number and favorite coffee flavor (kidding)---we’ll be able to keep you updated on upcoming events, special announcements and Crossroads news. All of your personal information is kept confidential and is stored securely.

You can also stay up-to-date on what’s happening around here on a daily basis. Follow us on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram (if you’re into that sort of thing).

If you’re looking for activities to enjoy as a family during the week, we’ve got you covered. You can see what kids learned each week in Kids’ Club, watch videos and listen to free music by checking out crossroadskidsclub.net.

I hope that you’ll find our websites to be useful tools as you explore Crossroads. But if you have additional questions, please feel free to contact us.

Brian Tome
Senior Pastor
