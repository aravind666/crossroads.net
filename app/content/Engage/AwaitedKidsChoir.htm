Awaited Kids Choir

 

Kids between the ages of 6 and 13 are welcome to sign up for an audition to be part of the show. We need kids who can sing well and have good stage presence.

 

This year, we’re dividing kids into two groups, so they will only be required to perform in 7 of the 14 shows. Kids are welcome to perform in all shows; however, please be ready to let us know that when you audition.

 

To be in Awaited Kids Choir, you need to be able to make ALL rehearsal times as well as all of the performances in one of the two groups below. Once you’re sure your child can commit and are ready to sign them up for an audition, simply click the sign up link below and select an audition time.
