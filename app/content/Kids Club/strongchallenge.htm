Strong Challenge 

Resources for talking Strong with your kids

 

During the Strong Challenge, adults and kids (3-years-old through sixth grade) are experimenting with challenges designed to open us up to the God who spiritually strengthens us. In Kids’ Club, we’re using videos to show a different part of Gods’ Story each week. We’ll post the weekly Kids’ Club video, along with the God’s Story poster kids are bringing home.
