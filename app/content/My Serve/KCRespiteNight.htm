Respite Night

 

Friday, September 24, from 6-9pm

 

We want to be a blessing to families of kids with special needs. So we’re looking for folks to hang out with some kids with special needs and their siblings, so their parents can get some much-needed downtime. Everyone is welcome, regardless of experience. We’ll be sure to find you a role that is within your comfort level. The event will take place in Kids’ Club at Crossroads Oakley and we’ll be serving food that evening. We just ask that volunteers arrive by 5:15pm.

 

To sign up to serve, click here. If you have additional questions, please feel free to email the Special Needs Team.
