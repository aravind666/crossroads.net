Serve: Go, Be the Church
Want to get involved? Here are some great places to start.

 

It takes thousands of volunteers to make Crossroads happen every weekend. We are always looking for more folks to do everything from hanging out with kids to making coffee. We've also got a lot of great ways that you can be the church outside of Crossroads, in our city and our world. So, pick a team and join the ranks of everyday revolutionaries. Just give it a try, two weeks a month for six months. You can meet new friends, grow spiritually and maybe learn the secret handshake.

 

For more information about the different opportunities available, click on any of the teams below.
