Serve at Super Bowl

February 1-2 in Florence

 

Kids’ Club 

Thousands of kids participate in Kids’ Club every weekend. Help make room for even more kids by volunteering on Super Bowl Weekend. Serve by greeting new families, helping toddlers acclimate to a new environment, or leading a small group of kids in a fun activity that helps them discover who God is. Don’t worry, we’ll pair you with an experienced Kids’ Club volunteer who will show you around. Click here to sign up.

 

 

First Impressions

Super Bowl weekend for First Impressions equals souped-up trucks and lots of popcorn. We’d love for you to help us man the trucks and feed the masses, as well as get cars parked, greet folks at the doors, and help people to their seats. Click here to sign up (if you’re not already signed in, you’ll be prompted to do so, or to register).
