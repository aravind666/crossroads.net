You and your friends now have two choices for attending Crossroads since Crossroads Mason opened last Sunday, October 5. At Crossroads Mason, you'll see the same message as Crossroads Oakley, thanks to state-of-the-art video projection. There will be live music, live Kids' Club and CSM and, most importantly, free coffee. Crossroads Mason service times are 9:00 and 10:30am (Kids' Club at both services; CSM at 10:30 only) and is located in Mason Middle School. Get directions to Crossroads Mason.
