Wednesday, June 18

Invest in Your Relationships

Our current series is all about redefining the words happiness, wealth, family and love. This week well explore what wealth means to us, no matter how much or little we have.

PRAY

Thank God for your friends, family and coworkers.

READ

Mark 10:29-30

REFLECT

If youve got friends and family who love you, then youre among the rich. Thats the simple truth. Its what we call relational wealth and its no different than money in the bank or an education---the more you invest in them, the richer those relationships will be.

In the video below, I Like Listening, a young couple realizes their relational wealth and makes an investment by simply listening.

Watch:I Like Listening

RESPOND

Today, listen to a friend, relative or coworker and invest in that relationship by being generous.
