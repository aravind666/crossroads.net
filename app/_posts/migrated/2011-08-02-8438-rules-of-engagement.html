Tuesday, August 2

Rules of Engagement

God, teach me more about your presence and power.

READ

Numbers 11:24-30

REFLECT

Regardless of our church background or lack thereof, its easy for us to fall into a thought pattern of the way things ought to be. The same was true for Joshua.It wasnt that he wanted the men to stop because they were saying anything wrong; rather, Joshua was concerned they were breaking rules by doing holy things outside of the place that was established for them.

The truth is that God can and will show up anytime, anywhere, to anyone. Like Joshua, we can learn from Moses, who insists that receiving God outside of the box is a good thing.

RESPOND

Are you sometimes distracted by rules of when and where God can show up? For example, you might think He only speaks through a pastor on Sunday morning, or through a devotional you receive daily in your inbox. But God wants to speak to you all the time, in both expected and unexpected places. Today, ask God to reveal himself to you in a new way. Look and listen for Him in unexpected places.
