Thursday, July 10

Five Minutes with God

This week, were examining our identities as treasured children of God.

READ

Deuteronomy 31:6

REFLECT

When Jesus was baptized, everyone who was there literally heard from God: This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased. We dont always have a physical experience like this, but todays Bible verse promises that God will always be with us--and no matter who "hears" or believes in our worth, we know the value God places on us.

RESPOND

Yesterday we compared the differences between orphan mentality and treasured child mentality (see the full list here). Today, choose one orphan statement that stood out to you. Now take that belief to God, asking him to help you walk into the freedom of a treasured child.

PRAY

God, thank you for always being near to me even when I dont know it. Help me to be brave and courageous.
