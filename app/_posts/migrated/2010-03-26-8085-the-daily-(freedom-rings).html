Friday, March 26
Freedom Rings

Todays edition of The Daily focuses on an original song written for the Free journey by Andrea VonAllmen. Follow along with the lyrics while you listen to the song. Ask God to meet you during this time, and to speak to your heart about the freedom he has in store for you.


From Andrea:
Freedom isnt free and it must be fought for. Unfortunately, bombs and guns will not work in this fight. When we learn that an enemy is putting forth an aggressive effort to steal our freedom, we realize we must put forth an aggressive effort to claim it.
