Thursday, October 11

The Promise of Home

Today, we hear from a young man who is from Germany, living and interning for six months in Cincinnati.

MEET BENAJA

"Being far away from home brings a lot of new challenges. There are new cultures, faces, characters, tasks, and no familiar people I can hold onto. Everything is breaking down to trusting in God. I would compensate back home for my insecurities through hiding in a group of friends, but now that brings me really fast to the point where I feel emotionally naked. My comfort zone is left behind "at home". So I am starting to understand, that it is not about the home I build by or for myself and through my virtue, it is about the all-surrounding home which is a life with God."

REFLECT

Benaja actually has a tattoo of symbols that tells the story of the lost son from the Luke 15.Luke 15:24is a key verse that sums up how God feels about us when we turn around and run home.
