Wednesday, April 4

Luke: Jesus Arrested

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily will study people from the Bible who changed the game. This week well continue studying Jesus, reflecting on the Easter story in the book of Luke.

READ

Luke 22:39-54

REFLECT

In this intense scene of prayer that follows the intimate gathering of the Last Supper, we see how Jesus is perfectly dependent upon and tied to his Father. In this moment, He is fully, humanly aware of the extreme violence and emotional torment thats ahead of him. So He presses into his Father for strength, for confirmation, for comfort. And He submits.

PRAY

God, thank you for experiencing the full weight of any pain and suffering Ive been through and will ever know.
