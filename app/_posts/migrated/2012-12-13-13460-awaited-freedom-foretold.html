Monday, December 17

Awaited Freedom: Foretold

We are in the final week of Advent. This week, you could light four candles to represent hope, joy, peace and this weeks topic, freedom.

READ

Isaiah 61:1-3

REFLECT

The year of the Lords favor is foretelling the year that Jesus would come. The Jewish people were hoping that the Lords peace would mean freedom from Roman rule. Jesus brought peace, but not the kind that ended government oppression. Jesus came to initiate a new kind of Kingdom that rules wherever men and women come into agreement with Jesus way of life in their heart, and give him territory. We carry it with us.

RESPOND

Take a moment today and journal about areas of your life where you feel freedom.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
