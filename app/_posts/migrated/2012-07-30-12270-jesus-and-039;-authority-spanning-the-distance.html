Friday, August 3

Jesus Authority: Spanning the Distance

This Friday, lets look at how Jesus words travel, through the story of Jesus healing the officials son.

READ

John 4:46-53

REFLECT

Jesus reputation preceded him. When Jesus arrived in Cana, the official had already come from Capernaum (20 miles away, quite a distance on foot). Jesus sent him back with only his word, healed the officials son from a long way off.

RESPOND

Jesus isnt distant, his words can travel with you. Take a moment today to choose a verse to write on a notecard and keep with you. If you are unsure of one to choose, here is a good one:1 John 3:19-20

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.


  • How do you view people in authority? Does this affect your view of God?
  • How do you think that authority in Jesus is connected to humility?
  • Do you need healing anywhere in your life?
