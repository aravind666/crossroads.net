Thursday, May 13

God is Spirit (Part 2)

Take a moment and ask God to reveal himself to you as you study His nature.

READ

Galatians 5:16-26

REFLECT

The passage we read today talks about the Fruits of the Spirit, a subject we looked at in detail a few weeks ago.

Most people look at a list like this and immediately feel intimidated; in part, that happens because we generally begin by looking at where we fall short.

Remember, this is a picture of what life lived with Gods Spirit looks like; it is not a checklist we have to live up to. This is the great thing about a relationship with God. As we grow closer to Him and allow his Spirit to flow through us, we begin to bear the fruit of God.

  • Which Fruit of the Spirit do you see God beginning to form in you? How do you know?

RESPOND

Write the Fruits of the Spirit on a post-it note and put it on your mirror. In the morning when you see it, pray this simple prayer:

Dear God, help me to be open to allow your Spirit to flow through me today. Thank you.

Ephesians 1:13-14
