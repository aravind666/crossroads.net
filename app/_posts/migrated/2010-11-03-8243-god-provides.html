Wednesday, November 3

God Provides

Take a moment to consider how God provides for you.


READ
Luke 12:22-24


REFLECT
Are you anxious about any of the things (stuff) in your life? Are you worried about whether or not God will meet your needs?


RESPOND
Ask God to remove any anxiety about his provision for you. Tell Him that you want to trust in his goodness toward you and that you want to receive all that he wants to provide for you.
