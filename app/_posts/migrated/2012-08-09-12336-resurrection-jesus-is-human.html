Resurrection: Jesus is Human

We are continuing to look at the miracle of Jesus resurrecting Lazarus.

READ

John 11:28-36

REFLECT

Jesus understanding of resurrection gave him incredible hope without lessening his humanity. Jesus didnt focus on heavenly things to mask his emotions. He was present and compassionate.

RESPOND

Where is your focus? Ask God if he wants you to increase your hope (focus on Him) or your humanity (compassionate focus on others) today?
