Sign-ups for community groups are under way. One of the more popular groups is Dave Ramsey's Financial Peace University, which teaches about eliminating debt, staying out of debt, building wealth and learning to live more generously. Sasha and Therese, a couple who completed FPU last spring, talk about why it's a great community group.

Why did you sign up for FPU?
We were striving to be better stewards of our money. We had always had some type of budget; however, we never really paid attention to the fact that we would go over in several categories. We knew we needed help.

What made the greatest impact on you?
The biggest impact was teaching us to know and change our behavior patterns. Everyone knows you should save more and spend less. It's actually doing it that's tough.

Why would you recommend this class to others?
Yes. It was all very practical and useful -- not over our heads. God truly blessed us by allowing us to attend FPU.

FPU starts this Saturday, but it's not too late to sign up.
