Thursday, January 5

Redirect

READ

Jeremiah 29:11

PRAY

Ask God to give you the dreams he has in mind for you this year. (This will probably be an ongoing conversation with God, so its OK if you dont hear clear answers right away.)

REFLECT

When we trust that God is constantly working in us and that the plans he has for us are good, then our hopes arent dashed when a day feels out of tune. We trust He has an eye on the fullness of our lives in a way we cannot, because we know that "in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose" (Romans 8:28).

God is the giver of dreams--and he gives them to all of us.

RESPOND

Write down anything you heard in response to your earlier prayer. Maybe its just a slice of an idea, an image or a word. Write it down and begin thinking how this dream (or dreams) will be part of your year ahead.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
