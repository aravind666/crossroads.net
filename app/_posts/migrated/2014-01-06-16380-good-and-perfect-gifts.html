Friday, December 27

Good and Perfect Gifts

READ

James 1:17

REFLECT

Weve read a few stories this week about God as a good Dad. He loves us more than anything. He wants to give us every good and perfect gift. When we trust Him, we experience the power and glory of his love. Its not always easy to trust or obey Him and sometimes it feels like the opposite of what we want, but our Dad never changes. He has always loved us and he will always love you.

RESPOND

Spend a few minutes today thinking about the last year, 2013. What unexpected gifts did your Dad give you? Thank him for those gifts.

ADVENT EXPERIENCE

No school? No plans? Spend time together as a family and revisit activities from the Kids Club Advent Experience.
