Confession: Clean

Were learning about confession in this final week of our study on the book of James.

READ

"If we say we have no sin, we deceive ourselves, and the truth is not in us.If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness."

1 John 1:8-9(ESV)

REFLECT

Yesterdays passage from Jamestaught us about the power of confession. Today, we see that confession isnt just about healing, community and forgiveness--its also about God cleansing us. Handing something over to God through confession can be refreshing, like a burden being lifted. And when God clears out whatevers holding us back, he makes room for better things to come.

RESPOND

Tell God about something thats been holding you back.
