James: Peter Chimes In

As we continue to study the book of James, were focusing on the power of our tongues.

READ

For whoever desires to love life and see good days, let him keep his tongue from evil and his lips from speaking deceit.

1 Peter 3:10(TNIV)

REFLECT

James wasnt alone with hispower-of-tongue message. Yet we see something fresh with Peters words: a straightforward promise of good days when we control what we say (even when were suffering, frustrated, wronged, blamed or entirely at the end of our rope).

RESPOND

Today, personalize Peters advice. If you controlled your tongue, what would actually happen? What heated conversations would you avoid? What relationship would be better off? Who would thrive?
