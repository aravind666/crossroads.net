Return Home: Sarah

Sarah just returned from a trip to Mumbai, India, where her Dad really met her.(If you missed this weekends message,check out avideo summary here.)

MEET SARAH

"From childhood onward I have sung about Jesus loving me, and have known to take my little light and let it shine. As a baptized-at-birth lifelong Christian, I thought I truly believed in these things as well. While I knew of Jesus truly loving me and knew of the beauty of Gods unconditional love, fear and anxiety often kept me from truly believing these things deep down into my core. This fear, in turn, kept me from experiencing the fullness of Gods love. On October 12, 2012 in Mumbai India, after a week of feeling hungry for the Lord, I had the opportunity to get baptized and re-commit my life to the Lord. As I spoke the name of our Savior and was engulfed by the water, I could literally feel every worldly fear and weight of the world being removed from my body. I had been carrying the burden for so long, but it lifted as I emerged from the water. I expected to have tears of joy streaming down my cheeks, but while I felt extremely joyful, a unique sense of calmness and freedom, deeper than I had ever felt, took over my being. For the first time in my entire life I was livingPsalm 46:10,feeling rooted and complete. For the first time in my entire life I felt a love greater than anything one could experience human to human. I felt completely forgiven, accepted, saved, and celebrated."
