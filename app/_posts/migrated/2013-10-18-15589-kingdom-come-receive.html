Kingdom Come: Receive

Lets keep on moving through this Kingdom Come journey. Today, well take a look at what it means to receive the Kingdom of God.

READ

Luke 12:29-32

REFLECT

The kingdom of the world tells us to be preoccupied with getting. This distracts us from what God might be saying about him and about ourselves, keeping us from receiving Him. We can worry about our next job promotion, relationship or meal. Or we can focus on what God takes great pleasure in giving us: the Kingdom itself.

RESPOND

Whats distracting you from receiving Gods Kingdom? On page 42 of your field guide, write down how you can stay focused on the Kingdom.
