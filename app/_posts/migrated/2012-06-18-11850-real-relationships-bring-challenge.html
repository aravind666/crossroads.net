Tuesday, June 19

Real Relationships Bring Challenge

This week were studying the boldly authentic friendship of Jesus and his disciple Peter.

READ

Matthew 14:22-31

REFLECT

Jesus challenged Peter to step outside his comfort zone in multiple ways--stepping from the safety of the boat into the water, and also taking a step into new faith territory. Authentic relationships give us the chance to both challenge and be challenged. And its in this new, unexplored territory that growth happens.

PRAY

God, help me hear you speak through my friends.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
