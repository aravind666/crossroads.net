The Kennedy Connector, which will give Ridge and Madison better access from I-71, is under way. The city is working with us to minimize our impact, and we are working hard to adapt traffic flow. What to know:

1. There is limited access on both sections of Ibsen Avenue (and sometimes it is closed).

2. Allow yourself a few extra minutes before and after services.

3. Consider parking at the Annex—you can avoid Ibsen altogether.

For the latest updates, visit facebook.com/kennedyconnector.
