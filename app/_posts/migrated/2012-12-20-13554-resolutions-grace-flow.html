Thursday, January 3

Resolutions: Grace Flow

Today, we continue looking at resolutions by increasing the way we function in grace.

READ

Luke 7:36:50

REFLECT

There are two ways that grace flows: into us from God, and out of us toward others (or one might say through us). To be a more gracious person: forgiving, kind and non-judgmental--try receiving grace. You can start with remembering how much God has forgiven you, or by confessing to God and asking him to forgive you.

RESPOND

Spend time today either writing a letter to God in your journal confessing, or making an appointment with a friend that you trust to listen to your confessions and pray with you.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
