Its not too late to participate in the OneCity Pro-Am Golf Event from June 26-28, either as a volunteer, golfer or hole sponsor. The tournament benefits the OneCity Foundation, a key ReachOut partner. More than $1 million has gone towards changing lives in Cincinnati since Brad and Colleen Johansen organized the event five years ago. Check out bradjohansengolf.com for more information.
