Tuesday, September 18

Marriage: Hello, Neighbor

We continue to look at marriage through Gods eyes this week.

READ

Matthew 22:37-40

REFLECT

If you are married, have you ever thought of your spouse as your neighbor? Jesus speaks of marriage twice in the book of Matthew before summing up that loving God and your neighbor is what its all about (sorry, hokey pokey).And the word neighbor in this passage literally translates to other. Think of your significant other as simply your closest neighbor.

RESPOND

Married: How could you value your "significant other" as your number one neighbor to love?

Single: How can you love all the others/neighbors in your life in a way that models Jesus?
