Wednesday, January 4

Retain

READ

John 5:17

PRAY

Talk to God about what you feel up against. Ask Him to give you a clear and open mind.

REFLECT

Staying tuned in to how God is working in our lives takes some intentional practice, but it becomes more instinctual. We learn to mentally pause--even in the heat of an argument, a toddler meltdown or a job thats disintegrating--and discern how God might be growing us. But its not a matter pretending like everything is puppy dogs and rainbows; its a matter of recognizing that Gods Kingdom is advancing with incredible force and precision, and that we are part of it. No life, no year, is wasted.

RESPOND

Spend some time thanking God for how he grew you in 2011. If youre uncertain of ways you might have grown, ask God to show you where he is at work in your life right now. Ask Him to bring clear images to mind.
