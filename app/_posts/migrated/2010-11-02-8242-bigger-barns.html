Tuesday, November 2
Bigger Barns

Take a moment and ask God to meet with you today.


READ
Luke 12:16-21


REFLECT
Jesus tells us to be careful not to allow our possessions to rule our life. Are there ways in which the things you own have come to own you?


RESPOND

Tell God that you want to be free of defining your life according to the things you own. Ask Him to show you where you are building "bigger barns."
