Acts: Healing Whats Broken

This week, were studying the book of Acts and learning about game changers in the early Church.

READ

Acts 3:1-10

REFLECT

Todays passage teaches us about healing in more ways than one. Before his bold, Spirit-filled actions like the one you just read, Peter was known as the disciple that publicly disowned Jesus (read the full storyhere). God can heal physical ailments, but he also wants to restore lost hope and rebuild broken relationships--even when those relationships are between us and him.

RESPOND

Spend a moment thanking God for something hes restored or healed in your life.
