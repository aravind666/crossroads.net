Wednesday, October 10

The Promise of Home

Kaytlyn has always looked and seemed older than she is, but she is actually 19 and a sophomore at University of Cincinnati.

MEET KAYTLYN

"Ive always thought of far away Heaven being my home, and that Im more of an alien here on earth. Recently, though, Ive come to understand that God made this planet, meaning he is all around me, and his kingdom isnt far off. That means it really is okay for me to make myself at home, maybe not with cultures that arent Gods, but with all that my Dad has created right here. I can feel at home anywhere, especially when I look at it all as my Dads house."

REFLECT

Psalm 139:7-10seems to echo Kaytlyns words.
