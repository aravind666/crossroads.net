--- layout: post title: "Statement About Wednesday's Awaited Show" subtitle: "" date: 2008-12-17 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Todd" --- CINCINNATI (Dec. 17, 2008)- "At approximately 7:30 p.m. on December 17, a cast member from the Awaited Christmas show fell from a suspended position during the performance. She was attended to by emergency medical personnel as quickly as possible and then transferred to a local hospital for medical attention. We do not have an update on her condition at this time, but she is receiving the best possible medical treatment available.

Our first concern is for this cast member and her family. We are not releasing her name to protect her privacy. We ask that you pray for her quick recovery and that God will heal her completely.

At this time, we have not made any decisions about the remaining performances. We will take care to respect this cast member and her family in any decision that we make regarding upcoming shows.

Again, we ask for your prayers for the cast member and her family.

We will provide more information as it becomes available."

In the meantime, out of respect for those involved please refrain from sharing individual accounts of the incident, however we encourage you to post your prayers and words of encouragement.
