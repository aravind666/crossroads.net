Each week during the "Roadmap for a Revolution" series we'll be providing you with an "on ramp" to next week's theme. We challenge you to spend some time in the Bible thinking through the questions presented here as a way to engage fully in each week's theme.

Next theme: Call
Scripture focus: Acts 12:25-13:3
Questions:
  • Do you relate to the characters in this story? If so, in what way?
  • What about this story makes it revolutionary?
  • What do you learn from this story that you can apply to your life right now?
