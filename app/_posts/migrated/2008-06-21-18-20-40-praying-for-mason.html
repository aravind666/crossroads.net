This weekend Brian Tome shared more about the launch of Crossroads Mason in October. A few hundred people from our community have been working on this for over a year, and we're getting close to launch! (You can read more about our multi-site plans here.)

We need people willing to pray to support and encourage the launch of Crossroads Mason, so please consider signing up to be a prayer partner for the Mason Launch Team. Once you sign up, we'll give you the name of someone on the Mason Launch Team to pray for. If you're interested in serving as a prayer partner, please email us.
