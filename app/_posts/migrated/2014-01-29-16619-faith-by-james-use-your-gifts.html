Wednesday, January 29

Faith by James: Use Your Gifts

PRAY

Thank God for a specific gift hes given you, and ask him to help you be aware of the gifts in others.

READ

James 4:13-17, Proverbs 25:14

REFLECT

God gave us unique gifts. Maybe its a knack for cooking, a gift for making friends easily or a talent for being organized. No matter what it is, God wants us to use our gifts to bring about his Kingdom. We can let pride use it to separate us or we can use our gifts the way God intended---to bring us together. When we humble ourselves, and are willing to think beyond ourselves, we can truly have the kind of connection with God and others we were designed for.

PRACTICE

Remember that list of your talents or gifts you made yesterday? (If not, make a list right now.) Pick one of those talents and use it today to bless someone else without seeking credit or drawing attention.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 1 Corinthians 2
