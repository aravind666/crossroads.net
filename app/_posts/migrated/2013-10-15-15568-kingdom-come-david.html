Wednesday, October 16

Kingdom Come: David

Today, David tells a story about his neighbors change of heart.

DAVIDS STORY

Our street has a block party where we get neighbor signatures to block the street from traffic. One neighbor refused to sign. I decided I needed to go have a conversation with said neighbor, something I was NOT excited about.

I prayed God would go before me, that whatever happened He would be involved. I timidly knocked on the door and waited. Finally the neighbor answered and we decided to have a seat on the front porch and talk. After 15 minutes of good conversation, getting to know each other, understanding each others position, and me feeling like I was able to extend her grace and love...she signed her name on the list.

With the party in full swing, this neighbor and her husband walked by (not joining the party). I walked up to her to simply say thanks. As I approached, she smiled and gave me a hug!....A HUG! I feel like I saw the Kingdom right there in that moment....and it was good.

REFLECT

The Kingdom of God is full of hospitable people like David. Just read these verses: Matthew 25:34-36 and Hebrews 13:1-2.
