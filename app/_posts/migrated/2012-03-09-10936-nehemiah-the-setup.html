Nehemiah: The Setup

Were changing up The Daily for a little while. As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily will study people from the Bible that changed the game. This week, were looking at the story of Nehemiah.

This study is presented in a translation called The Message, which gives us biblical text in simple, modern language. Try not to get hung up on the old-timey names and locations, and focus on what God might want to teach us through Nehemiahs story.

READ
Nehemiah 1:1-11

REFLECT
Even though this happened a long time ago (its estimated around 440 B.C.), people had the same feelings and struggles we have today. How can you identify with Nehemiah after reading todays passage?
