Friday, July 6

The Paralytic Healed: A Challenge

Have you ever seen a father give his kid a bike, a skateboard or a BB gun? Think of the words that a good father would say to a child in this moment, and then read Jesus words to the healed paralytic man.

READ

John 5:8-17

REFLECT

Jesus implies through the wordreturnthat there was a time when this man had a "sinning life". With his new mobility, the former paralytic could literally walk into sin again; so Jesus gives this man the spiritual equivalent of "be careful out there." Like a loving father cautioning his excited kid, Jesus balances freedom with wise perspective.

RESPOND

Picture that you are walking out the door to go to a place you might regularly go. What might Jesus say to you as you leave?

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Do you believe that Jesus still heals?
  • Share one area in your life where you want to receive healing of any nature: physical, emotional, mental or spiritual?
  • If God has given you any next steps this week, share that.
