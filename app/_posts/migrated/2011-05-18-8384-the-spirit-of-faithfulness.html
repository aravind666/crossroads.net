Wednesday, May 18
The Spirit of Faithfulness

Before reading the scripture today, spend a minute just being quiet and preparing to hear Gods voice.

READ

Romans 12:1-2

REFLECT

It might sound counter-intuitive, but you can have faith in God and still not live with faithfulness.

Faithfulness to God means you dont bury your relationship with him. Your whole life reflects the love between you--not just on weekends, not just when youre volunteering, not just during conversations with fellow Christ-followers. Faithfulness is choosing to be a "living sacrifice" to God.Faithfulness to God centers your life and seeps into every part of it.

RESPOND

Ask God to show you where your faithlessness lies. Consider one way that you could start releasing its grip on your life and filling that part with His Spirit.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
