Tuesday, March 9

A Healthy Dose of Reality

Todays edition of The Daily highlights an important point from this past weekends message for you to keep in mind as you work through your guide and meet with your group this week.


READ

John 10:10


REFLECT

Jesus says there is a thief who wants to steal our freedom and block us from Gods truth. As unnerving as that may sound, the story doesnt end there. Jesus has come to beat this thief and restore life as God intended. No matter how violent or subtle the enemy, Jesus cant be defeated. Take heart that He is fighting for you.


PRAY

Ask Jesus to show you the ways he is fighting for your freedom this week.
