Thursday, March 28

How are those beans tasting? Keep up the good discipline as we look today at transferring our life into adventure.

READ

2 John 1:8

REFLECT

Safety nets are not bad. A trapeze artist appreciates them greatly. In fact, the safety net causes the trapezist to take greater risks: swing higher, leap further, and trust the grip of their partner more.

Whether you have financial safety nets available in your life (savings, credit cards, family) or spend time wishing you had more, the bigger question is: Are those nets bringing you more freedom or more fear? No one can answer that question, but you. No one can decide for you whats too much or too little, because this isnt a number game its a heart matter.

RESPOND

Journal for a moment today about whether eating rice and beans this week (or choosing not to participate) has brought you more adventure or safety?
