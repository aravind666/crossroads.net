Friday, July 9

Trust with Anticipation

Prayer: God, give me the trust to believe that you answer my prayers to you.

READ

Acts 12:5-16

REFLECT

The prayers for Peter to be freed from prison were answered.

With an angel in front of him, Peter walks right out of prison. Meanwhile, the very folks who were praying for him dont notice the answer to their prayer when it literally knocks at their door. What kind of trust were they praying with if they werent looking for the answer to arrive? What a good God who answers our prayers even when we arent looking for him to really come through.

Peter trusts God even as it leads him to prison, and God also provides the way out. We need to learn to trust God in spite of our circumstances.

RESPOND

Ask God to increase your trust, and sit quietly and receive what he gives you. Anticipate that He will answer your prayer.

ANOTHER LOOK

Romans 12:1-2
