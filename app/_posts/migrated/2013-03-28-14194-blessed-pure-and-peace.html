Thursday, April 4

We continue with our look at Jesusfirst teaching to the crowds which starts with a series of blessings.

READ

Matthew 5:8-9

REFLECT

Do you feel like you have seen glimpses of God in conversations, community or movements? If the answer is yes, you might understand being pure in heart. Do you want to feel that sense of purity more? Continue to put yourself in a posture of need toward God: poverty of spirit, mourning for the separation, humble, longing for God alone to satiate, receiving and giving his mercy. These will refine us, purify our heart, preparing us to see Gods face. The outward signs that this is happening internally: peace, it should follow in our wake...a sure sign that Gods family is near.

RESPOND

Sit in contemplation again today. Picture what things want influence over your heart, and give those over to God. Sit in his peace.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
