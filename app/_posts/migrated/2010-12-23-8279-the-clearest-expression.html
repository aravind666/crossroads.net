Thursday, December 23

The Clearest Expression

READ
Matthew 28:1-10

REFLECT

God the Father walked amongst us. He told us how we can choose life, and he offered us his own self, in the person of Jesus, as a sacrifice on the cross. He then rose miraculously from the dead, taught his disciples in Galilee, and rose to heaven where he joins his father in still setting captives free.

The birth of Jesus, humble and small-scale, led to a torturous and public death. And it is the clearest expression of the Fathers love for us.

RESPOND

Ask God to give you the boldness to humbly proclaim that Jesus overcame the power of death, reversed the bad choice of Adam and Eve, and allowed us to experience a life of true freedom.

ANOTHER LOOK
Ephesians 5:14-15
