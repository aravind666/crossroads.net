Friday, December 4
Imitators of God

 Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.


READ
Ephesians 5:1-2


REFLECT
These two verses are a great summary of how we should approach relationships. In a nutshell, because of how Jesus has loved us, we can live a life of love even when it requires sacrifice. For the remainder of your time today, spend time connecting with God through prayer. To help you launch into your prayer time, ask God the following questions and write down any good thoughts or answers that come to mind:

  1. Who in my life am I called to love sacrificially? Write down any names that come to mind.
  2. Whats one practical thing I can do to demonstrate love to each person on my list?

RESPOND
When you are finished praying, resolve to act on what youve heard.
