Monday, May 13

Carry On: Relax

This week well look at remaining in rhythms, by focusing on different portions of Psalm 116. Read the passage, and then reread the first line. Thats where well focus today.

READ

Psalm 116:7-8

REFLECT

You may be a constant worker, and choosing to be still or relax is a tough choice for you. Alternatively, you may struggle with a lack of get-up-and-go, but without solid times of work, your rest may be restless. Either way, choosing genuine peace and trust in God--the best kind of rest--is just that, a choice. This passage starts with I said to myself. Beginning good rhythms that form habits of working from rest might need to start with forming a new internal dialogue.

RESPOND

Write this verse on a notecard, post-it, or piece of paper and place it somewhere where you can see it regularly throughout your week.
