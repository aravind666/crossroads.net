Friday, December 23

A Night Like No Other

LISTEN

O Holy Night

READ

Isaiah 11:1-9

REFLECT

Holy. It means to be set apart, other, or sacred. So this holy night is utterly distinct: a night that is in a category all by itself.

God did something on this night he had never done: He walked on earth as a man. God contained himself within a human body so he could move amongst the children he loved. That moment cannot be anything other than holy.

RESPOND

The wolf living with the lamb. A child putting her hand in a vipers mouth. Isaiah 11:1-9 gives us the full picture of what a restored creation will look like. We are still waiting for the full restoration, but Jesus restores every day. Spend time asking Him to restore something in your life thats broken.
