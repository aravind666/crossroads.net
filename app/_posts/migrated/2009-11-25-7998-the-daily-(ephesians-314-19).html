Wednesday, November 25
Filled Up

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.

READ
Ephesians 3:14-19

REFLECT
Imagine for a minute that you were part of the early community of Christ-followers to whom Paul wrote this letter. How encouraged would you be to know that this is Pauls prayer and desire for your community? His hope and prayer is that you would be fully empowered by and immersed in the love of Godthat you would be completely filled by it.

Picture a glass of water that is overflowing. Thats how full you can beso full of His love that you cant help but spill over.

RESPOND
Paul describes Gods love as surpassing knowledgebeing too great to understand. Take a couple of minutes and write down how youve experienced Gods love. Then, read that description out loud to God as a prayer, thanking him for the love he has shown you.
