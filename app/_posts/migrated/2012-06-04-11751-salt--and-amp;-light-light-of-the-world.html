Salt & Light: Light of the World

This week, were unpacking Jesus descriptions of his followers as salt and light.

READ

"You are the salt of the earth. But if the salt loses its saltiness, how can it be made salty again? It is no longer good for anything, except to be thrown out and trampled underfoot.You are the light of the world.A city on a hill cannot be hidden. Neither do people light a lamp and put it under a bowl. Instead they put it on its stand, and it gives light to everyone in the house. In the same way, let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven."

Matthew 5:13-16(TNIV)

REFLECT

We rely on light for the most basic tasks, like seeing where were going and interacting with our surroundings. Jesus description of his followers as "the light of the world" is a pretty big deal--hes telling us that we can bring guidance and clarity to the world around us.

RESPOND

Spend a moment asking God for an abundance of guidance and clarity, so you can share it with the people in your life today.
