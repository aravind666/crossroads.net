More than 200 volunteers from Crossroads took time away from football and turkey to serve hot Thanskgiving meals to the homeless, sick and elderly through City Gospel Mission. Thanks to those who helped make the holiday brighter for the under-resourced in our city. If you're interested in serving hot meals for The Diner at City Gospel Mission, you can participate all year.
