Monday, November 11

Kingdom Come: Seek First

In week 5 of the journey, The Daily will look at what it means to seek the Kingdom of God.

READ

Matthew 6:33

REFLECT

In the morning, one of the first things you might do is think about what the day might bring. And if its Monday--what will the week bring? These thoughts set into motion a string of worries. Will I eat lunch today? Will I get home in time for dinner with my family? Will I make it to Friday? And so on.

Jesus says not to worry, but to "seek first the Kingdom of God." By putting aside our worry and actively seeking--receiving His love, obeying his commands, sharing His love with others--our Dad will provide for all our needs and desires. In other words, He will give us the Kingdom.

RESPOND

What are you worried about today? It could be anything. Instead of worrying about it, seek out what your Dad is telling you about that worry. Take a minute or two and give that worry to God.
