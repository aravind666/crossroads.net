We finish out The Daily supporting you through your beans and rice fast today. Remember that rice absorbs the gas created by the beans--you can make it!

READ

Matthew 21:43

REFLECT

The American Dream says that you can make something of yourself-- go to college, start a business, make money, have a wife, kids, send them off to college, retire and die. The American dream says invest in yourself.

Kingdom Vision says invest into eternity, that we can be a part of something bigger than our own story, that there is a larger purpose to all of this hub bub and busy-ness. Jesus, the leader of this Kingdom movement invested (or maybe divested) his whole self toward an eternal purpose that you and I, 2000 years later, are still impacted by. Being a Christ follower means following his lead, Jesus vision of giving our life over to a King that we can trust, toward a mission larger than ourselves.

RESPOND

Where do you want to believe more in Jesus vision instead of the culture you are in? Write about it today.
