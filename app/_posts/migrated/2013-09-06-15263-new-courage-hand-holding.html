Wednesday, September 11

New Courage: Hand Holding

Today, we continue looking at Gods desire to help you walk from fear to courage.

READ

Isaiah 41:13

REFLECT

Imagine being a kid and holding an adults hand as you walk. It doesnt matter where you are walking--you can daydream or notice things when you know they are taking care of the direction and safety. God wants to hold your hand. He doesnt want you to fear, but to be free.

RESPOND

Quickly write down in your journal things that you are worrying about. Now, tell God you are handing those over to him, and go on about your day in freedom.
