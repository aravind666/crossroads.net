Monday, July 2

The Paralytic Healed

This week well be looking at a firsthand account of Jesus healing from the book of John. Jesus healings dont all look the same, and this story has several parts that might defy expectations. We may think weve figured out the road to the good life, but Jesus brings the unexpected.

READ

John 5:1-9

REFLECT

Hundreds of sick people were by the Bethesda pool, but Jesus approached just one. Why? To understand, we have to pull back from the limited view of an average life-span and consider the great depth of eternity. Jesus chose one man, but that one mans story still stands--and it points us toward God and his desire to heal and make whole. The miracle, or "sign" as John calls it, is a hint of a time to come.

RESPOND

InMatthew 6:9-10Jesus is teaching his disciples how to talk to God. Pray this part of the prayer today. Specifically, picture Gods Kingdom coming to bring us healing.
