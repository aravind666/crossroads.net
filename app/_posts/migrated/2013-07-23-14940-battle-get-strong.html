Friday, July 26

Battle: Get Strong

We finish out our week looking at the spiritual battlefield. Focus on verse 10 today, and well soak in how much God is strengthening us in the fight.

READ

1 Peter 5:6-11

REFLECT

God created all that exists. The enemy cant create, so he just looks to make chaos. God sent Jesus to walk through and conquer that chaos. God has restored his son to his place in heaven, and he is making a place for us there as well. All of the worry that you have cast onto God--he can remake into hope. The only way to be strong, firm, and steadfast is to plant all of your hope on the one who doesnt change--God.

RESPOND

Picture your feet firmly planted on a good solid place and name that place hope. Now read this verse and receive it as a blessing spoken over you by Jesus.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Which is harder for you--humbleness or not worrying?

  • Discuss this quote, The best trick of the devil is to persuade you that he does not exist.

  • If hope were a person or an object, what would it look like?
