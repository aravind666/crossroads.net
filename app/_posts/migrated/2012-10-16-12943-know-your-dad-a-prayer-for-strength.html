Friday, October 19

Know Your Dad: A Prayer For Strength

Today we visit Pauls letter to Ephesus in a section that is labeled in some BiblesA Prayer for the Ephesians, and in othersA Prayer for Spiritual Strength. Lets receive it as Pauls prayer for us, right now. Just be Ephesian for a moment.

READ

Ephesians 3:14-19

REFLECT

Knowing your Dad means understanding how much he loves you. Just sit for a moment today and let him, not for any label that you have, anything you have done, or for the identity you hold onto. Just let him under all of those covers. Just be for as long as you have time, and receive.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
