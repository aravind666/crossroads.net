Gods Own Children

This week, were examining our identities as treasured children of God.

READ

Romans 8:15

RESPOND

Spend some time today with this verse. Here are a few ways to interact with it:

  1. Commit it to memory.
  2. Write the verse on a card and post it in a place youll see it often.
  3. Rewrite it using your own words.

God, thank you for loving me so generously. Please give me the patience, wisdom and awareness to receive that love.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

The Friday edition of The Daily features discussion questions based on the weeks content. These questions are meant to spark conversation with your spouse, your roommate, or a small group of friends who also subscribe to The Daily.

  • What are some things that hold you back from seeing yourself as a true child of God?
  • List some examples of times when you felt like a treasured child.
  • Discuss areas in your life where you find yourself seeking approval, especially from God. How do these things impact your relationship with Him?
