Know Your Dad

The Daily continues to journey with you this week as we look at verses that discuss knowing God as your Dad, and listen to people from our community about their understanding of God as their Dad.

READ

Colossians 1:17

REFLECT

Take a moment and just meditate on this verse. Read it over a few times. Not only does a good Dad go ahead of you, but think of Him literally being so close that he holds you in your form. Like an infant swaddled in a blanket, you are swaddled in Gods arms daily.
