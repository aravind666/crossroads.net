No matter where and what God calls us to, we believe our primary call will always be to everyday, average Cincinnatians who are wrestling with whether there’s a God, and if so, if he has any relevance in their lives. Until every person in Cincinnati has seen their lives transformed by Jesus, we’re not done. Not done growing. Not done sacrificing. Not done living out the mission God called 11 people to, 14 years ago. And whether we number 10,000 or 100,000, we’ll continue to connect seekers with growing Christ followers who are changing the world. It’s who we are, and who we’re called to be.
