--- layout: post title: "Rebuild Resource" subtitle: "" date: 2008-08-18 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Todd" --- A few years ago I picked up a copy of Andy Stanley's book Visioneering. (Andy Stanley is the lead pastor at Northpoint Church near Atlanta.) The book is rooted in the story of Nehemiah and discusses how to uncover and pursue a personal vision.
For anyone wanting to stay tuned to what God's saying and act on it, it's a pretty good read and it's great supplemental material to our current Rebuild series.
