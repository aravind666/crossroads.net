Work: Gods Business

Last week we spent time moving Gods culture into our work. This week well look at whos in charge.

READ

Proverbs 16:11

REFLECT

The words "integrity" and "integrated" come from the same root. Havingintegritymeansintegrating your values into all the areas of your life. Following Jesus leads to unified living--not having a work life, a home life and a social life, but choosing to have a single, integrated, God-focused life.

RESPOND

Picture Jesus standing or sitting next to you at work. Really, picture it. What would He say to you?
