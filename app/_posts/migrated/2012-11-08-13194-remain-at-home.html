Monday, November 12

Remain at Home

We will finish outThe Journey Homethis week with some compelling verses and stories that reflect the goodness of remaining at home in our Dad.

READ

Romans 8:1-2

REFLECT

Jesus made a way for us to remain at home with our Dad. He has already done the work to overcome anything and everything that we believe separates us from God. How often do you still say "Ive got this" (do it on our own) or do cartwheels for God (try to earn his favor)? Remaining at home means remaining in a posture of receiving. Receiving that God already made the plan for us to remain with him, Jesus paid the price and did the work, and the Spirit walks with us all along the way.
