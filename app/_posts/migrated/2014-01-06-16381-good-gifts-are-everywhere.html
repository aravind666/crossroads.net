Monday, December 30

Good Gifts are Everywhere

READ

Ephesians 4:6

REFLECT

God is holy. He is also our Dad. Having a Dad that is "over all and through all and in all" means that wherever God is (everywhere) there, also, are his gifts.

He loves to give us good gifts. Sometimes they arent what we expect or what we think we want, but God blesses us so that we will trust him. We discover that leaning in and trusting our Dad is the greatest gift of all. He is all we need. This week, The Daily will continue to look at stories of people who have trusted their Dad and received his love.

RESPOND

Spend a few minutes today wherever you are---at work, in the car or out to lunch with a friend---and look around you. If your Dad is everywhere and in everything, what gifts do you see that you dont normally recognize?

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
