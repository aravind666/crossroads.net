Monday, December 28
Gift of the Holy Spirit

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.


READ
Acts 2:29-40

REFLECT
The parties are over, the wrapping paper is cleaned up, and gifts have been given. Its easy to think the Christmas season is over.

But we arent the only ones giving gifts this time of year.

Before Jesus left earth, he promised his followers the gift of Gods Holy Spirit. In fact, Jesus told his followers that it would be better for him to leave earth so that the Spirit would comea Spirit who would empower them in ways that would far exceed their natural abilities, strength and courage.

And we are offered this same gift. We can carry the Spiritthe power of Godwithin us.

  • Have you ever felt like you were experiencing Gods Spirit? What was it like?
  • If Gods Spirit lives in us, then that means his power is available to us in our daily lives. What does that change for you?

RESPOND
For the more significant gifts we receive in life, its generally expected that we write a thank-you note of some kind. Since the Holy Spirit is one of the greatest gifts we could ever receive, it certainly fits that category. Take a few minutes and write a thank you note to God for this incredible gift, and ask him to help you rely on the Holy Spirit on a daily basis.
