Tuesday, May 20

The New Man Assists the Team

This week, The Daily will focus on what the Bible says about how men play on a team.

PRAY

Ask God to show you how to be of great assistance to others.

READ

Philippians 2:3-4

REFLECT

Assisting is celebrated in many sports---when a player helps or assists another in scoring a point even if that means simply passing the ball. Jesus wants us to do the same, to take a mindset that when we assist or serve others, were contributing to a greater good even when we dont receive the glory. We can do some things on our own, but when we work as a team and assist each other, we can have astounding success.

RESPOND

Assist someone on your team today. Whether its someone in your family, a friend, coworker or even a stranger, give someone a helping hand. Dont expect a certain outcome, but pay attention to what happens because of your assist.
