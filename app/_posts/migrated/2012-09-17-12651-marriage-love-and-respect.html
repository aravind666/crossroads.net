Thursday, September 20

Marriage: Love and Respect

We continue this week to look at marriage through the lens of Gods convenant with us.

READ

Ephesians 5:31-33

REFLECT

Marriage is not a contract between two people, but a covenant between God and the betrothed couple.When a married couple gives each other respect and love, they create a balance of beauty like a well-composed work of art. We also create that beautiful balance when we (as the bride of Christ) receive love from Him, while also giving him loving respect.

RESPOND

Married? Go out of your way to lovingly adore your wife, or to lovingly respect your husband.

Single? Whether youre a guy or a lady, spend a moment telling God that you respect him. And see if He answers you throughout the day with signs of his love.
