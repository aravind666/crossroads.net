Wednesday, February 12

Truth: Humility

This week, The Daily assumes that the Bible is only a big book about God and hes the main character. Well look at verses and mull over what it means for us if what they say is true.

PRAY

Ask God to prepare you for whatever he wants to teach you today.

PSALM 149:4

For the Lord takes pleasure in his people; he adorns the humble with salvation.

ASK YOURSELF

What does it mean to you that God rewards humility? What does it look like to be humble in your actions at work, school or at home?

RESPOND

God takes pleasure in you. Dont look for ways to get approval or status today---that means stay away from the Facebook posts or being the only guy to talk at the meeting---lay low and put your pleasure in your Dad.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 1 Corinthians 12
