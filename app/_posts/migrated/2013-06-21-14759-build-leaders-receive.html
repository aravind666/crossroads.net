Wednesday, June 26

Build Leaders: Receive

God gives us spiritual authority and wants us to fully receive it. Today, focus on verse 4.

READ

Genesis 39:1-4

REFLECT

Potiphar realized that the Lord was with Joseph, and that pleased him. Because Joseph received the spiritual authority that God had given him, Potiphar recognized that authority. The more we fully recieve that the Lord gives us spiritual authority, the more of an example we set for others to easily receive, too.

RESPOND

Take a moment to think about ways that you can receive and implement your spiritual authority more often.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: James 5
