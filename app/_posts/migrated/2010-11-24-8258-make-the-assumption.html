Wednesday, November 24

Make the Assumption

God, please help me to have faith in your power. Please give me perseverance as I wait for your answers.

READ

Isaiah 26:3

REFLECT

Consider what happens when you give someone the benefit of the doubt. Instead of getting angry, you assume that person had good intentions. Instead of predicting failure, you give that person room to succeed. Or you assume that you dont know the whole story, and you leave it at that.

Giving someone the benefit of the doubt isnt necessarily easy. But it has a reward: peace. Because youve relinquished control. Youve chosen to trust.

What would happen if we gave God the benefit of the doubt? What happens when we acknowledge that we dont know the full story, but He does, and its a good story?

RESPOND

Look back at your paper. Underneath whatever you wrote, add your answer to these questions: If I gave God the benefit of the doubt, something I would start doing is:

ANOTHER LOOK
