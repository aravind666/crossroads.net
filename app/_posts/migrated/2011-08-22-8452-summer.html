Week 2 - Seasons

Monday, August 22

Summer

God, let me be reminded that I am to stay diligent in searching you.

READ

Proverbs 30:25, Psalms 139:23-24

REFLECT

Summer is a season of busy-ness, even for animals as small as the ants. They know their work and they are busy doing it. God has laid work before us, as well.

Its time to transition from the newness of spring to this season of work. Otherwise, our hearts will turn to idle restlessness and the luster of spring will lose its gleam. Now is our opportunity to put our love into action, to engage in the Kingdom. But equally important is the internal work of summer. Let God refine your heart during this time. Let Him be your motivation instead of doing work for show.

Choosing to move into the season of summer is a choice to sustain the good work that the Lord has begun in us.

RESPOND

Take a few minutes and ask God where he wants to work in your life. He wants to lead you in deep and unique ways, so ask Him what it looks like to engage in the Kingdom.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
