Friday, July 23

The Last Word

Pray through the Lords Prayer.

READ

Nehemiah 5:13, 8:6

REFLECT

Turn to the very last word in the Bible. What does it say?

This is one of those words that is used so much that it can lose its meaning. Its a word that is generally an expression of agreement, confirmation and ratification. Its like signing your name on the dotted line. And its binding.

Amen is not just a word we say; its a decision we make. Yes, Im a part of that, or Yes, I want that. Jesus is called the Amen in Revelation because he is the fulfillment of the binding contract that God has with us. And amen is used in the Old Testament when all the people agreed to do what was asked of them.

Can you say amen to the Lords Prayer and everything that it encompasses?

RESPOND

If you feel or have felt that the Spirit has asked for your amen, can you give it?
