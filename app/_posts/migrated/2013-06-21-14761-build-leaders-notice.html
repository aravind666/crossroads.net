Friday, June 28

Build Leaders: Notice

We finish up the week by focusing on verse 6.

READ

Genesis 39:1-6

REFLECT

We all may notice things in our work that others get stressed about. See those times as an opportunity to serve the people in authority over you. In stressful situations, the natural response is to turn away and avoid it as much as possible. The things that we naturally want to turn away from can often be things that the Lord is trying to grow us in--like patience, grace, self control or humbleness.

RESPOND

Today when a stressful situation comes up, instead of shying away, run toward being a servant. Be intentional about noticing where you can serve those in authority over you.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • What are some of your strongest leadership skills? What are your weakest?

  • Have you ever experienced anyone building into you? How did that alter the way you built into someone else?

  • Where in your life do you think God is trying to move you into a leadership position?
