Wednesday, July 24

Battle: Enemy Stats

Today we will continue looking at the spiritual battlefield with a close look at our enemy.

READ

1 Peter 5:6-11

REFLECT

Now that we have gotten low and light over the last two days, heres the offensive plan--be alert and sober. Why? The enemy is real, and he wants to devour you. God is not part of the frustration and pain in your life. Dont get me wrong, God works in crazy ways to take a struggle and redeem it into really good training and strengthening, but he doesnt write pain into your story. God wants a full, eternal life for you. The enemy wants to devour. God creates, the enemy destroys. God brings order, the enemy causes chaos. God authors life, the enemy functions in death.

RESPOND

Pinpoint an area of your life today where the enemy is creating chaos, and ask and invite God into that space. If He can order the universe--he can order your world.

ANOTHER LOOK

John 10:10
