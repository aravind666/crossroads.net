Wednesday, August 18

A God Who Stays

Today, ask God to reveal his faithfulness in your life.

READ

Genesis 12:1-3

REFLECT

So God created a perfect world, and mankind promptly messed it up.

By all accounts, God should have just walked away and scrapped everything. There was nothing to obligate Him to do anything else. He owed mankind nothing.

Yet He didnt leave. He stayed.

And God did more than just stay. Beginning with Abraham (originally called Abram), God set a plan in motion to redeem mankind. Through Abrahams lineage came Jesus, whose death and resurrection cancel out our sin, if we believe in him.

This is the faithfulness of Godthat he stays when he has every right to leave, and that he continually makes promises to care, protect and redeem.

  • What do you think about this idea that God stayed when he wasnt obligated to do so? How does that apply to you?
  • Why would God behave this way? What motivates Him?

RESPOND

God stays with you. Make a list of places where you need for Him to be in your life right now, and then spend a minute inviting him into those places.
