Monday, April 2

Luke: Jesus Into Jerusalem

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily will study people from the Bible who changed the game. This week well continue studying Jesus, reflecting on the Easter story in the book of Luke.

READ

Luke 19:28-40

REFLECT

Riding into Jerusalem on a donkey wasnt a statement of weakness or modesty, as it might come across today. In Jesus day, donkeys were ridden by non-military personneleven kings. So the donkey marked Jesus as a peace-bringer, not the warrior-Messiah that many people expected. Whats more, Jesus request that it be anunriddendonkey points to the rabbinical tradition that a kings riding animal be set apart and used for no other purpose. So by choosing this style of entry, Jesus makes a public statement that he is King. And He was received as such.

PRAY

God, show me the unexpected nature of your Kingdom.
