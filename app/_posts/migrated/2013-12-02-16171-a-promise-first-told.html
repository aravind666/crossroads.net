Tuesday, December 3

A Promise First Told

God had made the world, man and woman. And it was very good. What comes next in the story would change everything. Today, The Daily will look at when the promised Savior is first foretold.

LISTEN

Garden

REFLECT

In the last 20 seconds of the song, the instruments begin to play their own melodies and are out of harmony, much like the first man and woman when the serpent manipulated what their Dad had told them. Instead of choosing to have faith in their Dad, that he would provide their every need, the man and woman went their own way. This choice introduced pain into the world and disrupted a perfect relationship.

And their Dad lays out a punishment. But in Genesis 3:15, in the middle of the punishment, we read the first sign of a promised Savior. The womans offspring will bruise the serpents head. Our Dads promise that he would one day deal the enemy a fatal blow.
