Wednesday, July 13

You Dont Have to Be Perfect

God, do not let my fear of failure get in the way of doing your will.

READ

Joshua 2:1-24

REFLECT

Rahab was a prostitute, rejected by society. She was not a person who most of us would expect to be used by God. But God worked through Rahab, and because of her help, Joshua and the Israelites eventually toppled the walls of Jericho.

Rumors about the coming invasion were all over the city, and living on the city wall,

Rahab was especially vulnerable. But she didnt let her fear get the best of her. She

knew that God was in charge, and that he wanted the Israelites to conquer the land of

Jericho. So, she put her trust in the Lord and hid the spies at great personal risk. As a result, God rewarded her. This prostitute became one of Gods people and even became a part of the lineage of Jesus.

RESPOND

None of us are perfect, and we all sin. Yet God has big plans for us, and desires us to advance the Kingdom. There is no sin in your life that changes Gods mind about you or his plans for you. Do you have a specific sin in your life that is keeping you from being obedient to God? If so, ask for His forgiveness and ask him what his will is for you in that area of your life.
