Long Lay the World

READ

Isaiah 9:6-7

REFLECT

God had made the world a perfect place, but man and woman made a choice to not obey him. Yet He still loved them. He still loves us. Generations of people lived in darkness and waited. Through Isaiah and the prophets, God shared signs of the coming Saviors birth, power and kingdom to give them hope. His promise to save his people was about to be fulfilled.
