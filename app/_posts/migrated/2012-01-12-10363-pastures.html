Friday, January 13

Pastures

READ

John 10:7-10

REFLECT

What does it look like for sheep to find pasture (John 10:9)? It looks like sheep who are provided for, who arent left to fend for themselves, who get to roam on nourishing parts of the land without worrying that the gate is going to shut them out when they return.

"I have come that they have life, and have it to the full." (John 10:10)

RESPOND

Whats something in your life that feels like pasture? Find a way to spend time in that pasture today.

PRAY

Pray for someone close to you, that they would be open to the care of the Shepherd.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is:John 11
