Thursday, December 6

Awaited Joy: Mary Treasures

As we continue to look at the angels proclaiming to the shepherds, notice how Mary responds.

READ

Luke 2:15-19

REFLECT

The angels sang, and the shepherds went, but Mary is different. Mary had known the identity of her son throughout her entire pregnancy. The shepherds words must have felt like deep affirmation. Her joy was internal, and she didnt let it come and go fleetingly. When Mary treasures and ponders it, seems that she is fostering deeper joy.

RESPOND

Take a moment right now to let God remind you of ways that he has brought joy to you, either through the Christmas story or another personal way. Really ponder or meditate on it.
