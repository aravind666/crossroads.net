Wednesday, May 15

Carry On: Soul

We continue to look at Psalm 116 today as we focus on retaining our rhythms of rest and work. Focus today on the statement made to the soul.

READ

Psalm 116:7-8

REFLECT

In the middle of the frenzy of a day, its peculiar to stop and celebrate with our soul. Out of all that you are doing, all the busyness, is any of it more weighty or important than eternity? If eternity stretched wide like the line of the equator, our lives are equivalent to a molecule on that line. How much less important does the task seem when you compare it to eternity?

RESPOND

Take a moment to journal your gratitude to Jesus for making a way for you to experience eternal life.
