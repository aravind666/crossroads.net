Monday, April 9
Acts: Receiving The Spirit

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily is studying people from the Bible who changed the game. These next two weeks, were studying the book of Acts and learning about some key leaders in the early Church.

This study is presented in a paraphrase of the Bible called The Message, which gives us biblical text in simple, modern language.

READ
Acts 1:6-9 | 2:1-13

REFLECT
Throughout His time on earth, Jesus did the unexpected. And before Jesus left, he promised his disciples one more unexpected gift--that they would receive the Holy Spirit. In other words, they would know Gods power and love in a new and profound way. So begins the book of Acts, a story about the powerful things that happen when God moves through his people.

PRAY
God, please fill me with your power and love. Thank you for your willingness to give me such a wonderful gift.
