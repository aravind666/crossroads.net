Week 1: Seasons

Monday, August 15

Spring

God, I choose to believe that you work through all seasons of my life. Teach me to live fully in each season of faith.

READ

Ecclesiastes 3:1-14

REFLECT

Its an old adage (and a groovy song), but there really is a time for everything in our lives. A time for building, a time for moving, a time for happiness, a time for pizza, a time for the Bengals to win (well, maybe not everything), and so on.

There are things that God wants to grow in us, prune in us, and strengthen in us. Yet he doesnt do these things all at once-they happen in various seasons of our lives. Great Biblical figures throughout time have been awakened in the spring of their faith, grew and worked in a spiritual summer, understood harvest and beautiful death in the vibrant colors and enriched soil of autumn, and waited, deepening in meditative winters.

Over the next few weeks, we will look at these seasons of life- starting with spring. Spring is a time of new love.

RESPOND

Take a moment and look again at Ecclesiastes 3. Which statement did you resist the most? Write down the phrase that stood out to you, and ask God if it might be time for that in your life.
