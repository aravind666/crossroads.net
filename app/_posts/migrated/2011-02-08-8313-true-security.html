Tuesday, February 8
True Security

God, show me how to look to you for true security.


READ

Psalm 127

REFLECT

What do we rely on?

A great house? An awesome job? Money? Connections? Family?

The list is different for each of us, no matter what our background or social standing. Yet are we truly secure? All too often, these things dont free us from worry; they actually cause us to worry more.

But Solomon says, God gives rest to his loved ones. Do we catch the significance of that? When we rely on Godwe are able to truly rest.

RESPOND

Grab a blank sheet of paper and write down the first worry that comes to mind right in the center. Then take a few minutes to write down reasons why you have that worry. Ask God to help you see how he is already providing for you in this area.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
