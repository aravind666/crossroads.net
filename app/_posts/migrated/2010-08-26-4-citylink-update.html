Welcome to the CityLink blog. During the campaign and beyond, this feed will be your main source for information about whats happening with CityLink, including updates on the vision for combating the cycle of generational poverty to stories of how CityLink partners are already changing lives in Cincinnati for the better, and even ways that you can get involved.

CityLink leaders, including ReachOut Director Tim Senff, will provide updates on the latest happenings. Many of the posts will be conversation startersa place for you to respond with your own stories of how God is moving in your life. We encourage you to be part of the conversation about making the CityLink dream a reality.

Subscribe or check back to this blog for stories, photos, video and more. We hope youll follow and participate in the discussion about the fight for freedom in Cincinnati or across the globe.
