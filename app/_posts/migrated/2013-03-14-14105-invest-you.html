This week we will be looking at a parable, or story about investment that Jesus taught to help us understand the nature and principles of his kingdom. Well add a little more each day to look at the complete story through Friday.

READ

Matthew 25:14-18

REFLECT

In Jesus story, God is the man at the beginning of the story. This is a story about investment, that begins with the fact that God invests in us. He does not give equally, because this isnt giving, its investing. God invests like a sound banker or stockbroker: where he sees the best possibility for return. We made note last week of our resources, remember this week that all of those are actually on loan from the Father as an investment in us.

RESPOND

Journal today about how you could become a better investment. What areas of your life might you bring into better order.
