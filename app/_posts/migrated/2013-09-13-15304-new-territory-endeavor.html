Friday, September 20

New Territory: Endeavor

If you are eating Isaiah 54 with a spoon this week, then today, verse 5 will feel like dessert.

READ

Isaiah 54:1-5

REFLECT

No matter your gender, weve been putting ourselves into the story this week by being the childless woman (since God is speaking to his people personified as a whole in this woman). He has taken the one who is desperate and given her new children, new territory and comforted her. As if that isnt enough, today is a drop-the-mic moment. God reminds us that the creator of all that is, including you, is the one who will hold your hand and walk with you intimately like a husband through this new endeavor.

RESPOND

Put your journal down today. Picture the thing that you are nervous about right now. Now, picture holding Gods hand as you walk into that situation. If you want to pick your journal back up and write about it, its cool.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Did you connect with the barren woman this week?

  • Where is God wanting you to expand your tent?

  • Where are you experiencing fear?
