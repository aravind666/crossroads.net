Week Six, The Journey

During the Free journey, we'll start the week with a heads up about your Free guide or small group, and spend the next few days revisiting important ideas from the weekend message.

When you meet with your small group this week, youll get a chance to decide whats next: whether you want to keep getting together, call it quits, or create a pipeline for staying in touch. So this is a good time to start thinking and praying about what will keep you pursuing the freedom God has for you. Ask God to keep your heart open to what he reveals.
