Wednesday, April 17

We continue to look a passage in Isaiah deepening our trust in a good God. Yesterday we focused on rest and today on the word quietness.

READ

Isaiah 30:15

REFLECT

There are times to speak up and speak out. There are times for community that just involve the catching-up conversation. There are times that we hear the small internal nudge that tells us to step out of our comfort zone to encourage or connect. What about the space in between? Do you fill every minute with music, media, and chatter? The best way to hear Gods voice, communicating to him that you trust he will speak and that you want to hear him is to find the space for quiet in your life. Extrovert or introvert we have a choice: noisy clutter or make space for Gods spirit to function and flow.

RESPOND

Choose more quietness today. Make a list right now of some places in your day that you could make room for quiet.
