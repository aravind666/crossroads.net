Obedience Grows Faith

God, soften my heart towards you and your will, and take away my desire to be rebellious.

READ

Matthew 25:14-28

REFLECT

Often when we hear this passage, its in reference to talents or gifts. But its also about obedience. In any relationship, either with your parents or a boss or spouse, when you constantly rebel or ignore the other persons wishes, you tend to butt heads and argue, and your relationship suffers. But when you are obedient, your relationship strengthens and their trust in you grows.

The same is true with God. When you follow through on a prompting from God, more

often than not, you begin hearing him more often and more clearly. When you do

His will, God trusts you with more. Your obedience allows you to grow closer to Him, and your faith naturally increases. Obedience isnt just the foundation of our faith, it can grow our faith as well.

RESPOND

Consider and write down a time when you have rebelled. What led you to rebel? Was it fear? Laziness? Pride? What happened as a result? Today, look for opportunities to be obedient. When they appear, say yes to submission and no to rebellion.
