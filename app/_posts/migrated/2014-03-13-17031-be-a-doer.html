Friday, March 14

Be a Doer

PRAY

Ask God to help you make a change.

READ

Matthew 7:1-5, James 1:23-25

REFLECT

Sometimes change can be really easy (like switching to decaf), but most of the time its a challenge. The worst thing we can do in front of change is run the other way. James says when we know what to do, we have to do it. If we run the other way were not being true to what God is telling us or what he says about us.

RESPOND

What do you want to change? Whatever came to mind, think about one action step you can take. Then, take that first step toward obeying and make a change.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Galatians 5
