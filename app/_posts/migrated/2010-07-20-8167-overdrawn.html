Tuesday, July 20

Overdrawn

Reflect for a few minutes on Gods grace. Not as something in the past, but in the present.

READ

1 John 1:9-10

REFLECT

Forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors.

Most of us like the first part of this line. Its the second part where we struggle. Its the only part of the prayer that Jesus expanded on in Matthew 6:14-15. Why?

Maybe its because if we ask God to forgive us in the same way that we forgive others, technically, were asking to have as broken of a relationship with God as we do with the people who we cant forgive.

Jesus keeps writing checks to cover our mistakes. If we are to be more like Him, then we have to do the same. Withholding forgiveness leads to a hardened heart in which Gods will cannot be done and Gods kingdom cannot come to its fullness.

RESPOND

Ask Gods Spirit to soften your heart toward the people who have hurt or angered you.

ANOTHER LOOK

Jeremiah 31:34
