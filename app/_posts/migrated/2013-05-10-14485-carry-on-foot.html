Friday, May 17

Carry On: Foot

Today we finish out the series on Rhythms. Read Psalm 116, with a focus today on the foot.

READ

Psalm 116:7-8

REFLECT

Keeping rhythms, like agreeing to maintain a Sabbath, regular family or community meals, connecting with God or play is a commitment. You are going to stumble, make mistakes and learn what does and doesnt work for you. You will notice what distracts you and what woos you away. Take the time to notice, and then offer it up to God. Repent, which just means turn away from what doesnt work, and turn toward Gods goodness for you. Then ask God to protect your feet from stumbling.

RESPOND

Make a list of things that could rob your rhythms and commit them to God in prayer.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • What rhythms are you deciding you really want to continue in?

  • What might challenge those rhythms?

  • Ask to pray for each other about keeping them.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
