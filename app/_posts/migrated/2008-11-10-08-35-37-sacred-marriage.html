--- layout: post title: "Sacred Marriage" subtitle: "" date: 2008-11-10 08:35:37 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Todd" --- This weekend Chuck Mingo referenced a book in his talk called Sacred Marriage. The book poses the question, "what if God designed marriage to make us holy rather than to make us happy?"  For anyone wanting to better understand the dynamics of a healthy, Jesus-centered marriage or  how God uses our relationships to teach us about who he is and what he values, it's a great read.
