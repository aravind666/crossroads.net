Thursday, February 21

Discern: Life and Peace

We continue acting on what we hear from God by pausing to discern.

READ

Romans 8:6-7

John 10:10

REFLECT

We find a daily dichotomy in both of these verses. Every day, we choose to either agree with the enemys death-directed plans, or with the way that the Spirit advocates for us. A great place to start your discernment process is to ask: is this leading me to a fuller life, filled with fruit and blessing for me and those around me? Or will this choice rob me and others of Gods goodness?

RESPOND

Think of a choice youre about to make. Spend time journaling today about what options lead toward the fullness of God and life, and what will lead to destruction or death?
