Thursday, December 16

A Divine Birth Announcement

READ

Luke 2:8-20

REFLECT

Fathers are proud of their kids. Whether with wrinkle-faced birth announcements or pink-banded cigars, fathers desire to celebrate the promise of their newborns with friends and neighbors.

God allowed the birth of his son to be humble and lonely, but he also uniquely provided celebration and community to remind Joseph and Mary (and us) that the birth was the fulfillment of his loving promises to his children. All were filled with awe and wonder. Do you walk in awe and wonder for the many ways God announces the fulfillment of his promises?

RESPOND

Think about Gods ways of divine invitation to you-the smell of an evergreen tree, the wonder of joy of new births, the taste of spiked eggnog, the awe of the first snow. Ask to walk daily in awe and wonder of his invitations to you.
