Acts: Boldly Simple

This week, were studying the book of Acts and learning about game changers in the early Church.

READ
Acts 4:1-22

REFLECT
Its so easy for us to complicate our faith and get bogged down under all kinds of details. But in todays continuation of Peter and Johns healing story, Peter demonstrates a boldly simple faith. When questioned by the religious leaders about his actions, Peter shows them his faith begins and ends with simple, unyielding trust in Jesus.

RESPOND
Think of one simple way to live out your faith today. Then follow through with it.
