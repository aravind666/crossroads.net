Tuesday, September 3

New: Field

Yesterday we looked at how God has planters and waterers in his work. Today, make yourself the field.

READ

1 Corinthians 3:8-9

REFLECT

Before you think about how you want God to grow you in your gifts of planting or watering (starting new things or helping them grow), stop and consider that God is planting in you--you are the field. Contemplate and remember the good teachers and people who have planted and watered you.

RESPOND

Write a letter to someone today who has planted or watered you.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Ephesians 5
