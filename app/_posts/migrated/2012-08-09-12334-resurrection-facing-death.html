Resurrection: Facing Death

This week, The Daily looks at the resurrection of Lazarus--the last of Jesus seven signs from the book of John. Jesus resurrects his friend as a sign of what is to come, and a clue to his character as one who restores life.

READ

John 11:1-16

REFLECT

Because of the Pharisees active and public opposition to Jesus, visiting his dying friend Lazarus was a risky trip. Jesus first thought: if he trusted God, that meant he didnt need to fear. Jesus disciple Thomas nervously joked that they were going to their death. Jesus belief that God could restore life trumped everyones fear for their own safety.

RESPOND

Do you have a fear of death? If you believe that God resurrects things, what fear could you give him?
