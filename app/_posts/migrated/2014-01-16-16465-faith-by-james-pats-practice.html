Friday, January 17

Faith by James: Pats Practice

To end the week, Pat shares his story of putting love your neighbor to practice.

PRAY

Ask God to prepare you for whatever he has in store for you today.

READ

James 2:8-13

PATS PRACTICE

When I first started going to church I didnt like it even though I got a lot of free pizza and they always told me how great I was, but it was awful. At least I thought so---I just didnt like church. I thought Jesus was cool and all, but not for me. This small community persisted in showing me why I should give Jesus a shot. But I decided they didnt deserve my time.

Its been 5 years since Ive seen any of these people. But now I love Jesus and Ive shown them no appreciation for their patient persistence in showing me Jesus love. Ive used that church as an example of a place I didnt like and I havent spoken positively about them. But quite frankly, God used them to change my life.

I will thank them. I will write to them and share my story and the impact they had on me. I hope they are proud, I hope they understand the good they have done for me, and I hope they forgive my lack of appreciation. Im not a very emotional or sentimental guy, but as I write, its really teaching me something more about gratitude. Every good thing is from God, and they were simply reminding me how God feels about me.

PRACTICE

Try it again. Think of someone or a type of person in your life who you treat as a person beneath you. Take an action that shows them Gods love. Dont get caught up in the execution---the point is knowing that your worth and the worth of that other person is rooted in God.
