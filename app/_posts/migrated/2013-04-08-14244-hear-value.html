Tuesday, April 9th

Jesus continues to teach today about how he values us as we look at a passage in Luke.

READ

Luke 12:24-26

REFLECT

Jesus is letting you know how very much God cherishes you. (Reread verse 24 if you missed how much your Father values you.) You have a choice to listen to the voice of worry or to trust the voice of a good God who loves you. If you choose to invest your energy in worry, you will see it doesnt impact a single part of your life in a beneficial way. Dont do it. Dont invest more deeply in worry. Try investing in trust in God, and listen for his voice as he tells you your value to him.

RESPOND

Have a journal dialogue with God. Tell him about your worry, and then journal his response to the areas of your life that cause you stress.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
