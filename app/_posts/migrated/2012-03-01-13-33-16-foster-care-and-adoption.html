If you are interested in learning more about God’s heart towards foster care and adoption, and how you can become involved, sign up for the Foster Care Community Group. The group runs Saturdays, March 17–31, from 9am–12pm at Crossroads Oakley. Sign up online today.

 

If you’d like more information before committing to the group, attend an info session in the chapel at Crossroads Oakley:

Sunday, March 11 from 1–2pm.

Tuesday, March 13 from 6:30–7:30pm. Childcare provided.

 

If you are currently parenting kids who joined your family through adoption or foster care and are looking for support through training, education and opportunities to build community with other foster and adoptive families, email us for more information at care@crossroads.net.
