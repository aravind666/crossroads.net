Friday, October 7

The Invitation to Move

Spend a few minutes in prayer: "God, today I want to respond to your invitation to move in faith."

READ

Hebrews 12:1-3

REFLECT

These last three weeks weve looked at story after story of people who moved in faith and radically experienced God.

In all these stories there was an explicit or implicit invitation to move in faith. This same invitation gets extended to us every day. So throw off the things that keep you from moving in faith--whether thats guilt, fear, the need to control, the worry about what others will say. With boldness, respond to Gods invitation to move in faith.

RESPOND

What is something that keeps you from responding to Gods invitation to move in faith? Ask God right now to set you free from this weight so you can run in his direction.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 2 Timothy 4

Week Three Discussion Questions

The Friday edition of The Daily features discussion questions based on the weeks content. These questions are meant to spark conversation with your spouse, your roommate, or a small group of friends who also subscribe to The Daily.

  • Describe a time when you felt like you moved as a response to God. If nothing comes to mind, discuss an area of your life where youre hoping to hear Gods direction.
  • Do you know anyone who is a "mover" in his or her faith? What does that person risk?
  • What are ways you could put yourself in the position to hear Gods direction on your life? Consider the practical things--like times of day, places where its quiet, people who could hold you accountable.
