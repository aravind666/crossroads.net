Friday, January 18

Priests: You

Today, we look at two verses that make it clear that as believers, we are all called to be priests.

READ

1 Peter 2:4-5

1 Peter 2:9-10

REFLECT

Being a part of a church community does not mean that we are consumers, and the pastor or priest are the feeders. No. When we choose to follow Christ, we are a brotherhood of priests, a kingdom of priests, a royal priesthood.

RESPOND

Take a moment to picture yourself being formed by God into a priest. What might he want to add or take away from your life as he forms you?

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • What do you believe the role of a priest/pastor is?
  • How do you see yourself acting as a priest in your life?
  • What do you think it means to be a co-heir with Christ?
