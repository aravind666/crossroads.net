I’m told that giving thoughts periodically on our site could be healthy and helpful for our community, so here goes…
 
This was an awkward message for me to give this weekend. For starters, we did ministry reviews during the week with all of the ministry areas. On the positive side, I was encouraged by how great things are going in so many ministries as a result of how God is moving at Crossroads. On the negative side, it really set me back for my weekend message prep.

Then I had another moose on the table that was staring me in the eyes: the belief that you shouldn't talk too much about the person of the Holy Spirit because it will either freak people out  or it will disturb people because what is said may not match their personal experience. On the whole, I think that our community is responding great to stuff that is often mysterious, sometimes divisive but always healthy.
 
This weekend, I referenced some work that I did in outlining every person’s experience with the Holy Spirit as seen in the book of Acts. Below are my results straight from my journal. I think you’ll find that there is no standardized, linear process that the Holy Spirit takes in revealing Himself and in filling individuals. Enjoy.
