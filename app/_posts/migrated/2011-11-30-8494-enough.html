Wednesday, November 30

Enough

PRAY

Take a few minutes and reflect on what you know about the personality of Jesus.

READ

John 14:5-9

REFLECT

It happens again: someone asks to be shown God. This time, however, its thousands of years later. And its Phillip (not Moses) who is asking Jesus to do the showing.

What Phillip doesnt understand yet is that Jesus is God. He doesnt know its Jesus who restores the relationship between man and God to one of complete knowing and love. The same relationship Adam had with God before Adam turned away. The relationship Moses wanted from God but couldnt experience. The relationship were offered and can experience because God is in love with us.

RESPOND

Simple drawing assignment, no skills required: Draw an ocean wave. Then draw a line that splits it in half (i.e. the parting of the Red Sea miracle that Moses experienced). Write "God" above it. Then next to your wave, draw a cross (i.e. the Jesus that Philip experienced). Write "God" above that, too. Then consider that the same God who split the Red Sea in half and died on the cross is pursuing you.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
