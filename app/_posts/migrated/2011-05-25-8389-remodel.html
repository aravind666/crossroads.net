Wednesday, May 25
Remodel

God, show me how to get out of the way and make room for you in my life.

READ

Matthew 3:8


REFLECT

Todays verse says that the result of repentance (changing your beliefs and turning to God) is fruit. InGalatians 5:22-23, this fruit includes love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control. Sometimes repentance is just about getting out of your own way and making room for the fruit of the Spirit.

Heres what someone recently said about repentance:

I always think of repentance as raising the white flag...My attitude is that I am someone who needs saving and God is the one who saves.

RESPOND

Is there something that keeps coming to mind as you read about repentance? Something you want to change or something you at least want to talk to God about changing? Take a minute to raise the white flag and ask God if theres anything he wants you to surrender.
