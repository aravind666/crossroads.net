Tuesday, September 10

New Courage: Wisdom

We are looking at choosing courage over fear this week. Today, we look at fear a little differently.

READ

Proverbs 9:10

REFLECT

Fear demands a lot of attention. If you fear your boss, your desire to appear like you have it together in front of them will take center stage. If you fear missing out, it will drive your desire to check your phone or social media. God wants center stage, and when we make his opinion number one in our lives, it doesnt rob us of time and energy like other fears. Its the beginning of wisdom.

RESPOND

Write todays verse in your journal. Now, list some things that have your focus or attention or fear. Try substituting those words instead of the word Lord. It sounds ridiculous, which is a great reminder.

Ex: The fear of Bobs opinion is (not) the beginning of wisdom or The fear of missing out is (not) the beginning of wisdom.
