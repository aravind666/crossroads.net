Thursday, February 13

Truth: Eternal Life

This week, The Daily assumes that the Bible is only a big book about God and hes the main character. Well look at verses and mull over what it means for us if what they say is true.

PRAY

Ask God to help you see how he wants you to grow today.

JOHN 5:24

Truly, truly, I say to you, whoever hears my word and believes him who sent me has eternal life. He does not come into judgement, but has passed from death to life.

ASK YOURSELF

What does it mean to receive eternal life? How does it feel to get this back?

RESPOND

Change your perspective on your day. If youve gained new, eternal life, what becomes important and what falls away?

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 1 Corinthians 13
