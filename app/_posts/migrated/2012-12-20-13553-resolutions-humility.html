Wednesday, January 2

Resolutions: Humility

We are continuing to look at resolutions that are fortified with some spiritual goodness.

READ

James 4:6

REFLECT

Humility does not come natural to us. We are shameless self-promoters. Whether its on Facebook or Twitter, in board rooms or at sporting events, we love to tell the world, even in sly ways, that we are good: good parents, good students, good friends, and not to mention just straight up hip, right? Maybe, maybe not. Maybe we are just begging for God to oppose us.

RESPOND

Look for an opportunity today to not take credit. If you are quieter, try giving credit to God.
