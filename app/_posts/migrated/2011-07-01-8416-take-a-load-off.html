Friday, July 1

Take a Load Off

God, help me see your heart for work, and for rest, in my life.

READ

Exodus 34:21

REFLECT

After God created the world he rested. It wasnt like he said, Whew! I just cant create another universe today, Im beat! Rather, God includes rest as a part of the work cycle. God designed us with a need to rest, and so he modeled this discipline for us.

You can work really hard, but no matter what is going on, you need a break. Consider the many laws given to the Israelites that emphasized the need to rest: Harvest season or plowing season, free or slave, Israeli or foreignereveryone is commanded to rest. Even when our livelihood depends on the work, God still says, rest. Reject the temptation to feel guilty, but rather understand that resting is an act of obedience to God. Reclaim work by knowing that you were designed to rest.

RESPOND

This week, set aside a day to rest. Or if you cant find a day, start with finding a few hours and work your way up to a day. Consider how you can incorporate this discipline into your life.
