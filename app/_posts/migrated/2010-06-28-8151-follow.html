Week 1: Peter - A Trust Study

Monday, June 28

Follow

Take a moment and pray: God, I invite you to teach me about your character through how you trust me and through the life of Simon Peter as he trusted you.

READ

Mark 1:16-18

REFLECT

Hebrew boys who showed promise in early education were eligible to go on to become rabbis (esteemed Jewish teachers). These boys were hand-selected, and the honor was great. Simon Peter, however, wasnt chosen for this role. He stayed in his familys fishing business. Until Jesus chose him.

Peter could haveand culturally, maybe should haveasked his family if he could follow Jesus (especially because Jesus was a controversial teacher). Instead, he drops his nets. Theres no lengthy discussion of salary or debate of theological views. No, Let me get back to you after I check with my parents or after I consider how following you might impact my image. He doesnt even ask about the dorm life.

Peter just seems to know Gods voice. He trusts. He drops his net: his family net, his safety net, his net income. Then he simply follows.

RESPOND

Picture yourself with your net in hand, coming face-to-face with Jesus. What would your net be? Would you let it go to follow?
