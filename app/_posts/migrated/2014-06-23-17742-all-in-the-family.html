Monday, June 23

All in the Family

Were redefining the words happiness, wealth, family and love. This week, well rediscover how big, small, and present our families can be.

PRAY

Thank God for your family.

READ

Mark 3:31-35, Ephesians 2:19

REFLECT

Somewhere in a leather-bound photo album in moms house is at least one awkward family photo. Matching outfits, a sky-blue background, someone with a frown on their face---this is a typical image of a family. But Jesus says something different about what makes a family. Its more than someone who shares our DNA. Family is made up of people we share our lives with in the good times and the bad.

RESPOND

Who is your family? Make a list of all the people who you love, trust and those who challenge you. Post that list somewhere and make room this week to spend time together with them.
