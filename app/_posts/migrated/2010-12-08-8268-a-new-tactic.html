Wednesday, December 8

A New Tactic


READ

Genesis 3:21-24


REFLECT

Jesus story didnt begin with his human birth in Bethlehem. He existed as God from the beginning of time.

When Adam and Eve chose to separate themselves from God by choosing evil over truth, God banished them from the Garden of Eden so they wouldnt live in a perpetual state of choosing evil. But even amidst this pained separation, God never stopped loving his children. His tactic, however, changed. He would become a human, as Jesus, and live side-by-side with the children he so loved.

RESPOND

Ask God for the love to forgive others past bad choices, and for his forgiveness for any destructive choices you have made.

ANOTHER LOOK

Psalm 42:8-11
