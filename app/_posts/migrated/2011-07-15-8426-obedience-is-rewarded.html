Friday, July 15

Obedience is Rewarded

God, continue to teach me to submit to your will, and grow my hearts desire towards obedience.

READ

Genesis 22:15-18

REFLECT

Our motivation for obedience should always stem from a desire for deeper

relationship with God, but know that obedience also comes with a bonus-a promise of blessing.

God rejoiced over Abraham for his obedience, and he promised to richly bless him and all of his descendants. And Jesus, one of Abrahams descendants, would go on to bless all the nations of the earth, by dying on the cross for our sins. Jesus obedience through laying down his life was rewarded by providing a way for all people to be reconciled to God.

Remember, God just wants relationship with us. He wants us to choose to obey him out of love, and ultimately our faithful obedience is rewarded.

RESPOND

Think of a time God blessed you for your obedience. Take some time to thank Him for his kindness, his blessing, and for keeping his promise.

WEEK TWO DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

The Friday edition of The Daily features discussion questions based on the weeks

content. These questions are meant to spark conversation with your spouse, your

roommate, or a small group of friends who also subscribe to The Daily.

  • Has God ever asked you to do anything difficult or that made you feel uncomfortable? In what ways did you try to bargain or negotiate with God? What held you back from obeying? How did you overcome it?
  • Compare a time when you were obedient to God with a time you rebelled. How was your faith impacted in each situation?
  • None of us are perfect. We all have flaws and shortcomings that can keep us from obeying God. But God sees us as his perfect children and can use all of us for his good. Take some time to encourage one another and point out a strength or a way that you see God using each person.
