This Saturday, November 6, there will be a Fair Trade Market at The National Underground Railroad Freedom Center from 11am-4pm. Admission is free (donations will be accepted). Clothing, handbags, jewelry, toys, coffee, chocolate and other unique gifts will be available for purchase. There will also be interactive displays, a documentary screening and speakers, including two rescued slaves. For more info, visit unboundmovement.org/market.
