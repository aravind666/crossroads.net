Monday, April 28

The New Man

PRAY

Ask God to prepare you for a new way of thinking.

READ

1 Corinthians 16:13-14

REFLECT

Our culture is full of images, movies and YouTube videos that tell us what a man is. They say that drinking a lot, lifting a lot and farting a lot makes a man. But the Bible is full of men who set an example of what God says about manhood and how they should live---men set a vision for their lives and diligently work at it; men nurture and protect their communities; men are team players and stand up to fear.

Over the next few weeks, The Daily will take a deeper look at what the Bible says about manhood.

RESPOND

What do you think a man should look or act like and where did you come up with this image? Spend some time thinking about if these traits are from God or a cultural myth.
