Luke: Prepare the Way

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily will study people from the Bible who changed the game. This week, were studying the book of Luke and taking a look at Jesus, the ultimate game changer.This study is presented in a paraphrase of the Bible called The Message, which gives us biblical text in simple, modern language.

READ

Luke 3:1-14

REFLECT

John the Baptist doesnt beat around the bush when teaching about how to prepare for Jesus arrival. And when people ask, "Then what are we supposed to do?" his answer is straightforward. A literal to-do and to-dont. Look again at his answers and see if anything speaks to you.
