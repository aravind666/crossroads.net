Nehemiah: The Request

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily will study people from the Bible that changed the game. This week, were looking at the story of Nehemiah.

READ

Nehemiah 2:1-8

REFLECT

The king trusted Nehemiah. A cupbearer protected the king from poisoned drinks, so Nehemiah served in a position of great influence. Spend a few minutes today thinking about areas where you have influence on others. What do you do with your influence?
