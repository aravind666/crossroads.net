When David Beats Goliath

Take a moment and ask God to encourage you as you read Davids story.

READ

1 Samuel 17:33-37

REFLECT

Davids confidence in his ability to defeat Goliath was based on his faith in a big God, not on his own abilities.

RESPOND

Consider a time when God showed up in your life in a big way. How can this experience build your faith as you begin the Game Change journey?
