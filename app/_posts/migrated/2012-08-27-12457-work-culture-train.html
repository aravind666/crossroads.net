Thursday, August 30

Work Culture: Train

As we continue to look at the culture of the workplace, today well consider how to train in Gods culture.

READ

Ephesians 4:22-24

REFLECT

Choosing to follow Jesus is a process--a gradual and daily learning to let Gods culture saturate your life. And functioning in Gods ways at work can be hard; weve all been trained by the world. So look for people who seem to function in Gods culture well, and schedule time with them for a different kind of training.

RESPOND

Ask God to give you the name of one person who he wants you to learn from. Contact them.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
