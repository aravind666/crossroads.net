The weekend is near, and so is the end our beans and rice challenge.

 

BRING YOU BEANS & RICE MONEY TO SERVICES THIS WEEKEND

Saturday and Sunday, we’ll be collecting out beans & rice contributions within the first ten minutes of each service and investing in four great organizations outside Crossroads. If you’ve been out of town or avoiding this gassy experiment, all information about the why and where all the cash is going can be found at crossroads.net/beansandriceweek.

 

You can give to the "beans and rice week" fund in one of four ways:

1. Write a check. Put "beans and rice" on the memo line and bring your check to services this weekend.

2. Bring cash. It doesn’t matter if it’s a pile of fives or a crapload of dimes, you can hand it off in the service.

3. Give (early) via computer. Go to crossroads.net/give. Select "One-Time Gift" (you’ll be asked to log in), and select "Beans and Rice."

4. Give via smartphone or tablet. Go to crossroads.net/give and select "Beans and Rice."
