Thursday, March 6

No Pain, No Gain

This week were exploring what we can do when we find ourselves suffering or when we see trouble coming.

PRAY

Ask God to reveal something good in your suffering.

READ

James 1:2-4, Romans 8:28

REFLECT

Accepting suffering as a good thing sounds like a crazy idea, but when we suffer God is doing something in us---something good. James says we should rejoice because when we persevere through our trouble, our faith is stronger. Through pain and hardship, we will be more prepared for what God has in store for us and that is crazy good.

RESPOND

Reflecting on your past or current suffering, what good came out of it? Write it down and put it in a place where you can be reminded of the good God has done for you.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 2 Corinthians 12
