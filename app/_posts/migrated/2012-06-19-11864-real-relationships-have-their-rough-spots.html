Real Relationships Have Their Rough Spots

This week were studying the boldly authentic friendship of Jesus and his disciple Peter (also referred to as Simon and Simon Peter).

READ

Luke 22:31-34, 22:54-62

REFLECT

Even though Jesus knew in advance that his friend would betray him, he still prayed for Peters faith. In fact, Jesus not only trusted that this relational rough spot would be reconciled, but also that Peter would come out of it with the ability to strengthen other believers.

RESPOND

Were all human (in other words, imperfect), so rough spots are bound to come up in our relationships. The question is, what are we going to do when we hit those rough spots? If one of your relationships is struggling, ask God today to show you the path toward reconciliation.
