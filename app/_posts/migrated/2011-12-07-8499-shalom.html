Wednesday, December 7

Shalom

PRAY

Ask God to bring people who encourage your growth into your life.

READ

Romans 8:6

REFLECT

"Shalom" is the Jewish expression of peace, and its much fuller in meaning than our common notion of peace as a state of calm or cease-fire. Rather, shalom means a complete flourishing: a life thats fully engaged in what God created it to be.

If Jesus had merely wanted calm, he would have just helped people get along. He would have satiated them, like Caesar did, with more "bread and circus." He would never have challenged people to change and grow. But instead, He literally turned tables and upset the whole broken system with his love. He didnt avoid turmoil; he moved through it.

RESPOND

Think of how you move through turmoil (who you talk to, where you find relief, how you treat the people close to you). Is there anything about that pattern youd like to change? Ask God to give you specific strength in that area for the next time around.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
