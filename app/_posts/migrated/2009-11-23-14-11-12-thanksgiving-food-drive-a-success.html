--- layout: post title: "Thanksgiving Food Drive a Success" subtitle: "" date: 2009-11-23 14:11:12 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "WhatsUp" --- Thanks to everyone who helped make the Thanksgiving Food Drive a smashing success. Here are just a few highlights:
  • It didn't rain!
  • We met all our commitments to churches in Cincinnati, South Africa and New Orleans.
  • No one was hit by a car in the Annex lot or in Mason.
  • Tons of new givers and servers got engaged for the first time.
  • Every local TV station and the Enquirer picked up the story, and reporters were blown away by the fun everyone was having.
  • Even in these tough economic times, people gave 21% more food than last year.
Amazing.
