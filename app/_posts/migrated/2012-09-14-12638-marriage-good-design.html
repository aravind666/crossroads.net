Marriage: Good Design

During the Knock-Off series, well see what the Bible has to say about the real thing when it comes to marriage, dating and family. This week were learning how marriage is a covenant, not just a contract.

READ

Genesis 2:23-25

REFLECT

This describes Gods vision for marriage since the beginning of time. That vision includes two really good parts (no pun intended): that we would be as one, and that we would not hide ourselves from each other (or experience shame). Marriage is not two equal parties entering into a contract with each other. Its a covenant God makes with one party (man and woman together).

RESPOND

Try journaling for five minutes today about the implications this view of marriage might have on your relationship with God, or with your spouse.
