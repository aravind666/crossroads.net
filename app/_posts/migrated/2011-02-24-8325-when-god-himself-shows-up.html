Thursday, February 24

When God Himself Shows Up

God, please give me a bigger and more intimate picture of you.

READ

Mark 1:1-11

REFLECT

So this guy, John, he was a prophet. He was one of those men from the Old Testament who did miracles, spoke for God, and prophesied about God sending a rescuer. For all of Israels history, guys like John had been the voice of God. Then Jesus comes along, and John says he doesnt even belong in His presence.

Can you imagine the reaction of the crowd? The confusion? Maybe even the feelings of abandonment or distrust? While Johns listeners had been expecting a rescuer from God, few among them expected that rescuer to be God.

RESPOND

John had been telling people to repent and be baptized, which Jesus obviously didnt need to do. Spend a few minutes looking over this passage in Mark again, and ask God to help you understand the reason for Jesus baptism.
