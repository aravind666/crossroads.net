Wednesday, June 12

Goodness: Light

We continue to read through a passage in Ephesians 5 as we look at how God desires to walk with us in our sexuality.

READ

Ephesians 5:8-10

REFLECT

We are not meant to have sex out on fountain square in the daylight, so how do we let God shine light into our sexuality? We invite Him. He isnt just outside on a bright day, he is the creator of light. Ask God to come into every area of your life (even into your bedroom) and shine his light. Gods light dissipates shame and breaks up sin blockages in our heart.

RESPOND

Take time to picture yourself nude with a bright light shining on you. How do you feel? Where does it take your thoughts?
