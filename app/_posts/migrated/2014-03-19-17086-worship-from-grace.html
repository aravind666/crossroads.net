Thursday, March 20

Worship from Grace

What really makes Jesus unique when we line up all religions? His grace. This week, were looking at the grace of Jesus and wrestling with what it means for us.

PRAY

Ask God to show you he is worthy of praise.

READ

Colossians 3:16, 1 Peter 2:9

REFLECT

Think about the best gift youve ever received. What made that gift special probably had nothing to do with its cost or where it came from, but the thought and care someone took to put into that gift just for you. God gives us such a gift in Jesus. He knows exactly what we need and through him we receive grace. A gift as sweet as this means God is worthy of our praise and worship.

RESPOND

Worship and praise God today. It could happen while enjoying your morning coffee, during your afternoon workout or at the dinner table. You can start by listening to this song, How Deep the Fathers Love. You dont have to sing or clap your hands (you can if you want). Just give God your attention.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Ephesians 3
