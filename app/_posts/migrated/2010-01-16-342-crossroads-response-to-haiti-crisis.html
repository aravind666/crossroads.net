On Tuesday night, Haiti was hit with a catastrophic earthquake that rippled through the capital city of Port-Au-Prince and the hillsides of the surrounding area.

We've finalized plans to support relief and rebuilding efforts there with a $120,000 contribution to the Restavek Foundation. Founders Jean-Robert Cadet and Ray & Joan Conn are part of our community, and we believe strongly that we should support them in the way they say it's currently needed most.

We're extending ourselves with this unplanned expense to help with this important work. If you want to go above and beyond in your serving or giving to Crossroads, your generosity enables us to react in community in times of need.

Here are some specific prayer requests from Joan Conn:

  • Pray for health and strength in the days to come
  • Pray for our team members that we have not yet heard from
  • Pray for our children and that God will lead us to them
  • Pray that God will direct us and give us wisdom
  • Pray for a place to stay and headquarter efforts (we have no idea what that looks like at this point)
  • Pray that God will help us find Valerie, Gaelle and Dina, and that they are safe (these are three young girls that have very special places in our hearts)
  • Please pray that we do not have rain for a few days, as that would be devastating
  • Pray for peace and that violence will not erupt
  • Pray for all of Haiti: there is no water, no electricity, no food and no medical supplies, primarily in Port-Au-Prince
