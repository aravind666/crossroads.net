We love having our Oakley building open to the community. But a few times a year, we close the building for scheduled maintenance and do those big clean-up jobs you wouldn't want us doing while you were sitting and enjoying your coffee. So, take note: The building will close from Monday, August 24 through Monday, September 7. All 3500 Open activities are canceled, but weekend and Last Wednesday services will continue as scheduled.
