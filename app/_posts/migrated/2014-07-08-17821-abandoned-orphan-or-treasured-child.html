Wednesday, July 9

Abandoned Orphan or Treasured Child

This week, were examining our identities as treasured children of God.

READ

Romans 8:15

REFLECT

Well keep coming back to this verse over the next few weeks. Its one of the clearest ways that the Bible reveals Gods love for us. And the more we accept that God considers us his beloved children, the easier it is to see the areas of our lives where we act as if this isnt true.

RESPOND

The link below will give you a list that shows the differences between the mindsets of an orphan and a treasured child. Spend a few minutes looking at the list and note any areas where you identify with the abandoned orphan description. Moving from the mindset of an orphan to a treasured child typically isnt an overnight process, so take heart that youre making important steps in the right direction.

Orphan or Child Mindset

PRAY

God, thank you for loving me so generously. Please give me the awareness, patience and wisdom to receive that love.
