Real Relationships Rely On Forgiveness

This week were studying the boldly authentic friendship of Jesus and his disciple Peter.

READ

John 21:15-19

REFLECT

Yesterdays rejection passage isnt the end of the story. Jesus forgave Peter in a beautiful way; he challenged Peter with three chances to refute his previous denials, and then invited Peter back into a newly restored relationship.

RESPOND

All this relationship talk is applicable to our spiritual lives too. Today, commit to spending five minutes connecting with God.
