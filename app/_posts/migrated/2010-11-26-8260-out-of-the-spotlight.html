Friday, November 26

Out of the Spotlight

God, help me to focus more on you.

READ

Romans 8:5-8

REFLECT

Introvert or extrovert, what makes us prone to self-dramatization? What keeps us awake at night, worrying about potential disasters, fretting that God wont provide?

Sometimes we cant get our minds off ourselves and our own dilemmas. But living in the spotlight (especially when were the ones shining it) brings immense pressure. When were bound up in our own drama, we arent drawing near to God. We arent allowing His peace to quiet us, to heal us, to teach us.

Its not easy stepping away from our internal spotlights, but bringing God into the center is what brings us into the peace he offers.

RESPOND

If you feel bound up in your own drama, ask God to reveal to you why youre getting trapped or what you might imagine is the reward for self-dramatization. Then ask God to begin showing you how peace is the bigger reward.

ANOTHER LOOK
