Thursday, December 31
Gift of Purpose

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.


READ
Matthew 28:18-20

REFLECT
What am I here for? What is my life all about?

These are fitting questions to ask at this time of year. Regardless of what stage of life were in, we all need and crave purpose. So it shouldnt surprise us that Jesus last words were about this very thing.

He basically said, You know that stuff youve seen me doing for the past three years, the stuff you saw me sacrifice my life for? Thats your job now. Im going to go with you, but I want you to go out and help people connect with God just like Ive been doing.

Thats purpose.

  • If you are in a relationship with Jesus, it is because someone around you followed Jesus instruction. How did that person live out the purpose Jesus gives?
  • Whats one practical way you could live out this purpose in your daily life, today?

RESPOND
This is the time of year that we traditionally make resolutions for next year (even though we may be unlikely to keep them). Ask God to show you someone to help connect with him, and resolve today that you will pray for them and look for opportunities to live out your purpose. Be open to Gods creativity.
