Tuesday, September 2

Work with Excellence

Were looking at the book of Colossians for wisdom on life and work.

PRAY

Ask God to help you work with excellence.

READ

Colossians 3:22-25

REFLECT

When this book was written, most servants were working to pay off a debt (which might be a little like the workforce even today). Even today, we can look at this passage as a way to approach work. Its a great reminder for Christ-followers to always do work that represents Jesus. Its a good reason to be excellent.

RESPOND

Whether you are in school or work in or out of the home, how could you improve the excellence of your work?
