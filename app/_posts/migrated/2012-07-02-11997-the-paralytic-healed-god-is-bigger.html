Wednesday, July 4

The Paralytic Healed: God Is Bigger

Happy Independence Day! We have plenty of reasons to celebrate our country. We can also celebrate that God is bigger (even bigger than freedom of speech and sweet fireworks displays).

READ

John 5:1-11

REFLECT

The systems around getting into the pool at Bethesda were broken; this paralytic man couldnt get himself into the therapeutic waters, even if theycouldheal him. So Jesus completely bypassed the system. He didnt even bother with the water--Jesus simply spoke and the man was healed. God is simply bigger than than laws of nature, as well as the rules-based religious systems we create.

RESPOND

Today, ask God to show you where you trust anything more than him. This could be anything--a system, a set of rules, or simply a habit youve followed for years.
