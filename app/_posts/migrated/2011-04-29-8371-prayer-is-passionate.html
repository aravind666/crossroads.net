Prayer is Passionate

God, help me identify and tear down the walls I put up around prayer.

READ

Jeremiah 20:7-18

REFLECT

Prayer is passionate. If you look at prayers in the Bible (like Jeremiahs), you wont see many conversations with God that are muttered under the breath or sound limited by rules. When Gods followers talk with him, they are heartbroken, terrified, excited, hopeful, desperate, broken, angry and celebratory. In other words, they are themselves.

When we approach God fully, with all our brokenness and goodness mixed together, we find true conversation and relationship with him. It is during this passionate and authentic communication with God when we are the most known, the most at home.

RESPOND

Look back at the list of barriers you wrote on Monday. Has God given you perspective on any of those areas in recent days? Any new insights or things you hope for?
