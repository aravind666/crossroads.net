If you were thinking about signing up for GO New Orleans, but thought you missed the deadline, think again. About 100 spots are still open, so we've extended the deadline to March 9.

Don't miss this great opportunity to build homes, play with kids, and meet new friends.

Week 1 is July 11-19. Week 2 is July 18-26. You can sign up for one week or both weeks.
Sign-ups will remain open on a first-come first-served basis until Monday, March 9, or until we reach 600 people, whichever comes first. After that, sign-ups are closed and we’re rolling with however many people we’ve got. We’re expecting the spots to fill fast, so sign up soon.
