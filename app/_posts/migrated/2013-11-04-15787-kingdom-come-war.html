Monday, November 4

Kingdom Come: War

Its week 4 of the Kingdom Come journey and this week were fighting for the Kingdom of God.

READ

Ephesians 6:12

REFLECT

Weve seen war. Either on the battlefield or at a distance. Not many of us ever see the enemy of these wars face-to-face, but we know theres one to fight. There is one enemy we see up close and personal everyday. It may not look like a cartoon devil or a masked man, but the enemy constantly has us in the crosshairs.

How can we fight this enemy? Listen to our Dad. Obey our King. Obedience is our best war strategy.

RESPOND

Pin pointone battle in your life. Maybe its the number in your bank account, a relationship or your morning commute. What does it look like to be obedient in this battle? Today, fight this battle and obey your Dad.
