Acts: Living Differently

This week, were studying the book of Acts and learning about game changers in the early Church.

READ

Acts 2:42-47 and 4:32-37

REFLECT

Tucked in between all the stories of miracles and grand events are a few sentences describing how early believers lived. As short as they are, these descriptions give us a glimpse into a game-changing way of living in community.

RESPOND

Whats your reaction to the thought of living in community like the believers in Acts?
