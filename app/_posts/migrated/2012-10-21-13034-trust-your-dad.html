Trust Your Dad

Were on week three ofThe Journey Home.The Daily will continue to buttress what youre doing in your individual materials and in your groups, through visiting the Bible and a few folks who are learning to trust God as well.

READ

Proverbs 3:5-6

REFLECT

How much time do you spend everyday leaning on your own understanding? A good way to know the answer to that question is to ask yourself, "How far am I going into my day before I ask God what he has for me that day?" When you start your day acknowledging your Dad, like thanking him or pointing out where he is at work in your life, you can hear more clearly what he has for you. This week, try starting each day acknowledging your Dad, and then notice if your path doesnt become clearer.
