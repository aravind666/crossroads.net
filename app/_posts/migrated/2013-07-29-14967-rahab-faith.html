Friday, August 2

Rahab: Faith

We finish out our week looking at Rahab. Today, we skip ahead to the New Testament. 1500 years later, Israelites still tell the story of the prostitute with the red cord. We are a part of that lineage now, 3500 years later.

READ

Hebrews 11:30-31

REFLECT

Hebrews 11 is a list of people who acted in faith, and Rahab makes the list. The end of the story is that Jericho fell, but Rahab was saved. No matter what words you might hang over your head (like Rahab and the word prostitute) when we put our faith in God, he makes our story new.

RESPOND

Ask God today what words he wants to hang over you.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • How do you understand the concept of fearing God?

  • Have you experienced Gods lovingkindness?

  • Is there anything that you believe discredits you from Gods purposes?
