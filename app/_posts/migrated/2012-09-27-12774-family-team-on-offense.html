Monday, October 1

Family: Team on Offense

This week we look at Gods vision for the family.

READ

Psalms 127:3-5

REFLECT

How much time do we spend in our culture protecting children from danger? What if we are not meant to be on the defense with our children, but on the offense? Todays passage suggests that children are meant to be on mission with us, part of a bigger story with a purpose richer than potty training, grades, and clubs.

RESPOND

Ask God what bigger story he wants to write for your family. This can be you and your parents, you and your wife and children, or you joining mission with an existing family or a collective of Christ-followers.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
