Gift from a Good Dad: Hannah

Our Dad loves us and wants to give us good gifts. Hannah received a life-changing gift from her Dad. Today, read her story.

READ

Matthew 11:28-29

HANNAHS STORY

I went through a dark period where I had not felt home in over a year. I moved three times and I was desperately searching for a home environment that would nourish my weary soul.

I knew God wanted me to feel at home but I wasnt sure what that looked like until I confided in a friend. She pointed me into the direction of other single women who wanted the same things. I believed in Gods promise as a good father, so I took the risk and moved in with people I had never even met before.

Turns out, it was the best decision I have ever made and a concrete example of how God always follows through on his promises---it just may not look like how we expect it to or in the timeframe that we want. Now, I feel more at home, authentic and free than I ever have.
