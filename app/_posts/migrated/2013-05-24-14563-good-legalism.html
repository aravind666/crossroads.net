Good?: Legalism

We continue looking at Galatians 5 in comparing what happens when we offer sex up to God vs. taking it into our own hands (pun not intended).

READ

Galatians 5:23-24

REFLECT

When we live legalistically, it doesnt simply mean that we follow all of the rules about what not to do. Legalism means putting ourselves in the judges seat--believing that we get to decide what is both good and evil (sort of like a certain tree in Genesis 2). The judgment seat belongs to God alone. Start by handing your full sexual life (mentally and physically) over to God to assess. Next, back up and hand the seat of authority back to God when it comes to judging anyone elses sexual decisions.

RESPOND

Take time to journal today about how God might view your sexuality and sexual history.
