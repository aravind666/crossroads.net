Wednesday, May 4

The House of God

Jesus, help me to hear what you say about prayer, and then help me actually do it.

READ

Mark 11:15-17

REFLECT

Interesting. Jesus describes the Temple (the place where God dwelled with the Israelites) as a house of prayer. He didnt call it a house of religion, a house of perfection, a house of exclusiveness. He called it a house of prayer. The way Jesus described Gods earthly home is one built on prayer.

If God lives in a house of prayer, what does that mean for our own lives? For our own homes?

RESPOND

Prayer is foundational to Gods Kingdom. Its foundational to a believers life. Take the next few minutes and pray for God to strengthen your foundation.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
