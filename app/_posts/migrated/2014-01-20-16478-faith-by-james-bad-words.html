Tuesday, January 21

Faith by James: Bad Words

PRAY

Ask God for an open mind---open to new ideas, thoughts and actions for today.

READ

James 3:7-10, Colossians 3:7-8

REFLECT

Our words have a powerful effect on others, especially when theyre negative. Needless to say, we all have a hard time controlling our tongue. We get angry or jealous and pass judgement, often at a much higher volume than our inside voices. James says when we curse others with our words, we also curse God and thats just not OK.

PRACTICE

Hold your tongue. If you have the chance to say something mean or negative today, dont take it.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Romans 12
