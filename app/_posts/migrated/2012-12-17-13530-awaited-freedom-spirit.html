Awaited Freedom: Spirit

Jesus brought freedom, and left his Spirit to continue bringing it.

READ

2 Corinthians 3:17

REFLECT

If youre still unsure about Gods desire for you to experience freedom in your life through Jesus--and His Spirit--this verse is a heavy hitter.Freedom doesnt necessarily mean that you never work again and lay on the couch and eating Cheetos. Instead, it could mean that you walk with a confidence that doesnt dabble in fear, regret, rejection or stress. If youre interested in that kind of freedom, its available to you. (And it makes other things, like Cheetos, look like kids stuff.)

RESPOND

Today, memorize2 Corinthians 3:17. It can be a great freedom fighter for you.
