The Lords Prayer: We Want the Kingdom

This week, The Daily breaks down the Lords Prayer, line by line.

READ

Matthew 6:10

REFLECT

In the first line of the prayer, we know our relationship with our Dad. Were a member of His family. This gives us every right to talk with Him. When we do and and pursue a relationship with Him, we realize we want what he wants: for his Kingdom to come and for life and joy to overtake our world. So we pray for it--in every nook and cranny of our lives.

RESPOND

Think about a specific area of your life. Maybe its your work, family or a personal struggle. Today, pray for your Dads Kingdom to overtake that place.
