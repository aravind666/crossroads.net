Thursday, February 27

Hard Truth

This week, were exploring how we can let go of our "stuff" and give grace and truth to each other in the midst of hard situations.

PRAY

Ask God to reveal a way you can grow today.

READ

John 1:14, John 8:1-11

REFLECT

We are tested everyday in the same way the Pharisees tested Jesus. But Jesus gave them and us some hard truth to consider: are you without sin? To follow Jesus, we must look at our own wrongdoing and confess it. When we do, we are seeking a pure heart and a new life. And even when we find ourselves ready to give judgment, Jesus gives us an answer to that, too: give grace and forgive.

RESPOND

Today, before you jump in an argument or read a People magazine, consider your own sin. This isnt an exercise to make you feel bad, but be open to let God show you where you can grow.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 2 Corinthians 7
