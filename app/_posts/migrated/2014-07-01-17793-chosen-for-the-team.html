Wednesday, July 2

Chosen for the Team

Were redefining the words happiness, wealth, family and love. All this week were receiving Gods love for us.

PRAY

Thank God for choosing you for his team.

READ

Ephesians 1:4-6, Ephesians 3:18

REFLECT

Sometimes it feels like we never escape gym class, waiting to be chosen for team A or B. But when we receive Gods love, we dont have to wait. He chose us before we were even born. Before we were a twinkle in our mothers eye. Before we even made one mistake, He loved us. God knew us and he chose us to be part of his family. That is love.

RESPOND

Write a love letter to a spouse, one of your kids, a dear friend or relative---consider what you love about them. Then, read the note back to yourself and know that God feels the exact same way about you.
