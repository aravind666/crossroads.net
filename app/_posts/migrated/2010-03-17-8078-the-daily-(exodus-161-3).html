Wednesday, March 17

Blues

Todays edition of The Daily highlights an important point from this past weekends message for you to keep in mind as you work through your guide and meet with your group this week.


READ

Exodus 16:1-3


REFLECT

When we make a break from our old habits and beliefs, it can feel really uncomfortable at first. In fact, it can be downright terrifying to learn new ways of living. But God's desire for us isnt comfort; it's freedom. We must be willing to pursue freedom even when it gets uncomfortable--even when it means venturing out on a limb.


PRAY

Ask God to reveal places where youre resisting change, or falling back into old habits, because you prefer comfort. Pray for the courage and perseverance to endure.
