Acts: Seek God, Not Idols

The Daily continues to follow the book of Acts and how we can apply its lessons to our own lives. And check out a Beans and Rice Week video each day to help you make the most of your meals.

PRAY

Ask God to soften your heart towards him.

READ

Acts 17:24-31

REFLECT

Paul found himself in one of the most religious cities in the world: Athens. The city was filled with statues the Greeks idolized and worshipped---and it was easy because they could see and touch these idols. Pauls words to the Greeks speak to a common struggle we still face today: when we cant see or touch God, we find other things to put in his place. We worship our own idols. But God wants us to seek him. When we do, we will find him.

RESPOND

What idols could be keeping you from seeking God? It could be money, food, work, or a relationship. Think of one thing you idolize. Today, dont make it a priority.
