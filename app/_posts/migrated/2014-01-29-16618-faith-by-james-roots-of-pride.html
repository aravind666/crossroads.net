Tuesday, January 28

Faith by James: Roots of Pride

LISTEN and PRAY

Music is a great way to get focused on God. Heres a tune from Crossroads weekend service if you want to give it a shot.

READ

James 4:7-10, 1 Peter 4:10

REFLECT

Think about your talents or gifts. When youre good at something, it can become a source of pride. In other words, maybe you use that talent as a way to draw extra attention to yourself, cover up an insecurity, or use it to judge others who dont have the same gift. While being humble doesnt mean being ashamed of these gifts and talents, when we dont give God the highest place of value in our lives, we become disconnected from other people and from him.

PRACTICE

Write a list of talents or gifts that you feel God has given you. Then, mark the ones that tend to be a source of pride, the ones you talk a lot about. Today, dont use your gifts for attention. Record what happens.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 1 Corinthians 1
