Building Good Sons and Daughters: Recognize That You Have a Job

You are a son or daughter of God, who wants you to inherit his entire Kingdom. This week we will look into how to live a life as a good son or daughter.

READ

Matthew 21:28

REFLECT

God gives each one of us a job to do. He wants to send us out into the vineyard (the Kingdom) to tend to it and make it grow. Because we are heirs to the Kingdom, God wants to teach us the family business and have us invest in it.

RESPOND

Think of some ways you are currently investing or wanting to invest in the Kingdom. Write those down, then ask God how he sees you helping those grow.
