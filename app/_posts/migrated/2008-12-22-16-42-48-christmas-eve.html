--- layout: post title: "Christmas Eve" subtitle: "" date: 2008-12-22 16:42:48 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Todd" --- As many (or most) of you have heard, we have set times for our Christmas Eve services at Crossroads (Oakley location only). Here are a few answers to common questions:

Are tickets needed?
No. This will be open seating.

What time are the services?
11am, 1pm, 3pm, but arrive a little early to enjoy the atrium Christmas experience including free (of course) family portraits, hot chocolate, goodies and more.

How long will services last?
About an hour. Maybe less. Maybe more.

Is it "kid friendly"?
Oh yeah. Bring the whole family. (Even your weird uncle.) Please note that there won't be childcare at Crossroads that day. (For the record, there won't be "weird uncle" care either, so don't even think about it.)

What will the service be like?
We'll be walking through the Christmas story with music, images and storytelling.

Is it really going to be 58 degrees on Christmas Eve?
Derek said it would be like that, so yeah...I guess it probably will be.
