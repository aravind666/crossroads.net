Tuesday, December 18

Awaited Freedom: Proclaimed

Today, we look at how Jesus proclaimed that he was the foretold freedom.

READ

Luke 4:16-21

REFLECT

If you werent sure whether Jesus fulfilled the prophecy from yesterdays verse (Isaiah 61:1-3), Jesus is handed this exact scroll in the temple to read, and he pronounces that it is now fulfilled. This was bold of Jesus, but he had just returned from fasting and being tempted in the desert. He had spent time with God, and he knew his identity. He began his ministry from this point, healing and freeing people.

RESPOND

Journal about the places in your life where you need freedom.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
