Monday, November 18

Kingdom Come: Changed

With the Kingdom Come journey nearing its end, The Daily will take a deeper look at how to live in the Kingdom of God.

READ

Matthew 5:1-11

REFLECT

In this excerpt from the Sermon on the Mount, Jesus is describing a character who has chosen to seek the Kingdom and as a result, changed. He describes this character through a number of statements like "poor in spirit," "the meek" and "pure in heart." When we choose to seek the Kingdom of God we are this character. While the world might tell us something different, we are renewed and changed.

RESPOND

How has choosing the Kingdom changed you? Spend ten minutes and think about what might be different in your life since the journey began. Maybe you quit something, started something new or renewed a relationship. Write about it on page 104 of your field guide.

Share your Kingdom story

We want everyone to experience amazing stories about God and the Kingdom. If you have a story, share it with us. Email: story@kingdomcomejourney.com.
