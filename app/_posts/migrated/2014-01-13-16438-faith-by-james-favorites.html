Tuesday, January 14

Faith by James: Favorites

PRAY

Ask God for an open mind---open to new ideas, thoughts and actions for today.

READ

James 2:1-7

REFLECT

If our sense of self-worth is found largely in our possessions or social standing, then we will always struggle with the temptation to show favoritism---to treat people differently based on what they can or cant do for us. Today will be full of moments that challenge your social standing. From your 1,200 Facebook friends to the interactions you have at work, you could let these things dictate your importance.

PRACTICE

Go unnoticed today. Dont post that picture to Facebook or be the first one to speak at the meeting. Instead, take a low place and remember that God favors us all.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Romans 7
