I’m excited to tell you that my husband & I are taking a temporary assignment in Mamelodi, South Africa. For the next six months we’ll be helping move the initiative forward in a variety of ways.  We’ll dive into the Charity & Faith community, help at the AIDS hospice, host a few medical trips, capture some “on the ground” stories and enjoy the adventure. 

 

We’ll be sure to keep you posted, so stay tuned by reading this blog or get twitter alerts by following @Andrea_Summer.  I hope to share new music with you & maybe a video of my attempt to drive on the OPPOSITE side of the road.   Though we will miss our community, family & fav Cincy food, Tye & I feel INCREDIBLY BLESSED to go on this adventure.  

 

If you’re interested in going to Mamelodi, there will be several opportunities to do so this year. Info is coming your way this month.  Here’s to adventures!
