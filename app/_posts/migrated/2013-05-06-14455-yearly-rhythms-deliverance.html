Yearly Rhythms: Deliverance

This week, as we see how to develop yearly rhythms, we will look at four holidays that God gave as yearly rhythms.

READ

Leviticus 23:4-5

REFLECT

Passover is a key holiday celebrated in the Old testament. It symbolized the deliverance of Gods people from slavery in Egypt and from death. The blood of a lamb was smeared on the doorway to signify that Gods people were inside and should not be harmed by the Lords wrath against Egypt.

1,500 years later, Jesus died and resurrected during the week of passover to symbolize that he is the lamb that has come to save us from slavery and death. If you celebrate Good Friday and

Resurrection Sunday (Easter), you are still celebrating Gods deliverance.

RESPOND

Take a moment to journal about how God has delivered you and about how you could celebrate his deliverance.
