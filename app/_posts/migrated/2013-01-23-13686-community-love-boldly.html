Thursday, January 24

Community: Love Boldly

We continue looking at community through Romans 12.

READ

Romans 12:9-13

REFLECT

We all want to be loved. The best way to experience love (and a more loving community) is to begin giving it away. These verses keep us from believing love is a just a feeling; its about action. When it comes to community, there are clear ways that we can engage in the practice of love. Sometimes we feel inspired to function in these things like spiritual fervor or joy, and then we do them. Sometimes we dont feel like it, and when we engage in the practice of love, the experience of love follows.

RESPOND

Look at the list of things suggested in Romans 12:10-13. Which one do you feel prompted to grow in? Practice it today. Yes, you can even practice joy.
