Wednesday, February 26

Grace with Open Arms

This week, were exploring how we can let go of our "stuff" and give grace and truth to each other in the midst of hard situations.

PRAY

Ask God to help you share his grace with someone else.

READ

John 1:14, Luke 19:1-10

REFLECT

Youve probably heard the old jingle about Zacchaeus, but it leaves out the part that says he was a thief and he knew it. Even so, he wanted to see Jesus and went to great heights (literally) to make it happen. Jesus had come to save the lost, just like Zac. While the others grumbled, Jesus gave him grace with open arms, hung out at his house and even shared a meal with him. Thats what love looks like.

Watch "The Ballad of Zacchaeus" from Kids Club to see how Jesus gave him grace (and for the catchy tune). This one is great for kids, too.

RESPOND

Who is someone you should welcome with open arms? Theres a reason that person comes to mind. Today, take a step toward making that person feel loved.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 2 Corinthians 6
