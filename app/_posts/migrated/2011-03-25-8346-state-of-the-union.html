Friday, March 25

State of the Union

God, please give me a good ear for hearing your voice today as I read about your character.

READ

Isaiah 40:1-26

REFLECT

Weve all been inspired by finely-wrought speeches, gotten wrapped up in presidential promises, or felt motivated by the latest talk radio or Ted Talk. But no leader has ever said words as audacious and brilliant as what you just read in Isaiah 40. No leader has ever spoken that kind of galvanizing truth.

This is the God were invited to follow. This is the God who both gathers the lambs [us] in his arms and in the same breath, reduces the leaders of the world to nothing.

Who to better lead us?

RESPOND

From Isaiah 40:1-26 (or read throughout Isaiah), what characteristics of God are you longing to experience? Is it His force, his compassion, his creativity? Tell God what you want to know of him, and he will show you.
