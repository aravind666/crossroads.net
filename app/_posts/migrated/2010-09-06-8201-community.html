Week 2: The Early Church

Monday, September 6

Community

Take a moment to repent of trying to do things on your ownand being

disconnected from God.

READ

Acts 2:44

REFLECT

This isnt your big brothers frat party. If youre thinking that real

community only involves a dorm, neighborhood or kids school, think again.

Upon reading the phrase in common, you might assume that it refers two or

more people who have the same interests. But you dont all have to love

sushi to have everything in common. Common just means belonging equally to

a community. It means sharing your life (and maybe even your stuff) with one

another. Its rejoicing and grieving when someone else is.

Community is the family that isnt dysfunctional. Well, some people may put

the fun in dysfunction, but at the heart of the community of

Christ-followers is the One who never gets it wrong.

RESPOND

We all need other people to grow, to survive. Meet someone for coffee this

week. Or call a friend to see how their days going. Share your life.
