Family: Training Ground

Today, we look at our family as a training ground for the family mission.

READ

Deuteronomy 6:5-7

REFLECT

Teaching children about God was never meant to be relegated to 60 minutes on the weekend. Your house has so many opportunities for an ongoing dialogue about God. Every moment in your house can be a chance to start God-based conversations.

RESPOND

Today, include God in a conversation with your family.
