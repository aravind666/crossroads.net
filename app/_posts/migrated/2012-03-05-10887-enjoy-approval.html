Enjoy Approval

This week, were wrapping up our study on what it means to live as treasured children of God.

READ

Luke 2:52

REFLECT

Knowing that God is our source of approval doesnt mean we cant be well-liked or enjoy the approval of others. As it says in Luke, "Jesus...increased in favor with God and man." But a good litmus test for how much we count on the approval of others is how crushed we feel when were not at the top of everyones list.

RESPOND

It can be hard to admit (and even recognize) when were going overboard for someones approval. So start with this: ask yourself whose approval you seekmostright now. Then the next time youre about to see or talk to that person, pause, and thank God for cherishing you.
