Place at the Table: Nancy

Nancy is married, with a teenage son and daughter, and has been a part of Crossroads since the beginning. She also has a contagious laugh.(If you missed this weekends message,click herefor a video summary.)

MEET NANCY

"When I spend time with God, my Dad, I can completely relax because he knows me. I do not have to pretend with Him in any way, he knows it all. It feels good to be known by the one who really, really loves me. We often worry if people really knew us they might reject us; not so with God. Nothing can separate me from my Dads love. God made me so he could love me. Thats a beautiful reason to be created."
