Thursday, October 14

We Have Never Seen Anything Like This

Take a moment to thank God for the amazing ways he has shown up in your life.

READ

Mark 2:6-12

REFLECT

When people encounter Jesus amazing things happen. And yet even though Jesus made a lame man walk again, the greater miracle in this story is that the man went away forgiven and made right with God. The greatest miracle God does is transform hearts.

RESPOND

Thank God for the many transformed hearts in our Crossroads community. Ask Him to continue changing yours.
