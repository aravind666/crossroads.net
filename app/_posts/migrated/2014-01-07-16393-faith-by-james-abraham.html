Wednesday, January 8

Faith by James: Abraham

PRAY

Ask God to increase your understanding of how he wants to move in your life, today.

READ

James 2:21-24; Genesis 22:9-12

REFLECT

His faith was made complete, James says, when Abraham followed through. His words---the things he said to his friends, to God, even to himself---those words became real and actionable. Instead of just saying, I believe in God, he made an incredible move: he got his son ready to be sacrificed. He didnt withhold the best part of his life; he was willing to give God everything. In his willingness, his faith was made complete.

PRACTICE

Today, let your yes be yes and your no be a true no. The things you promise to do? Do them. Said you wont? Then dont. Practice matching your words with your actions.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
