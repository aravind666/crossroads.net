Tuesday, November 17
Praying for Power

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.

READ
Ephesians 1:15-23

REFLECT
In these verses Paul describes his prayer for everyone who puts their faith in Jesus. It's focused on a request for power. Power to be wise, to understand God's story (revelation), and to live a new life through Jesus.

According to verses 19 and 20, the same power that conquered death and resurrected Jesus is available to us here and now, not just someday in heaven.

How do we get access to this power? Reread verse 17.

  • How have you seen your life or others' lives change as a result of seeking, believing and following Jesus?
  • What barriers keep you from receiving more of this power?

RESPOND
Admit freely any barriers to seeking God that you experience (if you have a journal, go ahead and write them down). Ask God for more of a desire for him and the power he offers.
