Wednesday, November 14

Remain at Home: Kyle

Kyle is single, lives in Mariemont, and serves in the Crux Community Group.

(If you missed this weekends message, watch a video summaryhere.)

MEET KYLE

"I have never felt more like a son of God than on a recent Go trip, so getting baptized there made sense. I wasnt sure that I wanted to at first, but then our trip leader read Acts 22, and the last line in verse 16 says, And now what are you waiting for? Get up, be baptized and wash your sins away, calling on his name. Ive been following Christ for a while and I suddenly thought, what am I waiting for? Coming up out of the water, I felt like a new person with a new life and a new Father."

REFLECT

Baptism is one way to receive God as your Father, like a marking the moment of adoption or coming home.

Acts 22:16that Kyle mentions is the moment when Paul in the Bible was baptized.
