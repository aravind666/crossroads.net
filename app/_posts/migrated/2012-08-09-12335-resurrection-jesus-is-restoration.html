Resurrection: Jesus is Restoration

We continue this week to look at Jesus resurrecting Lazarus.

READ

John 11:17-27

REFLECT

Believing and following Jesus means believing in resurrection. Not because he raised Lazarus from the dead (spoiler alert), not because Jesus was resurrected (majorspoiler alert!), but because resurrection lies at the core of his identity. He resurrected because heisthe resurrection.
