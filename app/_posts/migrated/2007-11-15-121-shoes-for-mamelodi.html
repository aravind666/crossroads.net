CSM Jr. and Sr. High hosted TOMS shoes parties last weekend with painting, food and general teenage antics. For each pair of shoes the students bought, TOMS shoes donated a pair of shoes to a student in Mamelodi. In all, the CSM students bought 200 pairs!
