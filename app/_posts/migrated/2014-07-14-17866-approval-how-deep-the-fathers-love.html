Tuesday, July 22

Approval: How Deep The Fathers Love

This week, were spending time receiving Gods approval, as well as identifying areas of our lives where we unnecessarily seek the approval of others.

PRAY

God, help me to receive your love today.

LISTEN

How Deep The Fathers Love

READ

1 John 4:19

Romans 5:8

John 1:12-13

REFLECT

God aggressively pursues us with his love and approval; all we have to do is simply receive.

RESPOND

Think of one way you seek approval from others. Whenever that feeling arises today, remind yourself that God already loves and approves of you.
