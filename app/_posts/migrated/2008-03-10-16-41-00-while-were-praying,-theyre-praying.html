My husband is in Mamelodi with Beaded Hope. I talked to him this morning, and he told me he attended two Charity and Faith services yesterday. I just wanted to let you know that their community is still fervently praying for us to be freed from the bonds of materialism. They spent a good deal of each service praying over our pictures.

So, as you go through the Prayer Experience this week, know that your friends in Mamelodi are praying for you even as you pray.
