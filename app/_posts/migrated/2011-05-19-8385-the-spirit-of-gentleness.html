Thursday, May 19
The Spirit of Gentleness

Before reading the scripture today, spend a minute just being quiet and preparing to hear Gods voice.


READ

Matthew 18:1-4


REFLECT

The world often confuses "gentle" with "weak" or "timid." Yet, Spirit-led gentleness actually builds strength and resolve. The Spirit softens our hearts and stiff-necked rebellion to prepare the way for Jesus to reveal his image through us. We become pliable, teachable and submissive to our Creator.

A gentle person will still feel wrongs done against them, but gentleness doesnt allow them to inspire thoughts of hate or vengeance.Gentleness is a strong defense against self-righteousness, intolerance and critical judgment of others.


RESPOND

Ask the Holy Spirit to show you where you can be more pliable and less rebellious.


READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
