Wednesday, October 27

No Place For Worry

Take a moment to consider how God provides for you.

READ

Matthew 6:25-27

REFLECT

What worries you the most about surrendering your stuff? Are you looking to the things in your life to be your provider, or to God?

RESPOND

Ask God to reveal to you the ways in which he provides for you, and to help you surrender the areas where you feel the compulsion to be your own provider.
