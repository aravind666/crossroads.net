Friday, November 29

The Lords Prayer: He Guides and Protects

Today, The Daily will look at the final line of the Lords Prayer.

READ

Matthew 6:13

REFLECT

Up until now the prayer has been specific to our relationship with our Dad. Now, the prayer changes and we are praying for His strength and guidance as we go out into the world with his message of love and forgiveness. But the kingdom of the world is led by an enemy who will do anything to rob us of our joy and love. In the end of the prayer, Jesus tells us to pray that our Dad keep us safe as we work to spread his Kingdom.

RESPOND

Today, pray for your Dads guidance in a specific area--spiritual, physical, financial, etc. Then, pray for His safe-keeping as you walk in his guidance.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
