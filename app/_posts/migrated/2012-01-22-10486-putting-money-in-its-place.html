Monday, January 23

Putting Money in its Place

This week, well be looking at how to train money so that it serves us (not the other way around). This begins by aligning our priorities with Gods.

READ

Matthew 6:19-21

REFLECT

Spend a few minutes thinking about what this passage says about who God is. According to this passage, why might God want you to spend less time thinking about "treasures on earth?"

RESPOND

Heres a challenge for you: any time you think about money this week, ask God to help you put him first, and put money in its place. Feel free to use the prayer below or come up with your own.

God, help me to focus on you this week. Please give me a clear mind to receive your wisdom, and an open heart to receive your love. Help me to put You at the center of my week, not the stresses and concerns of money. Amen.
