Good Beginnings: Blessed

This week The Daily is going back to the beginning. Weve just started a new weekend series called Good Sex, and were going to look at what God created for us as man and woman in the beginning.

READ

Genesis 1:27-28

REFLECT

When God made mankind, he didnt make a nation, he made two people. He made one garden and two people and then blessed them to join with Him in the rest of the creating. What a blessing of trust and relationship he designed for us. He first made us in his image, and then he started it all with a blessing. He blessed us to have sex and to reign. What a good (and fun) God to create sex and give us authority.

RESPOND

Sex in our culture frequently feels connected to brokenness. Thats our fault, not Gods. Take a moment right now and clear your mind, then reread the verse for today as a blessing being given to you.
