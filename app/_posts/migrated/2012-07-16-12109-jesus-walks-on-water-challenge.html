Tuesday, July 17

Jesus Walks on Water: Challenge

Today, we look at the disciples facing a challenge.

READ

John 6:16-18

REFLECT

The disciples have seen Jesus miracles. At this moment in the story, though, it says "Jesus hadnot yetjoined them." They dont see Jesus, but they do see the wind and rough waters. It is naturally easier to zone in on what is in front of you, but how much more of a challenge is it to put the focus on "not yet?"

RESPOND

In what area of your life are you focusing on the chaos that you can see? There are opportunities to grow our faith in God in the "not yet" areas of our lives. Ask God to give you faith in the waiting.
