Monday, November 19

Give Thanks: Spot God

This week in the Daily well look at the power of being thankful.

READ

Psalm 42;6-8

REFLECT

When we are hurting, empty or just a little lost, choosing to give thanks is a discipline. It is the pathway to rediscovering joy. When we give thanks in the middle of the chaos, we can begin to see Gods rhythms again, his grace, and his closeness.

RESPOND

Look through rose colored glasses today. Set aside your cynicism, and trade it in for wonder today. Look out a window, go for a walk, or be present with someone and notice God. Journal about it.
