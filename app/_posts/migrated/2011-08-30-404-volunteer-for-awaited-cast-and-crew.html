Awaited: A Christmas Show is an original production that retells the familiar Christmas story in a new and immersive way. We need hundreds of volunteers to produce this show that attracts more than 30,000 people from our city.

Roles include singers, dancers, costume sewers and stagehands. Go tocrossroads.net/awaitedvolunteersfor more information and to sign up for the Awaited team.
