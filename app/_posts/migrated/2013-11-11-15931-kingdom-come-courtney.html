Kingdom Come: Courtney

This weekend, Courtney shared her story of seeking the Kingdom in her home.

CLICK HERE TO WATCH COURTNEYS STORY

REFLECT

The world would tell Courtney a lot of things about where and how to live, but she lives with both feet firmly planted in Gods Kingdom. John 14:23 says that when we move our perspective from this worlds kingdom (love God) and seek the Kingdom of God (obey), we are sure to experience his love and comfort. Courtney obeyed her Dad, followed his commands and found a home with him.
