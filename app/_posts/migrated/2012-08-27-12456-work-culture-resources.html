Work Culture: Resources

As we continue to look at the culture of the workplace, today well consider where were putting our resources to work.

READ

2 Corinthians 10:3-5

REFLECT

God has given each of us a variety of gifts, strengths and tools to advance his culture. Take a moment to consider if youre using your resources (those gifts, strengths and tools) to promote Gods culture or, even unintentionally, the worlds.

RESPOND

How can you stay in your current job and promote Gods culture there?
