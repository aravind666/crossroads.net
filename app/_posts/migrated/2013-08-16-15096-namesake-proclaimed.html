Wednesday, August 21

Namesake: Proclaimed

We continue to look at how God does things just to bring attention to himself. Its like God thinks its all about him.

READ

Exodus 9:16

REFLECT

So, wait, God wants to raise me up? He wants to show me his power? Why? The answer is so that Gods name will be proclaimed in all the earth. With the way that God continues to bring up his namesake and his name being proclaimed, this seems important. Do you want God to increase your name, your status, your finances, your influence? Or do you hope to use whatever you already have to honor Him?

RESPOND

Journal today about what you already have that you could use as a platform to make Gods name great.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Romans 12
