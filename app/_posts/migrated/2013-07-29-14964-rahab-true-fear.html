Tuesday, July 30

Rahab: True Fear

We will look at Rahabs character today through the lens of what she fears.

READ

Joshua 2:8-11

REFLECT

Fear can be unhealthy if we place it anywhere that doesnt deserve that level of reverence. On the other hand, a dose of fear around things like fire or deep water can be life preserving. Rahab has heard stories of what God has done for Israel, and she has a healthy fear. She seems to understand the holiness of the God of the Israelites.

RESPOND

Do you fear God? Journal about the ways that it would be healthy for you to have a deep reverence of God and his identity, capacity and abilities.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
