When Freedom Comes

This week, were spending time receiving Gods approval, as well as identifying areas of our lives where we unnecessarily seek the approval of others.

When Freedom Comes

READ

REFLECT

Gods love for us is more than a spiritual stamp of approval. He generously shows his approval again and again with gifts like freedom, peace and love.

RESPOND

Like weve done all week, identify a moment today when you realize youre seeking the approval of others. Try replacing that need for approval with the knowledge that God generously and actively approves of you.If you missed this past weekends message or want to share it with friends,heres a link to a short summary video.
