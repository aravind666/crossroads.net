Monday, June 2

The New Man is a Trailblazer

This week, The Daily will consider what it means for the New Man to have a vision.

PRAY

Ask God to give you a vision.

READ

Habakkuk 2:2-3

REFLECT

Some of the worlds most successful organizations have a vision: a vision to sell products, a vision to help those in need, or a vision to positively impact a world we dont yet know. For most of these organizations, setting a vision helps remind them where they are going. Its no different for the New Man. Hes got a vision and runs after it. Its not an easy thing to do---there are definitely bumps in the road---but to have a vision means to blaze a trail to a place you havent been before.

RESPOND

What do you want your vision to be? It can be specific to your work and family, or maybe its a specific theme of The New Man series. Write it down and put it in a place where you will see it.
