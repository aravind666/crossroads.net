Marriage: Feast

Today, well look deeper at Gods covenant in marriage, and with his people.

READ

1 Corinthians 11:23-25

RESPOND

Choose a meal today (anything from Arbys to Mortons to a PB&J sandwich). While youre eating, think of that meal as a feast to recommit to God. Married? Do this with your spouse. And share those curly fries.

  • Whats your reaction to thinking of yourself as the bride of Christ?
