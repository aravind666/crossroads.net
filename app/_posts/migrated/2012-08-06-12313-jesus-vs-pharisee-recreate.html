Tuesday, August 7

Jesus vs. Pharisee: Recreate

This week we will continue to look at Jesus healing the blind man, as he also seems to be sending freedom messages to the rule-following Pharisees.

READ

John 9:6-14

REFLECT

The Pharisees had scribed many rules about the Sabbath. So many that no common man could know them, let alone keep them. One law: no one can make mud on the Sabbath (this was written to keep potters and brick makers from laboring). Jesus isnt making mud for profit, though, he is making it to do his Fathers work. Remember, in the beginning God made humankind from "the dust of the ground". Jesus uses the same dust for re-creating this mans eyes, and recreating is good Sabbath work.

RESPOND

Plan to Sabbath. What would be re-creating or recreational for you? Make a plan to do it.
