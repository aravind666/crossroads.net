Relationships: Be Humble

This week in The Daily we continue the Knock-Off series by looking at the real thing in dating. In relationships we can spend a lot of energy trying to find the right someone. Lets look this week at how to be a good partner, putting the focus on forming good character in ourselves.

READ

Philippians 2:3-8

REFLECT

Its exciting to look at someone we love and imagine that we would die for them (insert picture of heroic opportunity to push them out of the way and stand in front of a train). The bigger question is: are you willing to lay down your "right to be right" on a daily basis?

RESPOND

Look for one opportunity to grow in humility today by choosing to overlook someone elses faults, give grace.
