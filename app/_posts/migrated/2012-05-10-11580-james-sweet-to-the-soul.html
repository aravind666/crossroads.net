James: Sweet to the Soul

As we continue to study the book of James, were focusing on the power of our tongues.

READ

Gracious words are a honeycomb, sweet to the soul and healing to the bones.

Proverbs 16:24(TNIV)

RESPOND

Todays passage complementsJames wordson the power of our tongues. Think of someone you know whos suffering or sick. Ask God to help you write or speak words of sweetness and healing for that person. God will use and guide your words--so dont worry about perfection.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Who has spoken words of healing or significant encouragement to you? Share that experience so others get a glimpse of the tongues power.
  • James gets pretty fierce about the tongue. Talk about how it was to study the tongues power this week--what you learned, what you skimmed over, what you tried.
