Tuesday, April 2

We continue this week to look at how we can receive what Christ did on the cross through the posture that Jesus called blessed.

READ

Matthew 5:4-5

REFLECT

If you went through the exercise yesterday of contemplating the poverty of your spirit, it could easily cause you to mourn and be meek. When we take note of Gods holiness and his vision for our lives and the world and then note the reality, there is definitely case for mourning. How often do we look to comfort ourselves with all that the world has to offer: food, drink, entertainment, and consumerism? What if instead we let ourselves feel the pain we are masking? If so, Gods comfort awaits you, and all that you are taking on your own terms, you might be poised to inherit.

RESPOND

Sit in contemplation again today. You were meant to be connected with God, and yet we feel the separation, let yourself long for connection with God and mourn.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
