As part of the House Work series, we are answering questions around the kitchen table. Discuss this weeks questions with your spouse, your family, your roommate, or other people who are in close community with you.

  1. List each team you are a part of. Describe your unique role and the authority structures on those teams.
  2. Do you bring life to those you are in submission to? How can you do this more?
  3. How can you bring life to those who are in submission to you?
  4. Does the authority structure in your household need to change in any way in order to better align your team with its dreams/goals?

Finally, gather up your financial records (bills, credit card and bank statements, etc.) and have your calendar handy. You will need them to complete next weeks assignment.
