This weekend during services, Brian Tome sharedthe news that Crossroads and Charity & Faith Mission Church have jointly decided to end their organizational partnership, including our GO Mamelodi trips. Despite this development, Crossroads will continue to pursue the medical component of the Game Change Campaign with other partners. ThisFAQprovides details about our decision to end the partnership.If you have more questions, visit theGO Mamelodipagefor information on theSouth Africa Update Meeting taking place on Wednesday, March 2 at 7pm in the Oakley Auditorium.
