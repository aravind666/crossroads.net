Friday, October 25

Kingdom Come: Kids

READ

Luke 18:15-17, John 1:12

REFLECT

Kids are who they are. They might paint a pretty picture that makes us smile or say the darndest things, but nothing they DO changes how weas a parent, teacher or their fun unclefeel about them. When they know we love them, they live from a place of love.

Have you ever thought thats how God loves YOU? Nothing you DO changes how He loves you. He doesnt NEED you. God WANTS you. When you receive His love without effort, like a kid, the Kingdom of God is yours.

RESPOND

Spend a few minutes and listen (or re-listen) to Receive Gods Love. On page 43 of your field guide, write down what God might be saying to you about his love.

To really receive Gods love as a kid, listen to this short Love video for kids. This has Family Night written all over it.
