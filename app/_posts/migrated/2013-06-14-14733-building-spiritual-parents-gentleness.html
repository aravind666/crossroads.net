Friday, June 21

Building Spiritual Parents: Gentleness

We finish out our week on spiritual parenting by focusing on verse 21.

READ

1 Corinthians 4:14-21

REFLECT

Good spiritual parents love gentleness. Correct others the way you would want to be corrected, with implied trust and a spirit of gentleness. Being disciplined by the Lord through gentle, spiritual parents is good. Ask God for more of it, and then be ready to listen and act.

RESPOND

Pray: God, I want to be matured by you. Give me more discipline through people who are gentle and let me listen and act. Mature me into leading others through being led well. Amen.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Have you ever experienced spiritual parenting?

  • Can you see the benefit of God maturing us in a family structure?

  • Do you have people who speak into your life in a place where you can be disciplined?
