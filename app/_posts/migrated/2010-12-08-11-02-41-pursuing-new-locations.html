Because of the incredible generosity our community has shown through Game Change commitments of over $47 million over the next three years, we’re pressing the go button on all four campaign initiatives, which means we’re actively pursuing strategic locations for Crossroads’ next sites.

 

The commercial real estate market is complicated, and so we won’t be able to announce which location is next until we’ve already inked the deal. But rest assured that we’re investigating many opportunities for a new location in Greater Cincinnati. As soon as we’re able to reveal the location of the next site, we will do so!
