Taking Pointers From a Liar


Take a moment and ask God to reveal truth and meaning to you through Jesus stories.


READ

Luke 16:1-9



REFLECT

This is definitely not the kind of character study we generally see from Jesus. In this story, He tells about this lying little conniver, and just when we would expect him to give some principle about the need for honesty and integrity, he throws in a completely different twist.

Jesus says that our world would commend a guy like this. He was willing to bargain, willing to make use of the position and resources in front of him to make friends and create a favorable situation for himself. Rather than worrying or trying to hoard, he used what he had to make the best of a bad situation.

It is that exact lesson that Jesus poses to usrather than worry or hoard, the resources in our control should be invested into others.

  • In what ways can you use the resources you have to create favorable situations for God to work through you?

RESPOND

God makes it clear throughout the Bible that everything we own is actually his. In shortwere all just renting. Spend a few minutes today asking God how you can invest the things hes given you into the lives of others.
