Wednesday, October 17

Know Your Dad: Scott

Today we hear from Scott. Scott is married with two daughters, one teenage and one elementary age. Those who know him best would call him wise and humble.

MEET SCOTT

"I am really learning that my life only has purpose and fruit in exact proportion with how much I rest in God. If I make my home in Him, and really take time to abide with him, the stuff I do with my time actually has so much more good that comes of it. There is clarity and understanding that only God, my Father, can give when I spend time with him."
