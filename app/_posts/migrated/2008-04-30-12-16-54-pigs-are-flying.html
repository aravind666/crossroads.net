--- layout: post title: "Pigs are Flying" subtitle: "" date: 2008-04-30 12:16:54 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Todd" --- This weekend every year we experience something akin to traffic armageddon in Cincinnati. It's the weekend in which police go around randomly closing streets and forcing cars like mice into mazes. For most, it's a losing game. But for the prepared, it's simply not a problem.

Before leaving the house this Sunday, make sure you've checked the Flying Pig Marathon Route to see which of your local streets will be closed. Trust me... you definitely do not want to miss the service this weekend...
