In Week Three of Reset we're looking at how Jesus Reset Surrender and learning about ways in which Jesus went against the grain of his culture. To follow Jesus today often means to swim against the flow of pop culture and to make changes that - to many - seem crazy. This is not "moralism" or "dogma" - Jesus did not come to make sure we have good morals - it is based upon a deep, abiding relationship with God that stems from God's very character and nature. The more we align ourselves with Him the more we experience life as it was intended to be.
