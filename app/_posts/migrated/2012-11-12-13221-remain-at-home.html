Friday, November 16

Remain at Home

The journey is coming to an end, but remaining at home in God, our dad, is a lifelong experience. Let the verse today refresh you.(If you missed this weekends message, watch a video summaryhere.)

READ

Romans 8:15-17

REFLECT

How often do we tend graves(do things that drain the life out of us and arent our burdens to carry)when God wants to hear us say, "Whats next, Papa?" Today, catch yourself when you are tending graves, and just say, "Whats next, Papa?" He has an adventurous, purposeful life for you. Just ask.
