Tuesday, December 29
Gift of Peace

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.


READ
John 14:27

REFLECT
What does it mean to have peace? For many of us, peace gets defined as the absence of conflict. If were not at war with someone, then we must have peace, right?

Yet God sees it as so much more.

Throughout the Bible, the peace of God is a way of referring to the wholeness, health, and vitality that comes from being in the presence of God himself. When we are in relationship with God, and as we get closer to him, our lives wont be conflict-free, but his presence gives us the kind of full life that we could never have on our own.

It is the peace of having God with us. We are never stranded, never left without His power.

  • It seems odd, but having the peace of God doesnt keep us from experiencing conflict. How do you deal with conflict that you encounter?
  • In what areas of your life do you need wholeness and health from God?


RESPOND
Ask God to help you experience the peace that comes with his presence in your life.
