Tuesday, October 9

The Promise of Home

Today, you get to meet Austin. Austin is 26. He lives in Oakley, works in Clifton, and can tell a great story or two.

MEET AUSTIN

"I can be rebellious. At times in my life when I ran the furthest from God, I never stopped hearing an innate calling. It seemed that no matter how out of step I would try to make myself with God, I was never too far gone, never beyond his reach. I think I thought at times in my life that there must be something better out there, or that I could do it better myself. I keep finding that what I have in God--nothings better."

REFLECT

When Austin came to understand that God didnt want to restrict him, but to fight for his freedom, he had a big shift around understandingJohn 10:9.
