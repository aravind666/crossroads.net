Friday, July 16

Changes

Take a moment to focus on God as your Father.

READ

1 Corinthians 1:26-31

REFLECT

Many people see the first half of the Lords Prayer as asking God to go about his business, off somewhere else, through someone else. But the way God goes about his business is through us. Through you. Were not praying to some distant God doing distant things. We are praying that our Fathers love, kingdom and will would be known in us and through us.

The religious elite rejected the followers of Jesus as not good enough. His disciples were petty, self-centered and concerned with personal power and position. But God still chose them. They were ordinary, imperfect and unprepared, but God knew their potential and promise. He knew he could work through them.

This is the transformative potential of this prayer: that God affects change in us. And the missional potential of this prayer: that God affects change through us.

RESPOND

Ask God to bring his kingdom and will to you and to affect his changes through you. If any specific ideas or promptings come to mind, jot them down.

ANOTHER LOOK
