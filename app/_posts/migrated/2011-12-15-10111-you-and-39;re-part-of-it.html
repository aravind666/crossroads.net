Friday, December 16

Youre Part of It

PRAY

Ask God to help you be a person who includes others and gives freely.

READ

Luke 3:4-6

REFLECT

When Jesus gets ahold of our lives--when we know him as a person verses an idea--we get the opportunity to bring others into the same knowingness. But we dont have to be subject matter experts or brilliant spokesmen. We can just pass on our stories. And in this way, we get to be part of the Christmas invitation.

RESPOND

Think of a Christmas carol you know (preferably one about Jesus, not Rudolph). At some point today, sing it out loud--even if its just the chorus. Or if you want to really have fun, get some friends together, do some spontaneous caroling and love thy neighborhood.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 1 John 3
