Wednesday, August 24

Summer: Turning Rocks

God, may you till my soul. Help me understand and surrender to the season I am in.

READ

Acts 3:19

REFLECT

Rocks require turning up the soil and removing them. And turning is exactly what the word "repent" means. Turning the soil over is good work and repenting isnt just a crazy statement on a homeless mans sign by the road. Repenting is good work.

The trouble is, unlike weeds, our rocks are often beneath the surface--like the rocks of unforgiveness, resentment or self-pity. As we set about serving or working, we can still be tempted to return the focus to ourselves and away from God.

We need rain and sun. We cant grow alone; lets surrender our growth over to God today through the beauty of turning the soil of our soul in repentance.

RESPOND

Ask God to show you the rocks beneath your soil and repent of them. Say aloud, "I repent for _____."

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
