Nehemiah: Rebuilding Relationships

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily will study people from the Bible that changed the game. This week, were looking at the story of Nehemiah.

READ

Nehemiah 5:1-13

REFLECT

Todays passage is a compelling detour in Nehemiahs story. In the midst of rebuilding Jerusalems wall, he stopped to help rebuild relationships between its people.

RESPOND

Is there a relationship in your life that could use rebuilding? If anyone comes to mind, take the time today to reach out to that person and begin mending that relationship.
