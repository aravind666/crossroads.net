Thursday, July 3

Seek Love

Were redefining the words happiness, wealth, family and love. All this week were receiving Gods love for us.

PRAY

Ask God to tell you something new about his love.

READ

Zephaniah 3:17, Psalm 9:10

LISTEN

There are a lot of hymns and songs written about Gods love for us. During this series, weve taken a handful of those tunes and given them a new sound so we might experience His love in a new way. Today, listen or sing along to this update of Be Thou My Vision and seek Gods love for you.

Be Thou My Vision
