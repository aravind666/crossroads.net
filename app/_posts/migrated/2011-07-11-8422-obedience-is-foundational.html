Week Two: Obedience

Monday, July 11
Obedience is Foundational

God, help me to make you the foundation of my life.

READ
Matthew 7:21-27

REFLECT

God is not interested in how "churchy" we sound, or how many Bible verses we have memorized. And He is not impressed by our good deeds-unless they are done out of obedience to him. We can do seemingly great things, like speak prophecies, cast out demons and perform miracles, but if were motivated by anything other than Gods will, weve missed the boat.

Jesus is not only warning the crowd against the danger of false prophets, hes again highlighting the importance of obedience. He says that following the will of the Father is

foundational to knowing Jesus, foundational to the Kingdom. Obedience to God equals a spiritual foundation that will withstand the tough times.

RESPOND

So the word of the day seems to be "foundation." On what is your foundation built? What motivates how you move through your day? How do you make decisions, big and small, about your life? As you go through your day today, practice making your decisions based on what you think God is saying you should do. Consider it "practicing obedience," or strengthening your foundation.
