Wednesday, March 21

Nehemiah: Completion

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily will study people from the Bible that changed the game. This week, were looking at the story of Nehemiah.

READ

Nehemiah 7:1-5

REFLECT

Rebuilding Jerusalems wall was a huge project. But it turned out to be part of a much larger vision: restoring an entire city. And as we see in todays passage, God didnt show Nehemiah the next step until he had obediently completed his first task.

PRAY

God, please help me complete the tasks you set before me. And help me to trust that you have a wonderful plan for me--even when I cant see what comes next. Thank you for caring enough about me to include me in your work. Amen.
