Thursday, April 11

We continue to look at Jesus teaching in Luke 12 this week, as we quiet worry and turn up the voice of God.

READ

Luke 12:29-32

REFLECT

Where are you investing your thoughts today? If someone could peek in your brain and see where you are focusing would you look like someone who is following Jesus? Jesus says not to invest yourself toward just eating and drinking, staying alive, existence. Real life begins with seeking the Kingdom, Gods kingdom. God wants to let you in on all of his goodness, listen for his clues, seek his Kingdom.

RESPOND

Take fifteen minutes right now to listen. See if anything at all comes to mind. Then dont stop there, continue throughout your day being on the lookout for God at work, the Kingdom.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
