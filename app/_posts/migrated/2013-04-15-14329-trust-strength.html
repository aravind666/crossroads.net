Thursday, April 18

Weve been looking at a packed verse in Isaiah, one day at a time this week. Lets look at how trust is your strength today.

READ

Isaiah 30:15

REFLECT

Have you ever seen someone choose to do a trust fall: a group of people gather behind someone as they willingly fall backwards into their arms? Its sort of like baptism where a person chooses to profess their faith publicly. Although, you can see the simplicity of the act, you can also implicitly see the power. Trust is an investment that pays dividends in strength.

RESPOND

Decide one way you want to be intentionally trusting of God today, and intentionally trustworthy.
