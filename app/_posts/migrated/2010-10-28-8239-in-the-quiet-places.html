Thursday, October 28

In the Quiet Places

Take a moment to thank God for his patience with you.

READ

Luke 5:16

REFLECT

Any relationship requires intentionality. How intentional are you about setting aside time to be with God and to hear from him? Do you have places in your life where you go to spend time with God?

RESPOND

Thank God for his patience when you are less than intentional about your relationship with him. Set aside intentional time today to just sit and be with God. Talk to Him about whatever is on your mind.
