Wednesday, July 23

Approval: Gods Pleasure

This week, were spending time receiving Gods approval, as well as identifying areas of our lives where we unnecessarily seek the approval of others.

PRAY

Ask God to show you how much he loves you.

LISTEN

Gods Pleasure

READ

Jeremiah 29:11

Ephesians 1:1-6

John 10:10-11

REFLECT

Gods desire is that we live full, hopeful, abundant lives. Knowing that He wants the best for us helps us perform from a place of approval, instead of working for approval.

RESPOND

Just like yesterday, be on the lookout for a time today when you seek the approval of others. When you notice it, remind yourself that God already loves you and wants the best for you.
