Boldly Authentic: Boldly Challenge

This week were learning four ways to live boldly authentic lives of faith, based on Jesus example in the book of John.

READ

John 3:1-8

REFLECT

When Jesus talked of being born a second time, he meant allowing Gods Spirit to direct our lives, all the way down to our everyday actions. Pharisees were religious leaders who had spectacular rule-following abilities, so this truly was a bold challenge for Nicodemus (and for all of us, if were being honest). Jesus was asking him to give up control and completely shift the foundation of his faith--to go from rule-following to Spirit-obeying.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • This weeks ways to be boldly authentic were 1) give access, 2) get vulnerable, 3) live congruently and 4) boldly challenge. Which one of these is the most difficult for you? Why?
  • Whats something you think God might be teaching you through this weeks study?
  • Whats one way you can live a boldly authentic life next week?
