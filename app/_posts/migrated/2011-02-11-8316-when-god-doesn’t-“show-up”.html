Friday, February 11
When God Doesnt Show Up

God, give me the courage to be emotionally honest with you.


READ
Psalm 44

REFLECT

Has someone ever let you down? Of coursethis is an experience common to most, if not all of humanity. They were late, they broke a promise, they told your secret, they didnt do what they said they would. In other words, they didnt show up when you needed them most.

But what do you do when that person is God?

If this song is any indication, you get angry. Full-on, raging mad. This journey of following God is not all good times, yet God is big enough (and caring enough) to hear our anger and heartache throughout it all.


RESPOND

Are we close enough with God that we will tell him when were angry? Thats the kind of relationship He wants with us. Take a few minutes to talk with Godjust talkabout your comfort level in opening up to him.


READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Acts 4
