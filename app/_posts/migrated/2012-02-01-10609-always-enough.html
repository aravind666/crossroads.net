Thursday, February 2

Always Enough

This week, were studying aspects of Gods faithfulness.

PRAY

God, help me rely on you daily; turn my dependence on you into an everyday habit.

READ

John 6:30-35

REFLECT

Jesus drew a parallel between our needs for both physical and spiritual nourishment. Just like God provided just enough manna each day to the Israelites, he also faithfully gives us the "bread of life." All we have to do is continually trust that God will always give us enough: enough grace, enough faith, enough of whatever we need.

RESPOND

How does the belief that God will provide you with just enough, every time, affect the way you approach your everyday routine?

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 1 Thessalonians 3
