Priests: Gods Ideal

This week, well look at how Gods plan has all of us in the role of priest.

READ

Exodus 19:3-6

REFLECT

About 3,500 years ago, God freed all of the Israelites (his people) from slavery in Egypt. They began their journey toward a land that they would be able to call their own. While they were on this journey, God began to train them how to live, not like slaves, but as a free people.

Todays verse comes right before Moses goes up the mountain to get the ten commandments (the first covenant God makes with them living in freedom). Moses first tells them Gods big vision for this new covenant: not to have priests, but to be a nation of priests--a vision that really doesnt begin to be fulfilled until Jesus.

RESPOND

Journal what you think is the job description a priest or pastor.
