Watch and discuss the classic movie Lawrence of Arabia on Friday at 6:30pm with Reel to Real, an Open Community Group. Twice per month, people gather to watch a film in the Auditorium and then stay for a discussion about its themes. Free popcorn and soft drinks provided.(And the movie's free, too.)
