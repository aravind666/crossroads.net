--- layout: post title: "This Weekend Will Be Huge" subtitle: "" date: 2008-03-11 17:22:46 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Todd" --- This weekend we're going to be talking about how generous communities change the world. A few weeks ago, we gave out cards titled What Could You Give that challenged all of us to be thinking about how we can be generous with our time and money to bring freedom to others. (For more on this, you can watch or listen to Brian Tome's talk from week four.) This weekend (March 15-16th) we're going to be using those cards to "mark the moment" and commit to living generous lives.

Last weekend we also handed out a brochure called You Are Here that highlights some of the great things happening in our community and some of the ways in which Crossroads is being a blessing to our city and the world. When generous individuals come together into generous communities, freedom is the result.

It's going to be an incredible weekend. Let's...hope...there's...no...snow....
