Wednesday, December 29
A Solid Support

God, allow me to hear your voice today.

READ
Proverbs 11:28

REFLECT
It seems like Solomons editor might have missed this one, right? After all, most of us can think of people whose trust seems to be in their money, yet it doesnt appear like theyve suffered or "fallen." But certainly, none of us know the full details of anothers life (even if were related). And none of us can see where each persons path is leading. Better, of course, to bank on Gods wisdom that relying on money for security will ultimately disappoint. But trust in Him for security, and we will be supported with everything we need.

RESPOND
In the midst of the frustrations of daily life, it can be difficult to feel like were "flourishing like leaves in spring." Grab a sheet of paper, and jot down a quick list of ways that God has blessed or provided for you in the last month.

ANOTHER LOOK

Matthew 6:19-21
