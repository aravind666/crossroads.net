Wednesday, August 22

Work: Good For Your Character

Stop and pray first this morning. Ask God how he wants you to view work today.

READ

1 Thessalonians 4:11-12

REFLECTThe Apostle Paul gives simple operating instructions here. New faith, much like new love, can make mundane daily functions seem unimportant, but Paul reminds his friends in Thessalonica that the basics of living out our faith start with engaging in work. Even if you dont currently call yourself a Christ-follower, working is the first step to being a healthy and competent part of a community.

RESPOND

Memorize todays verse. Its good spiritual work.
