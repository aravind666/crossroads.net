Tuesday, December 21

Threats Abound

READ
Matthew 2:13-23

REFLECT

Jesus humble and unusual birth in an animal feeding trough was incredibly vulnerable. Yet, God the Father went further to communicate to us the real threats that come against the life-giving promises of Jesus. As a young child, Jesus had to flee with his parents to a foreign land to escape death.

What threatens your willingness to embrace the promise of deliverance in Jesus birth?

RESPOND
Pray to be protected from threats. Ask God for boldness to move against them.

ANOTHER LOOK
Amos 5:14-15
