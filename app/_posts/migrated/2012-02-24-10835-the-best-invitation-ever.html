Friday, February 24

The Best Invitation Ever

This week, were looking at how our desire to be loved--if misdirected--can create unhealthy appetites in our lives.

READ

2 Peter 1:3-4

REFLECT

God knows that our earthly appetites, when left unchecked, leave us with a sick feeling in our stomachs and an unhealthy desire for more. The Bible promises us again and again that God wants something better for us.

RESPOND

Spend a few minutes considering the possibility that God is a loving Father who wants the best for you. How might that affect your life on a daily basis? (And if you havent triedthis weeks fasting challenge, maybe give it a shot this weekend.)
