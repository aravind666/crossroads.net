Friday, December 10

A Birth of Promise and Threat

READ

Matthew 1:18-25

REFLECT

What do you do with what you believe?

God the Father chose a most unusual way to come and rescue his children. He came as a childbut not a typical child. Rather, a child who was conceived of himself though his Spirit, so that he was fully God and fully man. And also a public disgrace. Joseph, despite these threatening and countercultural circumstances, listened to God and chose courageously to do what God commanded: he rescued Mary from disgrace.

RESPOND Ask God to do what is needed to love others sacrificially, despite any threats to yourself.
