Friday, May 7

God is Light (Part 4)

Take a moment and ask God to reveal himself to you as you study his nature.

READ

Isaiah 60:1-5

REFLECT

If we are in relationship with God, and God is light, then what does that mean for us?

Consider this: when the electrical power fails and our neighborhoods are in darkness, what happens? Light has a natural attraction. Light is a powerful force. People in darkness are drawn to light. They search for it.

This is why our relationship with God is so powerful. Just like the moon reflects the light of the sun, so we, too, can reflect Gods light.

  • Think about a time when things were dark for you. Who or what was the light that gave you hope?

RESPOND

As you draw closer to God, his image is revealed to others through you. Ask God to clear the things in your life that hinder his reflection from you to others.

ANOTHER LOOK
