Wednesday, December 14

Of Course

PRAY

Ask God to fill you so completely with his goodness and glory that you cant wait to tell others about what youre experiencing.

READ

Luke 2:8-20 (even if you know some of this by heart, read and enjoy)

REFLECT

Get into this story for a minute. God didnt yank the shepherds off the field, show them his son and then shove them into the surrounding neighborhoods to tell people about what happened. It was of their own volition. It was opening day and theyd been given Diamond Seats. They didnt even consider going back to work. They didnt worry about who was ready to hear about what happened, and who wasnt. It was an "of course" response. Like Jeremiah. Like a maven.

RESPOND

Whats something you loved or that happened and you were so excited about that you couldnt stop talking about it? (Dont pick Jesus for this one.) Consider the way you told other people. Did you text, call people you hadnt talked to in a while, create something in response to it? Think about your style of sharing.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 1 John 1
