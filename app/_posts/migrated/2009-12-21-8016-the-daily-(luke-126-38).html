Monday, December 21
God Steps into the World

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.

READ
Luke 1:26-38

REFLECT
One word made all the difference.

An angel appears to Mary and says that she is going to have a baby. Marys response is understandable; she asks, How can I have a kid? Im a virgin.

He answers by saying that the power of God will overshadow her.

This may not mean much to us, but it did to her. It was the same word used for the Holy Spirit (who took the form of a cloud) who led her people through the desert and filled the center of the temple. It was the word used for the physical, visible presence of God. He was stepping into the world, and he was doing it through her.

  • Have you ever felt overshadowed by Gods presence? Describe that experience.
  • If you havent ever felt overshadowed, what do you imagine it would be like?

RESPOND
Ask God to help you feel his presence today, then spend a couple of minutes in silence, ready for anything that he may tell you.
