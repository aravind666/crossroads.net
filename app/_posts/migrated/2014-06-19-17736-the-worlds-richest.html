Friday, June 20

The Worlds Richest - New Song

Our current series is all about redefining the words happiness, wealth, family and love. This week well explore what wealth means to us, no matter how much or little we have.

PRAY

Thank God for all of the wealth in your life.

READ

1 John 3:1, Matthew 6:26

REFLECT

It doesnt matter if we have a million dollars in the bank or a single dollar in our pocket, we are among the worlds richest people. That means all of us. How did we get this wealth? God gave it to us because he loves us, plain and simple. And when we lean into God and trust that he provides for our every need---friends, body, mind and finances---we are rich.

Spend some time with God and rest in the truth that, no matter what, hes got your back. Heres a song from the weekend to help you out. Listen to the YouTube below or download the song to your computer or smartphone.

All Creatures of Our God and King
