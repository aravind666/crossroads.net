Friday, August 15

James: Sweet to the Soul

As we continue to study the book of James, were focusing on the power of our tongues.

PROVERBS 16:24

Gracious words are a honeycomb, sweet to the soul and healing to the bones.

RESPOND

Todays passage complements James words on the power of our tongues. Think of someone you know whos suffering or sick. Ask God to help you write or speak words of sweetness and healing for that person. God will use and guide your words--so dont worry about perfection.
