Good Beginnings: United

Today, we continue looking at Gods good creation of man and woman in Genesis.

READ

Genesis 2:24

REFLECT

In Genesis 1, God blessed mankind to be fruitful and multiply, but this verse is really the beginning of sex and marriage. Woman was taken out of man, and sex brings the image of God (masculine and feminine), back together again. This means that the covenant created through sex is stronger than the bond created through being birthed by our own mother. Birthing a child and raising that child is a holy thing. The covenant of sex is even stronger.

RESPOND

Do you view sex as this powerful of a bond? Journal today about how you have valued or undervalued the covenant of sex.
