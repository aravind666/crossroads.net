Friday, October 12

The Promise of Home

READ1 Peter 5:7

REFLECT

When you come home, do you take your shoes off and hang your jacket, or throw all your crap on the couch or in the corner? No matter how you do it, it means your home. Take a moment today to be at home with God (wherever you are) and give him all of your worries and all your weight; cast it on him like an old sweatshirt thrown over a chair.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
