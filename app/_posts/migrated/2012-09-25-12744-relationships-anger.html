Wednesday, September 26

Relationships: Anger

Today we look at how to walk through anger as we pursue more of the real thing in relationships.

READ

Ephesians 4:26-27

REFLECT

This is one of the most frequently-misused verses with young couples, whether dating or married. Heres an interpretation and application from someone in our community with decades of dating and marriage under her belt:

Staying up late to argue your way out of anger is a losing battle. When I find myself in this situation, I try to do four things:

1. Set up another time to talk when Im clearer-headed

2. Ask God how he sees both the situation and the other person

3. Forgive

4. Go to bed.

RESPOND

Today, take time to journal forgiveness. Start by writing "I forgive," and see what comes next.
