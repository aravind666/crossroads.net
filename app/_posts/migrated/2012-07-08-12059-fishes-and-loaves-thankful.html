Tuesday, July 10

Fishes and Loaves: Thankful

Today, we continue learning about Jesus feeding the crowd. Reread this part of the story, looking for the moment when the miracle happened.

READ

John 6:10-13

REFLECT

So, when did Jesus say hocus pocus, or make a grand gesture to let the crowd know that he was the miracle man? Something simple but powerful happens between two commas in verse eleven: "gave thanks." It multiplies in the disciples and Jesus hands as they distribute it after his prayer of thanks.

RESPOND

Practice thankfulness today. Especially thank God for what he has provided in areas where you may have been critical or unthankful before.
