GO Cincinnati is taking place on Saturday, May 18 from 9am - 1pm. Its one of our favorite days of the year, as thousands love on our city and provide a boost of encouragement to a number of local non-profits.

This is our eighth consecutive year where weve been doing projects in places like Middletown, Burlington, Clifton and even Indiana.We need 7,000 volunteers and sign ups are open now.

Note thedeadline to sign up is this coming Monday, May 6 at 5pm. Visit crossroads.net/gocincinnati for more information.
