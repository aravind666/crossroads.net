Friday, June 14

Goodness: Husbands

We finish our week and our Good Sex series with a shout-out to husbands.

READ

Ephesians 5:25-28

REFLECT

Christ made himself one with us through a spiritual act that models the physical act of sex. He made himself one with us by entering us as the Holy Spirit. Christ is the perfect husband to all of the church, but husbands have the not-so-small task of replicating and representing Christ through the way they love their wives. Husbands--invite more of Gods kingdom to this earth through loving your wife well.

RESPOND

How can you bring more of the representation of Christs marriage to the church through your relationships?

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Which day stood out the most for you this week?

  • Did shining Gods light on your sexuality bring anything to the surface? Confess it.

  • How can you view your relationship with Jesus more like a marriage?
