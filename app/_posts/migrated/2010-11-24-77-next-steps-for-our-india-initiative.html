For several years, Crossroads volunteers have been actively engaged in the work of justice through International Justice Mission and other local initiatives. Amy Shermans article A Church on a Mission in Books and Culture highlights our involvement to-date.

Now, thanks to the incredible generosity of our community, we can truly change the game of aftercare in India. Our four-prong strategy involves:

  • Continuing to improve the quality of aftercare in Mumbai. Well be able to expand Trauma Focused Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (TF-CBT) to nearly 500 girls in Mumbai and improve the physical infrastructure of where they stay.
  • Creating new beds for an additional 170 girls by investing in establishment of two new aftercare homes, 12 group homes and one independent living project.
  • Expanding social enterprise opportunities for nearly 250 girls through our partners iSanctuary and Freeset.
  • Developing a long-term presence on-the-ground in India that includes developing our relationship with local Indian churches who can embrace rescued girls in loving community.

What an amazing God we serve one who loves us and treasures the girls were going run after. Stay tuned to crossroads.net for more updates as we press go on the India initiative of Game Change.
