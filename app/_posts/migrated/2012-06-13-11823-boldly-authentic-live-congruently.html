Boldly Authentic: Live Congruently

This week were learning four ways to live boldly authentic lives of faith, based on Jesus example in the book of John.

READ

John 18:19-23

REFLECT

Jesus stayed consistent, even in the high-pressure situation leading up to his crucifixion. His awareness of his identity--and his focus on his mission--helped Jesus live the same way in every situation, whether he was with close friends or out in public.

RESPOND

Ask God to speak to you about your identity and mission. (Even if youre confident of the answer, its worth going through the exercise again.) Spend as long as you can sitting in that question, then write down whatever comes to mind.
