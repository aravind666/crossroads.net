Jesus isnt holding out on us, he offers us the good life. Read a little more of the story today of Jesus turning water into wine. And look through the eyes of the servants. READ John 2:5-9 REFLECT The servants role was to work for the master of the banquet (who worked for the bridegroom). At this wedding, Jesus was a guest. The servants chose to be under Jesus authority and to act. Result: the miracle happened right in their hands. They experienced the mystery of the miracle closer than anyone at the feast. RESPOND Is there an area of your life where you might not be giving Jesus authority? (Hint: where in your life are you not experiencing the good life?) Can you almost hear Mary say to you, "Do whatever he asks?"
