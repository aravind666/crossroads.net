Tuesday, August 16

Spring: Awaken

God, here I am. I will remain present and connected with you in this moment.

READ

Isaiah 66:1-2

REFLECT

We dont usually think of God as a funny guy, but there is sarcasm in this verse. If you missed it, read it again with a sarcastic tone.

It is really easy for us to get immersed in life and forget that we are all just messing about in stuff that God made, that he began. What will we do for Him, when he uses the earth as a footstool? He seems to be joking with us here, as someone might do with a new friend or love interest.

He awakens us out of our small worlds to remind us how big he is. This is the beginning of a new love, and he makes it clear that the groundwork for this new relationship is humility, an awareness of our proper place in the universe. This is a delicious spring love affair that he woos us into.

RESPOND

What have you been doing to impress God? How have you been caught up in finding ways to "earn" His love? Write them down and then ask God to show you what it would look like to, instead, be humble, penitent, and trembling.
