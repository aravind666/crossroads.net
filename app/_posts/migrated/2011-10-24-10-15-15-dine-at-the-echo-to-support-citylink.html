It’s CityLink Week at the Echo Restaurant in Hyde Park! Enjoy some home-cooked comfort food while supporting CityLink, today through Sunday, Oct. 30. The Echo is raising funds and awareness—donating 10% of proceeds to CityLink and sharing about CityLink with all of its customers. Also, join us Thursday for dinner from 5-8pm; CityLink Director Johnmark Oudersluys and ReachOut Director Tim Senff will be guest hosting at the Echo.
