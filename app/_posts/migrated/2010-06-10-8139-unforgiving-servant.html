Thursday, June 10

Unforgiving Servant


Take a moment and ask God to reveal truth and meaning to you through Jesus stories.


READ

Matthew 18:21-35



REFLECT

When we owe something, we really want forgiveness. We want to be set free.

Yet when someone owes us, letting go is much more challenging. We want whats rightfully ours.

All of us would gladly be forgiven of millions worth of debt, yet it can be all too easy to hold a grudge over 20 bucks owed to us. But here, Jesus gives us a glimpse of Gods perspective when it comes to forgiveness. All of us (if we choose to accept it) are forgiven of a debt we couldnt pay in 10 lifetimes. Refusing forgiveness to others simply cant be an option for us.

  • Why do you think it is so difficult for us to forgive others?
  • How does Jesus statement in verse 35 make you feel? Why?

RESPOND

Regardless of where we stand in forgiving others, God has forgiven us all of a massive debt. Grab your journal (or a piece of paper), and take a couple of minutes to write a specific thank you letter to God for the forgiveness Hes given you.
