During the Strong Challenge, were creating space for you to participate fully in the journey by taking a temporary, six-week break from The Daily. But since we hope youll continue with the daily rhythm youve established, we created StrongChallenge.com to help you continue this rhythm.

Heres what you can do at StrongChallenge.com:

  • Sign up for (fun) daily email or text-message reminders to do your challenge cards (seriously, theyre fun).
  • View the challenge cards on your computer and journal online.
  • Access the challenge cards from your mobile phone, which means youll always have them with you. (No excuses for not doing your challenge for the day!)
  • Learn from others by reading journal entries they choose to share publicly.

To get started, register on the site, then set up your reminder notifications (click your name in the upper right corner, then select Reminders). Its going to be a great journey. We hope you find these tools helpful as you maintain the daily rhythm youve established with The Daily.
