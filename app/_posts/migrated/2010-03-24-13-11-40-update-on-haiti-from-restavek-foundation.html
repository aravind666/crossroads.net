After the January earthquake in Haiti, Crossroads responded by contributing $120,000 to the Restavek Foundation to aid in rebuilding efforts.We just received word about how the foundation is using the money to help with immediate needs. So far, the foundation has used our contributions to:


         •    Set up schools and temporary classrooms serving 1,160 kids
         •    Organize ongoing food distribution serving more than 5,000 people
         •    Purchase vehicles to transport food and supplies
         •    Repair a transitional home serving 30 kids
         •    Built security walls around their schools.


They also plan to set up six more schools this month, which will serve another 1,000 children, and they will continue using contributions from Crossroads to meet immediate and long-term needs on the ground in Haiti.
