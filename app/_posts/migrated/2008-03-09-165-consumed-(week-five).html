This past weekend, Gary Haugen spoke to us about how prayer has been the foundation of the work of International Justice Mission. We were also challenged to make prayer a part of our lives.

As always, if you missed the service you can listen to the talk, or watch it.

Also, don't miss the Prayer Experience that's happening all week. It is designed to be a simple, personal prayer journey with an audio guide to help you engage with God. It's great for those who have never prayed or for those who pray everyday. The experience is designed to last about an hour, but you can take as much time as you'd like.
