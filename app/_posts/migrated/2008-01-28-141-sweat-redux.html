--- layout: post title: "Sweat Redux" subtitle: "" date: 2008-01-28 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Todd" --- This weekend was a great reminder to me that every decision we make is important to God. Results are a huge factor, but it's also about what God is doing in us. It's about how we're choosing to engage in the journey.

It's easy for the workaholic in me to take this to mean that I should be sweating it out, putting in more hours than everyone else, and leaving it all on the field at work. When I take a few steps back, though, I realize that I need to be working hard in every area of my life - with my family, in friendships, and in how I engage spiritually and seek truth - not just at my job. That really raises the bar.

I guess the main learning for me is that I need to be "emptying myself" everyday and engaging with every ounce of who I am. This is what it means to do work worthy of honor.
