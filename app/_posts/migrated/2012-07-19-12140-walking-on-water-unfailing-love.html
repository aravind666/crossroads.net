Friday, July 20

Walking on Water: Unfailing Love

Weve been looking this week at the story of Jesus walking on water in John 6:15-21. Today lets look at a Psalm that feels like it could have been written by the disciples in the boat.

READ

Psalm 107:23-31

REFLECT

How often do we forget to recognize Gods hand at work? Whether He is the one stirring the waves or calming them, lets recognize today both his power and his mercy, his invitation and his challenges.

RESPOND

Write a few lines of your own psalm about how God is at work in your life.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.


  • Share with your community a challenge or storm that you are in right now.
  • Share a time when you experienced Gods invitation or how you are currently experiencing it.
  • Where do you lean in your relationships, are you more of an inviter or a challenger?
