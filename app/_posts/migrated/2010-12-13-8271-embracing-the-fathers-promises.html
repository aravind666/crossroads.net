Week 2: A Fathers Gift

Monday, December 13

Embracing the Fathers Promises

READ
Luke 1:26-38

REFLECT
God the Father wants to be with us to fulfill his promises to us, so that we have a life that is blessed.

Again and again, God chooses to respect human lives, to have an intimate relationship with his children, and to communicate his desire for his children to be dependent on him as their dad. These words from Luke illustrate the intimacy and care God has for his child Mary, despite her unusual task. Are you afraid of what God has for you? Do you believe His promise to you is to bless you?


RESPOND
Ask God to reveal any fears you have about his promises for your life. Ask God to take that fear and give you a heart that says in hope, "May it be as you have said".

ANOTHER LOOK
Deuteronomy 31:6
