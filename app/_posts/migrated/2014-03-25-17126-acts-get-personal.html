Wednesday, March 26

Acts: Get Personal

PRAY

As you read, ask God to show something new about his character.

READ

Acts 3:1-10

REFLECT

The beggar at the temple probably had no idea what was in store for him that day. Sitting near the gates of the temple was a great place to be noticed, but no one ever got involved. They walked right by him. But when Peter and John came, instead of ignoring him or just giving him a few coins, they looked him straight in the eye and personally got involved. The beggars life was forever different.

RESPOND

Peter and John got involved with what was right in front of them. Whats looking you straight in the eye at work, at school or at home? Today, take a step toward getting personally involved.
