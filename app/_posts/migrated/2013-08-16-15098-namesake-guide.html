Friday, August 23

Namesake: Guide

We finish out our week looking at Gods desire to promote himself.

READ

Psalm 31:3-5

REFLECT

Lets pause today and look at the word namesake a little differently. Your last name says whose name you are carrying on--your parents and their parents, etc. When God promotes himself for his namesake--he is not only bringing attention to himself, but he is blessing all that are his namesake--his people.

RESPOND

Give yourself a new last name today, just in your journal. It might be something like Bob Gods-namesake or Lisa Jesus-bride or Sam Gods-loved-one. Let God give you an identity in his name.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Are you a self-focused person or a God-focused person?

  • What does the phrase for his names sake make you think of?

  • Share the name that you wrote in your journal with each other.
