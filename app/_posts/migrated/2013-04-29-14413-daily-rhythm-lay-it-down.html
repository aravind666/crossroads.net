Thursday, April 25

We continue this week to slot ourselves into daily rhythms.

READ

Luke:9:23-24

REFLECT

Making a decision to follow Christ is a daily decision. There are big moments of making that decision for the first time, getting baptized, choosing to trust God in a tough time, and moving when you feel his calling. There is also the daily rhythm of choosing to lay down selfishness and pick up servanthood, just like Jesus did not only on the cross, but everyday of his life on earth.

RESPOND

Take a moment to look at your calendar or think about the details of your day and choose a moment today when you are going to choose to serve, today, over preserving image or self.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
