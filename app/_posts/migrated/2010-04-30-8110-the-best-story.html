Friday, April 30

The Best Story

Spend a minute asking God to quiet your mind and help you focus on him.

READ

Romans 5:12-19

REFLECT

God has a giant story. Through the Bible, we can begin to understand it. We can understand more fully His character and his desire for us to connect, to partner with, and to trust him. He continues to actively pursue us with his perfect grace and love, and were led into understanding his character.

He is a large and holy God. Seeking Him is the best story for us.

RESPOND

Ask God to reveal to you his big story and the place he has for you in it.

ANOTHER LOOK

Romans 3:21-24
