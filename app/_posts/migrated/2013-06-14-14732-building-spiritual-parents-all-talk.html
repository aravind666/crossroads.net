Thursday, June 20

Building Spiritual Parents: All Talk

We continue looking at Gods design for us to be spiritually parented and to mature into spiritual parents. Focus on verses 18-20 today.

READ

1 Corinthians 4:14-20

REFLECT

Spiritual parents know how to assess power and can see the fruit in the lives of those under their care. While spiritual orphans tend to brag about themselves, having good spiritual parents means that you get the encouragement you need without bragging. Spiritual parents dont judge or try to determine motives--they can see God at work through the power flowing out of their spiritual children.

RESPOND

Take a moment to note:

  • Do I need more encouragement?

  • Do I have evidence of God at work in my life?
