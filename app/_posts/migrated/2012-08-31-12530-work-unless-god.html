Wednesday, September 5

Work: Unless God

This week we continue to look at Gods authority in our work.

READ

Psalms 127:1-2

REFLECT

Our workplace gives us an area where we rule. We own a territory: whether it is managing our homes, a team of co-workers, our classload or a specific bolt on the assembly line, we are king there."Unless God"is a powerful statement in this passage. We are uselessly busy when we dont recognize Gods authority over all that we do.

RESPOND

Do you spend time stressing about work? If so, make a choice to pray Psalm 127:1-2 today whenever stress or worry crops up.
