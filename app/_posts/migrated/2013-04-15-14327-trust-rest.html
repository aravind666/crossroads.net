Tuesday, April 16

We continue to look at a passage in Isaiah with a focus today on rest.

READ

Isaiah 30:15

REFLECT

Our salvation is in trusting God.

When we live lives that are self dependant, thats what we have at the end of the day--ourselves, and ourselves alone. God-dependant lives wrap us in the loving arms of a good creator-father and all of his family and kingdom. When we rest, we communicate to God that we trust that hes got it. Really, Hes got it without us, and thats trust. That kind of trust, trust that lets us rest, it is our salvation.

RESPOND

Sit quietly for fifteen minutes and do nothing as a radical act of trust.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
