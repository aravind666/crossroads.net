Three Kings: Gifts for a King

Gods people werent the only ones searching for something. The Magi were on their way to meet the the King of Kings.

LISTEN

We Three Kings

READ

Matthew 2:9-12

REFLECT

The Magi followed the star until it settled over Bethlehem where they met Mary and her son, Jesus. They knew this little boy was the King they read about, the King they had waited for. They worshipped Him and brought him gifts fit for royalty.

Their gifts seem strange to give to a baby---no diaper genie, teething keys or blankets---but these gifts were signs of the life Jesus would lead:

  • Gold because Jesus is the King of Kings

  • Frankincense to recognize that Jesus was God in the flesh and worthy of worship

  • Myrrh, perfume used to anoint the dead pointing to Jesus suffering and death

RESPOND

Give someone in your sphere of influence (that means your family or coworkers) the gift of love.

Maybe its an apology, a letter telling them how much you care or buying them lunch. Today, go and give a gift that tells them how much they are loved.
