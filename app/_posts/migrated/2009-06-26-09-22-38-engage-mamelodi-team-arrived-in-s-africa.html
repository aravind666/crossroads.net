Africa" subtitle: "" date: 2009-06-26 09:22:38 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "WhatsUp" --- Our Engage Mamelodi team of Will and Beth Skillman and Jason Singh arrived safely in South Africa this week. After a delayed departure in Cincinnati and a full-out sprint to make the connection in Atlanta, everyone (and their luggage) arrived safely on Tuesday. They now begin the task of establishing life there: renting a home, purchasing a car, establishing bank accounts, negotiating cell phone plans, etc. The Skillmans' daughter, Carolina, even has an upcoming school entrance exam.

Shortly after they settle in, the group will be participating in GO Namibia, a service trip taking place in July led by a team from Charity & Faith.
