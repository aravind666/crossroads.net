Wednesday, July 18

Walking on Water: Listen

Today, notice how the disciples respond to Jesus showing up in the middle of a challenging time.

READ

John 6:18-19

REFLECT

Did the disciples respond to Jesus the way you think they would? How might you respond to a man walking on top of the water? The disciples were really busy rowing and weathering the storm, and Jesus showed up in the most unexpected way. He was literally above the problem.

RESPOND

Today, consider a storm youre weathering. Take a moment to visualize Jesus walking into the situation, and listen to what he says to you.
