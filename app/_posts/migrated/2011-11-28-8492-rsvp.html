Monday, November 28

RSVP

PRAY

Ask God to show you what it means to receive him as an invitation.

READ

John 1:14

REFLECT

Think about an invitation you really want-an exclusive event, a degree program, the Senior PGA Tour. If you get it, youre elated and cant wait to go. Compare that response to getting called for jury duty or into a mandatory meeting. Night and day difference, right?

As outrageous as it is that God became a man, it makes sense that Jesus would come to us as an invitation, not a summons. He came for us to choose him, because getting the choice to follow and love someone leads to a strong and active relationship. Otherwise, its a relationship burdened with things like obligation, guilt, fear or ritual.

For the next three weeks, well consider what it means to get invited into relationship with Jesus and what happens when we show up at the party.

RESPOND

Whether youre a new follower of Jesus or twenty years in the making, you might waver back and forth between feeling both invited and summoned into relationship with Jesus. How have you been experiencing Him recently?
