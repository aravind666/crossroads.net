Monday, April 22

Rhythms are the way God designed us to live. He designed day and night. He created Sabbath, the cadence of weekly rhythms, and God designed the ordered universe that writes our years.

This week well specifically look at places in the Bible where the word daily is used, as clues to what God wants us to approach on a daily basis.

READ

Job 23:12

REFLECT

Job is known throughout the world (not just within the Christian faith) as a story of suffering. Yet, Job who went through the toughest of times knew how much he was dependant on Gods words. Today, we have access to God and his communications to us his people through the Bible, which was canonized and mass printed long after Job. Much like Job we can listen for God to communicate with us daily, and in reading the Bible, we can see his voice daily as well.

RESPOND

By choosing to engage The Daily, you are pursuing reading the Bible and listening for God daily. Way to go! Take a moment to listen and feel Gods encouragement and pride over you.
