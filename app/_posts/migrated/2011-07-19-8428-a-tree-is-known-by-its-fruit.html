Tuesday, July 19

A Tree is Known by its Fruit

God, purify my heart and grow your good fruit in me.

READ

Luke 6:43-45

REFLECT

Imagine if every conversation you had every day, all day, was one big job interview. Keep smiling, have good posture, make eye contact. Say smart, good things. Dont say stupid, bad things. Ask interesting questions, dont tell dirty jokes. Seems exhausting, doesnt it?

No matter how hard we work at making a good impression, eventually the truth comes out. So the question is, what is the truth? Jesus says here that anyone can see to the very core of us by listening to us speak. Its inevitable. If we are cultivating our hearts with spiritually healthy things, then our mouths will bear delicious, inviting fruit. Rather than one big life interview, wouldnt it feel so much better to be ourselves?

RESPOND

Consider the following questions and write down your answers: Why do you say the things you do? Whats motivating your tongue to speak? Whats motivating your tongue to remain silent?
