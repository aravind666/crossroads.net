Tuesday, August 28

Work Culture: Focus

Last week we learned that fruitful work is an essential part of Gods design for our lives. This week, well closely observe our work culture.

READ

Romans 12:1-2

REFLECTWhen our days attention is on output or corporate agendas, this can begin to form our lives. (Neurological research shows that where you focus can form you.) Who is telling you what to value or influencing where you place your time and energy? Is it God?

RESPOND

Start today by writing down one confession related to work--however "work" looks for you. This is one way to "place it before God as an offering" (Romans 12:1).
