Friday, July 5

Building Good Sons and Daughters: Be About Your Fathers Business

We finish up the week by focusing on the vineyard. Read through the entire story.

READ

Matthew 21:28-32

REFLECT

Its good to be reminded that this story is about a vineyard, which represents the Kingdom. Just as the father in the story sends his sons out to reproduce fruit, God wants the same things for our lives. His intention for us is to reproduce fruit--whether that is fruit in our own lives or others. From the very beginning, God commanded us to reproduce. We are created in his image and we are about our Fathers business.

RESPOND

Today, simply listen. Ask God how he wants you to be about his business and wait to hear what he says.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • In what areas of your life are you saying no to God?

  • What are some of those things that you need to repent of?

  • What are the next actions steps for you to take to reproduce?
