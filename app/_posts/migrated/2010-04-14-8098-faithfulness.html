Wednesday, April 14

Faithfulness

Spend a minute asking God to help keep your mind and heart open to the things he wants to teach you.

READ

Romans 12:1-2

REFLECT

It might sound counter-intuitive, but you can have faith in God and still not live with faithfulness.

You show faithfulness to what you think is important, whether thats a relationship, a career, a sports team, money, PC or Mac. Faithfulness to God means you dont bury your relationship with him. Your whole life reflects the love between you--not just on weekends, not just when youre volunteering, not just during conversations with fellow Christ-followers. Faithfulness is choosing to be a living sacrifice to God. Faithfulness to God centers your life and seeps into every part of it.

RESPOND

Ask God to show you where your faithlessness lies. Consider one way that you could start releasing its grip on your life and filling that part with His Spirit.

ANOTHER LOOK

Matthew 25:14-30
