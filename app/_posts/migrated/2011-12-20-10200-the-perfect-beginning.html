Wednesday, December 21

The Perfect Beginning

LISTEN

O Little Town of Bethlehem

READ

Luke 2:1-7

REFLECT

If we really consider the beginning of Jesus life--the poverty, the isolation, the filth and low social status--it might bring up an unexpected response in us. Maybe the Israelites werent the only ones waiting for an ironclad hero, full-grown and wielding obvious power. Maybe some of us have a hard time respecting or trusting a baby who comes from a backwater town.

Or it could be that Jesus humble origins are what we connect with, because we know what it feels like to be cast aside or judged. Maybe we once felt slighted, gossiped about or like a disappointment. So maybe, as it turns out, His beginning is everyones.

Because Jesus lived it all, we are fully understood and known.

RESPOND

Imagine yourself as a bystander in Bethlehem, watching all the events unfold. What would you say to the person standing next to you? Would you go see for yourself?
