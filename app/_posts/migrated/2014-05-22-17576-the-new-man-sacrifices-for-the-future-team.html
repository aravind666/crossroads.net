Friday, May 23

The New Man Sacrifices for the Future Team

This week, The Daily will focus on what the Bible says about how men play on a team.

PRAY

Pray to God and ask him to show you how you can build community.

READ

Genesis 12:1-7

REFLECT

Abram had a pretty solid life. He was living in Canaan with his family where they lived for a really long time. They were comfortable. And then God asked Abram to pick up his whole life and move away from everything he knew. It would have been a really hard decision to make, but God gave Abram a vision for a team that would change the world. He didnt know what this team would look like, but Abram sacrificed his own doubts and desires for the future of this team.

RESPOND

Whatever team youre on, think about something you can sacrifice today that will benefit your team next week or next year. Go do it.
