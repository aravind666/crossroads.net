Wednesday, December 4

O Come, Emmanuel

Today, God reveals the coming Savior through the prophet Isaiah.

LISTEN

O Come, Emmanuel

REFLECT

Man and God were separated and the world was full of pain and darkness. Kingdoms fought for power. Lives were lost. Families were broken apart. But God had a plan to rescue the people he created.

His people waited and waited for the Savior to set them free. Finally, God revealed his plan to prophets like Isaiah. In the midst of darkness, Isaiah 7:14 reveals the Saviors name: Emmanuel. He would free Gods people.

RESPOND

What is holding you captive? God sent his son to save you, to give you freedom. Listen to the song again. This time worshipping your Dad, who rescues and saves.
