Tuesday, April 23

We are in a new series on rhythms, embracing that God created the rhythms of days, weeks, years. This week The Daily is specifically focusing on (ironically) the daily.

READ

Psalm 68:19-20

REFLECT

Do you want to be adored? respected? loved? encouraged?

You were made in Gods image, and all of those words are relationship words. You long for those things and so does the God who created you. When you take a moment to step into adoration for God (his identity and his rescue of you) your soul is postured to flow in relationship with God like a newlywed.

RESPOND

Take a moment to adore God, in any way you like. Take a walk, write a love note, or just sit and ponder His goodness.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
