Monday, August 27

Work Culture: Observe

Last week we learned that fruitful work is an essential part of Gods design for our lives. This week, well closely observe our work culture.

READ

Galatians 5:19-22

RESPOND

Get out your journal and begin two lists: "Worlds Culture" and "Gods Culture." Now go back throughGalatians 5:19-22and put the descriptions you read under the heading where they belong. (SAT flashback, anyone?) Next, underline the things you see in your own workplace from either list. If you go to school or work in the home, this still applies. Unemployed? Assess your home life or last workplace.

PRAY

God, give me eyes to see the culture of my workplace clearly. Help me to see which culture Im living out-yours, or the worlds. Amen.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
