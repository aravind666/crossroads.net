--- layout: post title: "The Shack" subtitle: "" date: 2008-08-20 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Todd" --- When a Christian bandwagon is approaching, I will typically step out of the way and let it go right on by. When many friends and people in my close community approached me about the William P. Young book The Shack, I had a similar reaction. And then I noticed that many of the people who were asking me about the book were not the typical "have you heard about the latest-coolest-hippest-relavantized-thingy" kind of people but people whose relationship with Jesus I admired and trusted. I decided to give it a try. I found it to be an intriguing read and well worth the time.

There's a discussion going in the forum about the book, and I'm curious if anyone else has read it and whether or not you have any opinions.
