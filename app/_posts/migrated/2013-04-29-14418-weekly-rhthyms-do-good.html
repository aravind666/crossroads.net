Thursday, May 2

Lets look today at the weekly rhythm of doing good.

READ

Matthew 12:9-13

REFLECT

The legalists of the time were trying to tell Jesus not to heal on the Sabbath. So many rules had been created around Sabbath at this point that Jesus is pointing out that the meaning had been lost. Its not a day to abstain from living, its a day to live like Gods Kingdom has fully arrived.

RESPOND

Today, write about what heaven might look and feel like. Next, journal how you could experience that on your Sabbath.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
