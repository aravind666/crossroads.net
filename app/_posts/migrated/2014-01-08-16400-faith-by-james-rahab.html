Thursday, January 9

Faith by James: Rahab

PRAY

Ask God to show you how He can spur change in your life today---whether its in your family, at work or in your time alone.

READ

James 2:25; Joshua 2:11-14

REFLECT

James says Rahab the prostitute was "right with God by her actions." In Jericho, a town of many, Rahab stood alone, despised. Yet her faith was firm and self-denying. Against the wishes of the king of Jericho, she hid the spies from Israel. She was willing to do anything for God---even give her own life. While her fate would surely be death, she asked for mercy on her family. When the walls came a-tumbling down, her faith not only saved her family but herself as well.

RESPOND

Did you hit the snooze button 5 times too many? Tonight, go to bed earlier. Or if youre a morning person, find something to change in your routine that will set you up for change tomorrow.
