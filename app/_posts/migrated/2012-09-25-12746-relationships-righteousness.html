Relationships: Righteousness

When we pursue righteousness, we create a good space for relationships to grow.

READ

2 Timothy 2:22-24

REFLECT

What does the writer mean by "evil desires of youth?" When were immature, we frequently seek the quickest form of comfort, relief, or pleasure (think quick fix, but long term pain). Maturity seeks bigger and longer-term good, even if it means short term discomfort, or requires more self-control.

RESPOND

Today, ask God what it means for you to "call on the Lord out of a pure heart." Spend five minutes journaling what comes to mind.
