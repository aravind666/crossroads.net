Friday, September 27

Culture: Put On

We finish out our look at a passage in Ephesians 4 this week by adding verse 24. Yesterday we took off the old self, today we clothe ourselves in something new.

READ

Ephesians 4:18-24

REFLECT

If you, this week, have begun to deconstruct some old ways of doing things, you might begin to feel a little exposed. Dont fear, you dont have to stand naked. God wants to clothe you with all that he is. And with your new garment in place, made to be like Jesus, it gets easier and easier to see with his eyes.

RESPOND

Picture yourself having a choice between a heavy deceitful garment of the world, or a light Jesus garment. Try them both on, and journal about your decision.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • How were you able to see the culture of the world this week in areas where you engage every day?

  • Did you spot a way that you have been agreeing with a culture thats not Jesus-based?

  • How do you think your life could look differently if God fully renewed your mind?
