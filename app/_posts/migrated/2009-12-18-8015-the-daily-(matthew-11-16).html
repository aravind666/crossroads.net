Friday, December 18
A Promise for Everyone

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.


READ
Matthew 1:1-16

REFLECT
Every family has imperfections, and Jesus was no exception. In fact, the list you just read (if you made it through it) includes liars, adulterers, and murderers. But whats fascinating is that at that time, it was perfectly acceptable to leave whomever you wanted out of these family lists.

Have somebody who doesnt fit or has embarrassed the family? Leave them out.

Except Matthew doesnt. He leaves them in, which says something very interesting to all of us who follow.

In Gods family, no one is left out. The Christmas story of restoration is for everyone, no matter what youve done.

  • Does anything ever keep you from feeling like part of Gods family?

RESPOND
Acceptance is trickybecause sometimes a feeling of rejection stems from areas where we dont accept ourselves. Ask God to show you any areas where you havent forgiven yourself. Then spend some time thanking Him for the unconditional acceptance he gives, and ask him to help you experience it this weekend.

Week One Discussion Questions

The Friday edition of The Daily features discussion questions based on the weeks content. These questions are meant to spark conversation with your spouse, your roommate, or a small group of friends who also subscribe to The Daily.

As we look at the story of Christmas, the idea of restoration shows up repeatedly. God sent a savior to people who were tired, oppressed, and waiting for him to keep his promises.

  • What has God restored in your life?
  • In what part of your life are you still waiting for restoration?


For many of us, Christmas means shopping, decorations, and stressful family meals; yet, Christmas is a much bigger story than this.

  • If the story of Christmas is true, then God is truly with us. Describe how that idea makes you feel.
  • Gods offer of restoration is for everyone, no matter what weve done. In what ways do you need to experience acceptance (and forgiveness) from God this Christmas?
