Wednesday, March 9

A Peaceful Conquest

God, help me experience your deep, peaceful love today.

READ

Zechariah 9:9-10; Matthew 21:1-11

REFLECT

We talked about it last week, but for hundreds of years the people of Israel had been waiting for Gods promised rescuersomeone of the warrior sort who would bring them to a new place of freedom.

Finally, as Jesus rode triumphantly into Jerusalem, it had begun, right? Only one problem: He came riding on a donkey, not a horse. It may not seem like much, but its a huge statement. A horse is what you ride when you go to war. A donkey is what you ride when you are at peace.

Jesus conquest would not come through swords and violence. It would be the long, slow conquest of peaceful love.

RESPOND

Among other things, this story highlights that we can be celebrating the presence of Jesus, and yet still misunderstand his mission. Take a few minutes and brainstorm (be sure to ask Him in on that session) some ways that you can connect with the mission of God right now in your daily life.
