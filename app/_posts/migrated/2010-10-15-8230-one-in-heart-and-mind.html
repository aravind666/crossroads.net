Friday, October 15

One in Heart and Mind

Ask God to unify the Crossroads community during the Game Change Campaign.


READ

Acts 4:32-35



REFLECT

One of the most powerful things God can do in the midst of Game Change is to make the Crossroads community one in heart and mind. A unified team, experiencing Gods presence, will change lives and can literally change the world.


RESPOND

Pray for unity todaynot just for the community at Crossroads, but for Christ-followers everywhere in the Cincinnati region. Pray that God would make us one in heart in mind.
