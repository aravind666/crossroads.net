Acts: Speak Up

The Daily continues to follow the book of Acts and how we can apply its lessons to our own lives. And check out the last Beans and Rice Week video to help you make the most of your meals (you made it---way to go).

PRAY

Ask God to give you courage to speak up.

READ

Acts 18:9-16

REFLECT

Paul led a lot of people to believe in Jesus. But it never would have happened if he hadnt spoken up. This isnt to say that Paul was a loud-mouth, but speaking in front of so many people can be an overwhelming task especially when not everyone likes what youre saying. Until God spoke to him, encouraging him to speak and not to be afraid. God took care of him and his voice.

RESPOND

Speak up and be bold. That doesnt mean say whatevers on your mind, but if youre holding back because of fear or something that doesnt feel like God, ask him to intercede and give you a voice.

READ THROUGH THE BOOK OF ACTS

Todays reading is: Acts 18
