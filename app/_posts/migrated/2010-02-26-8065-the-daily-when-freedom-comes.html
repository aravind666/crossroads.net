Friday, February 26

When Freedom Comes

Many people hear from God through music. Today's edition of The Daily focuses on an original song written for the Free journey by Andrea VonAllmen. In your time with God today, read Andrea's perspective on what she wrote, and then follow along with the lyrics while you listen to the song. Ask God to meet you during this time, and to speak to your heart about the freedom he has in store for you.


From Andrea:

This song is written from God's perspectives. I say "perspectives" (plural) because of the different views that God has looking onto our freedom. He sees the root and source of our bondage. He sees the intention or results of the bondage itself. He even sees the role the Spirit could play in setting us free. Simply put, this song is God's heart for his people. My prayer is that God has somehow infused his power and Spirit into this song, however mysterious that may be. I pray that freedom is a natural result of listening to this song. Enjoy.
