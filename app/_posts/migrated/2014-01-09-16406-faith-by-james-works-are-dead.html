Friday, January 10

Faith by James: Works are Dead

PRAY

As you read, ask God to open your eyes, your heart and your mind to something new.

READ

James 2:26, Philippians 1:6

REFLECT

Faith without works is as useless as a screen door on a submarine, but dont make that list of good-deed-doing just yet. God doesnt need you to do things---his work will be completed. And no matter who we are, God is already crazy about us and nothing we do will make him love us more. But our day-to-day lives can be a natural reflection of Gods love and grace if we let it.

PRACTICE

Today or all weekend, think of a way you can share God with someone else. Maybe its a phone call to a friend you dont see much or sharing a meal with grandma. Basically, how does someone in your life feel loved? Go and do that.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Romans 5
