Friday, October 5

Family: Blessings

Family is hard work, so lets allow ourselves to receive a blessing today.

READ

Psalms 128:1-4

REFLECT

What does it mean to fear the Lord? Its not necessarily about shaking in your boots (although if you came face-to-face with God, you might). Think of whose approval or opinion you want--No one can or should hold that seat but God. If you put all of your focus on the Lords approval, you can receive this blessing.

RESPOND

Ask the Lord to show you what it means to value His approval alone.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Who is your household? Spouse and kids? Parents? Family you are close to? Community?
  • Who leads in your household? Is there clarity?
  • What might be Gods unique calling or purpose for your family?

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
