Thursday, August 15

Spirit: Inheritance

Today, lets look at how the Spirit connects us to Gods eternal nature.

READ

Ephesians:13-14

REFLECT

Have you ever had a moment that felt outside of time, outside of the daily grind, out of this world even? Those are moments when the Holy Spirit, which functions outside of the everyday order of this world, fresh with the aroma of eternity, taps us on the shoulder and wakes us up. The Holy Spirit brings us glimpses of Gods fuller kingdom.

RESPOND

Take a moment and remember the last time that you felt the Holy Spirit intersecting your everyday life. Journal about it. If you cant think of a time, ask for it to happen for you today.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
