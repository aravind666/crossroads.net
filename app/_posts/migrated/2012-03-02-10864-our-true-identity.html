Monday, March 5
Our True Identity

This week, were wrapping up our study on what it means to live as treasured children of God.

READ
Romans 8:15

REFLECT
Weve looked at this verse several times in the past few weeks, because it so clearly reveals the nature of our relationship with God. We are his treasured children--this truth is the very core of our identity. And when our heart is motivated by this promise, everything changes. We may still love to make new friends, set big goals, and find pleasure in food or activities. But these things no longer define and control us.

RESPOND
Its a daily effort for most of us to remember our identities as children of God. Today, ask God to fill you with the approval and love that comes from being his adopted child.
