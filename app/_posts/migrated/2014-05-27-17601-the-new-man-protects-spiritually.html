Friday, May 30

The New Man Protects Spiritually

The Daily continues to explore what the Bible says about men. This week, well focus on how the New Man is a protector.

PRAY

Pray to God and ask him to show you how you can build community.

READ

John 10:10, Ephesians 6:10-18

REFLECT

There is an enemy who seeks to rob and distract us from Gods love and abundance. Its like a toothpaste commercial when the bacteria attacks the teeth. The bad news is these distractions can sometimes seem endless, but Paul tells us in Ephesians that we can wear the Armor of God to protect us from the enemys plan. The New Man is a spiritual protector and the best way to put on Gods armor is to pray often and encourage others gently.

RESPOND

Who in your life needs spiritual protection? Today, put on your armor and pray for their specific protection.
