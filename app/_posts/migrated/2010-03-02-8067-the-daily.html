Tuesday, March 2

God's Desire for You

Today's edition of The Daily highlights an important point from this past weekend's message for you to keep in mind as you work through your guide and meet with your group this week.


READ

Luke 4:18-19


REFLECT

How would you complete this sentence?

The main thing God wants is for me to be __________.

Good? Moral? Nice? More religious?

Today's scripture recounts how Jesus announced his own ministry. If we want to discover what God wants for us, there's nowhere better to look than Jesus' own words. He said he came "to proclaim freedom" and "to release the oppressed."

Remember: God wants you to be free.


PRAY

Ask God to bring you into increasing measures of freedom today, and to lead the process you're going through in your Free guide and small group.
