Friday, December 17

Your Fathers Interest

READ
Luke 2:25-35

REFLECT

God the Father desired Joseph and Mary to receive personal affirmation. He wanted them to be assured that their choices would lead to his promised salvation.

God always wants to assure his children. He knows this world threatens to distract, even dissuade us, from his promises that are so powerful and real. Are you assured or dissuaded about Gods interest in you?

RESPOND
Ponder your reflections on Jesus coming these past few days. Ask God to assure your hope in him.

ANOTHER LOOK
Luke 2:36-38
