This year's all-church journey, Reset, is just around the corner (beginning February 21). For six weeks, we'll all be on the same page, exploring the life and teachings of Jesus, and discovering where we need to reset our assumptions of him. A critical part of the journey is the small group experience, and we're expecting to need 1,500 small group hosts this year. That means we need YOU.

Hosting is easy and doesn't require any Bible expertise. All you need is a place to host a small group, a welcoming attitude, and access to a DVD player. We'll set you up with what you need, and even provide (optional) training for anyone who wants it.

To learn more about Reset, hear a short message from Brian Tome about the journey, and sign up to host a group, check out crossroads.net/reset.
