Tuesday, August 27

New: Song

God loves the word new. It seems to repeat in the Bible, and this week we are looking at that theme. Today, we look at singing a new song.

READ

Psalm 96:1

REFLECT

So, why a new song? I like singing that same old hymn, whats wrong with that? There is a place for tradition in the church, but actually, it doesnt say anywhere in the Bible to create a canon of good songs (and call it a hymnal) and then sing them over and over again. It does say in multiple places in the Bible to sing a new song. New songs require something of us--creativity, courage, faith, trusting God and honesty.

RESPOND

Write a poem to God today (or a song if you are so inclined). Keep it simple. Be okay with less than perfect.
