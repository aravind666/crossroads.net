Friday, May 21

Jealous

Take a moment and ask God to reveal a fuller picture of his character to you.


READ

Exodus 20:3-5

REFLECT

We often think of jealousy as negative emotion, which it can be if its based on selfishness. However, there is such a thing as righteous jealousy. For example, in marriage, a spouse should be jealous protecting the other from anyone who wants to entice them away from the marriage commitment. This is not selfish; it is the appropriate expression of their love and commitment to person to whom they are married.

So it is with God. We create idols out of things that we bend our life around with an outpouring of our time, money, talents and affections. And when we do that, God is jealous for us. There is no selfishness in His jealousy. It is the appropriate expression of His love and holiness.


RESPOND

What is in your life that pulls you away from your relationship with God? Consider what that thing or person offers you that you dont feel from God. Ask God to show you how he can fulfill you.


ANOTHER LOOK

Hosea 14:1-3
