Don’t forget that the official groundbreaking for Crossroads Florence is this weekend!

 

Please join us at 10am this Saturday, August 27, at 828 Heights Blvd, just off of Mall Road in Florence. 

 

We’ll have a time of celebration and worship, along with a sneak peek inside the building. We’ll also announce and introduce the staff leadership of the new site, and share some details about the construction that will take place.

 

Children are welcome to attend, but childcare will not be provided. And, since this is a construction site, gym shoes are recommended.
