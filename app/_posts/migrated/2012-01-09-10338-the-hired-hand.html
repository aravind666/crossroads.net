Tuesday, January 10

The Hired Hand

READ

John 10:11-14

REFLECT

If youve ever driven a rental car, you probably know what Jesus is talking about here In Luke. Because we know that when something isnt really ours, were at least a little more careless. Maybe we drive the rental car a little faster, a little harder, than we would our own. Maybe we even do a few doughnuts on a snowy road.

Thats how money treats us-like a rental car driver who doesnt have true ownership or any staying power. Although its a great resource, its a terrible owner. And it pales in comparison to the perfect care and everlastingness that we get from the Good Shepherd.

RESPOND

When has money ever felt like your owner?

PRAY

Ask God to reveal himself as your good shepherd in ways youve never experienced.
