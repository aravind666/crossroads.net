Spring: Animate the Ground

God, send your Spirit to fill me and make me new.

READ

Psalm 104:30

REFLECT

Spring rains bring us mud puddles, leave us digging through closets for umbrellas and jackets, and send us running inside for cover.

But that spring rain alsocleanses and brings about new life from the ground. Within days of those rains, we can see new growth of all kinds springing out of nowhere-completely changing the world around us.

In the same way, the springrain of the Spirit soaks us and quenches the seeds beneath our soil that in time will sprout and bear fruit. ThroughJesus, God created a way for his Spirit to be available to us all.We can delight in His Spirit, receiving it fully and allowing ourselves to be made new, re-created and ready to engage fully in the season of summer.

RESPOND
Take a moment and ask the Holy Spirit to soak into your heart like soil. Today spend time "soaking" in the Spirit. Be mindful of its presence.
