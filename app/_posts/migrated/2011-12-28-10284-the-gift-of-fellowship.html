Thursday, December 29

The Gift of Fellowship

READ

Ephesians 4:32-5:2(The Message)

PRAY

Ask God to strengthen you into a person who forgives without hesitating and loves without selfish expectation.

REFLECT

Henri Nouwen, priest and writer, on community:

"One of the most rewarding experiences of living in a strange land is the experience of being loved not for what we can do, but for who we are. When we becomes aware that our stuttering, failing, vulnerable selves are loved even when we hardly progress, we can let go of our compulsion to prove ourselves and be free to live with others in a fellowship of the weak. This is a true healing."

Henri Nouwen, The Modern Spirituality Series: Henri Nouwen

RESPOND

Think of someone who is hard for you to love. Be gentle with that person today.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is:1 Peter 5
