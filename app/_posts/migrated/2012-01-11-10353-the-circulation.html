Thursday, January 12

The Circulation

READ

John 10:14-15

REFLECT

If we learn to treat money as a resource that gets circulated through Gods Kingdom (no matter what amount passes through our hands, or how that amount changes year by year), then we knock money off its pedestal. And when we knock money off its pedestal, we will be able to give more easily, spend more easily...and receive more easily.

"I lay down my life for the sheep," Jesus says. We are children with an inheritance.

PRAY

Ask God to show you if theres any way that youre putting money on a pedestal.

RESPOND

In some way today, make a choice that puts money in its place: nothing more than a resource that gets circulated through the Kingdom.
