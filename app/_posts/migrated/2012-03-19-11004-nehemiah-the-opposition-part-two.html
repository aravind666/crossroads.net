Monday, March 19

Nehemiah: The Opposition, Part 2

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily is looking at people from the Bible that changed the game. This week continues our study on the story of Nehemiah.This study is presented in a paraphrase of the Bible called The Message, which gives us biblical text in simple, modern language. Try not to get hung up on the old-timey names and locations, and focus on what God might want to teach us through Nehemiahs story.

READ

Nehemiah 4:10-15

REFLECT

Fulfilling a God-sized vision isnt easy. On top of the hard work, we can be hit on all sides by naysayers and antagonists.

RESPOND

If you identify with the opposition facing Nehemiah and his co-workers, spend a moment today asking God to give you strength and perseverance, and to refresh the vision he has for you.
