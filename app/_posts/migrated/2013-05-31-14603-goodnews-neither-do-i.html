Friday, June 7

Goodnews: Neither do I

We finish out our week of looking at how Jesus gives grace and truth through a story in John.

READ

John 8:2-11

REFLECT

This was a heightened situation as the super religious Pharisees attempted to back Jesus into a corner. Can you feel the tension as they wait to see if he judges and throws stones or if he releases her and permits her sin? Jesus is bigger than that. The world will continue to create polarizing battles that pull people into divisive corners, but Jesus unites. The battle is truth vs. grace--or the world would stigmatize even further to say condemnation vs. permission. Jesus is truth and grace. Jesus says, I have the right to condemn, but dont. Go and leave your life of sin.

RESPOND

Journal today on whether you are more likely to default to truth or grace, both in your attitude toward others and toward yourself. Ask God to show you how it looks to be both.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Have you heard this story before? How did it affect you for the first time, or differently this time?

  • Do you connect more with the adulterous woman or with the Pharisees?

  • How do you want to grow in truth or grace?
