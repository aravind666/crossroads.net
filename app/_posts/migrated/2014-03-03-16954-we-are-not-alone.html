Wednesday, March 5

We Are Not Alone

This week were exploring what we can do when we find ourselves suffering or when we see trouble coming.

PRAY

Ask God to bring the right people alongside you in your trouble.

READ

Acts 14:19-20, Ecclesiastes 4:9-10

REFLECT

Paul was beaten and left for dead. His friends gathered around him and helped him get back on his feet. When were in trouble its so important to find people we can share our struggles with, to find someone who will walk alongside us and give us the encouragement we need to combat our pain. We were not created to go it alone.

RESPOND

Think of someone or a group of people you can include in your pain and trouble. It can be anyone, a friend, family or someone totally new, but they should be able to give you encouragement and walk with you through your suffering.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 2 Corinthians 11
