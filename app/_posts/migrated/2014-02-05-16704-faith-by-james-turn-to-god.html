Thursday, February 6

Faith by James: Turn to God

PRAY

Ask God to help you see how he wants you to grow today.

READ

James 1:12, John 16:20-22

REFLECT

We know that all of us will suffer. Theres no escaping it. Well continue to face hard situations, but what James is teaching us is that we can have a choice about how to handle our suffering. We can either turn away from God and other people, or we can continue to reflect Gods love and grace in the midst of our suffering. That goes just as well for the words we speak, the generosity we show and the favoritism we avoid.

PRACTICE

Make a choice today to turn toward God. Whether youre in the midst of suffering or life is pretty goodinvite God to be part of it. How can you reflect His love?
