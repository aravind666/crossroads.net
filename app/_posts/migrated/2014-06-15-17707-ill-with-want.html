Monday, June 16

Ill with Want

Our current series is all about redefining the words happiness, wealth, family and love. This week well explore what wealth means to us, no matter how much or little we have.

PRAY

Ask God to help you be more generous.

READ

Luke 12:34

REFLECT

Sometimes we let the things we want take ahold of us, controlling our thoughts and actions. Theres a song, "Ill With Want," by the Avett Brothers we think expresses just how this feels. Pay close attention to the lyrics and listen to the song by clicking the link below.

Listen to "Ill With Want" by the Avett Brothers

Excerpt:

Something has me

Oh something has me

Acting like someone I dont wanna be

Something has me

Oh something has me

Acting like someone I know isnt me

Ill with want and poisoned by this ugly greed

RESPOND

What are you "ill with want" over---a new tech device? A vacation? A larger paycheck? Today, replace that want with an act of generosity.
