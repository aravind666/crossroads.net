Thursday, August 4

Answered Prayers

God, give me humility. Please use me as a vessel of your love in someone elses life.

READ

Numbers 27:12-23

REFLECT

Moses is nearing the end of his life. He knows that, because he has disobeyed God, he wont be entering the Promised Land. But rather than argue with God, or sit in bitterness, Moses heart is for the Israelites. He advocates for them, asking God to send them someone to replace him. Not just a leader to give direction, but also a shepherd who will care for them. Gods answer is immediate: "Take Joshua."

How encouraging to see Moses leave on such a selfless high note. How awesome to see God honor Moses so instantly. And how humbling to be Joshua-the answer to a prayer.

RESPOND

Can you imagine yourself as the answer to someones prayer? Perhaps youve actually been told that you were. Of all the people on the planet, God picked you to be a vessel for him in that persons life. Today ask God to use you in someone elses life. And thank Him for the opportunity.
