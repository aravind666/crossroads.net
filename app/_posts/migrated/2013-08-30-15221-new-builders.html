Wednesday, September 4

New: Builders

We continue reading a passage in 1 Corinthians chapter 3. There is a shift in language today from planting to building.

READ

1 Corinthians 3:10-11

REFLECT

Yesterday, we saw ourselves as a field. Today, pause and see your faith as a building. This verse makes it clear that the only foundation that we can or should build on is Christ. If we want to begin to build new things or new things in people (remember the How To Build People series) we have to start with Jesus.

RESPOND

Ask God to point out some things in your life built on Christ and anything that isnt built on Jesus. Journal about it.
