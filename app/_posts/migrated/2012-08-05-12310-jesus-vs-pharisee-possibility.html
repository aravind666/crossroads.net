Jesus vs. Pharisee: Possibility

This week well look at Jesus healing the blind man in John 9. John calls all seven miracles in the book of John signs. This healing is a sign pointing to freedom.

READ

John 9:1-5

REFLECT

The hyper-religious Pharisees taught that imperfections like disease and physical challenges were the repercussions of sin, either within the individual or the family. Jesus teaching on this is all new. He sees possibility in our brokenness; its a possibility for God to engage and shine light. The night/day reference in verse four is a metaphor for life/death, and worthy of noting that he sayswein this verse.Weare called to join Jesus in Gods work.

RESPOND

Write down one thing that you consider not to be perfect in you. Ask God what he says about that thing, and write that down. Need more encouragement? Try this:2 Corinthians 12:9
