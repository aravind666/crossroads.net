Little Town: Birthplace of the King

Mary and Joseph made it to Bethlehem and God chose this unlikely place for the birth of his Son.

LISTEN

Little Town of Bethlehem

READ

Luke 2:6-7

REFLECT

Mary and Joseph had made it to Bethlehem, but the town was full, no bed or sofa to sleep on. So the couple ended up in a stable or a barn. We dont know exactly where they stayed, but we do know there were animals and a manger. It was a very humble spot.

After the long journey, it was time for Mary to give birth. In this unlikely place with two unlikely people, God began to rescue his people. When the baby was born, they named him Jesus, as the angels said. His mother wrapped him in scraps of cloth---not a cute onesie that said "Born in Bethlehem"---and laid him in the manger. This is where the King was born.

RESPOND

God shows up in the most unlikely places. What would be an unusual spot for Him to show up in your day? Whether its in the board room, at a stoplight or over the stovetop, pray that God shows up.
