Friday, December 14

Awaited Peace: Gift

We finish out the third week of Advent, looking not only at how Jesus was the prophesied peace, but how he brought us the gift of peace.

READ

John 14:26-27

REFLECT

Jesus was a gift that came in the strangest package: from a young teenage girls womb, in a manger, as a man. That man, Jesus, died and rose from the dead, and then left us with a gift-- a gift that means we have access to Gods peace for all time. He left us with the gift of the Holy Spirit and a very unique peace.

RESPOND

Ask God to give you more of his Spirit and the ongoing peace that the Spirit gives. Just sit quietly and let God give you a gift. If you are struggling with this, heres a verse to meditate on:1 John 3:19-24.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Give your best definition of peace.
  • Talk about one time that you have experienced real peace.
  • Do you give God access to your heart?
