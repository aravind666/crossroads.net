What a weekend.

 

Across the city, 7000 of us from 50 churches got dirty and sweaty, with paint-splattered hands and mulch-stained shirts, at one of 400 projects. And while all the projects were different, one similarity stood out: everyone was part of bringing hope and life to something that previously seemed hopeless and lifeless. New flowers replaced weeds. Vibrant murals covered dirty walls.

 

GO Cincinnati was an amazing day, but here are some ways you can GO and be the church 365 days a year:

  • Continue volunteering for the organization you served during GO Cincinnati. If you noticed other needs while you were there, feel free to grab your friends and make it happen.
  • Get engaged at Crossroads. Whether it’s digging deeper in ReachOut or serving regularly with Kids’ Club or First Impressions, there are always opportunities to be a blessing to others, while making friends as you do it.
  • Find ways to be a blessing in your neighborhood or at work. It could be as simple as mowing your neighbor’s lawn or offering to babysit for a friend. Just go make it happen.
