Gift from a Good Dad: Jess

On this Christmas Day, God has given us a Savior---his one and only Son to overcome the world. Today, read Jess story of how God grew her faith and her family.

READ

John 16:33

JESS STORY

I was convinced and believed the lie that I would have a really hard time conceiving children and becoming a mom. My friend heard from God and told me I would be healed and have children, yet I still doubted.

My husband and I tried to conceive for many months but nothing happened. Finally, I repented to others and asked Gods forgiveness for believing the lies and that God would never choose to heal me. A few weeks later I found out I was pregnant.

We now have a beautiful baby girl and another one on the way. I felt in my heart all along that God had created me with purpose and one of those purposes was to be a mom. Now I know that was a promise from God, and he had always intended to keep it.

RESPOND

Let Jess story be a reminder of our Saviors unexpected birth. With your family and friends or just one-on-one, thank your Dad for the best gift of all: a rescuer.
