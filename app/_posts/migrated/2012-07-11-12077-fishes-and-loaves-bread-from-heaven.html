Fishes and Loaves: Bread from Heaven

Weve been reading the story ofJesus miraculously feeding the crowd of over 5,000this week. Today well look at what happens next.

READ

John 6:48-51

REFLECT

After the miracle, Jesus speaks to his 12 closest disciples and nearby Jewish leaders. One of the main miracles that God had done for the Jewish people in the Old Testament was giving them manna (bread that came from heaven). Jesus referenced that story to make the point that he wasnt simply on earth to provide, but thathe was the actual provision.
