Wednesday, December 22

A Purpose Announced and Affirmed

READ

Luke 4:18-30
Luke 3:21-22
Mark 9:7

REFLECT

About 30 years after his birth, Jesus publicalLy declared the purpose of his ministry. He announced that he came to set the captives free-which was the fulfillment of his fathers thousand-year-old promise. He did what he said he would do. Then, as a proud father, God publically affirmed him.

Are you one of the captives Jesus set free? How were you set free?

RESPOND
Thank God for your freedom. Thank God for how he has set others free. Do something today that advances someones freedom.

ANOTHER LOOK
Galatians 5:1-2
