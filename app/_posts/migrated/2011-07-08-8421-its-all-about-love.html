Its All About Love

God, remove any obstacles from my life that prohibit me from receiving more of you and your love.

READ

John 14: 21-24

REFLECT

As children, we often show our parents affection through hugs, kisses and

handmade gifts. But one of the best ways to show them love is by obeying them. Thats

because love is more than a warm feeling; its an attitude that reveals itself in action.

When we love someone, we become willing to set aside our own feelings and desires for the other person. We naturally want to please them and make them happy.

Obedience is also the way that we show God that we love him. Jesus is pretty clear

about it, and he even repeats it several times. And as we obey, we experience Jesus

love in deeper ways. More of Gods character is revealed to us and we can

draw even closer to him.

RESPOND

On paper, reflect on all the ways that God has shown his love for you-maybe its how he has watched out for you, or prospered you through unexpected success in a business, or maybe youre especially thankful for your family and friends. Write down the ways you feel blessed. Also, highlight blessings youve received as a direct result of obedience to God.

The Friday edition of The Daily features discussion questions based on the weeks
