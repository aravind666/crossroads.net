Friday, March 23

Nehemiah: Celebration

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily is studying people from the Bible that changed the game. This week, were looking at the story of Nehemiah.

READ

Nehemiah 9:5-8

REFLECT

The book of Nehemiah is thirteen chapters long. Almost four of those chapters are devoted to meetings and celebrations held after Jerusalems wall was finished. What role does celebration play in your spiritual life?

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

The Friday edition of The Daily features discussion questions based on the weeks content. These questions are meant to spark conversation with your spouse, your roommate, or a small group of friends who also subscribe to The Daily.

  • What was the most striking part of Nehemiahs story for you this week?
  • As a group, discuss the main point of this story. Why do you think this book was included in the Bible?
  • Whats one way you can apply this main point to your lives in the coming week?
