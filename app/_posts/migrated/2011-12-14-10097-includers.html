Thursday, December 15

Includers

PRAY

By name, pray for the people youll interact with today.

READ

John 1:1-14

REFLECT

We might not all go the Tim Tebow route and paint "John 3:16" on our face, but we all have ways of spreading our excitement, even if theyre subdued. Yesterday, we considered those ways; in other words, we discovered our style of sharing what we love.

Consider the shepherds response when Jesus was born. They werent forced into anything. They didnt have a formula or try to mimic someone else. Rather, they had an experience with Jesus that they wanted to pass onto others. It wasnt just an abstract idea; it was a person they met and a power they saw.

RESPOND

Have you ever been in a situation where it felt completely natural to tell someone about how Jesus is part of your life? Be open this week to more of those situations. If this hasnt happened for you, dont feel guilty (because that guilt wouldnt be coming from God). Just spend some time praying for a friend.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
