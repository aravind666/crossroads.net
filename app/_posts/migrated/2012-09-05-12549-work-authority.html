Thursday, September 6

Work: Authority

When we put ourselves under Gods authority, we place ourselves under all authority.

READ

Romans 13:1-7

REFLECT

God ordered the universe, and he continues to allow structure on this planet for our benefit. Those in authority participate in Gods ordering even if they do it through their human imperfection. When we honor authority (whether its our boss or our governor), we honor God and his created order.

RESPOND

How might you treat or speak of co-workers, your boss, or the president differently if you saw them as a part of Gods design?

ANOTHER LOOK

If you struggle with looking at authority this way, you can also read1 Peter 2:13-15.
