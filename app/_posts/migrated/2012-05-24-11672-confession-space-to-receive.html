Confession: Space to Receive

Were learning about confession in this final week of our study on the book of James.

READ

Blessed is the one whose transgression is forgiven, whose sin is covered. Blessed is the man against whom the Lord counts no iniquity, and in whose spirit there is no deceit.

For when I kept silent, my bones wasted away through my groaning all day long.

Psalm 32:1-3(ESV)

REFLECT

The relief that comes from confessing something can feel like a blessing in itself. But by offloading the weight of our mistake and turning toward God, we also create new space to enjoy God and the good things he wants to give us.

RESPOND

Ask God to give you a glimpse of the blessings ahead of you.
