Wednesday, December 5

Awaited Joy: Shepherds Go

Today, we look at the shepherds response to the angels proclaiming Jesus birth. Advent season continues, and you can light a candle today to remember and celebrate joy with the angels and the shepherds.

READ

Luke 2:8-18

REFLECT

The angels broke into song mid-proclamation to the shepherds. The shepherds responded by going. They immediately went to see the Messiah with their own eyes, and then "spread the word" about what they had seen and learned. Joy beckons a response. When was the last time that you experienced this kind of joy; joy that longs to be expressed?

RESPOND

Be all about spreading joy today. If you feel empty of joy, ask God to give you joy to share.
