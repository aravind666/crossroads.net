Spirit: Sonship

We continue this week to look at the Holy Spirit as a part of our overall series on Gods identity.

READ

Luke 3:21-22

REFLECT

Jesus ministry began after this point. The Spirit descended on Him, and when it did, it marked him as a son. This is what the Spirit does for all of us--gifts us with adoption as sons and daughters. And the Lord doesnt ask us to act on his behalf until we receive that we are his children through the power of his Spirit. Think of the Spirit like having a permanent hashtag after your name. #childoftheKingoftheuniv...

RESPOND

Let God write you a letter today and you just take dictation. Start with Dear, ____ and end with Love, God.
