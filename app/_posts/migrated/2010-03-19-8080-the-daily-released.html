Friday, March 19
Released

Todays edition of The Daily focuses on an original song written for the Free journey by Robbie Reider. Follow along with the lyrics while you listen to the song. Ask God to meet you during this time, and to speak to your heart about the freedom he has in store for you.


From Robbie:
This song was inspired by a friend who carries a lot of weight on his shoulders: his job, family, personal expectations, desire to break habits. I imagined what I thought God would say to him about all the weight he carries. I imagined my friend as a farmer (weird, because my friend is NOTHING like a farmer), and heres why: its a farmers responsibility to prepare soil and plant seeds, but not to produce fruit. So I think God would say: its not yours to grow, not yours to carry, not yours to fully comprehend. And Im no different from my friend. I carry weight that was never mine to carry. Im just a farmer. Arent we all?
