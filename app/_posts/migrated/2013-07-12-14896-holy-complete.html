Friday, July 19

Holy: Complete

We finish our week looking at Gods holiness and what it looks like when we let it press in on our lives.

READ

Romans 8:29-30

REFLECT

God planted his image in all of us since the beginning of time. He desires to uncover that image, and for us to discover it. When we submit our lives to the creators care, He doesnt give up. He will begin that process of forming us and bring to full completion what he started. Hope is believing that you will be with Him someday, and that you will be complete, lacking nothing.

RESPOND

Journal today about what it might be like to run into your heavenly dads arms one day.

ANOTHER LOOK

James 1:2-4

DISCUSSION

You can use these suggestions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Share one thing that you are hearing from God in the middle of that pain.

  • Pray encouraging prayers for each other.
