Game Change

During Game Change, The Daily will serve the role of encourager during the spiritual journey we began a few weekends ago at Crossroads. READ

Psalm 46:10

REFLECT

This weekend, Chuck discussed the importance of listening to God about the stuff in our life. He emphasized the need for us to "lengthen the listen" by planning times to strategically hear from God.

RESPOND

How are you going to lengthen the listen in your life this week? What are some specific ways you will carve out time to hear from God?
