Friday, September 14

Work: Dressed for Success

Today, we finish looking at work and life through the book of Colossians.

READ

Colossians 3:12-14

REFLECT

Maybe you dont wear a uniform for work, but workplaces commonly have an unwritten code on clothing (like your typical tattooed, black T-shirted coffee shop barista). Following Jesus has its own style requirements. This list in Colossians sounds like a good wardrobe to wear--whether youre playing the role of co-worker, friend, spouse, student, parent or barista.

RESPOND

Thank someone today for a way you see them operating in this list from Colossians 3.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

  • What kinds of things besides God do you allow to give you purpose?
  • Of Fridays "clothing items" list, whats one do you want to focus on?
