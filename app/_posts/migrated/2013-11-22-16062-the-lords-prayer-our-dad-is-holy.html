Monday, November 25

The Lords Prayer: Our Dad is Holy

The disciples watched and listened to how Jesus prayed. There was something different about it and they wanted in. They asked him "Lord, teach us to pray." So, Jesus did.

This week, The Daily will break down the Lords Prayer, line by line.

READ

Matthew 6:9

REFLECT

Praying is a major part of our relationship with God and the first line of the Lords Prayer tells us who God is in that relationship. Hes our Dad. And we can have a real, intimate relationship with Him. At the same time, Hes different from us. He is holy--without sin or any of that icky, kingdom-of-the-world stuff. When we pray we remember how we want to live in Gods likeness so his glory will be revealed.

RESPOND

Today, pray to your Dad in heaven and think about what it means to have a relationship with him.
