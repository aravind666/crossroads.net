Week 1: Not What We Expected. The Life of Jesus.

Monday, February 21

What Are You Expecting?

Spend a few minutes asking God to help you set aside your assumptions about Jesus over the next four weeks, and to prepare you for a refreshed understanding of him.

READ

Isaiah 53

REFLECT

Chances are good its happened to you before: You bought a crummy car because the exterior looked good, you got into a bad business deal because it seemed lucrative, you wrote off a friendship because of a snap judgment, and so on.

It rarely changes with age. Regardless of the context, we tend to see exactly what we want to see.

The same rings true when it comes to Jesus. The expectations we bring to the table significantly impact the way we understand and relate to Him today. So for the next four weeks, were going to take a fresh look at the life of Jesus--and attempt to broaden or change our expectations of him.

RESPOND

Have you been in a situation where you made a poor decision based on idealized or faulty expectations? Whats a possible thing or experience you missed out on?

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
