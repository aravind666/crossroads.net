Wednesday, January 11

Tunnel Vision

READ

Luke 16:13-15

REFLECT

If we let money take an "owner" seat in our life, its usually not intentional or obvious.

But one indication that were giving undue authority to our money is when we see a paycheck (or its equivalent) as our only real resource. This paycheck tunnel vision blocks us from seeing the full goodness of God the shepherd. It keeps us from seeing how He is an abundant provider and has creative ways for delivering those provisions to each of us.

RESPOND

Consider the resources God gives you outside of a paycheck. These can be either literal or abstract. So think of what you get from friends, family, your workplace, government funds, school systems, side jobs, etc. If youre coming up short, ask someone what resources they see in your life.

PRAY

Thank God for how hes providing for you.
