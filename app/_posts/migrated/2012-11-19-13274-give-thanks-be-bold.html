Tuesday, November 20

Give Thanks: Be Bold

This week we are looking at thankfulness. Yesterday, we kicked the blues with thanks; today, we conquer fear.

READ

Psalm 56:10-13

REFLECT

Think about some of the most difficult times in your life, and imagine, just for a second, that God protected you in those moments. Weve all experienced hard times, but how much more did God rescue us from? Then take some time to think about blessings. Its likely there are many, and maybe its time to go public with thanking God for his role in your life. Going public with thanking God is a powerful fear buster.

RESPOND

Tell one person a way that you are thankful to God. Be bold. Journal about the experience.
