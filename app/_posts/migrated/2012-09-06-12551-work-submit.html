Friday, September 7

Work: Submit

Today we continue to look at those in authority as part of Gods design.

READ

Hebrews 13:17

REFLECT

When we know that authorities are a part of Gods design to give freedom through order, its easier to submit to that authority. The good news is, submitting to others benefitsus. When you bring joy to those who lead, it comes right back at you. This is true for those who are over you spiritually, and a principle just as true in the workplace.

RESPOND

Readers choice today--pick one:

1. Think of one way you can intentionally make your bosss job a joy today. Then do it.

2. Take a moment to encourage someone who leads you spiritually.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Is it difficult or easy for you to submit? Why do you think that is?
  • How do you give God authority over your work?
  • Who do you see as spiritual authorities in your life?
