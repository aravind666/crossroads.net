Tuesday, February 7

The Heart of an Underdog

READ

1 Samuel 16:1-13

REFLECT

The nation of Israel was looking for a leader. God sent the prophet Samuel to the house of Jesse in Bethlehem to anoint a new king. God warned Samuel not to be concerned with outward appearances, because what he cared about was the heart. After rejecting all but one of Jesses sons, God chose the youngest, a shepherd boy named David, to be king. And "from that day on the Spirit of the LORD came powerfully upon David."

RESPOND

Underdogs lean into Gods grace because they know they cant succeed by relying on their own abilities. Today, identify an area in your life where you feel like an underdog. Ask God to search your heart, and tell him you cant overcome whatever it is that youre facing by yourself. Lean into Gods grace, and trust that his power will work through you.
