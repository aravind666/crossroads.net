Tuesday, January 17

One or the Other

READ

1 Timothy 6:9-10

REFLECT

Fall, trap, plunge, wandered, pierced?Not a feel-good picture. This language surrounding the love of money in Timothy is starkly different from yesterdays image of a treasure hidden in a field and a man filled with joy. With contrasts and teachings like this throughout the Bible (800+ times), we can see that God is serious about putting a leash on money.

RESPOND

When youre faced with your next financial decision (which will probably happen in the next few hours), step back for a minute and consider which kingdom youd be serving: Gods or moneys.

PRAY

God, give me your spirit of discernment as Im making choices about how to use the financial resources youve given me.
