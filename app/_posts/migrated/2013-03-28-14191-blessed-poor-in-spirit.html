Monday, April 1

The best posture to receive exactly what Jesus did for us in death and resurrection comes from the very first large scale teaching, the sermon on the mount (mountain/valleys have great acoustics). They are called the beatitudes which stems from a word that means happiness, joy, or blissful.

READ

Matthew 5:1-3

REFLECT

What does it mean to be poor in spirit? Being poor in finances means you are in a place of dependence needing to trust day to day. God desires for our spirits to be in that place, longing for him day to day, and putting our dependence on him. When we focus on all that we have done right in our life, we dont need God. When we realize our poverty compared to Gods wealth, we understand the weight of our sin that Christ bore on the cross. When we are poor in spirit, the blessing isnt heaven in the future but a sense of knowing and experiencing it now.

RESPOND

Sit and contemplate today the poverty of your spirit, all of the ways that you are in need of God.
