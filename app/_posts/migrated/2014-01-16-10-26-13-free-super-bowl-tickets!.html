We’re excited to announce that tickets for the 2014 Super Bowl of Preaching services will be available this Friday. (Oh, you thought we were talking about a different Super Bowl? Sorry about that.) 

 

Here’s what you need to know:

 

GET YOUR TICKETS THIS FRIDAY

Free tickets for Florence, Mason and Oakley will be available starting at 10am on Friday, January 17. Go to crossroads.net/superbowl for service times and ticket reservations.

 

BRING YOUR BUDDIES

The Super Bowl of Preaching is one of the easiest ways to introduce your friends to God. It’s just like Awaited---except that we switch out all the artistry and beauty with absurdity and dumb jokes. If you have a friend willing to check out a church service with tailgating and a halftime show, this is the weekend to bring him. You could start by forwarding the link to this year’s Super Bowl teaser video.
