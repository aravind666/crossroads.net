If you teach in a ministry area at Crossroads (Community Groups, Kids' Club, CSM), youre invited to the Teachers Class to learn and practice the skills of effective communication. Youll also learn a simple method for reading the Bible for application. The class meets four consecutive Thursdays: January 28, February 4, 11, and 18 from 6:30-8:30pm at the Oakley campus.

You must be able to attend all sessions, as space is limited. Sign up today by sending us an email.
