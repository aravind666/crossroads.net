Wednesday, October 20

A Woman Gives Her Last

Ask God to help you trust him as you learn about surrender.

READ

1 Kings 17:7-15

REFLECT

How would you respond if a stranger asked you to forego your last bit of food so they could have a meal? The widow in the story from 1 Kings is faced with that decisionand the choice she made led to unexpected abundance. What strikes you about her response?

RESPOND

Ask God to increase your ability to freely surrender your life and your stuff to him.
