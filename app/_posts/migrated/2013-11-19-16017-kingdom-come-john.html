Kingdom Come: John

John was a regular Northern Kentucky guy with a day job. Then God showed him the mission of the Kingdom and it changed everything.

CLICK HERE TO WATCH JOHNS STORY

REFLECT

Jesus asks us to go on mission with him. In Matthew 9:35-38he says that meeting the needs of the Kingdom of God will require more hands to do the work. Like John, we all get to be part of the mission when we obey our Dad.

RESPOND

Where is God asking you to join in the mission of his Kingdom? Spend a few, quiet minutes asking your Dad how you can make the Kingdom come.
