REFLECT

In the first line of Genesis we learn that God is a creator. He created light and darkness, heavens and the earth, buffalo and flamingos. And then He made man in Genesis 1:26-31. He created man and woman in his likeness and gave them his creation. Their relationship was perfect. And life was so good.

RESPOND

Imagine a world where there is no sadness or pain. Its perfect. Its bright and full of light.
