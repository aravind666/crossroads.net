Boldly Authentic: Get Vulnerable

This week were learning four ways to live boldly authentic lives of faith, based on Jesus example in the book of John.

READ

John 11:32-37

REFLECT

Jesus bold vulnerability helped his followers relate to him. He knew that this story had a happy ending (Jesus was about to raise Lazarus from the dead), but he still allowed himself to embrace the moment and be authentic in front of others.

PRAY

God, thank you for Jesus willingness to model a life of bold authenticity for us. Show me areas of my life where I can open up more, and give me opportunities to be vulnerable with my friends.
