We will finish our week of looking at Jesus speaking on money and resources in Matthew. Focus on verse 24 today.

READ

Matthew 6:19-24

REFLECT

You have evaluated your resources this week and noted what has your eye. This last verse brings it all together with a powerful punch. We can spend a lot of time assessing our resources and our use of time. We can feel like there is room for growth in what has our attention, and in giving of resources, but Jesus uses stronger language. Evaluation is the right place to start, but we can not live in the gray. We must begin to make decisions and move toward God holistically.

RESPOND

Today, look back over your evaluation from the week, and ask God to point out an area where he wants you to move.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • What did you discover as a resource that you hadnt discovered before?
  • What is one thing you want to stop or start that pertains to your eyes?
  • Read Revelations 3:14-18 together and discuss how our culture might be like Laodicea.
