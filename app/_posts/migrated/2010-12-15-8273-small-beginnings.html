Wednesday, December 15

Small Beginnings

READ

Luke 2:1-7

REFLECT

God our Father determined that the birth of his son, Jesus-though long sought after and eagerly hoped for-would take place in a simple place, removed from family and friends. All of the thousands of years and many words about rejoicing over the deliverer were mysteriously unobservable when there was no room in the inn. Do you have room for this birth?

RESPOND
God has a way of producing big endings from small, simple beginnings. How has God produced something big in your life from small beginnings, whether thats in your relationships, your giving to others or your talents? Thank God for them.
