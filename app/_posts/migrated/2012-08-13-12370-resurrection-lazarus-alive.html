Thursday, August 16

Resurrection: Lazarus Alive

Today we continue in reading John 11, where Jesus has gone to see his dying friend Lazarus.

READ

John 11:38-55

REFLECT

Jesus did a miraculous and almost startling thing here, but Marthas concern was the smell! Seeing someone walk out of a tomb, mummy-style, must have been a sight to behold. Yet, how often do we avoid what God might want to do for something as simple as an inconvenience?

RESPOND

Ask God right now to tell you where there is a bad odor in your life...what does he want to resurrect in you?

ANOTHER LOOK

1 Corinthians 15:35-38
