Big Vision Culture: Holy Fear

Today, we do a flyby look at the very complicated story of Noah. #floodsareforsuckers

READ

Hebrews 11:7

REFLECT

Its hard to wrap up the story of Noah in a 15 minute look, but Noah was one of our faith fathers. When rain had never happened on the earth--he built a boat. Noah had favor with God. God could have told everyone to build a boat (I dont know, he might have), but hearing from God does not equal faith. Noah acted. Building the boat is an act of holy fear. It makes the statement--I fear and value Gods opinion of me more than any humans assessment.

RESPOND

Be about faith-doing today instead of faith-believing. Is there an act of faith that you could be about today that might even bring about holy fear? Walk into it.
