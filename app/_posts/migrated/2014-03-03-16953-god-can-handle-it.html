Tuesday, March 4

God Can Handle It

This week were exploring what we can do when were suffering or when we see trouble coming.

PRAY

Ask God for comfort and guidance in your suffering or trouble.

READ

Psalm 27:14, Philippians 4:6-7

REFLECT

When we are going through hard stuff, its so easy to turn inward, to keep our troubles to ourselves. But the first thing we should do---and not just when were suffering---is turn toward God. While we cant expect Him to automatically change our circumstances, when we include God in our pain we can find comfort and healing.

RESPOND

Tell God everything youre going through and how youre feeling. Its OK if youre mad or really sad. God can handle it and get you through it.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 2 Corinthians 10
