Thursday, September 26

Culture: Put Off

We continue adding another verse to the Ephesians 4 passage, today focus on verses 22-23.

READ

Ephesians 4:18-23

REFLECT

If you are looking for truth and clarity in your life--youll find it in Jesus. The culture of this world will continue to give you confusion through self-focused and deceiving desires. We want to take off those old ways like dirty clothes and throw them in the hamper.

RESPOND

Yesterday, we looked at ways the world has infected cultures that you participate in. Today, specifically pray for God to invade those arenas of your life.
