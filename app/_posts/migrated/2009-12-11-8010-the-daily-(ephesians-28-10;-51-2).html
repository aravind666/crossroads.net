Friday, December 11
A Look Back

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.

READ
Ephesians 2:8-10; 5:1-2


REFLECT
Today well look back at where weve been in the last month and take some time to reflect. One of the best ways to do this is through the discipline of journaling. Youll definitely want to have a journal or something to write on for todays exercise.

To start off, open your Bible and copy down Ephesians 2:8-10 and 5:1-2 as one solid paragraph, then read through it a couple of times. This is Ephesians in a nutshell.

  • The first part of Ephesians is all about how God has pursued us and loved us. The sacrifice that Jesus made on the cross restores our connection with God, if were willing to accept it.
  • The latter part of the book is all about how connecting with the love of God naturally changes us. We live a new life not out of guilt or obligation, but because we are filled with the love He has shown us.


RESPOND
Spend the rest of your time today writing down any a-has youve experienced during your time with God over the last four weeks. Maybe there are key verses that stand out. Maybe you had an experience based on one of the prompts you followed through with. Perhaps it was a conversation with a friend that really put things into perspective for you. Whatever its been, take some time to remember how youve experienced God.

After you have finished capturing your thoughts, say a prayer thanking God for what you have learned and experienced.
