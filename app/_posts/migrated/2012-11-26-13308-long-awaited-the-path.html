Tuesday, November 27

Long Awaited: The Path

The journey of the Old Testament points toward hope of a coming Messiah (the promised and expected deliverer of Gods people). Today, well take another step through prophecy toward Jesus.

READ

Isaiah 40:3-5

REFLECT

This is a prophecy of Jesus, but also of John the Baptist, who was the one calling in the desert (Matthew 3:1-3). John the Baptist (or baptizer) was Jesus cousin who leapt in his mother Elizabeths womb when Mary came near while pregnant with Jesus. John the Baptist comprehended in the womb that Jesus was the hoped-for king. John cleared a path for Jesus, calling people to repent, and baptizing them, opening their hearts to recognize the unexpected Messiah.

RESPOND

Lets agree with John the Baptist today. Take a moment and repent or confess (in your journal) ways that Christmas sometimes doesnt meet your expectations. Youll be better prepared to be tender hearted, and to look and hope for Jesus through this December.
