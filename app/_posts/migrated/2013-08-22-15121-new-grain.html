Monday, August 26

New: Grain

For the next next four weeks well be looking at Gods love of the new: new territory, making us new, and today, new grain.

READ

Leviticus 23:16

REFLECT

So, why new grain? This was an offering made during the Jewish feast of the weeks, but why couldnt they just bring whatever they had--like an old canned good to support the food pantry? When it comes to making an offering to God, he doesnt want our old and worn-out leftovers. God deserves our newest, our freshest, our first. Whatever is new to us captures our heart a little bit more, and the truth is--God wants our heart.

RESPOND

Do you have new ideas that have been brewing in your mind lately? Write them down. This doesnt mean that you are committing to them--dont commit to do anything. Offer them up to God and see what he has to say in response.
