Wednesday, September 12

Work: Look for God

Were looking at the book of Colossians for wisdom on life and work.

READ

Colossians 2:8-10

REFLECT

There are two forces we can lean on: God (whose authority brings purpose and meaning to life), or the philosophies of this world (with quick fixes yielding long-term emptiness). You can be dazzled by carny tricks, or push past and look for what God has to say to you.

RESPOND

Do you have moments where you feel drawn in by the culture of your environment? Take a moment today to look for God, even if it requires stepping outside.
