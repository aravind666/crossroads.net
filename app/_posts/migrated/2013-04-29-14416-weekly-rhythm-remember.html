Tuesday, April 30

We will continue to look at the weekly rhythm of Sabbath.

READ

Deuteronomy 5:15

REFLECT

God delivered the Israelite nation out of slavery in the Old Testament, and then he began to train them how to live like free men and women. One way was to give them a law about resting--Sabbath. This was a constant reminder of Gods goodness, and the opposite of slavery, which was constant toil.

Its easy to become addicted to work even if you are not a slave. Its a powerful thing to be able to create, produce, make money, and generally get it done. If were not careful, what makes us feel in control begins to control us. Sabbath gives us a day to remember that we are not slaves, and that God, our Father, loves us without our needing to earn it.

RESPOND

Take time to remember today. You can remember how far he has brought you, or spend time reminding yourself that God values you, not what you do.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
