Monday, December 12

Christmas Evangelism

PRAY

Ask God to direct your thoughts as you read the scripture from Isaiah below a few times, reflecting on how Jesus is described.

READ

Isaiah 9:6-7

REFLECT

Somewhere along the line, someone invited you to meet Jesus. Maybe that invitation came from a friend, a group of people, an author, a parent. Whoever it was, that person had received someone (Jesus) and then relayed their experience with Him to you.

Inviting people into knowing Jesus is not about selling an idea. Its about a person--and a relationship.

RESPOND

If you had to pick one characteristic of Jesus that draws you to him, what would it be? Write down one way youve experienced that characteristic (and hold onto it; well pick it back up tomorrow).

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Hebrews 12
