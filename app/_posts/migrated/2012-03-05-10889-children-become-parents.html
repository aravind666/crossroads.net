Friday, March 9

Children Become Parents

This week, were wrapping up our study on what it means to live as treasured children of God.

READ

1 John 3:2

REFLECT

As our relationships with God mature, we reflect his heart more and more. With this maturity comes the opportunity to become spiritual parents, sharing our journeys with others and walking alongside them as they grow closer to God.

RESPOND

The world needs good parents. If this notion of (physical or spiritual) parenting resonates with you, ask God today what your next steps might be.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

The Friday edition of The Daily features discussion questions based on the weeks content. These questions are meant to spark conversation with your spouse, your roommate, or a small group of friends who also subscribe to The Daily.

  • Share a way that you enjoyed approval, appetite or ambition this week. What did that experience reveal about Gods nature?
  • Discuss why you think God gave us appetite, ambition and a need for approval. How do you think this fits into His design for us?
  • Whats one way over the next few weeks to help someone else know that he or she is Gods treasured child?
