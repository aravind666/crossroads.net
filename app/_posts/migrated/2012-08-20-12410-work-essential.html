Work: Essential

Like cereal is "an essential part of this nutritious breakfast," work is an essential part of a healthy life.

READ

Ecclesiastes 3:12-13

REFLECT

Ecclesiastes is written by an older man looking back and considering his life (most likely Solomon, the wise king). He teaches that work is not only an important component of a life well lived--its agift from God.

RESPOND

ReadProverbs 14:23.Ask God where he wants to profit you in the place where you work currently.
