The Lords Prayer: Our Dad Provides

The Daily continues to break down the Lords Prayer, line by line.

READ

Matthew 6:11

CLICK HERE TO WATCH JON AND JESS STORY

REFLECT

Jon and Jess trusted their Dad would provide. In this line of the prayer, we learn what it means to be fed, no matter our hunger. Whether physical, spiritual or emotional our Dad cares for our everyday needs. We can go to Him because we know and trust he will provide for us.

RESPOND

On this Thanksgiving Eve you might be preparing for tomorrows meal or reconnecting with family. In the midst of the holiday preparations, pray about how your Dad feeds you beyond your dinner plate.
