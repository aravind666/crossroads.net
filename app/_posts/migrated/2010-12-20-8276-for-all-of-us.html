Week 3: A Fathers Gift

Monday, December 20

For All of Us

READ
Matthew 2:1-12


REFLECT

The wise men (magi) who visited Jesus after his birth werent Jewish, nor were they friends or neighbors of Mary and Joseph. Yet, God called them to worship Jesus as a sign that the existence of this child was for all peopleJews and Gentiles alike. (In fact, Gentiles would worship Him before many Jews.)

The wise mens gifts to Jesus reflect the immense value of his birth, life and death. Do you receive this birth as a treasured gift? Are you excited for all to share in it?

RESPOND
The wise men were only a part of the divine birth announcement that included the angels, the shepherds and a holy man named Simeon. Ask God for help more deeply treasuring the gift of Jesus. Pray for someone who is far from God to share in it with you today.

ANOTHER LOOK
