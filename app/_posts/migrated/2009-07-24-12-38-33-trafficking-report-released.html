Today the National Underground Railroad Freedom Center released a study of human trafficking in the Cincinnati area. The Greater Cincinnati Human Trafficking Report is the first of its kind locally, and is the result of a year-long study in which nearly 140 in-depth interviews were conducted with law enforcement personnel, judges, social workers, healthcare providers, government leaders and other affected parties.

Many volunteers from Crossroads' Justice Team were instrumental in the creation and distribution of this report, which highlights the fact that slavery continues to persist around the world and even here at home.
