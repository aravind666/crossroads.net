Wednesday, March 19

Grace Closes the Gap

PRAY

As you read, ask God to be present with you.

READ

Romans 5:21, John 17:1-3

REFLECT

Our world was once a place ruled by sin. We were separated from God. And even though it still sometimes feels this way, God is good and his grace rules. When He sent Jesus to rescue us, everything changed. Once apart from God, Jesus closed the gap so we could have an intimate, close relationship with him. So we could know Him. This is how we receive eternal life.

RESPOND

God wants to know you. If He were sitting right next to you, what would you say to him? Dont worry about preparing a speech, just tell God whats on your heart and mind. Hes listening.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Ephesians 2
