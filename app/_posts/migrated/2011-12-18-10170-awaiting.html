Monday, December 19

Awaiting

LISTEN

O Come Emmanuel

READ

Matthew 1:18-23

REFLECT

Its one thing for someone to say, "Im with you." Its another thing entirely for someone to say their name, their very identity, is "I am with you." Emmanuel. This is how God describes himself to the people who had waited generation after generation for him to return. People starved for rescue and love.

When we receive God as the life-changer he is, theres no space, no gap, no distance between him and us. It doesnt matter what the day, the hour or the circumstance brings. He is with us. The intimate relationship we were designed for is fully restored.

The wait is over.

RESPOND

Spend a few moments in silence and simply let God be with you. No words, no requests. Just spend time in His presence. (If you want, listen to the above song again as a way to connect with Him.)

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 1 John 4
