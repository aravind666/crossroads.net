Wednesday, November 21

Give Thanks: Build Trust

We have practiced thankfulness this week to the tune of clearing out sadness, conquering fear, and today, growing in trust.

READ

Psalm 52:8-9

REFLECT

When we thank God in the midst of difficulty, we grow roots. When we thank God in front of others, we grow topside. When we continually thank God, we grow like the olive tree in this verse. We grow in our trust of God, and we mature in our faith. Olive trees can have thick trunks with age, and as we practice giving God credit instead of taking credit (thats thankfulness), we can grow sturdy in our faith.

RESPOND

Today, practice giving God credit for anything and everything: a nice temperature in the room, a moment of free time, a drink of water, a good song on the radio, or taking a breath. Journal about it.
