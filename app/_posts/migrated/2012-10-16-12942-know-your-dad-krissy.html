Know Your Dad: Krissy

Krissy is a really sharp business consultant in her thirties. She is married and pregnant with her third daughter.

MEET KRISSY

"When I just sit quietly and listen to God, he always calls me his baby. His heart toward me is tender, protective, and gentle. His desire is to hold me in his arms, to care for me and nurture me. There are so many scriptures that tell me I am his daughter. So, when its hard for me to believe I just go to them.

He tells me that he knows everything about me inPsalm 139:13-14.He even says: the hairs on your head are numbered (Matthew 10:29-31), I made you in my image (Genesis 1:27), and your name is written on the palm of my hand (Isaiah 49:15-16).
