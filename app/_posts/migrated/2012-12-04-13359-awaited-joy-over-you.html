Friday, December 7

Awaited Joy: Over You

Yesterday, we looked at Mary treasuring the knowledge of Jesus identity up in her heart. Today, lets experience the joy of being adopted into the family through Jesus, and experience Gods fatherly joy over us.

READ

Zephaniah 3:16-17

REFLECT

Zephaniah is foretelling Jesus. There was, indeed, celebration when Jesus came: the angels, shepherds, Mary and many others, celebrated. Can you feel the celebration in your own heart when you read verse 17? Can you feel Gods joy over you? He calms your fears and He sings over you. Gods great plan to rescue and adopt you started with Jesus. Your heart can celebrate, too, as you receive Gods joy over you.

RESPOND

Listen again to a little Christmas music (Download the Awaited Musichere). Imagine that it is God singing over you.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • When were some moments of true joy in your life?
  • Are you more like the angels, shepherds or Mary in Luke 2?
  • What is it like for you to picture God singing over you?
