Outsiders

READ

Matthew 2:1-12

LISTEN

We Three Kings

REFLECT

These scholars--these magi--came in from the outside. They werent from Israel. They had no desperate history of waiting, no cultural history of exile and persecution, no intimate awareness of God and his promise--only the knowledge theyd gained from ancient texts that a beacon would guide them to a king. So when they saw the star, they followed.

God knew they would be looking.

RESPOND

Have you ever felt like God connected with you in a place you hadnt expected to find him? Maybe it felt like an ironic situation, an uncanny connection or even a brilliant miracle. Spend some time thanking God for revealing himself to you in those times.
