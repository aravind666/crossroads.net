Wednesday, October 13

A Place for our Friends

Take a moment to consider the people in your life who are far from God.

READ

Mark 2:1-5

REFLECT

Who does a financial campaign in the midst of the Great Recession? People who are passionate about having a place where their friends can encounter Jesus.

RESPOND

Today, you read about what happens when friends bring friends to Jesus. Pray for the people in your life who are far from God. Pray for them to experience a genuine encounter with Jesus.
