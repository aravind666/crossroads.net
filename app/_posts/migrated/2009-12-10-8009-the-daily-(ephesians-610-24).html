Thursday, December 10
Ready for Battle

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.

READ
Ephesians 6:10-24

REFLECT
Why would the armor of God be the wrap-up in Pauls mind? After all of the discussion on the character and actions of God, the love that he has shown us and the change that it causes in our lives, doesnt a battle cry seem out of place?

Of course it does, unless we are actually in a war.

Unless there are actually forces out there that want to keep us from experiencing and living in the love that God has for us. Unless we are actually in need of some armor, some protection from the battle that rages in and around us.

And if we believe what Paul has written thus far, then chances are pretty good hes right on this one, as well.

  • How does the idea of a spiritual war make you feel?
  • How might the armor Paul describes make a difference in your daily life?


RESPOND
The idea of spiritual warfare can be overwhelming or confusing, but the important thing to remember is that we are connected to and protected by God himself. Our strength comes from our dependence on Him. Here is a prayer you can use to help you remember and refocus:

God, I know that there is more to this world than what I can see with my own eyes. Help me to walk in your strength and protection, and fill me with your power.
