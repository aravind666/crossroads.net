Week 3: Movement

Monday, October 3

Move Courageously

Ask God to give you courage today.

READ

Daniel 6

REFLECT

Make no mistake about it: Daniel knew exactly what was going down. You dont spend a lifetime in politics without learning who your enemies are and what everyone is planning. When Daniel saw this law, he knew it was geared at one person. Him.

And he had a choice to make. He very easily could have stopped praying, or hidden it away, or even simply changed his daily routine. After all, he was so predictable that even his enemies knew what time of day they could catch him in the act. Yet he didnt. He moved forward into a choice that could have cost him his life, trusting that God would be with him, regardless.

RESPOND

Often it isnt the big moments where we lose our courage. It is the small ones--the temptation to cut short a prayer, to join in on a joke, to listen to the latest gossip. Take a few minutes and ask God to show you one small area where he wants you to be courageous and stand for him today.
