Wednesday, February 16
A Bigger God

God stir up an awe and wonder of who you are in my heart.


READ
Psalm 147

REFLECT

Many times in songs, we exaggerate for the sake of effect. We sing things like, you are better than the best thing of all time or I love you more than my iPhone, but we dont really mean it. (More than my iPhone? Lets not get crazy here.)

But when it comes to God, there is no exaggeration. All the statements made here in Psalm 147 are 100% true with no embellishment. But is this how we see Him?

How does this song make you feel? Why?

RESPOND

Take a few minutes to read back through this song and pick out a couple of the descriptions that stand out. Then sing (or just say, if you prefer) them to God to tell him what you think of him.
