Big Vision Culture: Foreigner

Abraham is epic. Lets look at him today while thinking of him as our own great-great-grandfather. #epicgp #whosyourdaddy

READ

Hebrews 11:8-10

REFLECT

Abraham left his family and all that he knew and followed God to a a beautiful but uninhabited land. When he got there, he could have set about creating a house or even a city for his family to live in forever, since God promised it to him and all of his generations--right? Abraham decided that setting the mindset of his family in eternity was a better place to start a generational city, so his family lived in tents for three generations. Abrahams long-term vision wasnt just housing foundations, but solid spiritual ground to build a family and a nation on.

RESPOND

Where are you jumping into worldly markers of achievement (like houses or other material goods) when God wants to build spiritual foundations in you? (hint: where are you suffering?)

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
