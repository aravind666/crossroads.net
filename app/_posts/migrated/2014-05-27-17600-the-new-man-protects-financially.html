Thursday, May 29

The New Man Protects Financially

The Daily continues to explore what the Bible says about men. This week, well focus on how the New Man is a protector.

PRAY

Ask God to show you how to protect your finances for the future.

READ

2 Corinthians 9:6-8

REFLECT

Being a provider is a tough job especially when their are dual incomes and a list of expenses. Single, dating or married, this conversation is never easy, but the truth is that God calls us to be good stewards of our money. That requires us to think about how were spending money today and how we can protect our finances for tomorrow. When men serve as financial protectors they are providers who recognize what is needed today and have an eye on what is needed for the future.

RESPOND

What can you do today to better protect your money? Maybe you pack your lunch, take better care of your car, or set up a savings account. Go and do it.
