Friday, July 25

Approval: When Freedom Comes

This week, were spending time receiving Gods approval, as well as identifying areas of our lives where we unnecessarily seek the approval of others.

LISTEN

When Freedom Comes

READ

2 Corinthians 3:17

John 14:27

1 John 4:16-18

REFLECT

Gods love for us is more than a spiritual stamp of approval. He generously shows his approval again and again with gifts like freedom, peace and love.

RESPOND

Like weve done all week, identify a moment today when you realize youre seeking the approval of others. Try replacing that need for approval with the knowledge that God generously and actively approves of you.

PRAY

Thank God for his generous love. Ask Him for the strength to live from his approval every day.
