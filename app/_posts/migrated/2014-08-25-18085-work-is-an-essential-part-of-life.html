Tuesday, August 26

Work is an Essential Part of Life

Like cereal is an essential part of this nutritious breakfast, work is an essential part of a healthy life.

READ

Ecclesiastes 3:12-13

REFLECT

Ecclesiastes is written by an older man looking back and considering his life (most likely Solomon, the wise king). He teaches that work is not only an important component of a full and good life--its a gift from God. So lets start living and get to work.

RESPOND

Read Proverbs 14:23. Ask God where he wants to profit you in the place where you work currently.
