Friday, January 1
Gift of Redemption

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.


READ
Isaiah 53:4-6


REFLECT
Christmas is a crucial piece of the redemption story that God has been telling throughout history. What we can see from Jesus life is that God will go to any length to restore our broken lives.

All of the mistakes, the struggles, and the pain of this past yearGod wants to redeem it all, if we will let him. We can start this New Year with a clean slate and fill it back up with the adventurous and love-filled life He promises.

  • What do you want God to redeem from the past year? Whatever comes to mind, write it in your journal.
  • What could that restoration look like?

RESPOND
Look back at your list of what you want God to redeem from the past year. Take a few minutes to give each of them to God, and then ask him to help you experience the restoration that he offers.
