Boldly Authentic

READ

John 1:14

REFLECT

Jesus generously shared a life of boldness and authenticity. As todays passage (taken from a modern paraphrase of the Bible called The Message) tells us, he "became flesh and blood, and moved into the neighborhood." In other words, the Son of God took the bold step of coming to where we were, in a way that authentically showed his love for us.

This week well learn from Jesus example in the book of John, and see four ways to live boldly authentic lives of faith.
