Work: Leveraging

Weve been looking at what the Bible says about work. Today, well look at how one of Jesus followers, Paul, leverages his everyday work with Kingdom work.

READ

Acts 18:1-4

REFLECT

Paul (one of Jesus apostles) models a life of ministry. He leverages his trade--tentmaking--as a way to network places to stay and work in his trade while he traveled to do ministry. Whether you get a paycheck from a church, corporation or trade, all who follow Jesus are called to a life of ministry.

RESPOND

Ask God how your current job is--and can be--a resource for his purposes.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • What do you love about your work now--even if your work islookingfor work?
  • What, if anything, at your workplace makes it hard to spot the Kingdom? (Stop and pray about those things.)
  • Have you begun to see your work differently this week? How so?
