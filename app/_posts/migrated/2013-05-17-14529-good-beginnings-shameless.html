Good Beginnings: Shameless

We finish out our week looking at our origins as man and woman in Genesis.

READ

Genesis 2:25

REFLECT

Mass media is selling sex daily, yet shame around sex has only increased. What a weird trick of the world. The more sex we see, the less we are capable of talking about it with the right people and in a way that benefits us. Being without shame is being comfortable with living open vulnerable lives. God intended for us to walk without shame.
