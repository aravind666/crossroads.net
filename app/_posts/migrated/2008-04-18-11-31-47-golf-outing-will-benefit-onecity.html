--- layout: post title: "Golf Outing Will Benefit OneCity" subtitle: "" date: 2008-04-18 11:31:47 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Natalie" --- Brad Johansen, a familiar face both at Crossroads and in Cincinnati, will host his fourth annual Brad Johansen Celebrity Golf Classic on May 30. Last year, this event raised more than $270,000 for OneCity, one of Crossroads' key partners in fighting poverty in Cincinnati. It also benefited other local ministries including Cancer Free Kids, Matthew 25 Ministries and Neediest Kids of All.

For more information, or to sign up to play, visit the Brad Johansen Celebrity Golf Classic website. Even if you don't play golf, you can help by volunteering or donating a silent auction item. Just contact Erika via email or at 513.579.0555.
