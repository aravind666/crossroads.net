Wednesday, January 5

Can You Keep a Secret?

God, help me understand the weight of my words.

READ

Proverbs 11:13

REFLECT

Sometimes its easy to make ourselves, or our situation, the exception. So for instance, what we consider gossip is different than what were doing: were just relaying the truth. Were just explaining to our neighbor why the other neighbor is getting a divorce (Yeah, he has a really bad drinking problem.) Or were just giving someone a heads-up (I heard Matt is going to sit each of us down and ask us to take a pay cut.)

God designed us with relationships in mind, both with him and with each other. And these relationships require trust, even if theyre boss/ employee relationships, neighbor/ neighbor, parent/ child. Trust cant exist when we speak (or make guesses) about others behind their back.

RESPOND

Spend some time today asking God how you can become a more trustworthy person.
