Week 5: Game Change

Monday, November 8
Fear vs. Love

Take a moment and ask God to meet with you today.


READ
1 John 4:18


REFLECT
Many things can stand in the way of us following through on what God is calling us to do. Fear can easily creep into our daily life unnoticed. If you take a close look, are there places in your life where fear is affecting your ability to obey?

RESPOND

Ask God to reveal fear in your life and tell him you want that fear to be replaced with love.
