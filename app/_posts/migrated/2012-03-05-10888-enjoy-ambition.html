Thursday, March 8

Enjoy Ambition

This week, were wrapping up our study on what it means to live as treasured children of God.

READ

Colossians 3:23-24

REFLECT

Were designed to work. But our ambitions arent meant to justify our place in this world; theyre ways to respond to the love and approval that God has already given us.

RESPOND

Think about your job, your schoolwork, your hobbies. Whatever todays work looks like, consider how you can do that work for God--not in hopes that it will earn his approval, but out of the belief that he has already adopted you as a treasured child.
