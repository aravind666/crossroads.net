Thursday, October 4

Family: Respect

Lets look today at how your family can be a model of respect to the world.

READ

Colossians 3:18-31

REFLECT

We find a lot of reciprocity in this verse. Everyone is both giving and receiving, protecting and being protected. How this works is often a mystery, but families who follow these rhythms can experience a unique and God-inspired level of equality and worth.

RESPOND

Ask God to show you where and why you are not in agreement with todays passage. Schedule a family conversation.
