A Life of Integrity

Spend a minute asking God to help keep your mind and heart open to the things he wants to teach you.


READ
Proverbs 11


REFLECT
There is a term for the type of life that Solomon describes in this chapter - it's called integrity. It's when what we claim to believe matches up with what we do, and it's exactly the kind of life that wise people pursue.

They treat people fairly, speak with honesty, and live out God's desire for the world, even when it costs them to do so.

And this is exactly the kind of life God honors.
  • What situation in your life challenges your integrity? How?
  • What will you gain if you refuse to give in to that temptation?

RESPOND
Read back through this chapter and choose one of the proverbs that most connects with whatever challenges your integrity. Spend a couple of minutes memorizing that proverb, and then ask God to help you remember it when you face that temptation.


ANOTHER LOOK
