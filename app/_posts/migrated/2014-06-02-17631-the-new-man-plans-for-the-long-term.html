Wednesday, June 4

The New Man Plans for the Long-Term

This week, The Daily will consider what it means for the New Man to have a vision.

PRAY

Ask God to give you strength for the long-haul.

READ

Genesis 17:1-8

REFLECT

Abraham and his wife, Sarah, were an old married couple with no kids. But God promised them a huge family, in fact a nation, if they followed and obeyed him. Thats a pretty big vision and not one that either Abraham or Sarah would see. Like Abe, men think about and plan for the long-haul, not just this weekend. This requires action and patience, and while it wont always be easy (spoiler alert: it will be hard), God keeps his promises.

RESPOND

Today, instead of planning for the weekend or what to pick up for dinner, spend some time with God and think about your long-term vision. What is it and who is part of it?
