Wednesday, October 3

Family: Mission

Today we look at training our kids and being on mission together.

READ

Deuteronomy 32:45-47

REFLECT

Moses led the Israelites through the desert and to their promised land. Their journey included losing the ways of Egypt and learning to trust God entirely, even as they moved into the good land. There is a promised land for your family, and your journey includes training your children to continually take new ground.

RESPOND

Take a moment and journal the answer to this question: where might God want to lead our family?
