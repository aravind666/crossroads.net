Friday, June 22

Real Relationships Change People

This week were studying the boldly authentic friendship of Jesus and his disciple Peter.

READ

Acts 2:22-24, 36-41

REFLECT

If you didnt pick up on it, todays passage begins with Peter addressing a crowd. This is recorded in the book of Acts, after Jesus death and resurrection. Its amazing to see Peters faith and strength, especially compared to the weakness we saw earlier this week. And its proof that a single relationship can change someones life forever.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.


  • How do your own relationships compare to what we learned about Jesus and Peters friendship this week? Is their story encouraging or discouraging to you?
  • Did a particular relationship of yours come to mind this week? If so, whats a next step you can take to build that relationship?
