Thursday, August 28

We are Free to Work

When Jesus came, he initiated a new kingdom. And we all have a role in that kingdom.

PRAY

Ask God to show you what youre working toward.

READ

2 Thessalonians 3:10-13

REFLECT

Thessalonica was ruled by Rome. Caesars bread and circus strategy provided his subjects with a little food and entertainment, in order to keep them dependent and oppressed. Because of this environment, early Christ-followers only knew how to function in an oppressive system, undervalued and overcontrolled. Working for the gain of your family and your community was freedom, and Paul cheered them on toward this freedom.

RESPOND

Write a list today of ways that you experience freedom in your work.
