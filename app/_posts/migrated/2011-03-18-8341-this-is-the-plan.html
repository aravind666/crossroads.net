This is the Plan?

God, thank you for letting me play a part in your plan to restore the world.

READ

Acts 1:1-11

REFLECT

So if you were going to start a movement to radically change peoples lives and alter the course of human history, how would you do it?

Many of us would probably start by recruiting the most talented, experienced, well-connected leaders available. Then maybe have a fundraising campaign, set up the org chart, write the business plan, launch the marketing campaign, engage worldwide media channels, and so on.

Jesus took 12 uneducated nobodies, hung out with them for three years, and then set them loose. No funding, no marketing, no business development plan. More importantly, there was no Plan B. It was, is, and always will be in the hands of people like Peter, and James, and you and me.

  • What do you think about the idea that God doesnt have a Plan B?
  • How have you experienced this plan in action?

RESPOND

Gods plan doesnt rely on a particular leader or favorable markets. It relies on individual people connecting with others and showing them what a life with God is about. Take a few minutes today to think through who God may be wanting you to connect with.
