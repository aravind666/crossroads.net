Wednesday, September 19

Marriage: Reflect Jesus

Today, we look at a verse from the Old Testament that foretells of Jesus covenant with us.

READ

Isaiah 62:5

REFLECT

The covenant that God makes with all of his people (thats you, if you follow Jesus) is a marriage covenant. Isnt it good that God commits to us beyond a contractual obligation? His covenant with us through Jesus still stands, and goes deeper than any earthly contract.

RESPOND

Married: Journal today about ways that you want your marriage to reflect Jesus love for Gods people.

Single: Ask Jesus to show you ways that he loves you. Then spend a few minutes journaling about about what comes to mind.
