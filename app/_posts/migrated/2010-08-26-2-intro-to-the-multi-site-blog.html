Welcome to the Multi-site blog. Here youll read more about the vision for multi-site expansion, as well as updates on how multi-site is playing out at Mason and other future locations.

Youll also read stories from volunteers working at Mason and how multi-site has affected them, and youll be able to respond with your own stories of how God is moving in your life through the multi-site vision.

Subscribe or check back to this blog for stories, photo, video and more. We hope youll follow and participate in the discussion about where God is taking multi-site at Crossroads.
