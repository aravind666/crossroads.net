Monday, January 20

Faith by James: Watch Your Words

PRAY

As you read, ask God to reveal something new to you through his words.

READ

James 3:1-12

REFLECT

The average woman says about 20,000 words a day. The average man says 7,000 (hes shy). Regardless of gender, and whether were introverted or the life of the party, we all do a lot of talking. Which means theres plenty opportunity to either build people up or tear them down. This week, The Daily will look at what James says about the power of our words.

PRACTICE

Pay close attention to what you say today. How much of what you say has the potential to build a person up or tear them down?

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Romans 11
