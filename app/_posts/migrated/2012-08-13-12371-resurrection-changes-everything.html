Friday, August 17

Resurrection: Changes Everything

Today is the last day of looking at the signs that Jesus performed in the book of John. This final glimpse is how people responded to the resurrection of Lazarus.

READ

John 11:45-48

REFLECT

The Israelites were distracted by politics. They did not walk into fear or even political controversy with the ease that we saw in Jesus earlier in the text (Mondays Daily). Resurrection and believing in it changes everything. Jesus raised Lazarus as a sign that he himself would rise, and that points to the door being opened to us. We can believe on a daily basis that he is making us new.

ANOTHER LOOK

1 Corinthians 15:42-43

RESPOND

How often do you stop to think about Jesus making a way for us to resurrect? Today, spend time meditating on the fact that he gives you resurrection, eternal life.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • How is resurrection important to the life of a Christ-follower?
  • What fear or dead thing did you give to God this week to resurrect?
  • What do you want prayer for in your life that feels dead?
