Tuesday, November 9
Proof

Take a moment and ask God to meet with you today.


READ
Hebrews 11:1-2


REFLECT
We live in a world that demands proof of beneficial results before action. But faith is acting even when we dont know the certainty of the outcome. Are you acting in faith, or are you playing it safe?


RESPOND
Tell God that you want to live in faith, not in fear. Ask Him to show you specific ways you can act in faith today or this week.
