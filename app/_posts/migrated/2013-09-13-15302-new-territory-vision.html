Wednesday, September 18

New Territory: Vision

We are continuing to unfold Isaiah 54 this week. Today we look at verse 3.

READ

Isaiah 54:1-5

REFLECT

So far we have this formula: barren woman + God = children = grandchildren = nations. Lets stretch our short-term views into long-term vision. As we put ourselves in the shoes of the childless woman who God is speaking to, lets imagine the long-term ramifications to the short-term trust that God is asking of us. We are watering our small acts of trust to grow a life of faith.

RESPOND

Reflect again today about where God might want to take a desolate part of your life and turn it into fruit. Journal about what the long-term picture might be--fast forward 10, 40 or 400 years.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
