Wednesday, January 23

Community: Gifts

We continue today looking at another section of Romans 12 to understand Gods view on community.

READ

Romans 12:6-8

REFLECT

God has planted gifts in you. Truly, who you are is Gods gift to you. He made you with intrinsic value, in His image. What you do with the gifts He has given you is your gift or offering up to God and out to your community.

RESPOND

Two other places where gifts are listed is: 1 Corinthians 12 and Ephesians 4. You probably have more than one of these gifts, but choose right now one that you know or think you might have. Then decide one small humble way you can boldly use that gift and do it.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
