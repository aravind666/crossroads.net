Gifts from a Good Dad

READ

Luke 11:10-13

REFLECT

Our Dad loves us. Whether we receive it or not, God is a good father to his children. You and me. He loves to give us good gifts. Sometimes those gifts arent what we expect or what we think we want, but God blesses us so that we will trust him. We discover that leaning in and trusting our Dad is the greatest gift of all. He is all we need.

This Christmas week, The Daily will look at stories of people who have trusted their Dad and received his love.

RESPOND

Think about your greatest desire---what you want most in the entire world. Hold on to it. God wants to give it to you. Spend some time with Him today and trust that he will take care of that desire.
