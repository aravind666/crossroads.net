Wednesday, February 1

What Is It?

This week, were learning about Gods faithfulness to us.

PRAY

God, help me lean into your faithfulness. Please give me the strength to fully trust in you today.

READ

Exodus 16:2-4

Exodus 16:14-15

Exodus 16:31-32

REFLECT

Manna means "what is it" in Hebrew. The sudden appearance of this Frosted Flake-like food from the heavens demonstrates how Gods faithfulness often comes in unexpected ways.

RESPOND

Spend a few minutes thinking about how God might be demonstrating his faithfulness in areas you arent expecting. Maybe its a "blessing in disguise," or just a different way of looking at a current situation in your life.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: 1 Thessalonians 2
