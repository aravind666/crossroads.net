Wednesday, January 6
A Key to Wisdom

Spend a minute asking God to help keep your mind and heart open to the things he wants to teach you.


READ
Proverbs 3:5-8


REFLECT
Pride is a huge barrier to growing in wisdom. It keeps us from seeing ourselves in the proper perspective, and keeps us from being teachable. As Solomon (a wise king of Israel) describes here, humility is a prerequisite to wisdom, and the only posture from which we can grow.

One way to practice humility is to submit to the direction and guidance of someone else. Todays passage is an invitation to submit to Gods direction and guidance. Though challenging at times, Proverbs says this kind of submission will bring health to your body and nourishment to your bones, meaning a life of continual growth and wholeness.

  • What area in your life is the most difficult to submit to Gods guidance and direction? Why is this challenging?
  • If you chose to take a posture of humility in this area, what might that look like? What kind of health and wholeness might result?
  • Is there anyone in your life that you need to submit to, but arent? Why?

RESPOND
Follow through on one or more of the ways you can practice humility. Ask God for the strength to continue submitting to his guidance and direction.
