Week Two: Growing Into Freedom

Monday, April 18

Changing Hands

God, give me the selflessness to see how your story reaches past me.

READ

Deuteronomy 34:1-12; Joshua 1:1-6

REFLECT

If youve been coming to the weekend messages at Crossroads (or listening online), youve been hearing how each of us is just a scratch on the enormous storyline of God. And thats what youre seeing here, as the story of Moses leadership ends and Joshuas begins.

God used Moses to lead a nation through the first part of their freedom journey, but Moses never sets foot onto the land God sets aside as a final destination. Rather, God shows him the land from afar.

Moses, a scratch on Gods enormous storyline. And then the baton is passed.

RESPOND

Step back from your life for a minute. Think of the children and the generations who will follow you. What freedom story will you pass to them?

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
