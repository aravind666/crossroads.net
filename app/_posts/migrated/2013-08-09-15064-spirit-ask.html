Friday, August 16

Spirit: Ask

This is our last day looking at the Spirit. Dont be sad. If you want to learn more, go to Bible Gateway and search Holy Spirit.

READ

Luke 11:9-13

REFLECT

God has all that you are looking for and if you seek, knock and ask, he wants to be generous toward you. But did you know that what He gives you in response to your seeking, knocking and asking is the Holy Spirit? When you are lost, He doesnt just give you a quick fix, he gives you his full authority through the gift of the Spirit. No really, its like that.

RESPOND

Take a moment to ask God today for more of his Spirit. Hint: if it just doesnt feel like its happening for you, read Acts 2:38.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • How do you think about or picture the Holy Spirit? Were you raised with any beliefs about the Holy Spirit?

  • Is it possible that you are seeking right now, and the Holy Spirit is the answer to your seeking?

  • How do you think the Holy Spirit connects to being a son or daughter of God?
