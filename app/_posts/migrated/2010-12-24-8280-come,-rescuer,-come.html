Friday, December 24

Come, Rescuer, Come

READ

Matthew 25:1-13
Revelation 22:12-17

REFLECT

During Advent, we celebrate God the Fathers promise of Jesus, who came as a baby 2,000 years ago. Jesus is now with the Father (while His Holy Spirit lives in us) and he will come again. We dont know when this will happen, but Jesus will return to earth to reward some with the tree of life. This is the very tree of life from which Adam and Eve were banished.

The best of Gods promises was, and is, the coming of Jesus. And the further promise that He will come again.

RESPOND

Be filled with joy over the fullness of Jesus birth. See the vulnerable baby as the way God loves you like a perfect father: tender, gentle, yet full of promises that he will fulfill. God, the father, is for you. He has proclaimed it in Jesus birth.

ANOTHER LOOK

Matthew 24:36-47
