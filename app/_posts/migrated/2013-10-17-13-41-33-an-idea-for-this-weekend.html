This thought just crossed my mind, as I prepared for the upcoming weekend: this is a great weekend to invite a friend who you want to hear about God. This wasn’t meant to be a strategic part of our journey plan, which is why this video is just a crummy smartphone video. We will be talking about the beautiful complexity of God and explaining the Gospel in as plain a language as we ever do.

 

There’s no obligation and no expectation--but if you have somebody in mind and are looking for a way to connect them, this weekend could be very effective.
