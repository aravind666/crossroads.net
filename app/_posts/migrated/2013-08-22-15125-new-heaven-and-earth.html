Friday, August 30

New: Heaven and Earth

Dang, weve been obsessed with the word new this week. Its a good thing, since God seems to be, too.

READ

Isaiah 65:17

REFLECT

You are a part of Gods epic story that began with the creation of the world and ends (or starts new) with a new heaven and a new earth. Jesus was the beginning of the new way of doing things--a new testament you might say. But a day will come when all will be as God intended--all will be made new.

RESPOND

We cant even imagine what that time will be like, but lets try. Journal today about what you think a new heaven and new earth might look like.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • How do you feel or what do you think when you hear the word new?

  • Do you think of God more as a lover of old ritual or the maker of the new thing?

  • Share one new or fresh thought that you had this week (could be about God, a new business, or a new way to rearrange your furniture).
