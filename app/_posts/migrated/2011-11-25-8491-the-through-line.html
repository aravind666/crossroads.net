Friday, November 25

The Through-Line

Spend some time thanking God for his unconditional, complete love for you.

READ

Ephesians 2:4-9

REFLECT

Getting rescued isnt an everyday experience for most of us. In fact, we might even feel more comfortable being the rescuer (i.e. project manager) because we get more control over the situation, and its more predictable.

But the through-line of Gods story is redemption. God alone does the rescuing and the saving. And He does it out of love, not because he expects us to return the favor. We cant. ("And this not from yourselves, it is the gift of God," Ephesians 2:8.)

So we get to surrender our striving efforts and know that His love doesnt come with a dimmer switch.

RESPOND

If someone offers to help you with something today, take them up on it (even if you think you could do it better). If no one offers, ask for help.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Hebrews 1
