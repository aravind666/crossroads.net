Your Kingdom Come

Over the last six weeks weve been looking at our lives, our city and our world through the lens of the Kingdom of God. This morning, listen to an original song from the journey and think about what God might be revealing to you about his Kingdom.

LISTEN

Out Loud.

REFLECT

In Mark 4:30-32 Jesus tells us that the Kingdom of God starts out very small (that means within you). It grows and grows when we decide to love, receive, obey, fight and seek his Kingdom. We can make the Kingdom come.

RESPOND

What are you waiting for? Whether God is asking you to do something big or small, obey him. The first step is praying that his Kingdom comes. If you need some help, try the prayer written in Matthew 6:5-13. Youve probably said it before.
