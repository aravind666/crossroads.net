REFLECT

Craig knew God had a perfect plan and this made fighting his fear so much easier and freeing. This kind of obedience has great power and it can bring about the Kingdom of God. Just read 1 Corinthians 4:20 and 2 Timothy 1:7.

RESPOND

Pray against the spirit of fear. If this is new to you, heres a little help: "Dad, thank you for giving me a spirit of power, love, and self-control. May today be the day it overcomes my fear of ________."
