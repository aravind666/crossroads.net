Friday, September 13

New Courage: Wait

Have you been deleting fear and adding courage all week? Its almost time to go get em tiger.

READ

Psalms 27:14

REFLECT

The more fearless you get as you take courage from the Lord, the more apt you might be to jump up and run. The one reason to pause is to make sure you dont run out ahead of God and the pace that he knows is best. If you are the type who doesnt get up and run, then focus on taking courage. Movement and the ready position is best, but God sets the pace.

RESPOND

Are you more likely to run ahead or get caught sitting still? Journal about how God wants to grow you today, either way.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Do you struggle with fear? Share a story of getting courage from God.

  • Where do you misplace your fear that should be reserved for God alone?

  • Do you tend to pace too fast or too slow?

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Colossians 3
