Acts: Obeying Without Hesitation

This week, were studying the book of Acts and learning about game changers in the early Church.

READ

Acts 8:26-40

REFLECT

Philips encounter with the Ethiopian was an exercise in obedience. God didnt tell Philip why he needed to walk down that desert road, but todays passage says Philip simply "got up and went" without asking or questioning. Think about your closest relationships; think about the people for whom youll do anything--all they have to do is ask. Now consider that God wants to have that same level of intimacy and closeness with you.
