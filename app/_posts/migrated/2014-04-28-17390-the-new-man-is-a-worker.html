Tuesday, April 29

The New Man is a Worker with Vision

Our culture has a lot to say about manhood, but God says something different about it. Over the next few weeks, The Daily will take a look at what the Bible says about manhood.

PRAY

As you pray, ask God to give you vision to set and achieve realistic goals.

READ

Genesis 2:15

REFLECT

There are obvious differences between boys and men, and there are some we dont think or talk about. One of them is that a boy only cares about instant gratification. A man sets a vision and works at it. Sometimes this vision comes from God, like he did with Adam. He gave Adam a garden to tend and watch over, giving him purpose and a vision to work at. Like Adam, men who have a vision take the steps and do the work to see the vision become a reality.

RESPOND

Think about vision. What is your vision today or what could it be? It could be about your job, your family or a hobby. Think about what it looks like to work at that vision.
