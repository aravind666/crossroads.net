Love Crashed into the World

Jesus, the Savior and King was born. Gods people were deeply suffering, enslaved and powerless---it seemed that only a mighty, warring king could rescue them. But God entered the world in a way that was as countercultural as his life on earth would be: He came as a baby. He came as love.
