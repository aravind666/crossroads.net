Friday, October 8

Malachi


Prayer: God, teach me how I can trust you more.



READ
Malachi 3:8-12



REFLECT
This is one of the only times in the Bible where God says to test him. And He says to test him on tithing.

When we give ten percent back to God, were thanking him for the gifts hes already given usincluding the ability to have a job and bring home income. When we tithe, its an act of faith reminding us that were connected to all of his resources. And Hes a big God. Were also trusting Him to allocate those resources to where they need to go.

God loves when we trust him, because trust is what relationships are founded upon.

RESPOND
Ask God to show you where you trust him and where you can grow in trusting him.



ANOTHER LOOK
Isaiah 26:4-7
