Bread Alone (Part Two)

This week, were looking at how our desire to be loved--if misdirected--can create unhealthy appetites in our lives.

READ

Revelation 3:20

REFLECT

Fasting is a practice that helps us focus more on God, and less on the physical aspects of our lives. And the most wonderful part of this is that God--the creator of the universe--actuallywantsto spend time with us.

RESPOND

Yesterday, you were challenged to fast at least one day from anything that may be keeping you from feeling like a treasured child of God. Today, we simply encourage you to find the time and the focus to follow through on that challenge.

God, thank you for wanting to spend time with me, and for considering me your treasured child. Help me make the time and the space to meet with You.
