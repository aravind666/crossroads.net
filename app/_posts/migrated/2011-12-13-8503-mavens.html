Tuesday, December 13

Mavens

PRAY

Ask God to make his love and grace flow out of you and into the lives of people youll be with today.

READ

Jeremiah 20:9

REFLECT

In the ancient world, a proclaimer of any kind of good news was considered an evangelist. Today we might describe an evangelist in other ways--like Malcolm Gladwell (author of The Tipping Point), who made popular the term "maven":

a person who finds something, loves it, and then brings others into the fold.

This describes Jeremiah to a tee. His love for God is a pure fire in him that is impossible to contain. And this wasnt because Jeremiahs life was easy or perfect (read more of Jeremiah and youll quickly find that out), but because Jeremiah had the radical experience of a relationship with God.

RESPOND

Yesterday in The Daily you considered one of Jesus characteristics that has drawn you to him. Tie that characteristic directly to an experience youve had where that characteristic significantly affected the outcome.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
