James: Testing Your Faith

Last week, we read the entire book of James. This week, were focusing on one passage and going phrase by phrase.

READ
Consider it pure joy, my brothers and sisters, whenever you face trials of many kinds, because you know thatthe testing of your faith produces perseverance. Let perseverance finish its work so that you may be mature and complete, not lacking anything.
James 1:2-4

REFLECT
Yesterday we talked about how James looks reality square in the face. All of us will experience trials in some way--whether its physical suffering, the temptation to fall into an old habit or just the daily grind. We cant prevent these "tests." But instead of letting them derail or immobilize us, James reminds us that we can be strengthened through them. And then were more fully prepared for the next round.

RESPOND
Think of an area of your life where you feel like God has strengthened you--a "trial by fire" of sorts. Is there someone who would be encouraged by your experience? Today, look for a chance to share it.
