Kingdom Come: Vision

LISTEN

Set aside 4 minutes and listen to this song, Be Thou My Vision.

REFLECT

In Philippians 4:8, Paul writes that among other things we should focus on what is true, honorable and lovely to be free from anxiety, free from the world. By focusing on these things, the vision for our day, relationships and our life changes. Through that renewed vision we can see the Kingdom of God.

RESPOND

Think about something you might consider a chorewhether at work or at home. Treat that chore as if the Kingdom of God depended on it. Write what happened on page 93 of your field guide.
