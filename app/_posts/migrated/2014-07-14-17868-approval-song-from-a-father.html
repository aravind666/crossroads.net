Thursday, July 24

Approval: Song from a Father

This week, were spending time receiving Gods approval, as well as identifying areas of our lives where we unnecessarily seek the approval of others.

LISTEN

I Am Here (Song From a Father)

READ

Psalm 8:3-4

Isaiah 54:10

Acts 17: 24-27

REFLECT

God is the all-powerful Creator of the universe---and yet he chooses to pursue us individually, like a caring father.

RESPOND

Once again, keep an eye out for a time today when you seek approval from others. Remind yourself that the Creator of the universe loves you, and chooses to pursue you.

PRAY

Thank God for loving you like a dad.
