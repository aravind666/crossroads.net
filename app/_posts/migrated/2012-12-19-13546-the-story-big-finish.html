Friday, December 28

The Story: Big Finish

We end the week with the Magi (wisemen), small children being murdered, and the death of a ruler. Who said that Jesus birth story was all angels and shepherds?

READ

Matthew 2:1-23

RESPOND

This reading is a lot to process. Jesus really was born into "the real world." Take a moment and ask Jesus to enter into your real-world problems. Hes been doing it since birth.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

You can use these questions to spark conversation with people who also do The Daily.

  • Were there any parts of the story that you didnt know?
  • Discuss anything that stood out for the first time. What hit you most?
  • With which person in the story did you connect the most, and why?
