Friday, July 18

Appetites: The Best Invitation Ever

This week, were looking at how our desire to be loved---if misdirected---can create unhealthy appetites in our lives.

PRAY

God, thank you for inviting me to be part of your family. Help me make the time and the space to meet with You.

READ

2 Peter 1:3-4

REFLECT

We looked at this passage earlier in the week, but its worth checking out again. God knows that our earthly appetites, when left unchecked, leave us with a sick feeling in our stomachs and an unhealthy desire for more. The Bible promises us again and again that God wants something better for us.

RESPOND

Spend a few minutes considering the possibility that God is a loving Father who wants the best for you. How might that affect your life on a daily basis? (And if you havent tried this weeks fasting challenge, maybe give it a shot this weekend.)
