Wednesday, January 22

Faith by James: Good Words

PRAY

God wants to teach you today. Ask Him for a clear lesson.

READ

James 3:7-10, Ephesians 4:29

REFLECT

Our words can encourage and have a positive effect on us. Whether its a compliment on your outfit, an insight into the good man that you are or screams of encouragement from a friend as you cross the finish line---our words can reflect the loving character of God. When this happens, we walk a little taller, we accomplish more and we know God that much better.

PRACTICE

Build someone up. Share words of encouragement with someone in your inner circle. It doesnt have to be earth-shattering, just a genuine word or two of encouragement.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is: Romans 13
