Tuesday, December 28

Defend the Underdog

God, help me see where I have the opportunity to speak truth about people and defend those who are wrongly accused.


READ
Proverbs 14:25

REFLECT
Testifying about someone doesnt just happen in court. It happens in a casual conversation on the phone, when were out with friends, when were at the water cooler.

In this proverb, Solomon describes the effect we have when we either tell the truth about someone or add to the lie. If we choose integrity and defend the person whos getting wrongly accused of something, and when we do that even at the risk of being unpopular, we "bring life" to the person accused. If we go the bully route (whether thats adding our own lies to the accusation or pretending not to hear the lies being said), then we destroy the innocent.


Defender or bully: its a daily choice.


RESPOND
Ask God to show you if there is anyone youve betrayed by the way you talked about him or her. If He reveals someone to you, ask him to give you opportunity to apologize and make things right.

ANOTHER LOOK

Zechariah 7:9-10
