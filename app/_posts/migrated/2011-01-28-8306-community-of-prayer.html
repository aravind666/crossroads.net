Community of Prayer

Before you begin, consider the fact that the Lord created you for relationship, and that his deepest desire is for intimate relationship with you.

READ:

James 5:13-20

REFLECT

When you take a look at the early pictures of Christ-followers, a common refrain of conversation is their prayer for one another. They always seem to be lifting each other up in prayer before God, interceding for those who dont know him yet, and calling for healing and power in the name of Jesus.

Have you ever considered what itd look like to practice the same thing in your community of Christ-followers? If you dont currently pray together as a community, then start. Its as simple as that. There arent any special incantations you have to say, or rituals you must follow. Is someone hurting? Pray for them. Is someone struggling with fear or worry? Go to God on their behalf, and ask him to make his love perfect in them, and get that fear out of there.

RESPOND

Next time youre together with a group of friends and you hear someone hurting (or celebrating), ask them if you can pray for them. Thank God for your friendship with that person, and ask him to bless your friend. A community that prays together...(well, never mind, its corny).
