Good?: Our Own Way

This week were looking at where we end up when we separate our sexuality from our spirituality.

READ

Galatians 5:19-21

REFLECT

God wants wholeness for you. Wholeness and integration means letting God have access to your whole life. When we try to connect to God, but close our bedroom door assuming we can leave God in the hall, or think that he is not there with us in the bar, at our computer, or anywhere outside of a church building, we begin to feel the force of disintegration.

RESPOND

Of the list given in todays verse, which stood out to you? Maybe it surprised you. Journal about that.
