Friday, December 9

The Path of Peace

PRAY

Tell God about a decision youre in the middle of making, large or small. Ask for His wisdom as you move forward.

READ

John 16:33

REFLECT

Peace isnt the avoidance of problems. Peace is the ability to flourish even in the midst of our problems. Because although well sometimes experience serenity and ease in life, Jesus tells us we will also suffer and be challenged.

Consider the Christmas story. The Bible says Joseph and Mary were incredibly afraid, experienced public humiliation, risked nearly everything they had--yet they did what God commanded. They chose obedience over the worlds measure of comfort. They trusted Gods promise that we will flourish when were fully engaged in what were created to do and be.

RESPOND

Think about someone who could use some encouragement in the midst of struggle. Encourage them today, as soon as you can.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
