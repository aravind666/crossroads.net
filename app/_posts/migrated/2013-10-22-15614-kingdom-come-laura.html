Wednesday, October 23

Kingdom Come: Laura

Today, Laura shares how receiving Gods love filled her up more than any romantic movie, song or TV show.

LAURAS STORY

I love a good love story. But I allowed them to influence my thoughts and feelings. I found myself wondering when my own Mr. Darcy would come to sweep me off my feet. My life didnt look like I thought it was "supposed" to look, and I believed the lie that I needed romantic love to add value to my life.

These feelings that my life was empty without romantic love typically brought on a major depression. I cried out to God to help me. He revealed to me that the messages from music, TV and movies are not his messages. I clearly heard Him say that I needed to do a "Romance Fast"--no more Twilight movies, love stories, or romantic music.

My depression dissolved into peace as I began to focus on how love is defined according to the Kingdom of God. I no longer needed to seek validation through romantic loveGod already loves me with a deeper love than I can imagine.

My "fast" turned into a way of life. I want to know love according to the Kingdom of God and that means quieting the loud voices of the kingdom of the world.

REFLECT

God revealed his love to Laura. It overflows. It never runs out. Understand what Laura learned in Ephesians 3:18.
