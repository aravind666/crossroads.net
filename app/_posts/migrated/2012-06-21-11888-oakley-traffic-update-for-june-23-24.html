The Kennedy Connector project is well underway, and it may impact weekend traffic in Oakley.

  • Part of the gravel lot off Ibsen has been encroached by the city, so park there at your own risk.
  • Both sections of Ibsen still have limited access and may be closed to traffic.
  • You can still use the Annex for overflow parking, though you will need to access the Annex lot from Marburg or Madison, as entry from Vandercar will be blocked.

For the latest updates, visitfacebook.com/kennedyconnector.
