--- layout: post title: "Over A Thousand Show for Angel Tree!" subtitle: "" date: 2008-12-24 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Todd" --- Here are some quick stats for Angel Tree '08. It was an amazing outpouring of love from our community to the families of those who are incarcerated.


A few stats:
560 families received gifts, including 62 delivered from Mason. (That means around 1300 kids and over 3160 gifts!)
Over 1000 delivery volunteers at Oakley
Over 100 delivery volunteers at Mason
2 reindeer (delivery volunteers brought their dogs into the Atrium)
1 over-used, worn and tired Buddy The Elf suit going into retirement until next year

Thanks to all who participated in making Angel Tree a success!
