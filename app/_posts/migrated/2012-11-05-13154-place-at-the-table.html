Friday, November 9

Place at the Table

Lets end this week with a glimpse at what we bring to the table, and what our Dad offers us.(If you missed this weekends message,click herefor a video summary.)

READ

Isaiah 61:7

REFLECT

Since Adam and Eve, the human race has experienced shame and disgrace. Thats why we cover ourselves. The first couple did it with leaves, and God covered them with better threads by making them animal skins. Hes still in the business of covering us. We bring him our shame and disgrace, and he gives us a double portion, an inheritance, and joy. It a great trade-off and a sweet reason to take our place at the table. Why not trade in what we have for what our Dad has for us, and experience the blessing of this verse?
