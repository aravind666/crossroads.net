REFLECT

Jesus told the disciples to go out and spread the good news about the Kingdom and to heal others in Luke 9:1-6. Sometimes that means flying half-way around the world like Dave or just crossing our own street. To spread the Kingdom, we must listen to what our Dad is telling us and follow his command.

RESPOND

Think about someone that needs healing. Maybe they live in a different city or right next door. Spend a few minutes and pray for them. Or, knock on their door. Maybe a conversation is just what they need.
