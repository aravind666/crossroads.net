Tuesday, March 6

Enjoy Appetite

This week, were wrapping up our study on what it means to live as treasured children of God. If you missed this past weekends message,heres a short summary video.

READ

1 Timothy 6:17

REFLECT

This verse shows us Gods generous nature. He "richly gives us everything for our enjoyment" and he provides his treasured children with the appetites to enjoy these gifts he lavishes on us.

RESPOND

Today, enjoy something you love. As you savor that steak, listen to your favorite band or lie down for that late afternoon nap, take a moment to thank God for his generosity.
