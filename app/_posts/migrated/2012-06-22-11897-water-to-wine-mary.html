Monday, June 25

Water to Wine: Mary

Reading the Bible from the viewpoint of different characters can make these ancient stories come alive. This week well look at the story of Jesus turning water into wine, through the eyes of different people who were there. Well start today with Mary.

READ

John 2:1-5

REFLECT

Mary knew Jesus well, and wasnt afraid to ask. She didnt approach Jesus cautiously and say, "Jesus, whatever you think is best..." Mary knew what she wanted, asked boldly, and even began to move into action.

RESPOND

Take a moment and ask yourself: what do you want? List five things quickly. Now, ask God for those things. This doesnt mean that Jesus will answer yes to all that you ask, but you are opening yourself more to relationship with Him and the good life just by asking.
