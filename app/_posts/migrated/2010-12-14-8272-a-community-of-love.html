Tuesday, December 14

A Community of Love

READ

Luke 1:39-45

REFLECT

God the Father directed Mary to visit her cousin, Elizabeth, as a loving provision. Elizabeth provided affirmation, comfort, knowledge and community for Mary for three months as Mary grew in her publicly disgraceful (but blessed) pregnancy. Are you finding community that affirms you as you follow Gods direction?

RESPOND

Ask God to grow your community in number and in love. Affirm someone today for how they follow Gods direction.

ANOTHER LOOK

Luke 1:46-56
