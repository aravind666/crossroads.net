This Is What It Feels Like


Todays edition of The Daily focuses on an original song written for the Free journey by Andrea VonAllmen. Follow along with the lyrics while you listen to the song. Ask God to meet you during this time, and to speak to your heart about the freedom he has in store for you.


From Andrea:
Ive heard people describe what freedom is, but never what it feels like. This song is a laundry list of experiences that resemble the feeling of Gods kind of freedom.
Notice: all the things listed in this song feel AWESOME!
