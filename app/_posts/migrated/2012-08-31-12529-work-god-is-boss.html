Tuesday, September 4

Work: God is Boss

Today well look at placing all of our work under Gods authority.

READ

Colossians 3:17

REFLECT

A lot of peoples jobs are to represent their organization. Sales reps, customer service reps, client reps: the people in these roles become the face of their workplace to many people. What would it look like to represent your faith--to be a representative of Jesus?

RESPOND

Ask God how he wants you to represent him at work, and why he has sent you specifically to this job.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT

Todays reading is:Ephesians 5
