Liberian refugees will receive a new Habitat for Humanity House this Saturday during a dedication at 10am at 4222 Kirby Avenue in Northside. Two hundred and twenty-one volunteers from Crossroads logged more than 5000 hours rehabilitating a foreclosure property to provide Austino Lewis and Kama Quaye, along with their two young sons James and Jeffrey, with a new home. The Lewis-Quaye family had lived in tents and makeshift housing in Ghana for the last 15 years escaping civil unrest in their native Liberia, until now.  Learn more about the dedication here.
