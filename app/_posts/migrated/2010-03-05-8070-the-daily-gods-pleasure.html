Friday, March 5

Gods Pleasure

Many people hear from God through music. Todays edition of The Daily focuses on an original song written for the Free journey by Andrea VonAllmen. Follow along with the lyrics while you listen to the song. Ask God to meet you during this time, and to speak to your heart about the freedom he has in store for you.

From Andrea:

This song is pretty much straight from Ephesians 1:4-6. Josh says, Its simply for Gods pleasure that I am anything: happy, free, satisfied, etc. Thats both very cool and very strange.
