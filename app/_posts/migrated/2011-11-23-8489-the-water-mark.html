Wednesday, November 23

The Water Mark


Spend a minute asking God to take over in your life today.

READ

Romans 6:3-5 (from The Message translation)

REFLECT

If you were at Crossroads last weekend, you watched people step into a pool of water and declare publically that they are surrendering to God. Letting go and asking God to take over is a mark of strength that leads to incredible strength.

What are some practical ways to die to our old selves and enter into the new grace-sovereign country that Paul writes about in Romans 6? It could look like giving credit where its due when someone praises you for work you didnt do. (God gives you that strength.) It could look like asking God to remind you of your worth in him when youre tempted to do something just to impress your friends. (He will give you that strength.)

Every day, we have many chances to surrender and rely on Gods strength.

RESPOND

Put a glass of water on your desk or table today. Dont drink it. Leave it there as a reminder to surrender.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
