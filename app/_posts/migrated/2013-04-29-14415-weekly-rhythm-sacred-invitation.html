Monday, April 29

This week well look at the weekly rhythm that God intended for us since the beginning: Sabbath rest.

READ

Exodus 31:13

REFLECT

We as Americans are self made, right? We are busy and its a status symbol. Were so important that we cant stop. So what if we do stop? What if we rest? Sabbath was a day set aside by God since the beginning of time to renew our connection with him. Instead of being self-made, we can be re-created every week by the only one who can make us perfect and holy, the creator of the universe. We can be God-made, and re-made again weekly. Its a sacred invitation.

RESPOND

Have ten minutes of Sabbath right now. Close your eyes and invite God to make you holy.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
