Thursday, November 4
Where is Your Heart?

Take a moment to ask God to clarify your thoughts and meet with you today.

READ
Luke 12:32-34


REFLECT

If you are truly being honest with yourself, where is your treasure? What is most valuable to you?

RESPOND
Ask God to help you place value on the things that truly matter most. Ask Him to give you the courage to orient your life and everything you do around whats really most important.
