Jesus vs. Pharisee: Unfolding Miracle

While the Pharisees focus on mud laws, there is a deeper miracle happening. Look at how the man Jesus healed is expanding in his beliefs about Jesus.

READ

John 9:17-33

REFLECT

The man who has been healed, first calls Jesus a prophet. Then in an attempt to wiggle out of giving the Pharisees proof to judge him by, he points to the healing. Lastly, the man makes it clear that he believes Jesus is sent by God. He makes it clear in an eloquent way. He is bold in his testimony and in his understanding of Jesus.

RESPOND

Its hard to be bold when your beliefs are unclear. Write down today what you believe about Jesus.
