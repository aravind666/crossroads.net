Tuesday, August 9

First things First

God, help me get rid of the stuff that hinders my faith.

READ

Joshua 3:5

REFLECT

Joshua was about to lead the Israelites across the Jordan River. Skipping ahead in the story, we know that God performs a miracle, by holding back the flow of the Jordan River at flood stage. But the dry river bed would not appear until the priests carrying the ark of the covenant stepped into the water. Literally, they would have to step out in faith. Pretty amazing!

In this verse, we see the importance of first things first. Unconfessed sin, fear or other spiritual muck can hinder our faith. The day before the miracle, Joshua instructed the Israelites to be sure their hearts are pure andthat they are devoted to God, because they could not afford to have anything jeopardize their faith.

RESPOND

God wants to do amazing things in your life. Take some time to ask God about any muck in your heart that hinders faith. Maybe you have fear, or maybe a sin or secret no one knows about. Call someone and confess to them - consecrate yourself. And watch God show up.
