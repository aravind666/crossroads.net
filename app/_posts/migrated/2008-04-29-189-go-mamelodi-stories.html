--- layout: post title: "GO Mamelodi stories" subtitle: "" date: 2008-04-29 category: "Crossroads.net" tag: - crossroadsnet comments: true created-by: "Todd" --- Each year for the past two years Crossroads has sent hundreds (yes...that is hundreds) of people to the township of Mamelodi, South Africa to partner with Charity and Faith Mission Church in bringing hope and healing to Mamelodi. While there they built houses, planted gardens, cared for kids and built relationships. And guess what... we're going back again this year.

In this series of podcasts, we'll be learning what happens when everyday, normal Crossroads people make the decision to engage their world.
