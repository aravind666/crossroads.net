Thursday, August 25

Summer: Hide and Seek

God, I know you are as close as the air, but help me to spot you.

READ

Matthew 6:28-34

REFLECT

In the spring, God pursues us and we fall in love. In the summer, He calls, "Youre it," and runs to hide nearby. This is not abandonment. This is learning to respond to Him, to not shrink up in fear, but to learn the adventurous and beautiful art of discovery.

Have you ever seen a game of hide and seek won through worry? Of course not. Do not worry. His caring for you is the same. None of His promises have changed.

Now go. You cant hide alone and discover. Seeking is an action. Look for God today. You might find Him in a tree, or in a friends words, in your work, in the Bible, or in a song. Hes not far, and discovering Him will bring joy in unexpected ways.

RESPOND

Ask the Holy Spirit to give you hints on where to find more of God today. If you think of a person or a location or an action, follow through on the next step of looking for God there.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
