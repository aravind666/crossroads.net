Hello, family and friends! We wish you could be here experiencing this rich culture with us, but know that you are not far from our hearts.

 

We are a little slow at reacting, but the six of us are finally on line and all of us are also jet lagged.  We arrived at the airport/hotel late last night, 1:00 am.  The hotel is great and we were all in bed by 2:30 am, and up again at 7 am for a wonderful day exploring the city.  We saw the Gateway to India, walked the city, and shopped at Colaba.  Everyone purchased gifts for family and friends.  Reaching the restaurant for lunch was an unexpected challenge, but we had an awesome meal when we finally arrived after at least 4 u turns and 3 driveways.  We had the entire staff in India supporting Crossroads with us all day and they are tired but in great spirits (also Shannon from IJM joined us).  We didn’t experience rain until we returned to the hotel. We waited in traffic for 10 minutes only to realize we were steps from our hotel as the driveway was blocked!  An adventure for all of us.  We are working late this evening to prepare for the Conferences we will be attending over the next two days. More to come!

 

Roberta
