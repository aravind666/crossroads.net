Wednesday, July 31

Rahab: Hesed

Yesterday, we looked at a healthy fear of God. Today, we will learn the Jewish word: hesed.

READ

Joshua 2:12-14

REFLECT

Rahabs fear of God does not make her feel bitterness or anger--instead her response to the Israelite spies is the word hesed. Hesed is translated in this passage as kindness--an even fuller meaning is loving-kindness. When we place ourselves under Gods holiness, with true reverential fear, it moves us to loving-kindness.

RESPOND

Place yourself reverently under God today, and let God prompt in you more of his loving-kindness.
