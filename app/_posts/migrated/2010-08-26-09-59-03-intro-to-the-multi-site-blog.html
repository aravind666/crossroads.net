Welcome to the Multi-site blog. Here you’ll read more about the vision for multi-site expansion, as well as updates on how multi-site is playing out at Mason and other future locations.

 

You’ll also read stories from volunteers working at Mason and how multi-site has affected them, and you’ll be able to respond with your own stories of how God is moving in your life through the multi-site vision.

 

Subscribe or check back to this blog for stories, photo, video and more. We hope you’ll follow and participate in the discussion about where God is taking multi-site at Crossroads.
