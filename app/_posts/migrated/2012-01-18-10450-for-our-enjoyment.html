Thursday, January 19
For Our Enjoyment

PRAY

God, help me identify and be thankful for the ways you have provided for me.

READ

1 Timothy 6:17

RESPOND Yesterdays Daily gave context to the first part of this verse, warning us not to put our hope in our wealth, which is uncertain, but rather to put our hope in God, who is steadfast. Today, focus on the second part of this verse, which describes how God meets our needs when we put our trust in him: he "richly provides us with everything for our enjoyment." Not just for our survival or to meet only our bare necessities. For our enjoyment. Similar to yesterdays challenge, take a few minutes to write down and describe as many details as you can of ways youve enjoyed Gods provision. (If you made a list yesterday, that would be a great place to start.) Your list might include experiences with friends, a delicious meal or the roof over your head, all of which God has provided for your enjoyment.
