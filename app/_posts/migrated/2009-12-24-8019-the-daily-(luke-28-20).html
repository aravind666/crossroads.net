Thursday, December 24
Who Let These Guys In?

Take a moment to pray and ask God to meet you during this time.

READ
Luke 2:8-20


REFLECT
Its just like God to use the most unexpected messengers possible.

Shepherds didnt have the best reputation in Jesus day. They were considered unclean, dishonest, and unreliable. They werent allowed into the temple, and they couldnt testify in court.

So who does God announce the birth of his Son to? Shepherds. He sends the whole angelic choir to sing for some shepherds. And after seeing the baby Jesus, they run throughout the city (in the middle of the night, no less), announcing the birth of Gods savior. These guys hadnt sat through seminary classes; they hadnt memorized the right way to tell people about Jesus. They were just excitedfilled with joy.

If God uses shepherds as messengers, then he really does have a place for everyone.

  • Has anything ever held you back from being a messenger of God?
  • In what ways do you struggle with trying to be good enough for God?

RESPOND
Spend a few moments thinking through a way you can be a messenger in your daily life, and ask God for the courage and opportunity to follow through on it.
