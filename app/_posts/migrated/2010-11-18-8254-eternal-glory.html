Thursday, November 18

Eternal Glory

Ask God to make you strong through his word today.

READ

1 Peter 5:8-11

REFLECT

The initiatives God has called our community to pursue will require strength and endurance. And make no mistake about it: we have an enemy who will oppose us. Yet, God himself promises to make us strong so that we can endure and ultimately experience victory in him. This victory is just a small taste of the ultimate prize, which Peter describes as eternal glory in Christ.

RESPOND

Today, ask God to remind you of the ultimate prize of being faithfully committed to him.

ANOTHER LOOK

2 Timothy 2:1-7
