Nehemiah: Back To The Basics

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily is studying people from the Bible that changed the game. This week, were looking at the story of Nehemiah.

READ

Nehemiah 8:1-12.

REFLECT

A new season was starting for the Jews who had come back to Jerusalem. Nehemiah marked the occasion by having the prophet Ezra read and teach from scripture--beginning this new season by focusing on the fundamentals of their faith, and celebrating the gifts that God had given them.

RESPOND

Spend a moment today thinking about the "season" youre currently in. Ask God to meet you in that place, whether he feels intimately close or incredibly distant.
