Friday, October 18

Kingdom Come: Reveal Yourself

READ

Song of Songs 2:4; Luke 17:20-21

REFLECT

The Kingdom of God is built on love. Thats why Jesus said it starts within us. When we accept Gods love and share it with others, amazing things happen. His Kingdom is revealed.

Take a minute (or 4) and listen to the lyrics of this song, Reveal Yourself. Ask God to reveal something to you. Maybe its His love, a glimpse of the Kingdom or just a simple love for music. Whatever God wants to tell you, just listen for it.

RESPOND

What came to your mind while listening to this song? Do you think God revealed something specific to you? On page 21 of your field guide, write about what it might look like to act on what He revealed to you.

Share your Kingdom story

We want everyone to experience amazing stories about God and the Kingdom. If you have a story, share it with us. Email: story@kingdomcomejourney.com.
