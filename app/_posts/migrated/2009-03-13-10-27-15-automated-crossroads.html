Q: Do I need an iPod to listen to or watch "podcasts"?
A: No. You can listen to messages on any audio device or watch messages on any computer with Quicktime installed.

Q: When are the messages released?
A. Messages are typically released by noon on Mondays.

Q: Why don't you release all the music from services?
A: A lot of the music we do at crossroads is protected by copyright. While we have permission to play them live in our services, we don't have permission to record and release them. We do, however, release original crossroads music as its recorded.

Q: Will there be more podcasts in the future?
A: Yes. We already have lots of content ready and more is coming soon.
