Acts: An Introduction

PRAY

Ask God for his patience today.

READ

Acts 1:1-11

REFLECT

In the book of Acts, Luke describes the beginning of the early church after Jesus went up to heaven. The people in these stories werent perfect, but in everything they followed Jesus even when it was hard---were talking imprisonment and even death. For the next few weeks, The Daily will follow the book of Acts and look at how we can apply its lessons to our own lives.

RESPOND

Before Jesus returned to heaven, he instructed his disciples to wait. Waiting isnt an easy to thing to do, but God blesses us when we do. What might God be asking you to wait for?
