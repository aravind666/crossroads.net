Know Your Dad: Heath

Heath is a thirtysomething entrepreneur, strong husband, and new dad.

MEET HEATH

"Many of us have dads who dont seem trustworthy or maybe we dont think about knowing them, because maybe we think they dont want to know us. I have come to understand that God, my Dad, wants me. When I realize that the creator of the universe desires me, to hang out with me, to love me, to know me, it evokes in me a desire to be with Him, too. That changes everything."

REFLECT

Psalm 37:4reflects the kind of relationship that Heath is explaining with his heavenly dad.
