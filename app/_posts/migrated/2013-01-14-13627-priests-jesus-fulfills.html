Thursday, January 17

Priests: Jesus Fulfills

Today we will look at Jesuss identity, and how we receive that same identity through him.

READ

Hebrews 7:24-25

Romans 8:16-17

REFLECT

When Jesus was here on earth, he loved perfectly, and showed us a perfect life. Its radical to think that we are co-heirs with Him. We are imperfect, yet we get His inheritance. This is because Jesus is the perfect priest. He is always interceding on our behalf.He makes a way for us to be heirs of all that God has.We are truly perfect and holy priests through Jesus, Gods grace.
