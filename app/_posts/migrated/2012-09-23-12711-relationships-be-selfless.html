Tuesday, September 25

Relationships: Be Selfless

We continue to look at growing good character this week so we can have the real thing in relationships.

READ

Psalms 119:35-37

REFLECT

The Psalmist gives two paths that we can go down: Gods ways or selfish gain. In relationships we can be at risk of believing another person should fulfill our needs and wants like a consumer product. If we let God refine us by spending time pursuing his best for our lives, we take the focus off of selfish pursuits.

RESPOND

Ask God to show you one place where you are being self focused instead of God focused.
