Because one of the main purposes of our weekend services is to provide a place to bring friends who might have given up on church, but not on God, and because Halloween festivities this Saturday evening will occupy most of our friends and neighbors, we’ve decided to cancel the 6:15pm service on Saturday. Trick-or-treat night can be a great opportunity to connect with neighbors, and we want to emphasize the importance of those relational connections, even above and beyond attending a church service.

While we realize not everyone wants to participate in Halloween festivities, we don’t want to participate in pulling people out of their neighborhoods on a rare evening when people interact with each other instead of their televisions.

We’ll see you (and maybe your friends) at the 4:30 on Saturday in Oakley, or any Sunday service in Oakley or Mason this weekend. (Don’t forget the time change Saturday night so you don’t show up an hour early on Sunday.)
