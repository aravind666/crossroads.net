Wednesday, August 17

Spring: Awe

God, may you cause my heart to fall deeper in love with the mystery of you.

READ

Hebrews 12:28-29

REFLECT

Have you ever burned with new love? The goose bumps consume your skin. The butterflies consume your stomach. Every part of your life - thoughts, actions, goals-begins to bend toward that infatuation.

New love can feel exciting, awkward or even scary. But God is already head over heels in love with you. This passage reminds us that we are receiving a kingdom that cannot be shaken. So surrender to Him, and let him consume you. Like falling for your first love, open yourself up to the awe and wonder of God.

Picture falling in love with the Lord, knowing that he will never leave you. You need not worry that he will ever lose his love for you. Hes in. Hes yours. Forever.

RESPOND

Write a letter to God, or speak to him and let him know that you receive his unshakable love.

READ THROUGH THE NEW TESTAMENT
