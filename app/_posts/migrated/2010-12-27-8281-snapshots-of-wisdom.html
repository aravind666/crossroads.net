Week 1 - Proverbs: Snapshots of Wisdom

Monday, December 27
Snapshots of Wisdom

God, open my mind to the wisdom you have for my life.


READ
Proverbs 1:1-7

REFLECT
So what is a proverb, anyway? For many of us, its a short statement closely related to the fortune cookie. In other words, its disconnected from real life.But according to Solomon (writer of most of these things and the wisest guy who ever lived), the opposite is true. Biblical proverbs are supremely practical and teach us how to live wisely.

Over the next weeks, well spend some time learning about what it means to be wise, but always in the practical context of our daily life.

RESPOND

Take a few minutes and ask God for wisdom in any area you feel a lack of clarity. (He has promised that if we ask for it, he will give it to us.)
