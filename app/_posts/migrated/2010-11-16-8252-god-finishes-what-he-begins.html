Tuesday, November 9

God Finishes What He Begins

Take a moment to ask God to help you notice and celebrate his work in your life.


READ

Philippians 1:3-6



REFLECT

God finishes what he begins. These words are cause for celebration and hope as we think about the big challenges Gods calling us to tackle as a community--and in our individual lives. No matter how great the challenge, we can bank on the fact that God finishes what he begins and his grace never runs out.


RESPOND

Think of one challenge you are facing right now. Thank God for his ability to finish the good work hes started in you.


ANOTHER LOOK

Philippians 1:9-11
