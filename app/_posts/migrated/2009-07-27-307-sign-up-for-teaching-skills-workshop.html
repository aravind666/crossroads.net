If you teach in any setting here at Crossroads (Kids Club, CSM, Community Groups, etc.) youre invited to learn, practice and sharpen your skills. There will be two, three-hour sessions, and you must be able to attend both. The first will be a skills workshop, and at the second, youll teach and receive feedback in small groups. Two dates are available: Wednesdays, August 12 and 19 (6-9pm) or Saturdays, September 12 and 19 (9am-noon). All workshops take place at Crossroads Oakley. Space is limited; sign up by August 5.
