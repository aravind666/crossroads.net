Kingdom Come: Good News

Through the Kingdom Come journey, The Daily will be complimenting the guide and small group experience. Well visit some verses and some stories of others that are on the journey with you. Today, lets kick off with a verse.

READ

Colossians 1:13-14

REFLECT

The word redemption in the original greek is apolytrosis which is actually made up of two greek words: one which means to release or to free, the other which means by way of payment or ransom. We are born onto a planet that can feel quite frequently like the domain of darkness with no way out. The good news (quite literally) is through Jesus we have been delivered from that domain and enter into the Kingdom of God.

RESPOND

Its so easy to notice the darkness over the good news. Today, challenge yourself to spot the Kingdom of God shining through.When those instances occur, journal in the back of your guide about why you thought those glimpsesdemonstrated Gods character to you.
