Monday, February 11

Listen: Hidden

For the next four weeks well be looking at a repeating cycle within the life of a maturing follower of God. Growing and acting on our faith involves listening for God, discerning, connecting, and acting (although every step is active). This week we look at listening.

READ

Proverbs 2:1-6

REFLECT

Do you believe this verse? Did you read it and think something like, yes, in my mind that sounds very Bible-y and accurate? Maybe you got hung up at fear of the Lord. However it hit you, wrestling with not only knowing these words, but truly believing them, is the beginning of a desire to listen for Gods voice. When we believe that He has hidden treasures and vast knowledge to give us, we remember how small we are in our little corner of the universe. God has the biggest perspective, and realizing that can pop your spiritual ears right open.

RESPOND

Take some time and picture what Gods view of the world might look like.
