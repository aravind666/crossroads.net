Tuesday, August 10

The Kingdom is Creative

Today, ask God for the opportunity to experience the creativity of his Kingdom.

READ

Isaiah 43:18-21

REFLECT

Think about the most creative person you know. Its no leap to say that any time we are exercising creativity, were reflecting the character of God. The Kingdom is Gods imagination at work.

Its easy to assume that God retired from the business of creation after he set the world in motion. But in todays scripture from Isaiah, were reminded that God is still using his perfect, redemptive, creative force to make things right in a broken world. He never disconnects from us.

RESPOND

Think about the places in the world--or in your own life--where you notice brokenness and pain. Now envision for a moment how things would be different if God totally redeemed these situations. What specific pictures come to mind? Ask God to bring about the kind of redemption you envisioned in the world, and in your life.

ANOTHER LOOK

Revelation 21:1-5
