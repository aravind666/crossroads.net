Acts: Paul In Athens

Reminder: this weeks study is about the Apostle Paul, an early follower of Jesus who "changed the game." The study is presented in a paraphrase of the Bible called The Message, which gives us biblical text in simple, modern language.

READ
Acts 17:16-34

REFLECT
The city of Athens was both the cultural and religious center of Greece, home to some of the most renowned philosophers in history and a place where virtually any god or deity known to man could be worshipped. Paul used language the Athenians would understand and even quotes from some of their philosophers to reason with them about the good news of Jesus and his resurrection.

RESPOND
Even for people who know and follow Jesus, its easy to fall into the trap of worshipping idols instead of God. Consider any "idols" in your life that have consumed too much of your time and attention (your "worship")--whether that be your job, your finances or even your family--and ask God to focus your mind and heart on the good news of Jesus and his resurrection.
