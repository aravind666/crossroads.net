GO Cincinnati, a multi-church, citywide serving day, takes place this year on Saturday, May 17. Like last year, we'll unleash thousands of volunteers all over greater Cincinnati to paint, landscape, organize and more in a one-day, city-wide blitz of compassion.

If you have a heart for our city and would like to use your organization and people skills to lead a project, you can sign up now.
