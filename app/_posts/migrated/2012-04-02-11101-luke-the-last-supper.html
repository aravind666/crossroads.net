Tuesday, April 3

Luke: The Last Supper

As we go through the Game Changers series on the weekend, The Daily will study people from the Bible who changed the game. This week well continue studying Jesus, reflecting on the Easter story in the book of Luke.

READ

Luke 22:14-27

REFLECT

During this intimate and weighty gathering between Jesus and his closest friends, Jesus must listen to them argue about who is the best. Somehow, Jesus followers havent yet comprehended his daily example of the Kingdom being powered by the servant. This thinking is so countercultural (then and now), that even the men who walked alongside Jesus day after day had a hard time putting it into practice.

PRAY

God, help me become a true servant.
