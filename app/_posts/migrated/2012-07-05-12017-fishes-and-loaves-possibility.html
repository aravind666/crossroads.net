Monday, July 9

Fishes and Loaves: Possibility

This week well look at the miracle of Jesus feeding over 5,000 people with a few loaves of bread and a couple of fish. This miraculous sign gives us a glimpse of the good life and at the way God provides.

READ

John 6:1-13

REFLECT

Jesus asked about food for the crowd that had followed him to the mountainside. Philip and Andrew, two of Jesus disciples, responded in very different ways to his question. Philip pointed out the impossibility of the task; Andrew left an open window in his response for the possibility of what Jesus could do.

RESPOND

Be an Andrew today. Look around at the resources you do have. What might God want to make of the resources that he has already given you?
