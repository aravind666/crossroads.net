This Changes Everything: Joseph

God had chosen Mary to be the mother of his Son. And she was all in. Today, The Daily will look at the story of Joseph, the man who would join her on the journey.

LISTEN

Mary and Joseph

READ

Matthew 1:18-25

REFLECT

Joseph was a carpenter in Nazareth. When he learned that his betrothed, Mary, was pregnant he assumed she had been unfaithful. He hadnt signed on for this. It wasnt just Joseph and Mary now. It was Joseph, Mary and baby. But he was kind and sensitive to Mary and decided to quietly end their engagement to spare her shame.

He was in a hard place, but God had a different plan for Joseph. He sent an angel who told him that Mary carried the Son of God and he shouldnt be afraid to be her husband.

God asked Joseph to take Mary as his wife. More than that, to be an earthly father his own Son. Joseph, fully aware of the public humiliation that he and Mary would face, willingly obeyed.

RESPOND

When the going gets tough, the tough get going. Joseph got tough when God asked him. Is God asking you to get tough? Pray for His strength and guidance in an area of your life that needs it.
